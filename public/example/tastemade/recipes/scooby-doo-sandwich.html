Scooby Doo Sandwich

Rut-roh, this towering sandwich — made with all the fixins' and an awesome aioli — is inspired by one of our favorite Saturday morning cartoons.

(1)

By zoe kelly

Updated: 10/5/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Scooby Doo Sandwich

  • 3 eggs
  • 2–3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 loaf thick-sliced white bread, toasted
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1 pound deli honey ham
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 pound swiss cheese
  • 1 large red onion, sliced
  • 1/2 pound bacon, cooked
  • 3 avocado slices
  • 1 jar pickle slices
  • Mustard
  • Hot sauce
  • 1 large green olive

Sun-dried Tomato Aioli

  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. In a frying pan on medium heat, add 2–3 tablespoon of cooking oil.
  2. Add eggs and begin to baste with the oil until cooked to desired doneness. Remove to a plate and season with salt and pepper.
