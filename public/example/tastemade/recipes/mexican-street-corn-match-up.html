Mexican Street Corn Match Up

Game day, movie night or a last-minute get together; set out TOSTITOS® Scoops! paired with this cheesy, crunchy Mexican street corn dip for a match made in snack heaven.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 10
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • TOSTITOS® Scoops!® Original Chips
  • 4 corn on the cob, husks removed
  • 1 jalapeño
  • ½ white onion, chopped
  • ½ jar of TOSTITOS® Queso Blanco Flavored Dip
  • 1 block cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup shredded quesadilla cheese
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoons ancho chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili lime seasoning, plus more for garnish
  • ½ cup cilantro, chopped and divided
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cotija, for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Heat a grill pan or grill to medium high.
  3. Grill the corn, jalapeño, and onion until the onion has softened and everything is slightly charred. Remove from heat and set aside and allow to cool.
  4. Cut the corn off of the cob, and reserve.
  5. Chop the onion and jalapeño, removing the seeds for a less spicy version.
  6. To a medium bowl, add ¾ of the grilled corn (reserving ¼ for garnish), the peppers, the onions, TOSTITOS® Queso Blanco Flavored dip, cream cheese, shredded cheese, smoked paprika, chili powder, garlic powder, chili lime seasoning, and half of the cilantro. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Place into an oven safe dish and bake for 10 minutes or until warmed through.
  8. Garnish with the reserved grilled corn, chili lime seasoning, cotija cheese, and cilantro. Serve with TOSTITOS® Scoops!®
