Stretchy Cheese Ice Cream

If you've ever wanted to recreate booza, a trendy, stretchy ice cream, this recipe is for you. This unusual ice cream has a cheesy twist, making it a dessert you have to try!

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup milk powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons konjac root powder (glucomannan)
  • 1 cup processed cheese, cubed

Steps

  1. In a pot, combine milk, sugar, milk powder, vanilla, and cheese. Gently heat to a low simmer and constantly whisk until the cheese has melted.
  2. Gradually add the konjac root powder, while whisking, until the mixture is thick and smooth. Remove from heat. Transfer to a container and chill. If the mixture is lumpy, use a mesh strainer to remove any lumps.
