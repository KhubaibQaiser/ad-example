Bacon-Wrapped Onion Rings

For extra crispy and flavorful onion rings, skip the breadcrumbs and use bacon instead.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 12
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Onion Rings

  • 2 sweet onions
  • 24 strips bacon
  • 1 tablespoon Kikkoman® Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce

Sauce

  • 1/4 cup Kikkoman® Sriracha Mayo Sauce
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons mustard
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice

Steps

Onion Rings

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F.
  2. Line two baking sheets with foil and place cooling racks on top.
  3. Slice onion into 1/2-inch slices. Pop out rings containing two layers of onion. Brush with Kikkoman® Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce.
  4. Wrap one layer of bacon around onion and repeat with another, trimming any excess. Secure bacon ends with toothpicks or skewers. Brush again with Kikkoman® Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce.
  5. Place six rings on each baking sheet and bake in the oven for 30 minutes. Flip after 15 minutes and continue to cook until onion is cooked and bacon is crisp.
  6. Allow to cool.

Sauce

  1. Stir dipping sauce ingredients together and let flavors marry while you prepare onion rings.
