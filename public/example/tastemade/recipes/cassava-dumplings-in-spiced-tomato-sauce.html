Cassava Dumplings in Spiced Tomato Sauce

These veggie-based dumplings are accented with spicy tomato sauce.

By Nyesha Arrington

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Cassava Dumplings in Spiced Tomato Sauce

  • 2 cups cassava
  • 1 cup cassava flour
  • 1 teaspoon himalayan sea salt
  • 1 pinch fresh nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salted water for blanching dumplings, plus 1 tablespoon hot water reserved for dough
  • 1–2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • Spiced tomato sauce, recipe below
  • Parsley, chopped, for garnish

Spiced Tomato Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon cumin seed
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 6 roma tomatoes, peeled and deseeded
  • 3 tablespoons organic coconut oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 teaspoons fresh ginger, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fresh turmeric, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 10 sprigs cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1 small lime, for juice
  • 1 tablespoon organic extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut the cassava in quarters, bake or steam until fork tender then remove skin.
  2. In a food processor, pulse cooked cassava, with the reserved hot water until smooth.
