Breakfast Biscuit Sandwich

Craving a classic breakfast sandwich? Wake up and whip up chef Frankie Celenza's sausage egg and cheese sandwich served on a buttery, homemade everything bagel biscuit.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Everything Bagel Cheddar Biscuits

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 1 cup cold buttermilk plus 2 tablespoons, divided
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons minced chives
  • 3 tablespoons everything bagel seasoning

Sandwich Filling

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 16 eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 8 frozen sausage breakfast patties, thawed
  • 8 slices american cheese
  • Hot sauce or ketchup (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the flour, baking powder and salt. Add the butter and use your hands or a fork to break it up into little pieces. Make a well in the center. Add 1 cup of buttermilk, sour cream and honey and gently stir together, incorporating the dry ingredients. Fold in the cheddar cheese and chives.
