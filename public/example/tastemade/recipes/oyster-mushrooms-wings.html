Oyster Mushrooms "Wings"

Want all the flavors of wings without the meat? Oyster mushrooms make a perfect replacement in this gameday app.

By Derrell Smith

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Mad Good Food

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Oysters Mushroom Wings

  • 2 pounds oyster mushrooms
  • 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup cashew milk
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup corn starch

Lemon Pepper Sauce

  • 1 cup butter, warmed
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 2 teaspoons lemon pepper seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine together all of the ingredients and mix well.
  2. In a medium bowl, stir together cashew milk and apple cider vinegar. This will serve as a vegan buttermilk to tenderize the mushrooms and create a juicy center to our “fried chicken.
