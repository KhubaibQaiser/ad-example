Chalkboard Cookies

Tell people how you really feel with these deliciously legit chalkboard cookies that use natural, edible chalk.

By jerrelle guy

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 12 min
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Shortbread cookies

  • 2 sticks softened butter (dairy-free if you like)
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups plus 3 tablespoons flour, plus more for dusting
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Chalkboard icing

  • 2 tablespoons water from a can of chickpeas (or 2 tablespoons egg whites)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2–2 cups powdered sugar
  • Black or forest green food gel, for coloring

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat the butter, sugar and vanilla with a handheld beater until fluffy, about 3 minutes.
  2. Add the the flour, beat everything together until just combined. Shape the dough into a disc, wrap with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 20 minutes.
