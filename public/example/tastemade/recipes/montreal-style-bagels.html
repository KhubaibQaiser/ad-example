Montreal-Style Bagels

Have you ever tried a Montreal-style bagel? Learn how to make these trendy bagels right in your own home kitchen — a recipe perfect for breakfast, brunch, or whenever a craving strikes.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

Montreal-Style Bagels

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 3 tablespoons canola oil
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 large egg
  • 4 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 14 cups water
  • 1/3 cup honey

Topping

  • 1 cup sesame seeds

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk warm water, honey, canola oil, yeast, and egg. Then stir in salt and flour in 3 parts, until a soft dough forms.
  2. Knead dough on a lightly floured surface for about 10 minutes, until smooth. Set aside on a floured surface, cover with a clean towel, and let rest for 10 minutes.
