New York Style Pizza

A classic New York style thin-crust cheese pizza.

(2)Recipe Notes (2)

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

New York Style Pizza Dough

  • 2 cups lukewarm water
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 5 1/2 cups bread flour, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Cheese Pizza

  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 5 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons cornmeal, for dusting
  • 8 ounces low-moisture mozzarella
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, divided

Steps

  1. Note: this recipe yields 4 14-inches thin crust pizzas.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the water and yeast and let sit until it begins to bubble, about 10 minutes. Next, we will add 4 cups of flour and the salt. Stir with a wooden spoon to bring the dough together. Gradually stir in up to 1 more cup of flour, stopping when the dough is slightly sticky to the touch and can hold shape.
