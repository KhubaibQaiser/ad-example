Grilled Strawberry Jalapeno Mezcal Margarita

A smokey, spicy, and fruity cocktail.

By danni rose

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Simple Syrup

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • 1/2 cup lime juice (about 5–6 lime)
  • 2 jalapeños, halved

Grilled Strawberries

  • 6 ounces strawberries, trimmed
  • 1/4 cup water

Margarita

  • 4 ounces strawberry puree
  • 4 ounces mezcal
  • 1 ounce orange liqueur
  • 1 1/2 ounces lime jalapeño simple syrup, to taste
  • Sliced jalapeño, for garnish
  • Sliced lime, for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine the sugar, water, lime zest, lime juice, and jalapeños in a small sauce pot.
  2. Cook over medium heat just until the sugar dissolves, about 2–3 minute.
