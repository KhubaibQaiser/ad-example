Insalata Di Carciofi

Shaved artichoke is the star of this cheesy, lemony salad.

By daniele uditi

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 6 shaved baby artichokes
  • 4 cups of wild arugula
  • 1/2 cup of toasted pine nuts
  • 1 squeeze large lemon and the zest
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup shaved parmigiano reggiano
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch pepper

Steps

  1. In a large salad bowl add the arugula, pine nuts, the baby artichokes.
  2. Squeeze the juice of the lemon and add the olive oil.
