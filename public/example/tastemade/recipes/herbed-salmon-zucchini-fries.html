Herbed Salmon & Zucchini Fries

Banish boring weeknight meals with this easy sheet-pan dinner, which features salmon topped with lemon and fresh herbs roasted alongside breadcrumb-coated zucchini "fries."

(1)Recipe Notes (1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Zucchini Fries

  • 2 large zucchinis
  • 1 cup panko or italian
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Herbed Salmon

  • 2 (6 ounces) skinless salmon fillets
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil, as needed
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Fresh dill, to taste
  • Fresh parsley
  • 2 garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F.
  2. Trim the ends and cut into 1/2 inch slices. Set aside.
