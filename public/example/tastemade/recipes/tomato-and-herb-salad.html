Tomato and Herb Salad

A fresh take on a caprese, this tomato-based salad features an anchovy, olive, and herb dressing.

By daniele uditi

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 30 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 heirloom tomatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 3 eaches anchovies filets
  • 1/2 cup of fresh desalted capers
  • 1/2 cup black kalamata olives
  • 1/3 cup fresh basil
  • 1/3 cup fresh oregano
  • 1/3 cup parsley
  • 1/2 ounce extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup toasted breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup mozzarella ciliegine
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the garlic and anchovies finely and add in a small bowl.
  2. Rough chop the herbs and add to the bowl.
