Evelyn's Arroz Con Pollo

Traditional Hispanic chicken and rice with tons of flavor for any family meal

By Alex Hill

Updated: 9/25/2024

Recipe from:

Spice Spice Baby

Prep Time: 2 hr
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Evelyn's Arroz Con Pollo

  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 red pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 green pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 1 cup parboiled extra long grain rice, rinsed
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 packet goya sazón achiote seasoning
  • 1/4 cup sofrito
  • Kosher salt & fresh cracked black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • Cilantro, for garnish

Seasoning for the chicken

  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon diamond kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons premade sofrito

Steps

  1. In a bowl season chicken with adobo seasonings (salt, pepper, onion & garlic powder) and sofrito.
  2. In a dutch oven, over medium high heat add EVOO and let it get hot.
