Roasted Strawberry Balsamic Chicken Salad

A refreshing spring salad with fruity dressing.

By danni rose

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Roasted Chicken and Strawberries

  • 21 ounces boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, divided
  • 3 tablespoons chopped rosemary
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 10 ounces strawberries, trimmed

Roasted Strawberry Balsamic Dressing

  • Roasted strawberry
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Salad Assembly

  • 5 cups mixed greens
  • 1 bunch watercress
  • Tri-color quinoa, cooked
  • Heirloom cherry tomato, halved
  • Candied pecan, whole or chopped
  • Strawberry, sliced
  • Roasted chicken
  • Roasted strawberry balsamic dressing

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a two sheet trays with parchment paper.
  2. Place the chicken on one sheet tray and the strawberries on the other.
