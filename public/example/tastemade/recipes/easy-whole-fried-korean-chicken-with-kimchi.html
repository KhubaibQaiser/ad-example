Whole Fried Korean Chicken With Kimchi

We deep fried an entire chicken because we couldn't think of a reason not to.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 1 hr 20 min
Servings: 1
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1 whole small chicken, spatchcocked
  • Salt
  • 3 1/2 ounces cornflour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 1/4 ounces plain flour
  • 4 1/2 fluid ounces cold water
  • 4 1/4 fluid ounces vodka

Sauce

  • 4 tablespoons gochujang
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil

Kimchi

  • 1/2 chinese cabbage, quartered
  • 4 tablespoons sea salt
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, peeled
  • 2 teaspoons caster sugar
  • 2 tablespoons korean shrimp paste
  • 3 tablespoons dried gochugaru
  • 3 1/2 ounces daikon, chopped into matchsticks
  • 6 spring onions, chopped into matchsticks

Steps

  1. Combine 2 teaspoons salt, 1 ounce cornflour, and 1/2 teaspoon baking powder in a large bowl and whisk to combine.
  2. Make two slices into the chicken thigh to make sure no moisture builds up, as this can cause an oil explosion. Add chicken thigh and toss until every surface is coated.
