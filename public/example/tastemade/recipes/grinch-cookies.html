Grinch Cookies

Easy and cute little holiday cookies that anyone, even the grumpiest of souls, can enjoy!!

By Mia Starr

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 24
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 2 eaches eggs, large
  • 1/3 cup vegetable or canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon extract
  • 1/2–1 teaspoon green gel color
  • 1 box white cake mix
  • 24–30 mini candy hearts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line 2 baking sheets with parchment.
  2. In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, mix the egg, oil, cinnamon extract and gel color on low to incorporate.
