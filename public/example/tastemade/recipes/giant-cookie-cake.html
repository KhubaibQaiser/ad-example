Giant Cookie Cake

Cookie or cake?! All we know is it's immense

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 170 grams unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 175 grams soft light brown sugar
  • 25 grams caster sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 8 milliliters vanilla extract
  • 250 grams plain flour
  • 5 grams baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 200 grams chocolate chips
  • Frosting
  • 54 grams butter
  • 12 grams cocoa powder
  • 155 grams icing sugar
  • 2 1/2 milliliters vanilla extract
  • 1–2 tablespoons whole milk
  • Sprinkle

Steps

  1. With a stand mixer or a handheld beater, beat the butter until it is light and fluffy, 1–2 minutes.
  2. Add both sugars and beat again for another 2 minutes. Add the eggs and vanilla and beat until incorporated. Scrape down the sides where needed.
