Carrot Cake Pakoras with Cream Cheese Dip

Pakoras, a type of deep-fried Indian fritter, are typically savory. But chef Nadiya Hussain puts a sweet spin on her pakoras by emulating classic carrot cake, complete with a cream cheese frosting dip!

By Nadiya Hussein

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 20
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Cream Cheese Dip

  • 100 grams full-fat cream cheese
  • 100 grams unsalted butter, softened
  • 51 grams icing sugar
  • 30 milliliters maple syrup, plus extra for drizzling
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch mixed spice

Pakoras

  • 1 1/2–2 liters vegetable oil
  • 300 grams carrots
  • 50 grams walnuts, roughly chopped
  • 50 grams raisins
  • 120 grams self-raising flour
  • 2 1/2 grams baking powder
  • 20 grams caster sugar
  • 5 grams mixed spice
  • 3 grams salt
  • 2 medium free-range eggs, lightly beaten
  • 0–1 gram icing sugar, for dusting

Steps

  1. Mix the cream cheese and butter in a bowl until smooth and fully combined, with no lumps.
  2. Mix in the icing sugar, maple syrup and salt, then transfer into a serving bowl.
