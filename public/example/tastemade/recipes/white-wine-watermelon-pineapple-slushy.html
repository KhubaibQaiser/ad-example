White Wine Watermelon & Pineapple Slushy

Using just a handful of light ingredients and Kim Crawford’s Sauvignon Blanc, this is the showstopper for your next girls’ movie or game night. The double layer of pineapple and watermelon flavors pair beautifully with the full tropical tasting notes of the wine.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 3 hr
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Watermelon Slushy

  • 1 cup watermelon, diced
  • 1 ¼ cups ice
  • ½ cup Kim Crawford Sauvignon Blanc
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp lime Juice

Pineapple Slushy

  • 1 C pineapple, diced
  • 1 ¼ C ice
  • ½ C Kim Crawford Sauvignon Blanc
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp lime Juice

Melon Ball Garnish

  • 1 watermelon
  • Handful of mint
  • Cocktail sticks

Lime Sugar Rim

  • 1 lime, zested
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
Get Ingredients

Steps

Watermelon Slushy

  1. Freeze chunks of watermelon overnight.
  2. When you’re ready to make the slushy, remove the watermelon from the freezer and add along with the crushed ice, honey, lime and Kim Crawford Sauvignon Blanc into a food processor. Blitz in 5 second bursts until combined into a smooth, slushy consistency.
  3. Pour mixture into freezable containers and place into the freezer while you prepare the pineapple slushy.

Pineapple Slushy

  1. Freeze chunks of pineapple overnight.
  2. When you’re ready to make the slushy, remove the pinapple from the freezer and add along with the crushed ice, honey, lime and Kim Crawford Sauvignon Blanc into a food processor. Blitz in 5 second bursts until combined into a smooth, slushy consistency.
  3. Pour mixture into freezable containers and place into the freezer while you prepare your ganishes.

Melon Ball Garnish

  1. Using a melon baller, scoop out balls of watermelon. Skewer the melon onto cocktail sticks and freeze for 30 minutes.

Lime Sugar Rim

  1. Zest a lime and mix with 2 tbsp sugar. Dip the rim of the glass in water and then straight into the zesty sugar.
  2. Pour slushies into individual glasses, top with garnishes, and enjoy!
