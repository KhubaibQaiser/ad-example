Cheese and Onion Chips Quiche

This simple quiche recipe uses a surprising ingredient to get a perfect crispy crust: potato chips!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy

Ingredients

Tart Case

  • 250 grams good quality cheese
  • & onion crisp
  • 50 grams unsalted butter
  • 50 grams parmesan cheese

Filling

  • 18 grams plain flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 200 grams double cream
  • 1/2 jar caramelised onions
  • 15 milliliters olive oil
  • 75 grams gruyère cheese
  • 75 grams cheddar cheese
  • Salt and black pepper

Steps
