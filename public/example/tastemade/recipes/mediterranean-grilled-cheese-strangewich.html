Mediterranean Grilled Cheese Strangewich

Drop the rainy day routine and brighten any day with our Mediterranean Grilled Cheese Strangewich.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 1

Ingredients

Mediterranean Grilled Cheese Strangewich

  • 2 Japanese eggplants, cut into 1/2' thick slices
  • 1 red bell pepper, 1/4rd and seeded
  • 1 red onion, sliced into 1/2' thick slices
  • 1 zucchini, cut into 1/2' thick slices
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons thyme, picked and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons oregano, picked and chopped
  • 1 lemon, cut in half for juicing

Romesco Aioli

  • 1 6 ounces jar Romesco
  • 1/2 cup Best Foods mayonnaise
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Sandwiches

  • 8 slices sourdough
  • Extra mayonnaise for brushing
  • 1 cup fontina cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup Parmigiano Reggiano, grated fine

Steps

Mediterranean Grilled Cheese Strangewich

  1. Preheat grill or grill pan to 400 degrees. Brush all veggies with oil and season with salt and pepper. Grill veggies until golden and tender. Remove from heat and toss together with chopped herbs and a squeeze of lemon juice. Set aside.
  2. Distribute grilled veggies amongst 4 of the slices. Mix cheeses together and distribute evenly as well.
  3. Top with remaining bread and griddle until golden and cheese is melted, flipping once (7-10 minutes).
  4. Remove from heat, serve immediately.

Romesco Aioli

  1. In a bowl combine Romesco, mayonnaise and lemon juice.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.

Sandwiches

  1. Assemble sandwiches by brushing bread on one side with plain mayonnaise.
  2. Build sandwiches on the non-mayonnaise side and brush all with Romesco Aioli.
