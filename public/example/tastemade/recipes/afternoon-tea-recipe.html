Scones: Clotted Cream or Jam First?

Jam or cream first? Time to pick a side once and for all!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 900 grams self raising flour
  • 10 grams baking powder
  • 200 grams butter, cold and cubed
  • 200 grams caster sugar
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 100 milliliters double cream
  • 100 milliliters whole milk
  • 5 milliliters vanilla paste
  • Clotted cream
  • Strawberry jam
  • Tea to serve

Steps

  1. In a large bowl mix together the flour with the sugar and baking powder so well blended. Making sure the butter is cut into small pieces, rub quickly through the flour mix to make a breadcrumb like consistency. You don't want to overwork the mixture so try to handle it as little as possible.
  2. In a jug add the beaten eggs, milk, cream and vanilla and whisk together. You may need 15 milliliters extra milk later so keep it close.
