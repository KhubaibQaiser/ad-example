Shaken Beef With Crispy Rice

Delight in the bold flavors of shaken beef, served with crispy rice for the perfect crunch, creating a tantalizing Vietnamese-inspired culinary experience.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4

Ingredients

Shaken Beef

  • 2 pounds diced rib eye, cleaned up, no fat
  • 3 shallots sliced into rounds
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 3 cloves of garlic, smashed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes whole
  • Salt and butter
  • Avocado oil

Garlic Brown Butter

  • 2 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 thai chiles smasheds

Crispy Rice

  • 3 cups cooked white rice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 pound butter

Steps

  1. Season salt and pepper the beef.
  2. Add oil to a hot pan.
