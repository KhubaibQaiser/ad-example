Healthy Macaroni and Cheese

Try a healthy twist on classic macaroni and cheese with Chef Erwan Heussaff veggie-forward recipe! Creamy sweet potatoes and cauliflower melt together with plenty of cheese for a decadent dinner you'll feel good about eating.

By Erwan Heussaff

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Lightened Up

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 4
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 pounds sweet potatoes
  • 2 1/2 cups small cauliflower florets
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons plain flour
  • 2 cups full fat milk
  • 1 teaspoon english mustard (or dijon)
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika (plus another 1/4 teaspoon to sprinkle on top)
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded aged cheddar (plus 1/4 cup to sprinkle on top)
  • 1/3 cup almond flour (or as needed – depends on dish size)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Cook the potatoes: Preheat oven to 400°F
  2. Boil salted water in a large pan with lid.
