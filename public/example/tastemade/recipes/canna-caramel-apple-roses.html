Canna-Caramel Apple Roses

These cannabis-infused caramel apple roses are as elegant as they are delicious. *For informational purposes only. Observe all applicable laws and regulations.

By Ingrid Nilsen

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Cooking With Pride

Prep Time: 55 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 3
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pastry dough (usually made from scratch but can use packaged)
  • 3 red apples
  • 2 cups pomegranate or beet juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon rose water
  • 1/2 cup cannabis infused caramel sauce
  • 1/2 cup non-infused caramel sauce
  • 1/2 cup apricot preserves
  • Confectioners sugar
  • 1 fuji apple, peeled for plating
  • 1 cup bourbon
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Fill a large bowl or container with pomegranate or beet juice.
  2. Add lemon juice and rosewater.
