Creamy Braised Short Rib Pasta

A pasta dish that will impress anyone with a surprise ingredient - coffee.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 4 hr
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant coffee
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 tablespoon plus 2 teaspoons kosher salt, divided
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper, divided
  • 3 pounds beef short ribs
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 medium yellow onions, diced
  • 1 1/2 medium carrots, diced
  • 1 medium celery stalk, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2/3 cup red wine
  • 1 cup beef stock
  • 1/2 cup coffee
  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 8 ounces pappardelle pasta, cooked
  • Parmesan, shaved, for garnish
  • Chopped parsley, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the instant coffee, onion powder, garlic powder, paprika, 2 teaspoons kosher salt and 2 teaspoons black pepper.
  2. Season the short ribs on all sides with the spice mixture.
