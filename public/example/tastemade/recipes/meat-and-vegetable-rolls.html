Meat and Vegetable Rolls

Meat And Vegetable Rolls recipe

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pound filet steak pieces, pounded thin
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 yellow pepper
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 onion
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh thyme
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 1/2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable broth

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables into thin strips and place in a mixing bowl. Add salt and olive oil and mix with hands.
  2. Season the steaks with salt, pepper and fresh thyme. Arrange 1 handful of vegetable strips at the end of each piece of meat, roll up and secure with toothpicks.
