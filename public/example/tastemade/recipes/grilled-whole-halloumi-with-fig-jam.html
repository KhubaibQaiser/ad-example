Grilled Whole Halloumi with Fig Jam

Halloumi cheese can be fried or grilled and maintain its shape, resulting in a crispy, golden crust and a slightly melty center - this version gets topped with caramely fig jam to balance the cheese's briny bite.

By Numra Siddiqui

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

If These Meals Could Talk

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Average
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 400g (14 oz) fresh figs
  • 4 dried figs, roughly chopped
  • 1 star anise
  • 112g (4 oz) caster sugar (superfine sugar)
  • Juice 1 lemon
  • 4 sprigs sprigs basil
  • 2 x 250g (9 oz) blocks halloumi
  • Olive oil for drizzling
  • 2 tbsp runny honey

Steps

  1. Set 4 fresh figs aside, then roughly chop the rest.
  2. Put the chopped figs in a pan with the dried figs, star anise, caster sugar, lemon juice and 75ml (5 tbsp) water.
