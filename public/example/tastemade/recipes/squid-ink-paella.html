Squid Ink Paella

This stunning paella recipe is packed with a variety of seafood, from pawns to mussels to clams. If you've ever wanted to try cooking with squid ink, this is the dish for you!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 2
Hard
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 head-on prawns
  • 6 mussels
  • 4 1/4 ounces asari clams (manila clams)
  • 3 1/2 fluid ounces white wine
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 chili peppers
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups of uncooked rice
  • 2 teaspoons tomato paste
  • 6 3/4 fluid ounces squid ink sauce
  • 10 1/4 fluid ounces consommé soup
  • Seafood stock from the mussels and clam
  • 15 baby octopuses
  • Italian parsley, as needed

Steps

  1. In a pan, add olive oil and sauté the prawns. Once cooked, remove the prawns from the pan and set them aside.
  2. Using the same pan, steam the mussels and asari clams in white wine.
