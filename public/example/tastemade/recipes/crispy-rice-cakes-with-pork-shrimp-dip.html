Crispy Rice Cakes with Pork & Shrimp Dip

Use up your leftover rice in this easy appetizer recipe, which pairs crispy rice cakes with a savory pork & shrimp dip.

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 6
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Crispy Rice Cakes with Pork & Shrimp Dip

  • 3 cups cooked white rice or sticky rice
  • Canola for frying

Dip

  • 1 tbsp cilantro stem and root, roughly chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ½ tsp whole black pepper
  • 1 ½ cups coconut milk
  • ½ cup raw shrimp, cleaned and minced
  • 1 ½ cup ground pork
  • 3 tbsp shallots, minced
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts, finely chopped
  • Chili oil for serving

Steps

  1. Form 1 tablespoon portions of cooked rice into flat rounds using a round mold or your hands.
  2. Place on a greased parchment or silicone-lined baking sheet and dehydrate in the oven at 200°F, until completely dry.
