Toasted Coconut Tres Leches Cake

How do you create a slice of heaven? Use 100% real milk, just without the lactose, in this delicious Toasted Coconut Tres Leches Cake so you can enjoy it even if you’re sensitive.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Ingredients

Toasted Coconut Tres Leches Cake

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup coconut sugar
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 cup Lactaid 2% milk
  • 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened condensed coconut milk
  • 1 (12-ounce) can evaporated coconut milk

Topping

  • 2 cups coconut whipped topping
  • 1/2 cup toasted coconut flakes
  • 1 cup raspberries

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 13 by 9 baking pan.
  2. In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, beat the sugar, coconut sugar and eggs on medium speed until thickened and pale.
  3. Add vanilla and mix until incorporated.
  4. In a medium bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, sea salt, cinnamon and nutmeg.
  5. Slowly incorporate the dry ingredients into the sugar and egg mixture until just combined. Pour into prepared baking pan.
  6. Bake cake for about 30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. While the cake is baking, mix together Lactaid 2% milk, condensed coconut milk and evaporated coconut milk. Set aside.
  8. After the cake has baked, allow it to cool for 5 minutes then poke with a toothpick all over the surface of the cake.
  9. Pour the milk mixture over the warm cake and allow to soak in for 30 minutes.
  10. After the cake is completely cooled, top with coconut whipped topping, toasted coconut flakes and raspberries. Chill for 2 hours, or up to 24 hours, and serve.
