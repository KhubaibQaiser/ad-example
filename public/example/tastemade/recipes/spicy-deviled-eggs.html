Spicy Deviled Eggs

Classic deviled eggs get spiced up with cayenne.

By Keith Corbin

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Spicy Deviled Eggs

  • 15 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup spicy mayonnaise
  • 1/2 tablespoon champagne vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon chives, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chervil, minced
  • 1 teaspoon tarragon, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Paprika, for garnish
  • Chive, minced, for garnish

Spicy Mayonnaise

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 cloves garlic, roasted
  • 1 clove garlic, raw
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne
  • Hot sauce, to taste
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. For the eggs: Add 1 cup eggs to pot and fill with water to cover and place over high heat. Bring to a boil and turn off heat. Let eggs sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Drain and peel the eggs and let them cool.
