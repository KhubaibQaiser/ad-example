Baked Oyster with Marmite

We travel North to the Montreal to take a peek inside four kitchens to get the stories behind their signature dishes.

By derek dammann

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Behind The Dish

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Duxelles

  • 9 ounces white button mushrooms
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • Leaves from 2 sprigs fresh thyme, finely chopped
  • Kosher salt
  • White pepper
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup very thinly sliced green onions

Marmite Mayonnaise

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 2 1/2 cups canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • Kosher salt and black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons marmite
  • 2 tablespoons reserved oyster liquor
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • Kosher salt, to taste

Oysters

  • 6 extra-large beach angel oysters
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 pieces bull kelp

To Assemble

  • Duxelle
  • Marmite mayonnaise
  • Oyster, sliced and cooked

Steps

  1. In a food processor, add the mushrooms and pulse them until they have the texture of coarse bread crumbs.
  2. Heat the olive oil and the butter in a medium, heavy saucepan over medium heat.
