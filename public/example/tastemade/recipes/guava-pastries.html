Guava Pastries

These sweet, flaky pastries feature a cream cheese and guava paste filling.

By Monti Carlo

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 9
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla paste or extract
  • Egg, divided
  • Zest of 1 1/2 lemons
  • 2 sheets puff pastry, thawed
  • Ap flour, for dusting your work surface
  • 5 ounces guava paste, cut into 1/4 inch thick strips
  • 1 tablespoon confectioner's sugar, for dusting finished pastries

Steps

  1. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper or a baking mat. Preheat your oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix the sugar and salt together.
