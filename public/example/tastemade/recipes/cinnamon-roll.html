Cinnamon Roll Protein Cookies

Satisfy your sweet tooth and get your gains with a batch of cinnamon roll-style cookies made with spelt flour and vanilla protein powder.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 8 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups spelt flour
  • 2 scoops vanilla protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg white
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter, warmed
  • 3 tablespoons pure maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Combine flour, protein powder, baking powder, and cinnamon in a large mixing bowl.
