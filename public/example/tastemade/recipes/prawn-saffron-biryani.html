Prawn Saffron Biryani

Nadiya Hussain shows us how to create the perfect dishes to complement any kind of day; like her zesty and comforting one-pot prawn saffron biryani made using Indian five-spice.

By nadiya hussein

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 17 3/4 ounces basmati rice
  • 1 large pinch saffron
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 3/4 ounces unsalted butter
  • 5 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • ½ lemon
  • 2 large red chilies, quartered
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon indian five-spice (panch phoran)
  • 1 large handful fresh parsley, roughly chopped
  • 12 1/4 ounces raw king prawns, peeled
  • 5 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Put the rice, saffron, cinnamon and salt into a large saucepan with 53 fluid ounces of cold water.
  2. Place over a high heat, bring to the boil and cook for 8 minutes, stirring occasionally to stop the rice sticking to the base. Drain through a sieve and set aside.
