Vegan "Fake Meat" Burger

Vegans and meat-lovers alike will drool over Chef Erwan Heussaff's "fake meat" burger, which uses ingredients like mushrooms, beans, and broccoli for nutrition and texture.

By Erwan Heussaff

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Lightened Up

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups portobello mushrooms, cubed (smaller pieces); gills removed
  • 15 ounces canned red beans, rinsed and divided
  • 1 cup minced broccoli, fresh only
  • 1/2 cup red onion, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of chia seeds in 6 tablespoons of water
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 4 tablespoons of avocado mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon of flaxseed meal
  • 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons panko bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon steak spice seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon tamari
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 3/4 cup grated vegan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Whole wheat burger bun
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup (real sugar)
  • Lettuce
  • Vegan cheese slice (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl add 1 cup of black beans and mash with a masher. Save remaining whole beans to add to mix later.
  2. Sauté the mushrooms in 1 teaspoon of olive oil for 5 mins until colored and water is released.
