Tiramisu

Join Guy as he explores Palermo, Sicily on the mission for the best coffee Italy has to offer.

By Guy Turland

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Sourced

Servings: 1
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup frangelico or local liquor
  • 2 cups espresso
  • 2 cups mascarpone cheese
  • 30 ladyfinger sponge biscuits
  • 7 ounces dark chocolate

Steps

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar in a bowl or electric mixer till light, fluffy and very thick.
  2. Add 1/4 cup of liquor, 1/4 portion of the espresso and mascarpone cheese and continue to whisk till mix is smooth.
