Fried Steak Sandwich

he BEST Italian Fried Steak Sandwich!

By Noah Galuten

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Don't Panic Pantry

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • Skirt steak, pounded thin and seasoned with salt
  • 4 eggs, beaten with about 3 tablespoons of water
  • 1 equal parts panko and fine breadcrumb, seasoned with a hefty pinch of garlic powder, dried parsley, and salt, to taste
  • All-purpose flour, seasoned with salt and pepper
  • Tomato sauce, warmed on the stove
  • Grated mozzarella
  • Maybe something sharp like caciocavallo or pecorino, grated
  • Hot giardianera
  • Roasted bell pepper
  • Oil for frying
  • Some chopped italian parsley
  • A nice east-coast style hoagie roll, toasted briefly before sandwiching
  • Sandwich paper

Steps

  1. Dredge the steak in flour, then egg, then breadcrumb and set it aside.
  2. Preheat the oil to 350°F, then fry the steak, one piece at a time until it is crispy and golden brown. Once fried, set it on a paper towel or wire rack to cool briefly, then dip it in tomato sauce, place it on the hoagie roll and top it with the cheese, giardianera, parsley and peppers.
