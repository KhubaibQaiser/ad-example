Crispy Pork Bo Ssam

Chef Erwan Heussaff puts his spin on a classic Korean dish, Bo Ssam. Crispy roasted pork belly wrapped in lightly pickled cabbage leaves and topped with a range of condiments makes for a fun twist on basic weeknight dinner.

By erwan heussaff

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 1 day
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Cabbage

  • 1/8 cup kosher salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/8 cup sugar
  • 1/2 head napa cabbage (tender yellow leaves)

Pork Belly

  • 1 1/2 pounds slab pork belly, skin-on
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces korean fermented soybean paste

Ssamjang:

  • 1/2 fluid ounce doenjang
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 fluid ounce gochujang
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed roasted sesame seeds
  • 1 medium garlic, minced
  • 1/56 fluid ounce toasted sesame oil

Cucumber Slaw

  • 8 sliced baby cucumbers
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 apple, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, grated
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon gochujaru
  • 1/8 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed roasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Dilute the salt and the sugar in the water.
  2. Place in your nappa cabbage and turn every 30mins for at least 2 hours.
