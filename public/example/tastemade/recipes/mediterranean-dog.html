Mediterranean Dog

This Mediterranean-inspired hot dog is full of fantastic flavor.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Average
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 tomato, chopped small
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, chopped small
  • 1 radish, sliced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, chopped small
  • 1/2 cup red pepper, chopped small
  • 1/4 cup dill pickles, chopped
  • 3/4 cup chopped parsley, divided
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup tahini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 green onions
  • 1/4 cup mint
  • 1 cup dry chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 1/3 cup chopped mint
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • 4 pita
  • 4 Hebrew National hot dogs

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix tomatoes, cucumbers, radishes, red onions, red peppers, dill pickles, 1/4 cup parsley, lemon, olive oil, salt and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Using a small food processor, blend the tahini, olive oil, green onions, 1/4 cup parsley, 1/4 cup mint, and salt and pepper. Set aside.
  3. In a food processor, blend soaked chickpeas, 1/4 cup parsley, chopped mint, cilantro, garlic, cumin and coriander. Blend until mixed well. Form into loose balls.
  4. Heat oil in a medium pot and fry the falafel balls. Remove to a plate lined with paper towels.
  5. Heat a grill pan over medium-high heat. Drizzle the pita with olive oil and grill until browned on both sides. In the same pan, grill the hot dogs.
  6. Place one hot dog in each pita, top with tomato salad, and 1-2 crumbled falafel balls. Drizzle with tahini sauce.
