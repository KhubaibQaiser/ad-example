Vegan Veggie Pizza Wrap

This zesty veggie wrap filled with vegan chickpea sausage, mushrooms and sauce is packed with pizza flavor.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Vegan Veggie Pizza Wrap

  • 2 large spinach or whole wheat tortillas
  • 1/4 cup fresh baby spinach
  • 2 crimini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced

Sauce

  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pure maple syrup

Vegan Sausage Crumbles

  • 15 1/2 ounces chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 tablespoon fennel seeds, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon almond meal

Steps

  1. To make the wraps, preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Spread approx. 2 tablespoons of the pizza sauce across the tortilla.
