Golden Milkshake

Made with ice cream, turmeric, honey, ginger, cinnamon and more, this is the ultimate milksha ... hangover cure.

By jenny barber

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 pint coconut milk ice cream
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup coconut shreds, plus more to toast and use as topping
  • 2 tablespoons ground turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon, plus more for topping
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • Fresh cracked pepper

Steps

  1. In a blender, add all ingredients and blend until smooth.
  2. Top with toasted coconut and cinnamon.
