Grilled Pork and Shrimp Shumai with Bacon-Apple Slaw

Pork and Shrimp paired with a zesty Bacon-Apple Slaw, crafting each dumpling with a savory blend of pork and shrimp, grilled to perfection, complemented by a crunchy slaw infused with the sweet-smoky essence of bacon and apple.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 4
Hard
 :

Ingredients

Grilled Pork and Shrimp Shumai

  • 1/2 pound ground pork shoulder
  • 1 pound large shrimp, ground
  • 3 cloves garlic, grated
  • 3 scallions, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 eggs white, whisked
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Thin, wonton style wrapper
  • Cooking spray

Dipping Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon toasted coffee beans
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Bacon Apple Slaw

  • 1/2 pound applewood smoked bacon
  • 2 granny smith apples, small dice
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Combine the ground pork, ground shrimp, garlic, scallions, ginger, sesame oil, soy sauce, sugar, egg whites and salt in a large bowl and mix well.
  2. Place 1 1/2 tablespoons of filling into each wrapper and fold up the sides, leaving it open on top.
