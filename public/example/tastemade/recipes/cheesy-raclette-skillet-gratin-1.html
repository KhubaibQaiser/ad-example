Cheesy Raclette Skillet Gratin

Name something cheesier than The Notebook. If you said our Cheesy Raclette Skillet Gratin you would be absolutely correct. This simple and cheesy dish is guarenteed to satisfy your cheesy cravings whilst tingling your tastebuds at the same time.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 500 grams small waxy potatoes
  • 200 grams thick smoked bacon lardons
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 6 sprigs thyme, leaves stripped
  • 200 milliliters white wine
  • 8 stems cavalo nero, stalks removed
  • 250 grams raclette cheese

Steps

  1. Halve the potatoes lengthways and boil in a pan of salted water for 10 minutes, or until just cooked through then drain.
  2. Preheat the grill to 200°C.
