Brown Crab Arroz Caldo

Brown crab, coconut, and ginger flavor this savory rice side.

By Numra Siddiqui

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

If These Meals Could Talk

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Brown Crab Arroz Caldo

  • 15 milliliters neutral oil
  • 30 milliliters coconut oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 30 grams ginger, diced
  • 200 grams jasmine rice
  • 1 liter shellfish/fish stock
  • 20 grams squid brand fish sauce
  • 80 grams picked brown crab
  • 300 grams creamed coconut (block, not liquid)

Garnishes

  • 40 grams ginger, peeled and julienned
  • 50 grams spring (green) onion, thinly sliced into rounds
  • 2 bird’s eye red chillies, thinly slice
  • 1 lemon, cut into cheeks

Steps

  1. Heat the oils in a pan over a medium heat. Sweat the garlic, onion and ginger for 4–5 minutes, or until softened.
  2. Add the rice and stir to coat. Cook out for 2 minutes.
