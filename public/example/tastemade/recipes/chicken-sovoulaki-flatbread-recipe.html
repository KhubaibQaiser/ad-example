Chicken Souvlaki Flatbread

Delicious chicken marinade

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Chicken Marinade

  • 4x Filleted chicken thighs
  • 500g Greek Yoghurt
  • Juice of One Lemon
  • 180ml Olive Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 Tbsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Paprika
  • 1 Tbsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 Tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 Tsp Black Pepper

Pickle

  • 1 whole cucumber
  • 1 Red Onion Diced
  • 80ml Red Wine Vinegar
  • 40ml Water
  • 20g White Sugar
  • Salt
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 2 garlic Cloves
  • Peppercorns

Tzatziki

  • 500g Greek yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • Juice of lemon
  • 2 Tbsp Fresh Dill Chopped
  • 2 Tbsp Fresh Parsley Chopped 1 Cucumber Grated
  • 1 Tsp Black Pepper
  • Pinch Salt

Flatbread

  • 100g self raising
  • 100g natural yogurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl, place the chicken thighs in, coating all sides and leave to marinate for at least 4 hours.
  2. When marinated, layer on a kebab stick, place on a tin foil lined oven tray with a tin foil lid covering the chicken.
