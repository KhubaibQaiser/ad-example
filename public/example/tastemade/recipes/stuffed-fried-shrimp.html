Stuffed Fried Shrimp

Shrimp wrapped in crispy, breaded fishcake is finger food for seafood lovers.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 6 shrimp, cleaned and deveined
  • 2 hanpen fishcakes
  • 1/4 onion, minced
  • Flour, for breading
  • 1 egg, whisked
  • Panko bread crumb
  • Oil for cooking
  • Shredded cabbage
  • Tomato slice
  • Lemon wedge

Steps

  1. Make small slits along the bottom side of the shrimp to prevent curling.
  2. In a bowl, combine onion and hanpen and mix until well combined.
