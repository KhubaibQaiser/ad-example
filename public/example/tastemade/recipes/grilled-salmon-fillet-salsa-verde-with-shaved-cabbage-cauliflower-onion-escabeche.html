Grilled Salmon Salsa Verde Tacos with Shaved Cabbage & Cauliflower Onion Escabeche

Charred salmon tacos with a spicy salsa verde in flour tortillas with crunchy cabbage and a tangy escabeche

Ingredients

Grilled Salmon Salsa Verde Tacos with Shaved Cabbage & Cauliflower Onion Escabeche

  • Taco
  • 2 pounds center cut salmon filet
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 cups shaved green cabbage
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro leaves
  • High quality corn tortilla

Cauliflower Onion Escabeche

  • 3 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 1/2 cup water

Salsa Verde

  • 3 tomatillos
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Tacos
  2. Place the sliced cabbage and onion in a bowl of ice cold water.
