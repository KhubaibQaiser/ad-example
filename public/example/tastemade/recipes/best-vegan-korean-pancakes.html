Vegan Korean Pancakes

These Korean pancakes make the perfect vegan dish!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :

Ingredients

Dip

  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 4 tablespoons tamari
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1 chopped green chilli
  • 1 chopped diced shallot
  • 4 tablespoons veggie stock
  • 1 tablespoon coconut vinegar
  • 1 small piece of chopped diced ginger

Vegan Korean Pancakes

  • 1 bunch spring onions
  • 1 cup plain flour
  • 1 cup veggie stock
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon pink salt
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Dip

Steps

  1. Toast the sesame seeds until golden brown and set aside. In a separate bowl add all the dip ingredients including the toasted sesame and mix well.
