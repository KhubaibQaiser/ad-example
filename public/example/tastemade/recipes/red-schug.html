Green S’chug

This slightly spicy red pepper condiment is the perfect accompaniment to shakshuka.

By einat admony

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4–5 medium fresno or medium-hot fresh red chilies, stemmed, ribs and seed left intact
  • 10 medium garlic cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon sweet paprika
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 fluid ounces vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a food processor and process until almost smooth.
  2. Store in a glass container with a tight-fitting lid in the fridge for up to two weeks.
