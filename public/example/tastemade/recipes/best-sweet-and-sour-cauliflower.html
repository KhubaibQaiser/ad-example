Sweet & Sour Cauliflower

Our best-ever sweet & sour cauliflower recipe is a vegetarian alternative to everyone's favorite, sweet & sour chicken!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Cauliflower

  • Small cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 7 ounces plain flour
  • 1 bottle soda water, chilled
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger puree
  • 1 tablespoon garlic puree
  • 1 tablespoon soya sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mirin
  • 1/2 tablespoon tomato purée
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 3 1/2 fluid ounces water
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons runny honey

Slaw

  • 1/4 chinese cabbage, finely
  • Shredded
  • 1 celery stalk, julienned
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 2 spring onions, julienned
  • 1 small red chilli, finely sliced
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind
  • 1/2 mirin

Steps

  1. Start by mixing the flour with a good pinch of salt and enough of the cold soda water to create a thick paste. Add in the cauliflower to coat. Fry the cauliflower at 355°F in frying oil for a few minutes. Blot on kitchen paper at set aside.
  2. In a hot wok, heat up the sesame oil and add to this the ginger and garlic paste. Add in the remaining sauces, the chilli flakes and the tomato purée. Once thick and well combined, add in the cauliflower quickly to coat.
