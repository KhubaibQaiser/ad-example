Honey Cajun Skewers

A touch of Louisiana flavor can be made anywhere using Hillshire Farm® Smoked Sausage and shrimp, brought together with the punch of the honey cajun mixture.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 Hillshire Farm® Smoked Sausage
  • 1/2 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 lime, juiced and zested
  • 1 tablespoons cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 cup parsley leaves
  • 3 scallions, thinly sliced
  • Lime wedges for garnish

Steps

  1. Skewer the sausage, shrimp and red onion on long metal or bamboo skewers.
  2. In a small saucepan, melt the butter and honey.
  3. Stir in the lime juice, zest, and cajun seasoning.
  4. Brush the cajun honey mixture on the skewers.
  5. Heat a grill pan over medium high heat.
  6. Place the skewers on the grill and cook for 3-4 minutes per side, brushing with cajun honey butter as they cook.
  7. Remove and serve garnished with parsley, scallions and lime wedges.
