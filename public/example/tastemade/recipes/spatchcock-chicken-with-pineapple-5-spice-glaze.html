Spatchcock Chicken with Pineapple Five-Spice Glaze

Best way to cook a whole chicken. Its juicy and sweet.

By danni rose

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Spatchcock Chicken

  • 1 whole spatchcock chicken
  • 1/4 cup onion powder
  • 1 cup minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons black pepper
  • 1/2 cup kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup paprika
  • 1/4 cup chicken bouillon powder
  • 1 cup soy sauce

Pineapple Five-Spice Glaze

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 6 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sriracha
  • 1 teaspoon five-spice blend
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder

Steps

  1. Mix the soy sauce, salt and spices and rub the chicken on all sides and let marinate overnight.
  2. In a large dutch oven, heat oil to 350°F. Making sure not to go over 350.
