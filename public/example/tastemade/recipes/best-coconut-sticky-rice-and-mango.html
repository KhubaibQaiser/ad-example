Coconut Sticky Rice & Mango

Coconut Sticky Rice with a sweet twist!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 300 grams glutinous sticky rice
  • 2 strips pandan leaves
  • 1 tin coconut milk
  • 2 ripe kesar mangoes
  • 1 liter water
  • 3 grams himalayan salt
  • 72 grams coconut sugar
  • 1 banana leaf
  • 10 grams pea flower

Steps

  1. Brew 1 liter of dehydrated Pea Flower tea and set aside to cool. Once cool wash and rinse the glutinous rice thoroughly and soak for 2 hours minimum.
  2. Drain the stained sticky rice and steam for 20 minutes.
