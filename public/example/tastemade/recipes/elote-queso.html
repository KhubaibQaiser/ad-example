Elote Queso

Amp up your standard queso with the delicious flavors of elote.

By Derrell Smith

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Mad Good Food

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Queso Dip

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 jalapeño, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 cup shredded mexican blend cheese
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups corn, roasted
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup queso fresco
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder

Soy Chorizo

  • 8 ounces soy chorizo

Steps

  1. Heat the butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the jalapeños, garlic and sauté for 5–10 minutes, until very soft and fragrant
