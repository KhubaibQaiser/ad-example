Noodle Salad with Crispy Garlic Chili Dressing

A refreshing Asian noodle salad.

By danni rose

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Noodle Salad

  • 4 ounces glass noodles
  • 1 medium cucumber, thin rounds
  • 1 medium carrot, peeled
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 fennel bulb, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup red cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 2 sprigs scallions, chopped
  • 1/3 cup peanuts, chopped
  • 1/3 cup cilantro or parsley, chopped
  • Sesame seed, to garnish

Dressing

  • 2 1/2 tablespoons seasoned rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger grated
  • 1 tablespoon szechuan peppercorn, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • Neutral oil
  • 5 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • Fennel frond sprinkled on top for garnish

Steps

  1. Add the glass noodles to a bowl and pour hot 2 tablespoons over them.
  2. Cover and set them aside to soak for several minutes (about 4–5) to soften and turn from white to clear noodles.
