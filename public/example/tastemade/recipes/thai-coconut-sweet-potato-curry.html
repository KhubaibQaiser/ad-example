Thai Coconut-Sweet Potato Curry

This easy Thai-inspired curry, packed with sweet potatoes and warming spices, is perfect for weeknights.

By dini klein

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons powdered ginger
  • 1 tablespoon curry
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons coriander
  • 28 ounces diced tomatoes
  • 28 ounces tomato sauce
  • 3 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and small dice
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • Pepper
  • Cooked rice
  • Cilantro
  • Mint
  • Fresh squeezed lime
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil on medium-high heat. Saute the onion until translucent, 5 to 6 minutes. Season with salt and add the garlic and let cook an additional 2 minutes.
  2. Add all the spices, salt and pepper and mix for another minute to warm through. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce and bring to a boil. Add the sweet potatoes, cover and let simmer 20 to 30 minutes until cooked through and flavors have blended.
