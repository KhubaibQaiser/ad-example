Frankie's Focaccia Pizza

A salty, fluffy focaccia pizza topped with bright olives and sun-dried tomatoes.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 2 hr
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 2 cups lukewarm water
  • 1/2 cup olive oil, divided
  • 2 shallots
  • 2/3 cup sun-dried tomatoes, drained
  • 1 cup pitted green olives (castelveltrano)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes, optional

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and instant yeast. Add the lukewarm water and 1 tablespoon of olive oil and stir with a wooden spoon. Mix with your hands until a sticky dough ball forms.
  2. Rub the surface of the dough lightly with 1 tablespoon olive oil. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and place in the refrigerator for at least 12 hours. (Dough can be refrigerated for up to three days).
