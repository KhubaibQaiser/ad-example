Cornbread Waffle with Chili

Cornbread waffles with a sweet potato chili are the right meal for any time of day.

By zoe kelly

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Cornbread Waffle with Chili

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cups yellow cornmeal
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup scallions

Chili

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup white onions, diced
  • 1/4 cup celery
  • 1/4 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 1/2 cups sweet potato, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, diced
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 4 teaspoons cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon espresso powder
  • 14 ounces diced tomatoes
  • 15 ounces kidney beans
  • 15 ounces white beans
  • 2 1/2–3 cups water

Garnish

  • Sour cream
  • Shredded mexican cheese
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle maker to medium heat.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the dry ingredients. Add the milk, vegetable oil, eggs and scallions; mix until smooth.
