Rainbow Pizza

This colorful pizza is the cheesy, delicious way to eat your daily vegetables.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 18 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet ready-made pizza crust
  • 3 tablespoons pizza sauce
  • 3 1/2 ounces shredded cheese
  • 7 cherry tomatoes
  • 1/3 carrot, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons corn
  • 1/2 bell pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/3 leaf red cabbage, cut into bite-sized pieces

Steps

  1. Spread pizza sauce onto the pizza crust.
  2. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
