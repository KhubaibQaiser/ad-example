Nanaimo Bars

Nanaimo Bars are British Columbia's best known confection and they are wonderfully rich.

By julie nolke

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 10
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

Base Layer

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup sweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten

Filling

  • 1/4 cup softened butter
  • 2 tablespoons custard powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups icing sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk, plus more as needed

Topping

  • 4 ounces semisweet chocolate
  • 1/2 ounce butter

Steps

  1. In bowl, stir together graham crumbs, coconut, walnuts, cocoa powder and sugar.
  2. Drizzle with butter and egg, stirring until combined. Press into a parchment-lined 9-inches square cake pan.
