Arroz con Pegao (Rice with Crispy Bits)

Buttery rice gets cooked in a Dutch oven until it crisps for the perfect fluffy and crunchy side.

By Monti Carlo

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Arroz con Pegao (Rice with Crispy Bits)

  • 3 cups boiling water
  • 3 tablespoons salted butter
  • 2 cups medium-grain white rice, washed until water runs clear
  • Salt, to taste

Escabeche

  • 3 small yellow onions, cut into rings
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 bay leaf

Steps

  1. In a dutch oven, melt the butter over medium heat. When it froths, add the rice, mix it into the butter and let it toast for a minute. Add the 1 teaspoon boiling water into the rice. Add salt to taste. Stir.
  2. Cook uncovered for 5 to 10 minutes until most of the water has evaporated but still bubbles out of the steam holes over the surface of the rice.
