Italian Sausage Pigs in a Blanket Skillet

When pigs in a blanket relax they take a dip in a hot tub of mozzarella cheese, pizza sauce, peppers and onions.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 7 fully cooked sweet italian sausages, halved
  • All-purpose flour, for dusting
  • 13 ounces store-bought pizza dough
  • 4 slices provolone cheese, cut into 1-inch strips
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 8 ounces pizza sauce
  • 4 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Coat a 10 1/2-inches cast-iron skillet with oil.
