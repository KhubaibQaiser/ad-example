Mixed Green Side Salad with Avocado and Homemade Balsamic

Bright and crunchy green salad to pair with any Hispanic meal

By Alex Hill

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Spice Spice Baby

Prep Time: 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 english cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • Good extra virgin olive oil, to drizzle
  • Balsamic vinegar, to drizzle
  • Flakey finishing sea salt & fresh cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. In a salad bowl add mixed greens to the bowl.
  2. Top with salad toppings drizzle with EVOO & balsamic vinegar.
