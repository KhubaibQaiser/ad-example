Moussaka

Moussaka is a delicious Greek dip perfect with pita and tzatziki.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 2
Average

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese, divided
  • 1 eggplant, peeled and sliced into rounds
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add the olive oil.
