Prosciutto Wrapped Grilled Trout

If you’re intimidated by grilling whole fish, don’t be. Binding the trout in layers of prosciutto will help to hold the fish together, while giving it an irresistible charred ham flavor. It’s a simple, satisfying meal for your cabin, campsite or your own backyard.

By Andrew Zimmern

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Andrew Zimmern's Wild Game Kitchen

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Prosciutto-Wrapped Grilled Trout

  • 4 small whole trouts, about 14 ounces, cleaned
  • 12 slices prosciutto
  • Fresh sage sprig
  • Fresh thyme sprig
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper

Herb Sauce

  • 1 bunch parsley
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill
  • 1/4 cup fresh tarragon leaves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 scallions, chopped
  • 4 anchovies
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice, or more to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup olive oil

Lemon Rice Pilaf

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/3 small cup of angel hair pasta, broken into 1-inch lengths
  • 2 whole garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup long grain white rice
  • 2 1/4 cups chicken broth

Steps

  1. Wash and dry each fish. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Stuff each cavity with sage, thyme and lemon slices.
