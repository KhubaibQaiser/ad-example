Summer Vegetable One Pot Pasta

Sometimes throwing everything in a pot and seeing what happens is a valid culinary idea.

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cups water
  • 12 ounces rotini
  • 1 yellow squash, halved and sliced
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cup green beans
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • Fresh grated parmesan, to garnish
  • Fresh basil, to garnish

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet and add onion and garlic. Cook until softened.
  2. Add all ingredients up to marinara sauce; season with salt and pepper.
