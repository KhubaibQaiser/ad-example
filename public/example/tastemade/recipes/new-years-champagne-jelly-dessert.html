New Year’s Champagne Jelly Dessert

Don't just drink champagne — put it in a dessert! This sparkling wine jelly recipe is perfect for celebrations and parties, like New Year's Eve.

By julie nolke

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle sparkling wine
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1 packet gelatin
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup white chocolate, chopped in pieces
  • White sanding sugar
  • Edible sugar pearl

Steps

  1. Measure out 1/2 cup of boiling water in a small heat proof bowl then add 1 packet gelatine to the water. Stir gently and set aside. In a medium sized bowl, pour in 1 1/2 cups of sparkling wine. Add in the dissolved gelatine mixture and stir. Ladle into champagne glasses. Let set in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
  2. In a pan over medium heat, add sweetened condensed milk and butter. Stir together.
