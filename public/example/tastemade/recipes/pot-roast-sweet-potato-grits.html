Pot Roast and Sweet Potato Grits

Introducing the ultimate comfort food: pot roast and sweet potato grits with Deliciously Entertaining host, Danni Rose.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 4 hr 30 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Pot Roast

  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons light brown sugar
  • 3 pounds chuck roast, cut into 2-inches cubes
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, plus more as needed
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 1 1/2 cups cognac, divided
  • 4 cups beef stock
  • 2 tablespoons worcestershire
  • 1 herb bouquet thyme, rosemary and bay leaf
  • 2 large carrots, halved and cut on the bias into 2-inches pieces
  • 3 medium celery stalks, cut on the bias into 2-inches pieces
  • 1/2 pound frozen pearl onions
  • 12 ounces baby gold potatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch

Sweet Potato Grits

  • 2 cups milk, plus more as needed
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 cup stone ground white grits
  • 8 ounces sweet potato, cooked, peeled and pureed
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, at room temperature

Steps

  1. Heat a Dutch oven pot over medium-high flame.
  2. While the pot heats, mix the onion powder, garlic powder, 1 tablespoon salt, pepper and brown sugar together in a small bowl. Season the meat on all sides with the mixture.
