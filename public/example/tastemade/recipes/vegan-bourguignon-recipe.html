Vegan Bourguignon

Here's a vegan hack you weren't expecting, Bourguignon for the win!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pack linda mccartney sausages or fry's family
  • Brand
  • 1 kilogram potatoes
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 celeries, sliced
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 white onion, diced
  • 1 leek with greens attached, chopped
  • 1 bunch thyme
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 bunch parsley
  • 2 sprigs of rosemary
  • 1 small bunch basil
  • 200 grams brown button mushrooms
  • 1 bulb garlic, minced
  • 10 black peppercorns
  • 350 milliliters of red vegan wine (pinot)
  • 500 milliliters veggie stock
  • 100 milliliters olive oil
  • 27 grams vegan butter
  • 1 aubergine
  • 1 courgette
  • 1–2 mixed bell peppers
  • 30 milliliters tomato purée
  • 1/2 tin plum tomatoes
  • 86 grams nutritional yeast
  • Salt/ pepper, to taste
  • Plain flour, for dusting

Steps

  1. Peel and steam the potatoes until done (15–20 minutes. Cook the vegan sausages in a pan with olive oil, once done place on some paper towel and set aside.
  2. In a big casserole pot heat olive oil and add in finely diced white onion, sliced celery, sliced carrots, minced garlic 1 piece potato diced, a few sprigs of thyme, tomato purée, black peppercorns and chopped white part of the leek keeping the greens for later. Season as you go with salt and pepper!
