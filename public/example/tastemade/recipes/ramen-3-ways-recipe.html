Ramen 3 Ways

3 awesome way to pimp your ramen!

(1)Recipe Notes (1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Gourmet Ramen

  • 1 packet your favourite ramen flavour
  • 1 tea stained egg
  • 100 grams smoked bacon lardons
  • 2–4 slices bamboo shoot/menma
  • 2 spring onion greens, shredded
  • Nori sheet
  • Sesame seed and nori sprinkle

Breakfast Ramen

  • 1 packet spicy chicken ramen
  • 15 milliliters tomato ketchup
  • 2–4 slices streaky bacon
  • 3 chipolata sausages
  • 1 handful of assorted mushrooms
  • Shittake/chestnut
  • 10–12 tomatoes, on the vine
  • 1 egg

Ramen Burrito

  • 1 packet spicy chicken ramen
  • 1 flour tortilla
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 1 spring onion, sliced
  • 15 milliliters tabasco
  • 1 chicken breast, poached and shredded
  • Guacamole
  • 25 grams cheddar
  • 25 grams mozzarella
  • Sesame/nori, to sprinkle
  • 1 handful coriander

Steps

  1. For the Bacon lardons, fry the Bacon lardons in a little oil in a pan until golden and crispy. Remove from the pan with a slotted spoon and allow to cool for 10 minutes before placing in a blender. Blitz until the lardons are finely ground and have a floss like texture.
  2. Meanwhile, add the ramen noodles to the pan with the bacon fat and add water according to the packet instructions - you want this dish to be brothy so you may need to add a little more water than recommended.
