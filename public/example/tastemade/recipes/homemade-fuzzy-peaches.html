Homemade Fuzzy Peaches

Make your own homemade peach candy with this easy candy recipe, which recreates a classic childhood favorite: Fuzzy Peaches!

By julie nolke

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces jell-o powder
  • 1/4 ounce unflavoured gelatine
  • 1/2 teaspoon citric acid
  • 1/2 cup corn syrup
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 5 drops yellow food coloring
  • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon citric acid

Steps

  1. Combine Jell-O, unflavoured gelatine and citric acid in a medium microwave-safe bowl and stir to combine.
  2. Add the corn syrup, food coloring and water, and stir gently with a soup spoon until all of the ingredients are blended together. Stirring too fast may add air bubbles to the mixture, so keep it slow and sweeping. Let the mixture sit for 5 minutes so that the gelatin can bloom.
