Prawn & Dill Linguini with Feta

This simple weeknight pasta dish gets a jolt of Mediterranean flavor from fresh dill, creamy feta cheese, and perfectly cooked prawns.

By Conor Maynard

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Recipe for Love

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 200 grams linguini
  • 45–60 milliliters olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 red chili
  • 150 grams vine cherry tomatoes
  • 150 grams cooked and peeled prawns
  • 1 lemon
  • 15 grams dill
  • 2 grams sugar
  • 100 grams feta
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large saucepan of salted water to the boil.
  2. Finely slice the garlic cloves. Finely slice the red chili. Halve the cherry tomatoes. Zest the lemon. Finely chop 1/2 chopped dill.
