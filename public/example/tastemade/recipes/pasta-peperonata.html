Pasta Peperonata

This spicy, vinegary pasta is the perfect side for your protein of choice.

By Numra Siddiqui

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

If These Meals Could Talk

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 red peppers, halved and deseeded
  • 250 grams fusilli pasta
  • 1 red onion, peeled and diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, bashed and peeled
  • 100 milliliters white wine
  • 1 splash red wine vinegar
  • 70 grams parmesan cheese, grated (plus extra for serving)
  • 1 handful fresh parsley (plus extra for serving)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/Gas mark 7.
  2. Roast the peppers, cut-side down, on a parchment lined baking tray for 10 minutes, until the skin is shrivelled and lightly blistered. Place in a bowl and cover for 10 minutes, then peel the skins off.
