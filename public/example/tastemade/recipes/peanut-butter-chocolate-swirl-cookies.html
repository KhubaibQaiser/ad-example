Mini Peanut Butter and Chocolate Swirl Cookies

Bite into the best of both dessert worlds with a batch of peanut butter and chocolate swirl cookies made even more delicious with M&M’S® Peanut Butter Minis.

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 85
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Peanut Butter Cookie Dough

  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ½ cup light brown sugar
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg, room temperature
  • ¼ cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt

Chocolate Cookie Dough

  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ½ cup light brown sugar
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg, room temperature
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder, sifted
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 bag M&M’S® Peanut Butter Minis

Steps

Peanut Butter Cookie Dough

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and line a sheet tray with parchment paper.
  2. For the peanut butter dough: In a large bowl add butter and both sugars and whisk. It will be a bit crumbly, but make sure all butter and sugar is fully combined. Add egg and peanut butter and whisk to combine. Switch from a whisk to a rubber spatula and add flour, baking soda, and salt. Gently stir until flour is fully mixed into dough.

Chocolate Cookie Dough

  1. Repeat the same process as for the peanut butter cookie dough, but with the ingredients for the chocolate cookie dough.
  2. To make a swirl, use ½ teaspoon of both doughs. Add both doughs to each other and gently roll into a ball. Place on a sheet tray and slightly press down.
  3. Top with 6 M&M’S® Peanut Butter Minis and repeat the process with the rest of the cookies.
  4. Bake for 7-8 minutes, rotating pans halfway through baking.
  5. Let the cookies completely cool, then enjoy!
