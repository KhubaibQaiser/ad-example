Coconut Cauliflower Rice & Lentils

Chef Nyesha Arrington's coconut cauliflower rice and lentils is a flavorful, vegetable-forward spin on rice and peas.

By Nyesha Arrington

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons grapeseed oil
  • 1 celery stalk, small diced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, diced small
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh turmeric, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 3 cups cauliflower, riced
  • 1 teaspoon + 1 teaspoon himalyan salt, divided
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk, organic
  • 1 cup french lentils, cooked
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice, and zest
  • 1 cup fresh herbs (mint, parsley, cilantro, basil), chopped

Steps

  1. In a nonstick pan, heat grapeseed oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Place the ginger, garlic, celery, bell pepper in saute pan and season with himalyan salt.
