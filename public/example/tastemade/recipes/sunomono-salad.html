Sunomono Salad

This simple side salad is a fresh, crunchy accompaniment to Korean short ribs.

By Logan Sandoval

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 2 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 6 persian cucumbers
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt, divided
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 1/2 teaspoon shoyu
  • Black sesame seed, for garnish

Steps

  1. Slice cucumbers very thin on a mandolin and transfer to a medium-size bowl.
  2. Sprinkle with 1 tablespoon of salt and toss with your hands or rubber spatula to ensure even salt coverage and draw out moisture.
