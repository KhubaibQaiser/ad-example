Slow Cooker Sweet Potato Turkey Chili

Thanksgiving vibes are strong with this one. Enjoy this slow cooker recipe to warm up to Turkey Day!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 3 hr
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 1/2 pounds lean ground turkey
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 15 ounces black beans, drained
  • 2 1/2 cups low sodium chicken stock, plus more as needed
  • Avocado, for garnish
  • Parsley, for garnish
  • Plain greek yogurt, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the turkey, onion, salt, garlic, black pepper, chili powder, oregano, and cumin. Cook and stir, breaking the turkey into crumbles, and cook until the turkey is well-browned.
