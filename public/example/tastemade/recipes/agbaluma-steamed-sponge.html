Agbaluma Steamed Sponge

Nigerian chef Minjiba Cookey fuses jasmine tea into this delicate, minimal dessert.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • Agbalumo (african star apple) puree
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup jasmine infused milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 4 teaspoons of agbalumo puree
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla

Steps

  1. Put ingredients in a bowl, mix together (dough should be a little runny)
  2. Pour dough into ramekins
