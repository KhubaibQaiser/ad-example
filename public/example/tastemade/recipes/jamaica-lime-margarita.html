Jamaica Lime Margarita

Jamaica Lime Margarita is a refreshing and tangy cocktail made with tequila, fresh lime juice, and hibiscus tea syrup, and served with a salt rimmed glass.

By derrell smith

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Sugar Rim

  • 1 lime cut in wedges or tamarind sauce
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon cane sugar mixed

Jamaica Lime Margarita

  • Ice
  • 1/3 cup tequila blanco
  • 4 1/2 tablespoons lime juice or triple sec
  • 2 tablespoons agave syrup
  • 1 cup jamaica tea chilled

Steps

  1. Use the wedge of 1 lime to moisten the rim of your cocktail glass. Dip the rim of the glass into either the sugar or the salt (or both). Repeat with the second glass.
  2. Set the glasses aside to dry. Use the remaining wedges as garnish.
