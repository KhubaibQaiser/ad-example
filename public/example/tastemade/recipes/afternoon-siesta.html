Afternoon Siesta

Looking for tequila recipes that just hit different? Grab a bottle of smooth, rich Tequila Mi CAMPO and mix things up with your new signature cocktails like this refreshing Afternoon Siesta.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Servings: 1
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • ½ oz lime juice
  • ½ oz orange juice
  • ¾ oz Campari
  • ¾ oz Blanco Tequila Mi CAMPO
  • ½ oz agave
  • Beer to top up
  • Lime
  • Orange

Steps

  1. Add the lime and orange juices, Campari, tequila and agave to a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake.
  2. Strain the shaken mix in a glass over ice, and top with Mexican lager. Add a lime and orange slice.
