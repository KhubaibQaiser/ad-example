Cheesy Grilled Peach Turkey Burger

Let's Make This Tickler Extra Mature Cheddar Cheese Stuffed Turkey Burger with Grilled Peaches and Coriander Mayonnaise

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 500g turkey mince
  • 30g breadcrumbs
  • 1 red chilli
  • 3 tbsp coriander
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper
  • 150g Castello Tickler Extra Mature Cheddar, crumbled
  • 2 ripe peaches, stoned and cut into wedges
  • 4 brioche buns
  • 100g mayonnaise
  • 1 lime, zest and juice
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped coriander

Steps

  1. Make the burgers: Place the mince, breadcrumbs, red chilli, 50g crumbled Tickler Extra Mature, garlic and coriander into a large bowl, season and mix well until combined. Next, roll into 4 equal-sized patties, approximately 150g each.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200°C. Over a high heat, on a griddle pan, fry the burgers for 2 minutes on each side, then place onto a baking sheet. Cook the burger in the oven for about 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and add the remaining crumbled Tickler Extra Mature. Bake for 2 more minutes until the cheese is melted and the burger is fully cooked.
  3. Make the mayonnaise: Mix all the mayonnaise ingredients in a bowl, season and set aside.
  4. To serve: Cut the bun in half and toast on a griddle pan, set aside. Next, place the peaches in a griddle pan over a high heat and cook for 2 minutes on each side until caramelised. Set aside. Spread the mayonnaise on the top and bottom of the toasted bun. Top the bottom bun with the patty, peaches, and coriander, before finishing with the top bun.
