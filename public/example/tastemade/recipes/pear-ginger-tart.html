Pear Ginger Tart

This beautiful tart will take the pressure off your oven during

By Mia Starr

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 1 hr 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 8
Hard
 :

Ingredients

Pie Dough

  • 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 8 ounces unsalted butter, cut into 1/4 inch pieces and placed into the freezer
  • 7–8 tablespoons ice cold water

Pie & Ginger Filling

  • 2 anjou pears
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Salt 1 pinch

Assemble

  • 2 remove the sides so that you leave the core intacts
  • 2 slice each pear side thinly but do not separates
  • 2 lightly drizzle with cut lemon to keep from turnings
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. In a food processor, pulse together the flour, sugar and salt.
  2. Add the ice cold butter and pulse until you get pea sized pieces in the food processor.
