Homemade European Fanta

Not all Fanta is created equal, and this homemade version uses fresh orange juice and zest.

By vijaya selvaraju

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • Zest and juice of 2 oranges
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon citric acid
  • Club soda

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, add zest and juice of oranges, water, sugar and citric acid and bring to a simmer.
  2. Cook for 15 minutes or until the mixture has reduced by 1/3 volume of its volume.
