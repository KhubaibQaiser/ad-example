Deviled Eggs Three Ways

One base makes 3 unique toppings.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 24
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Classic

  • 12 large eggs, hard boiled, peeled and halved
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons yellow mustard
  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/3 cup sweet salad cubes
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika, plus more for topping

Spicy

  • Classic base
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped hot & sweet pickled jalapenos, plus more for topping

Sour Cream and Cheddar

  • 2 tablespoons sour cream
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons finely grated white cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped chives, plus more for topping
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese for cheddar crisp garnish, optional
  • Cheddar crisp, for topping, optional

Steps

  1. Remove and place all of the cooked egg yolks into a medium bowl, and mash with a fork, or use an electric mixer to blend the yolks. Set hollowed egg white halves aside.
  2. Add all of the ingredients, and stir until well combined.
