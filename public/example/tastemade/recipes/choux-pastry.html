Choux Pastry

If you've ever wanted to learn how to make choux pastry from scratch, this easy tutorial will have you feeling like a French pastry chef in no time! Choux pastry is the start to many classic desserts, from profiteroles to éclairs.

By julie nolke

Updated: 10/16/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 6 grams of sugar
  • 125 grams flour
  • 150 milliliters of water
  • 60 grams salted butter in pieces
  • 4–5 large eggs

Steps

  1. Combine the sugar and flour in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place the water and the butter in a saucepan and bring slowly to boil. The butter should be melted.
