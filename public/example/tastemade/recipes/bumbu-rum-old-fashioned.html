Bumbu Rum Old Fashioned

Bumbu Rum Old Fashioned is a delicious and tropical twist on the classic cocktail, made with Bumbu rum, muddled orange and cherry, and a dash of bitters, served over ice and garnished with a slice of fresh orange.

By derrell smith

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 1 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 2 parts boukman botanical rhum
  • 2 dashes bitters
  • 1 splash water
  • 1–2 teaspoons sugar
  • Ice, cubed

Steps

  1. Add the sugar cube/granulated sugar and Angostura Bitters to a rocks glass with a single ice cube
  2. Stir the mix until the ice cube has melted enough to dissolve the sugar it if you're using a sugar cube you'll need to crush it
