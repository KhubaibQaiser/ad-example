Triple Chocolate Mug Cake

Made with raw cacao, coconut oil and more, this single-serving triple chocolate mug cake is both guilt-free and decadent!

By michel janse

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 3 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Mug Cake

  • 4 tablespoons perfect blend gluten-free flour
  • 3 tablespoons coconut sugar
  • 1 tablespoon raw cacao
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 3 tablespoons almond milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Salt
  • 2 tablespoons dairy-free dark chocolate chips

Sauce and Topping

  • 1 tablespoon melted coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons raw cacao
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Soy whipped cream
  • Dairy-free chocolate chip

Steps

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix together cake ingredients.
  2. Microwave for 1 minute and 20 seconds.
