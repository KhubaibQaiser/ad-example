Katie Q's Easter "Egg" Cake Pops

Pretty cake pops are easier to make than you might think.

By katie quinn

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 20
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 box cake mix, prepared
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 2 cups confectioner's sugar
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 16 ounces white chocolate chips
  • Sprinkle
  • Popsicle stick
  • 1 piece loaf of bread, to stick the popsicle sticks in to hold them upright as they dry

Steps

  1. Bake a cake and allow it to cool completely.
  2. Using your hands, break down the cake in a bake, making it into fine crumbs.
