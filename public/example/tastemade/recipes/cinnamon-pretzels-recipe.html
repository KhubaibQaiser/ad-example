Cinnamon Pretzels

Don't get yourself in a knot – give these cinnamon treats a go!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 350 milliliters warm water
  • 3 1/2 grams instant yeast
  • 125 grams bread flour
  • 375 grams plain flour
  • 6 grams salt
  • 26 grams light brown sugar
  • 470 milliliters water
  • 30 grams baking soda
  • 55 grams melted butter
  • 150 grams sugar
  • 20 grams cinnamon

Steps

  1. Mix the yeast with the warm water, and leave to sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the two flours, brown sugar and salt to a bowl, and slowly pour in the yeast mixture and knead for 5 minutes until you have a soft dough.
