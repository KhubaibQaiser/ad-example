Cheesy Korean Fried Chicken

We found the only way to improve Korean Fried Chicken... cover it in melted cheese!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 5
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Gochujang Sauce

  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp finely grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice wine
  • 2 tbsp hot sauce
  • 4 tbsp ketchup
  • 2 tbsp Gochujang
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Cheesy Korean Fried Chicken

  • 1.5 kg chicken wings - wings and tip separated
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1kg cooking mozzarella

Steps

  1. Make the Gochujang sauce by whisking all of the ingredients together. Put to one side until needed.
