Monkfish Piccatta

Monkfish gets bathed in a lemony, buttery, caper-studded sauce in this decadent main dish.

By Tiffani Faison

Updated: 10/2/2024

Ingredients

Monkfish Piccatta

  • 7 ounces portions of monkfish, bloodline removed and pounded flat
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 whole garlic clove, smashed
  • 1 sprig thyme
  • 1/2 lemon, thinly sliced and grilled
  • 1/2 tablespoon capers
  • 7 caperberries
  • 6 eaches parsley sprigs
  • 2 cups wondra flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated garlic

Piccatta Sauce

  • 2 cups brown butter
  • 1 cup garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup white miso
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 cups vegetable stock
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
