Corn and Quinoa Zesty Salad

Fresh corn and perfectly cooked quinoa make this simple side salad a textural delight.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Easy
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Corn and Quinoa Zesty Salad

  • 1/2 cup dry quinoa
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 - 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 ripe avocado, cubed
  • 1 can (14 ounces) sweet corn, drained
  • 2 cups arugula
  • 1/2 cup coriander, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mint, chopped
  • 1 ear of corn, grilled (optional)

Dressing

  • 1 lime, juiced (approx. 3 tablespoons)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Pinch salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed and chopped

Steps

Corn and Quinoa Zesty Salad

  1. To cook your quinoa, simply add the quinoa and water to a small pan with a lid, bring to a boil, then reduce to a gentle simmer with the lid on for 10 minutes, until all the water has been absorbed and the quinoa is fluffy.
  2. Pop quinoa into a large bowl, add the rest of the salad ingredients and herbs, and mix together.
  3. For some added flavor, char an ear of corn on the grill, then cut and add to the salad. Yum!

Dressing

  1. In a small bowl, combine the dressing ingredients. Pour over the salad and mix.
