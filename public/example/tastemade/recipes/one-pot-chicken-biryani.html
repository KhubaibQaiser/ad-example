One Pot Chicken Biryani

This might be the most effortlessly scrumptious chicken biryani ever.

(2)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 2 hr 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Chicken marinade

  • 2 pounds bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 cup coconut yogurt
  • 4 garlic cloves, mashed to a paste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

One Pot Chicken Biryani

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup yellow onion, diced small
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1/2 inch piece of ginger, grated fine on a microplane
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup canned, diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup golden golden raisins
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves, picked
  • 1/4 cup almonds, toasted and chopped
  • Lime yogurt (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large Ziploc bag, combine everything for the marinade and let marinate for an hour, preferably overnight.
