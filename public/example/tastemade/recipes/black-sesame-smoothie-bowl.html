Black Sesame Smoothie Bowl

This healthy morning smoothie bowl gets its dark hue from black sesame paste.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon black sesame seeds, plus more as needed
  • 2 chopped bananas, frozen
  • 1 tablespoon black sesame paste
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Blackberry
  • Dried coconut chip
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. With a mortar and pestle, grind the black sesame seeds, then transfer to a mixer. Add bananas, black sesame paste, honey and milk. Mix until blended.
  2. Transfer to a bowl and top with blackberries, coconut chips, sesame seeds and thyme.
