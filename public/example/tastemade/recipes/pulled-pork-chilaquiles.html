PULLED PORK CHILAQUILES

When nachos aren't enough, kick things up with flavorful pulled pork, queso, fried eggs and more layered over lightly fried tortillas.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

Pulled Pork

  • 3 1/2 pounds boneless pork butt, cut into large chunks
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons anchos chile pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 red onion, chunked
  • 5 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
  • 15 ounces tomato purée
  • 3/4 cup beer (such as dos equis)
  • Juice of 1 orange
  • Juice of 1 lime

Chilaquiles

  • 4–5 cups tortilla chips
  • 4 ounces shredded monterey jack
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 ounces crumbled queso fresco
  • 1/2 cup mexican crema
  • Finely chopped white onion
  • Thinly sliced red radish
  • Chopped fresh cilantro
  • Sliced avocado
  • Lime wedge
  • Jalapeño slice

Steps

  1. Place pork in a heavy Dutch oven.
  2. Whisk together the cumin, salt, oregano, chile pepper, and cayenne.
