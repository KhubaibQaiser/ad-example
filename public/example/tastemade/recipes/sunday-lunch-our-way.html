Sunday Lunch Our Way

Chef Nadiya Hussain puts her own spin on a classic British Sunday lunch. Ginger-spiced chicken, a tamarind-infused tomato gravy, and aromatic sides come together for a flavorful spread that'll end your weekend on a high note.

By Nadiya Hussein

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Ghee Roasties

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks (about 800g/1lb 12oz)
  • 50g/1¾ oz clarified butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder

Chicken

  • 1.5 kg/3 lb 5 oz whole chicken
  • 25 ml/¾fl oz olive oil
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 tsp garlic granules
  • 1 tsp salt

Coriander Stuffing Balls

  • 30 g/1 oz fresh coriander
  • 20 g/¾ oz pine nuts
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 40 g/1½ oz fresh breadcrumbs
  • 250 g/9 oz chicken pieces
  • 1 free-range egg
  • 3 tbsp oil, for frying

Turmeric Greens

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 450 g/lb green beans

Tomato Gravy

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée
  • 3 tomatoes, roughly chopped (about 450g/1lb)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 300 ml/10fl oz boiling water
  • 1 tbsp cornflour, mixed with 1 tbsp cold water

Steps

  1. Start by putting the potatoes into a large saucepan of cold water.
  2. Turn the heat up to high and let the potatoes cook for 6 minutes.
