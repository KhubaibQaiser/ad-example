Dairy Free Passion Fruit Bars

Transport yourself to a tropical island with these super simple coconut passion fruit bars. Using whole ingredients, this sweet treat is indulgent while being health-conscious.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 5 hr 30 min
Servings: 20
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Coconut Base

  • 1 cup graham crackers
  • ½ cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla paste
  • ½ cup desiccated coconut

Cashew Cream Filling

  • 1 cup cashew nuts, soaked overnight
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • ½ cup coconut oil, melted
  • ½ tbsp vanilla paste

Passionfruit Topping

  • ½ cup passion fruit pulp
  • 2 tbsp agave syrup (or any syrup of your choice)
Get Ingredients

Steps

Coconut Base

  1. Place all the dry ingredients for the base into a food processor and blitz until smooth.
  2. Next, add in the wet ingredients and pulse until combined.
  3. Line a square baking tray with baking parchment. Using a spatula, evenly spread the biscuit base, pushing down to make sure it’s as solid as possible.
  4. Place in the base in the fridge while you make the filling.

Cashew Cream Filling

  1. Add all the filling ingredients into the food processor and blitz on high until smooth.
  2. Once smooth, pour on top of the base. Allow to sit for 5 minutes, then gently tap the baking tray against the surface, to release any air bubbles that may have formed.
  3. Return to the fridge until set, ideally 3 hours.

Passionfruit Topping

  1. In a small saucepan, add the syrup and passion fruit pulp.
  2. Heat on low for roughly 8 minutes until the mixture has thickened and started to bubble.
  3. Remove from the heat and allow to cool before pouring over the filling in an even layer.
  4. Place the tray back into the fridge and allow to firm up for another 2 hours.
  5. Remove and cut into 20 even squares.
