Thai BBQ Hot Pot

If Korean BBQ and Japanese Shabu Shabu had a baby, you'd get this glorious Thai communal meal. It's never been easier to make hot pot at home thanks to this easy-to-follow Thai hot pot recipe.

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Nam Jim Jaew Sauce

  • 1/3 cup fresh lime juice
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup shallots, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon dried ground thai chili
  • 1 tablespoon toasted ground rice powder

Nam Jim Seafood Sauce

  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup cilantro root, stem, and leaves, roughly chopped
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 6 birds eye chilies, roughly chopped

Thai BBQ Hot Pot

  • 1/2 pound pork belly, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 pound ribeye, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 pound pork jowl, sliced
  • 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled with head on
  • 1/2 pound squid, cleaned and sliced
  • Assorted fish ball
  • 5 ounces enoki mushrooms, trimmed
  • 4 ounces shitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups shanghai cabbage, sliced
  • 2 cups chinese broccoli, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup chinese chives, chopped
  • 1 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1–2 packages glass noodles
  • Water for soup base

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and divide into individual bowls for serving.
