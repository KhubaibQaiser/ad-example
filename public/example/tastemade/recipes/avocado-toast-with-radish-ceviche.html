Avocado Toast with Radish Ceviche

Nadiya Hussain shows us how to create the perfect dishes to complement any kind of day; like her lunchtime avocado toast topped with a delicate pickled radish ceviche.

By nadiya hussein

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 7 ounces radishes, sliced very thinly
  • 4 limes, juice only
  • 2 teaspoons caster sugar
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large handful fresh dill, finely chopped
  • 4 slices sourdough or wholemeal bread
  • 2 avocados, peeled and chopped
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Put the radishes in a bowl.
  2. Put the lime juice (reserving 2 tablespoons), sugar, red onion, oil and dill in a smaller bowl. Stir, then pour onto the radishes and mix gently. This only needs 10 minutes to infuse.
