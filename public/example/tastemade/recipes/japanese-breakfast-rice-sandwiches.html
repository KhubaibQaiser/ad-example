Japanese Breakfast Rice Sandwiches

Your morning needs this protein boost so you can make it until lunchtime and have another one.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 fresh eggs
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 2 sheets nori
  • 2 packs natto
  • 2 stalks green onion, chopped
  • 2 pieces grilled salmon
  • 2 pirella leaves
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Combine chopped green onion with natto and mix well. Set aside.
  2. Combine the raw eggs, cooked rice and soy sauce and mix well.
