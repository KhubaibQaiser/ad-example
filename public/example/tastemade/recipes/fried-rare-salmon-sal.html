Fried Rare Salmon

Here's a restaurant-quality salmon recipe that's surprisingly easy to make! Learn how to make perfect fried rare salmon topped with a flavorful herb sauce right at home.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 24 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Average
Â :

Ingredients

  • 3 hardboiled eggs
  • 1 net used for oranges (or onions, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon parsley
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 fillet of salmon, chilled
  • Wheat flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Breadcrumb
  • Frying oil
  • Salmon roe

Steps

  1. Place hardboiled egg inside an orange net and squeeze to force it through the holes.
  2. Place chopped egg in a bowl along with parsley, onion, mayonnaise, salt, pepper and lemon juice. Stir to combine.
