Crispy Gnocchi Panzanella

An easy, unexpected twist on classic panzanella. Air-fried gnocchi adds a light, crispy element to this bright summer sharing salad.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Crispy Gnocchi Panzanella

  • 17oz gnocchi
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small red onion, finely sliced
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 3 tbsp capers, drained washed and patted dry
  • 1 pound of mixed colored tomatoes, quartered
  • ⅓ cup of pitted mixed olives

Serving

  • Basil leaves
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil
Get Ingredients

Steps

Crispy Gnocchi Panzanella

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Once hot, coat the gnocchi in 1 tbsp of olive oil and place in the airfryer basket. Air fry for 12 minutes, shaking the basket every 2-3 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, mix the sliced red onion and the red wine vinegar together, and set aside.
  4. Remove the gnocchi from the air fryer and season with salt and pepper.
  5. In a medium frying pan, add the remaining olive oil. Once hot, add the capers and fry for 3- 5 mins until crispy. Remove from the pan and place onto a lined tray to remove excess oil.

Serving

  1. When ready to serve, mix the gnocchi, tomatoes and olives in some olive oil.
  2. Top with the red onions and capers.
  3. Finally, place some leaves of basil onto the dish and enjoy with a cold glass of Kim Crawford Illuminate Sauvignon Blanc.
