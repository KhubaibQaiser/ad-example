Bun Cha

Let Erwan show you how to put together this easy-to-make Vietnamese rice noodle dish.

By erwan heussaff

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Bun Cha

  • 1/2 package vermicelli noodles
  • 4 fried wonton wrappers
  • 4 tablespoons carrots, julienned
  • 4 tablespoons cucumbers julienned
  • 4 tablespoons peanuts, chopped
  • 1 bunch mint
  • 1 bunch thai basil
  • 1 scallion, chopped

Sausage

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 red onion, minced
  • 1/2 red thai chili, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 jalapeno, minced
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil

Sauce

  • 1/2 red chili, sliced
  • 1/3 cup of fish sauce
  • 3 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of cane vinegar

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, add uncooked vermicelli noodles. Cover with boiling water and let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. To plate: In a large serving bowl add noodles. On top of noodles, add cooked sausages, carrots, cucumber, peanuts, mint, Thai basil, scallion, drizzle with sesame oil, and sauce. Top with a wonton wrapper. Serve
