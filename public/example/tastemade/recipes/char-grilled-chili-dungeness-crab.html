Char Grilled Chili Dungeness Crab

Messy fingers are worth it for Chef Dale Talde's chili Dungeness crab, which are tossed in a savory sauce and grilled to perfection.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 large shallots, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ketchup
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon minced thai chilis
  • 3 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1/2 cup fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon toasted shrimp paste

Crab

  • 4 dungeness crabs, cooked

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add the oil.
  2. Saute the ginger, shallots and garlic.
