Grilled Pork Chops With Spicy Grilled Peaches

Bring a little sweet to your savory pork dish with satisfyingly smoky grilled peaches.

(2)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, plus more for the grill
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon chili paste
  • 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup
  • 2 2 bone-in pork chops
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 peaches, quartered
  • Fresh basil leaf, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat grill to medium-high.
  2. Combine the chili paste, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and maple syrup in a small bowl, and set aside.
