Chai-Spiced Vermicelli

Nadiya Hussain shows us how to create the perfect dishes to complement any kind of day; like her chai-spiced vermicelli, an aromatic and warming vegan breakfast.

By nadiya hussein

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 15 grams coconut flakes (or desiccated coconut)
  • 100 grams pasta vermicelli
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cardamom pod
  • 30 milliliters coconut oil
  • 500 milliliters coconut cream
  • 40 grams caster sugar

Steps

  1. Put a saucepan over a medium heat and drop in the coconut. Toast for a few minutes, stirring occasionally. Be careful not to leave the pan, because it will burn quickly.
  2. Transfer the coconut to a bowl and set aside.
