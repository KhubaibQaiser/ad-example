Loaded Sofrito Sandwich

A protein-packed sandwich loaded w/ beautiful fresh herbs, spices, cashew mozzarella, smashed chickpeas, & maple roasted potatoes. Best part: you can serve the magical sofrito & chickpea mixture with chips, salads, lettuce wraps, tortillas, and more!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 2
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Overall Ingredients

  • fresh sofrito (see below)
  • toasted french baguette
  • 2 slices vegan mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups cooked white beans or chickpeas (smashed)
  • 1 cup fresh chopped greens
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/3 cup sliced tomatoes
  • roasted sweet potatoes (see below)
  • roasted rainbow potatoes (see below)

Fresh Sofrito

  • 1/2 cup green bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup red bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup white onion
  • 3 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 1/4 cup green cubanelle, cuban, or Italian frying pepper
  • 1 aji dulce habanero or sweet chili pepper
  • 1/3 cup cilantro
  • 1/3 cup recao
  • salt (to taste)
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas or plain hummus

Roasted Sweet Potatoes

  • 1 sweet potato cut into rounds
  • 2 tbsp poultry seasoning
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 1/2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp oil (neutral)
  • salt and pepper (to taste)

Roasted Rainbow Potatoes

  • 2 - 4 cups small rainbow potatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

Overall Ingredients

  1. Set the oven to 415Â°F in preparation for baking the potatoes.
  2. In a large bowl, combine sweet potatoes, poultry seasoning, smoked paprika, maple syrup, oil, salt and pepper. Mix together until the potatoes are fully coated in seasoning.
  3. In a separate large bowl, combine chopped small rainbow potatoes, chili powder, cayenne pepper, garlic and onion powder. Mix together until the potatoes are fully coated in seasoning.
  4. On a large covered baking sheet, place the sweet potatoes on one side and the chopped rainbow potatoes on the other side.
  5. Bake the potatoes for 20-35 minutes or until they are soft enough to pierce with a fork.
  6. While the potatoes are baking, make the fresh Sofrito by combining all of the ingredients together in a food processor or blender. Add in cooked chickpeas or plain hummus to the blended Sofrito to make it creamy and smooth.
  7. Set aside some of the Sofrito to use for dipping, and combine the rest with smashed chickpeas in a medium bowl. Mix well and set this aside.
  8. Slice your bread in half and then add a bit of vegan mozzarella cheese onto each side of the bread. Toast the bread until the cheese is slightly melted and then build your sandwich with the sliced cucumbers, sliced tomatoes, roasted sweet potatoes, fresh Sofrito smashed Chickpeas, fresh greens and creamy sofrito sauce. Add in as much or as little vegetables and other ingredients that you enjoy to create a seriously stacked sandwich.
  9. Serve the sandwich with a side of roasted rainbow potatoes and the creamy Sofrito dipping sauce.
  10. Sing your song. Do your dance. Speak your truth and enjoy the Sofrito Sandwich!
