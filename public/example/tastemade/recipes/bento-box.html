Bento Box

Laura makes vegan chili, builds a fresh veggie salad with a very special guest, and shows you how to make the best bento box for lunch on the go!

By Laura Miller

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Raw. Vegan. Not Gross.

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Quinoa Pizza Bites

  • 1/4 cup onion, roughly chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, diced
  • 2 flax eggs
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup brown rice flour
  • 2 cups cooked quinoa

Pizza sauce

  • 1/2 cup sun dried tomatoes
  • 5–6 roma tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 lemon juiced
  • 1/2 piece of the onion/garlic mixture from the pizza bite recipe

Kale chips

  • 1 bunch kale
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 cup soaked walnuts
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Saute onion and garlic and olive oil on a skillet with a dash of olive oil until softened
