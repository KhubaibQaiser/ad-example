Red Wine Hot Chocolate

Meld two of your favorites into one with this boozy red wine hot chocolate.

By lani nishiyama

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 ounces dark chocolate, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 dash salt
  • 1/2 cup merlot or fruity red wine
  • 2 large marshmallows, for presentation/garnish

Steps

  1. Use a whisk and mix the chocolate, cocoa powder, sugar, and milk in a saucepan. Bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. Whisk occasionally until smooth (3 minutes) and add salt to taste.
