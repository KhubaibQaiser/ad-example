Butternut Squash Oatmeal Risotto

This creamy oatmeal with rich butternut squash and touch of sage is comfort in a bowl.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 8 hr 20 min
Cook Time: 8 hr
Servings: 1
Average
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 2 cups butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 sage leaves, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 3 1/2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup steel-cut oats
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400°F. Line a sheet tray with parchment paper.
  2. Toss squash, olive oil, and salt and pepper on the sheet tray. Roast until tender, about 25-30 minutes. Cool and refrigerate.
  3. Melt the butter in a large sauté pan over medium-high heat. Add onions and garlic, and sauté until soft. Add the sage the last minute of cooking, and season with salt and pepper. Stir in the wine and scrape up any bits on the bottom of the pan. Bring to a simmer, then transfer to a crockpot.
  4. Pour in chicken broth. Stir in the oats and cover with lid. Cook for 6-8 hours.
  5. Stir butternut squash and Parmesan cheese into the oats before serving. Enjoy!
