Lavender & Beet "Cheesecake"

This beautiful raw, vegan cheesecake is appropriate for all kinds of gatherings.

By Laura Miller

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Raw. Vegan. Not Gross.

Prep Time: 1 hr 20 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Crust

  • 3 cups coconut flour
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup beet pulp
  • 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 teaspoons lavender
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil

White Layer

  • 2 cups coconut meat
  • 1 cup cashews
  • 1 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 vanilla bean
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil

Pink Layer

  • 2 cups coconut meat
  • 1 cup cashews (soaked)
  • 1 cup coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup beet juice
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons lavender
  • 1 1/3 cups maple syrup

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients for your crust and press into an 8 inches springform, making sure to reinforce the elbow and sides so it doesn't break.
  2. Dehydrate overnight, or put in the oven for 2 hours at 200°F
