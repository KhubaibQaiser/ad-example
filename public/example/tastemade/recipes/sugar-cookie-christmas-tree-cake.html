Sugar Cookie Christmas Tree Cake

Show stopping cookie cake that will delight any crowd

By Mia Starr

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 24
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Sugar Cookies

  • 3 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs, room temperature
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract

Buttercream

  • 2 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 pound powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • Gel food coloring

Decorate

  • 2–3 cups of mini marshmallows
  • Buttercream
  • Piping tip 1m
  • Large french
  • Sprinkles or dragee

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, salt and cornstarch and set aside.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment.
