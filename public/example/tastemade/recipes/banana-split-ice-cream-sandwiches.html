Banana Split Ice Cream Sandwiches

Enjoy the flavors of a classic banana split in the form of a homemade ice cream sandwich, thanks to this easy dessert recipe.

By julie nolke

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 3 bananas
  • Neapolitan ice cream
  • 6 ounces good quality dark chocolate, melted
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • Chocolate shell
  • Chopped walnut
  • Maraschino cherry

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F and line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl whisk together the flour and cocoa together. Using a stand mixer or a hand held mixture and a large bowl, whip together the eggs, sugar and brown sugar until pale and fluffy. Add in the chocolate and butter and mix.
