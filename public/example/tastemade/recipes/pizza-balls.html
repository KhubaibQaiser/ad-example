Pizza Balls

This savory pull-apart bread is stuffed with mozzarella and topped with pepperoni, tomato sauce, and, of course — more cheese.

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 18
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons yeast
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 3/4 cup lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup warm milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 3/4 cups flour, divided
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 5 ounces mozzarella cheese, cubed
  • 3/4 cup mozzarella, shredded
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni
  • 1 tablespoon oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the yeast, olive oil, water, milk and sugar. Add 2 cups of flour and the salt and mix well.
