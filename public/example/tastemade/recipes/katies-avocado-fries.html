Avocado Fries with Spicy Mayo Dip

What's not to love about creamy and crispy avocado fritters?

By katie quinn

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :

Ingredients

Avocado Fries

  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Oil for frying
  • 1–2 cups milk
  • 1 egg, beaten with a drop of water
  • 1/2–1 cup flour
  • Salt, to taste

Spicy Mayo Dip

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lime
  • Sriracha, to taste

Steps

  1. Dunk a slice of avocado in the milk, flour, eggs, and then back into the flour.
  2. Put the slices on a cookie sheet and then fry them!
