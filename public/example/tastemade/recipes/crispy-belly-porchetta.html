Crispy Belly Porchetta

Massage in the flavor, roll it tight, then roast to crispy perfection.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 3 hr
Cook Time: 8 hr
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 5–7 pounds boneless pork belly, skin on
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground pepper
  • 2 tablespoons ground fennel
  • 1 tablespoon crushed red pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped rosemary
  • 2 tablespoons chopped sage
  • 12 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Kosher salt

Steps

  1. Place pork belly on large cutting board skin side up. Use a sharp knife or skewers to prick holes across the entirety of the skin.
  2. Flip the belly so flesh side is up. Score the flesh to create a diamond pattern.
