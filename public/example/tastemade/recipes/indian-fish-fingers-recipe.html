Indian Fish Fingers

Spice up your life with these epic Indian-inspired fish fingers. Delicious.

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 35 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Indian Fish Fingers

  • 225 white fish fillets, skinless & boneless cut into fingers
  • Salt
  • 1/4 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1/2 shallot
  • 1 small handful fresh coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar

Coating

  • Plain flour, for dusting
  • 2 eggs, beaten with a splash of water
  • 2 slices white bread made into breadcrumbs, seasoned with salt, turmeric, paprika and chilli flakes
  • Sunflower oil for frying

Steps

  1. Sprinkle salt over the fish fingers.
  2. Using a food processor, grind together the rest of the ingredients, along with 1/2 teaspoon water to make a smooth paste.
