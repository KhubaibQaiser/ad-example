Mummy Jalapeños Poppers

Jalapeños filled with a yummy cheese filling and wrapped in pie dough for a great snack

By Mia Starr

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 22 min
Servings: 10
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened to room temperature
  • 4 ounces shredded jack cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 5 eaches jalapeños, cut in half lengthwise with seeds removed
  • 1 box prepared pie dough
  • 1 egg, slightly beaten for egg wash
  • 1 small can sliced black olives

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F and line a cookie sheet with foil or parchment and spray lightly with oil.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the cream cheese, jack cheese, garlic and onion powder and salt and pepper. Mix until well combined.
