Black-Eyed Pea Fritters with Spicy Herb Sauce

Chef Keith Corbin combines California cuisine with classic Soul food in this black-eyed fritters pea fritters recipe.

By keith corbin

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Black Eyed Pea Fritters

  • 1 quart dried black eyed peas or 4 cups canned black eyed peas, rinsed and drained
  • 2 quarts water
  • 1 1/4 inches piece ginger, peeled and minced
  • 2/3 cup yellow onion, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flake
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Water, as needed
  • 1 quart canola or vegetable oil, for frying
  • Spicy herb sauce

Spicy Herb Sauce

  • 2 serrano peppers, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 bunches cilantro, leaves and stems
  • 2 bunches flat leaf parsley, leaves and tender stems
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 3/4 cup olive oil
  • 5 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Combine dried black eyed peas and water and let soak for 24 hours.
  2. Drain and rinse beans.
