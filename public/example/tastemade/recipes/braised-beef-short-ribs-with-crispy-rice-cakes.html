Braised Beef Short Ribs With Crispy Rice Cakes

Succulent beef short ribs and crispy rice cakes combine for the perfect entree.

By Derrell Smith

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Mad Good Food

Prep Time: 8 hr
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Braised Short Ribs

  • 5 pounds beef short ribs, bone in
  • 10 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons of ginger, minced
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup sesame oil
  • 3 tablespoons chili oil
  • 1 cup packed dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon toasted white sesame seeds
  • 1/4 cup green onion, sliced
  • 1 bunch cilantro, chopped
  • 1 bunch scallion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup spicy mayo
  • Lime, wedges

Crispy Rice Cakes

  • 2 1/2 cups cooked sushi rice (start with 1 1/4 cups dry)
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • Oil, for frying

Pickled Cabbage

  • 1 small cabbage, thinly sliced about 2-pounds.
  • 2 medium carrots
  • 1 medium bell pepper, any color
  • 2 cups water
  • 7 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1/3 cup olive oil

Steps

  1. In a blender, add the garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, brown sugar, rice vinegar and black pepper blend until smooth.
  2. Add the marinade and the meat to a sealed container and refrigerate for at least 8 hours.
