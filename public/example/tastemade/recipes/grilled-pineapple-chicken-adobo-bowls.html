Grilled Pineapple Chicken Adobo Bowls ​

For a flavor-loaded dish that never fails, whip up pineapple chicken adobo made with the creamy goodness of Kraft Sauces. One bite, and you'll have this recipe on repeat all summer.

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 6
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Marinade

  • ¼ cup KRAFT Garlic Aioli​
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce​
  • 2 tablespoons, white vinegar ​
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar​
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper​
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs, about 4 ​

Adobo Bowl

  • 4 thick slices of peeled, fresh pineapple​
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges​
  • ½ bunch cilantro, chopped ​
  • 1 avocado, cubed ​
  • ¼ cup KRAFT Buffalo Mayo​
  • 1 cup cooked rice

Steps

Marinade

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the marinade ingredients, pour, over chicken and allow to marinade for at least an hour, up to overnight. ​

Adobo Bowl

  1. ​When the chicken is marinated, preheat a grill pan to medium high heat.​
  2. ​Grill the chicken for about 4-6 minutes per side, until the internal temp is 165 degrees F. Set the chicken aside to rest.​
  3. ​On the same grill pan, grill the pineapple slices, just until warmed and slightly caramelized. Remove and set aside.​
  4. ​Slice the grilled chicken into strips and cut the grilled pineapple into bite size chunks.​
  5. ​Serve the chicken and pineapple over rice with diced avocado, cilantro, lime wedges and a drizzle of KRAFT Buffalo Mayo.​
