Smoked Turkey Gravy

The Smoked Turkey Gravy To Cover All Your Thanksgiving Sins. You Should Probably Start Cooking ... Soon!

By Noah Galuten

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Don't Panic Pantry

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 quarts turkey stock
  • 1 1/2 cups flour (plus more as needed)
  • 2 1/2 sticks butter (plus more as needed)
  • 2 cups chopped shallots
  • 1 cup chopped sage
  • 1 very large dash of soy sauce

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a heavy-bottomed pot over medium. Add the shallots and sage and cook them until they are aromatic and fully tender.
  2. Add the flour and stir constantly, toasting the flour until it is fully cooked -- about 3 minutes.
