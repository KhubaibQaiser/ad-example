Rainbow Donuts

Restaurant owners and married couple, Jasmine & Sharky, design donuts and discuss diversity in the kitchen. They and Ingrid roll out rainbow donuts and revel in running a queer friendly restaurant.

By Ingrid Nilsen

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Cooking With Pride

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Donuts

  • 700 grams all-purpose flour
  • 30 grams instant yeast
  • 10 grams kosher salt
  • 20 grams ground flax
  • 50 grams sugar
  • 50 grams coconut oil
  • 415 grams water
  • 15 grams vanilla extract

Glaze

  • Hibiscus
  • Orange: mango/turmeric
  • Matcha
  • Blue magik/vanilla
  • Lavender/ube
  • 120 milliliters coconut milk
  • 600 grams confectioners sugar

Steps

  1. Combine all dry ingredients in a stand mixer and liquids in a deli.
  2. Set to speed 1 and slowly start adding liquid mix until dough starts to form.
