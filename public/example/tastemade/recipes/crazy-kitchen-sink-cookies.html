Crazy Kitchen Sink Cookies

These "crazy kitchen sink cookies" use random ingredients hanging around the kitchen! This budget-friendly cookie recipe tastes delicious and keeps dessert super simple.

By dini klein

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 12
Â :

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 8 tablespoons butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons tahini paste
  • 3 tablespoons white sweet miso paste
  • 1 teaspoon low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 1 1/2 cups crushed pretzels
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F, and line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
