Chili-Cumin Lamb and Portobello Mushroom Sticks

Prepare Chili-Cumin Lamb and Portobello Mushroom Sticks by marinating tender lamb and meaty portobello mushrooms in a zesty chili-cumin blend, then grilling them to smoky perfection, creating a mouthwatering fusion of flavors and textures on skewers.

Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4

 :

Ingredients

Chili-Cumin Lamb and Portobello Mushroom Sticks

  • 1 1/2 pounds lamb shoulder, trimmed
  • 1/2 pound portobello mushrooms
  • 12 inches bamboo skewers, soaked in water

Marinade

  • 1/2 cup chili oil
  • 1/4 cup neutral oil
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried ginger
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the lamb into 1/2 inch cubes and marinate for 2 hours.
  2. Remove the gills from the mushrooms and drizzle with oil and salt and pepper.
