Holiday Apple Cocktail

Celebrate the holiday season with a festive apple cocktail.

By Greg King

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 green apple
  • 1 red apple
  • Apple schnapp
  • Add sweet & sour
  • Vodka
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Peel apples with a vegetable peeler. Add peels to a blender with Triple Sec and blend until smooth. Pour Triple Sec mixture into a pitcher. Add Apple Schnapps, Sweet & Sour and Vodka to the pitcher and stir to combine. Pour into a cocktail glass filled with ice.
