Grilled Fennel Cheeseburger

Megan takes you to one of her favorite spots to give her take on the classic American cheeseburger — with a few artisan twists.

By megan mitchell

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 3
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Quick Pickle Red Onion

  • 1 large red onion, sliced in 1/4-inch rounds
  • 1/4 cup of seasoned rice vinegar
  • Juice of 1 orange
  • Juice of 1/2 grapefruit
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon of allspice berries
  • 1 bay leaf

Potato & Root Chip Melange

  • 1 1/2–3 pounds assorted color potatoes, beets, & sweet potatoes
  • Peanut oil
  • Garnish with: coarse salt & pepper & fennel frond

Grilled Fennel

  • 2 fennel bulbs
  • Salt & pepper

Cheeseburger

  • 1 pound ground chuck
  • Mount tam cheese (cowgirl creamery) or similar soft-brie cheese ciabatta roll or hamburger buns
  • Arugula
  • Spicy brown mustard

Steps

  1. Into a jar or container that has a tight sealing lid, combine all the ingredients and seal.
  2. Let the onions rest in the pickling liquid for at least 10 minutes prior to eating.
