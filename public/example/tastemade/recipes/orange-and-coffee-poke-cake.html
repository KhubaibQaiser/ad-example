Orange and Coffee Poke Cake

Perfect for brunch or afternoon tea, chef Nadiya Hussain's coffee cake recipe delivers on tart orange flavor. Plus, it stays moist all weekend long by using the "poke cake" method.

By Nadiya Hussein

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 9
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Coffee Cake

  • 225 g/8 oz sunflower oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 225 g/8 oz dark brown sugar
  • 4 free-range eggs
  • 5 tsp instant coffee, mixed with 2 tbsp boiling water
  • 225 g/8 oz self-raising flour, sifted
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Orange Curd

  • 6 free-range egg yolks
  • 3 oranges, zest and juice
  • 100g/3½oz caster sugar
  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter
  • 3 tbsp cornflour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C /160C Fan/Gas 4. Grease the base and sides of a square 23cm/9in cake tin and line with baking paper.
  2. Put the oil and sugar into a bowl and mix well. Add the eggs and whisk in well, then stir in the coffee mixture.
