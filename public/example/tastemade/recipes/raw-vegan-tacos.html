Raw Vegan Tacos

Just because they're not authentic doesn't mean they're not delicious.

(1)

By Laura Miller

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Raw. Vegan. Not Gross.

Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 3
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Taco Shells

  • 3 cups fresh corn
  • 3/4 cup finely ground flaxseed
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 tablespoon ground chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast

Filling

  • 1 portobello mushroom
  • 1 cup sunflower seeds soaked 2 hours
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon agave nectar
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 chopped jalapeno
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 handful fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

Vegan Sour Cream

  • 2 cups cashews, soaked 4–6 hours
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 cups spinach and 1 cup to coat

Steps

  1. Blend all of the ingredients, except the flaxseed, in a high-speed blender or food processor.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and mix in the flaxseed.
