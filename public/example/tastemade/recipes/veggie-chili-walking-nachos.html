Veggie Chili Walking Nachos

Veggie Chili Walking Nachos are a tasty and healthy twist on classic nachos, loaded with a vegetarian chili made with beans, vegetables, and spices, and served over a bed of crispy tortilla chips with melted cheese and fresh toppings.

By derrell smith

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 large white onion, diced
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 bunch of cilantro, chopped
  • 1 chipotle chili in adobo, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon adobo sauce
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 15 ounces pinto beans
  • 15 ounces black beans
  • 1/4 cup evoo
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 28 ounces fire roasted tomato
  • 1 pinch kosher salt
  • 1 snack bag favorite salty chip
  • Labne for topping
  • Lime
  • Shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Heat EVOO in a large saucepan or Dutch oven over medium-high. Add onion, garlic, cilantro, and 2 teaspoons chili powder and cook, stirring frequently, until onion is starting to soften, 5–6 minutes. Add tomato paste mixture and cook, stirring frequently, until brick red and starting to stick to the bottom of the pan, 2–3 minutes.
  2. Add 28 ounces fire-roasted tomatoes and bring to a simmer. Cook, stirring frequently, until tomatoes are cooked down and slightly darker in color, 8–10 minutes.
