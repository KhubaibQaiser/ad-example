Arroz Caldo

Everyone loves this hearty rice porridge, made with chicken broth, ginger, garlic and patis (a Filipino fish sauce).

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

Arroz Caldo

  • 3 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 piece red onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 (1 inch) ginger, cut into strips
  • 42 1/4 ounces whole chicken, cut into 12
  • 3/4 cup long-grained rice
  • 1/2 cup glutinous rice
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons fish sauce/patis
  • 6 cups water

Toppings

  • Spring onion
  • Fried garlic
  • 6 pcs hard-boiled eggs
  • 6 pieces calamansi, cut in half

Steps

  1. In a large pot with hot oil, sauté onions. Garlic, and ginger. Brown the chicken and add rice. Season with 1 tablespoon fish sauce.
  2. When rice is coated in oil, add water and bring to a boil. Lower heat and skim off the scum that floats. Cook for 45 minutes or until the rice has “popped” or expanded. Season with fish sauce and serve with spring onions, garlic, eggs, and calamansi.
