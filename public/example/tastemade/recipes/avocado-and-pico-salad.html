Avocado and Pico Salad

This bright, spicy salad makes the perfect accompaniment to crispy empanadas.

By eduardo garcia

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 firm but ripe avocados, cut into 8 pieces each
  • 4 roma tomatoes, sliced 1/4 thick
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and cut into 1/4 slice slices
  • 1/2 white onion, quartered and thinly sliced, and rinsed
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro, rinsed, dried and roughly chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons montana mex chile seasoning
  • 2–3 tablespoons montana mex avocado oil
  • To taste: salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Arrange tomatoes, cucumber, and onions on a platter.
  2. Disperse avocado slices evenly on top.
