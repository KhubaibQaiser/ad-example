Banana Leaf Minced Chicken & Rice Noodles with Thai Chili Lime Sauce

Looking for a unique grilling recipe that's sure to impress everyone at your next barbecue? Try Chef Dale Talde's minced chicken patties, flavored with Thai flavors like chili, lime, and curry.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 12 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Banana Leaf Minced Chicken

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 2 shallots, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon red thai curry paste
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup minced cilantro stems
  • Bananas leaf

Thai Chili Lime Sauce

  • 3 thai chilis, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup sugar

Rice Noodles

  • Vietnamese rice noodle
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup cilantro leaves

Steps

  1. Light a charcoal grill.
  2. Mix together the ground chicken, shallots, garlic, sugar, Thai chili paste, fish sauce, and cilantro stems.
