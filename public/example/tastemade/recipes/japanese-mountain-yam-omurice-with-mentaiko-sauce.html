Japanese Mountain Yam Omurice with Mentaiko Sauce

Mushroom and okra fried rice is best served underneath a fluffy omelet topped with a sticky, savory sauce.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 100 grams mountain yam
  • 1 pouch mentaiko (pollock roe)
  • 5 milliliters mentsuyu (soba soup base)
  • 150 grams cooked rice
  • 1/4 onion, chopped
  • 1 okra, chopped
  • 30 grams maitake mushrooms, finely chopped
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 5 grams butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 15 milliliters milk
  • 10 milliliters olive oil
  • 2 perilla leaves, thinly sliced
  • Nori, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Grate the mountain yam and combine with the mentaiko and mentsuyu.
  2. In a frying pan, melt some butter and saute the onions until almost translucent. Add in maitake mushrooms, okra, rice salt, and saute for a few minutes.
