Watermelon Caesar

Try a twist on a traditional Caesar salad with this refreshing watermelon salad!

By Greg King

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 romaine lettuce hearts
  • 1/4 cup watermelon, chopped into 1/4 inch squares
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons coarse ground coarse ground black pepper
  • Caesar dressing, to taste

Steps

  1. Chop two lettuce hearts on diagonal angle into 1/2 inch pieces. Place into a large mixing bowl. Add the parmesan, mint, coarse pepper and dressing and toss. Top with watermelon, lightly mix with wooden spoon.
