Israeli Salad

This fresh salad adds brightness to any plate - pair with your favorite protein and grain for a light meal.

By einat admony

Updated: 10/2/2024

Ingredients

Israeli Salad

  • 4 ripe and firm tomatoes, 1/2 inch chunks
  • 3 persian cucumbers, 1/2-inch chunks
  • 1 small red bell pepper, 1/2 inch chunks
  • 1 medium carrot, 1/2 inch dice
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons mint, finely chopped
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground sumac
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Ground black pepper, to taste

Tahini

  • 1 cup raw tahini
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, plus more to taste
  • Ice water
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more as needed
  • 1 small garlic clove, grated or minced

Steps

  1. Put tomatoes, cucumbers, bell pepper, carrot, onion, parsley and mint in a large bowl and toss with lemon juice and olive oil.
  2. Season with sumac salt and pepper.
