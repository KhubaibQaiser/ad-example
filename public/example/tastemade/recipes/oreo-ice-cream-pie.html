Oreo Ice Cream Pie

This easy ice cream pie is packed with Oreo cookies.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 5 hr 30 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 package oreos
  • 6 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 14 ounces condensed milk

Steps

  1. In a plastic bag crush Oreos into crumbs, saving 1 cup for decoration.
  2. Add melted butter to crushed Oreos and mix well. Press into a pie pan and set aside.
