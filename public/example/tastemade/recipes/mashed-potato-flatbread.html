Mashed Potato Flatbread

A light and pillowy flatbread made from leftover mashed potatoes, topped with bacon, cheddar and leeks

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 15 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup leftover mashed potatoes
  • 4 tablespoons butter, divided

Flatbread

  • 2 leeks
  • 12 ounces bacon
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup minced chives
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Whisk the honey, warm water and yeast together in a bowl. Allow it to sit for 5 minutes, until it becomes frothy. Stir in the flour, sour cream, salt, olive oil and mashed potatoes. Continue to stir until a cohesive dough is formed. Turn the dough onto a floured work surface and knead for 10 minutes.
  2. Place in an oiled bowl and cover with a kitchen towel. Allow it to sit in a warm area for 1 hour until doubled in size.
