Maple Cake

A cinnamon spiced maple cake with a sweet brown sugar frosting.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 12
Average
 :

Ingredients

Cake

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/3 cup full fat greek yogurt
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk

Maple Frosting

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Grease and line a 9x13 cake pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt together.
