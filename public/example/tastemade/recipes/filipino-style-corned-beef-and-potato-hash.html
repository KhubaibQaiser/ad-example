Filipino-Style Corned Beef and Potato Hash

For your next breakfast or brunch, try a Filipino twist on the classic corned beef hash.

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound cooked (or 1 can) corned beef
  • 1/2 pound russet potato, peeled and cubed small
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons scallions, sliced
  • 1 jalapeño, thinly sliced
  • Ground black pepper, to taste
  • Steamed white rice, to serve
  • Oil for the pan

Steps

  1. Shred or cut cooked corned beef into small pieces and set aside.
  2. In a pan on medium heat, heat a drizzle of oil, then add potato cubes and 1 cup water and cook potatoes until the water has evaporated.
