Chickpea Meatballs

There's plenty of protein in these vegetarian meatballs, which use chickpeas for a satisfying bite atop any plate of pasta.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

  • Oil for frying
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups pasta sauce

Steps

  1. In a saute pan over medium heat, add the oil and saute the onion and garlic. Set aside.
  2. In a food processor, add the chickpea, egg, tomato paste, breadcrumbs, parsley, salt and pepper along with the sauteed onion and garlic. Blend until it forms a dough.
