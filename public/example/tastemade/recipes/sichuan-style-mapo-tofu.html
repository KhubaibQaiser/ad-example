Sichuan-Style Mapo Tofu

Here's a tofu recipe that won't let you down on flavor! Packed with Szechuan pepper, fermented black bean paste, and other aromatics, this Chinese recipe is an easy dinner you'll crave every night.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Sichuan-style Mapo Tofu

  • 12 1/4 ounces silken tofu
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 3 1/2 ounces ground beef
  • 5 1/4 ounces ground pork
  • Red peppers 1 piece
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon water-soluble potato starch
  • Green onion, as needed

A

  • 1 tablespoon fermented black bean paste
  • 2 tablespoons spicy bean sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sake
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

B

  • 7 fluid ounces hot water
  • 1/2 tablespoon chicken stock

C

  • 2 tablespoons chili oil
  • 1/2 scallion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon szechuan pepper

Steps

  1. Crush the Szechuan pepper using the bottom of a glass to make coarsely ground Szechuan pepper.
  2. Mince the garlic.
