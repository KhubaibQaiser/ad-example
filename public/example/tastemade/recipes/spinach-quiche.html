Spinach Quiche

Start your morning off the tasty way with a warm, flaky quiche of spinach and ricotta.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 2 hr
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 4
Average
Â :

Ingredients

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 4 tablespoons water
  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • Salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 bunch spinach, chopped
  • 5 eggs
  • Black pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the egg yolk and water.
  2. In another bowl, add the flour and salt, and place the cubes of butter on top. Blend with your fingertips until mixture becomes flaky.
