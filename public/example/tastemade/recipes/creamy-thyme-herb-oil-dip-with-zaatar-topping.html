Creamy Thyme Herb Oil Dip with Za'atar Topping

This recipe is ideal for a party but also great for meal prep (think salads, bowls, wraps). Store extra herb oil in the fridge and use it to make salad dressings, dipping oil for bread, or even to fry eggs!

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 10
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Creamy Goat Cheese Dip

  • 12 oz goat cheese
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil

Herb Oil and Za'atar Topping

  • 5 sprigs of thyme, stems removed
  • About 20 basil leaves
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 Cup olive oil
  • Za’atar, to taste for garnish

Steps

Creamy Goat Cheese Dip

  1. For the whipped dip, add all ingredients to the nutribullet SmartSense blender's 32 oz cup and select "auto" and blend for a full cycle. Transfer to a serving dish and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Herb Oil and Za'atar Topping

  1. For the herb oil, add all of the ingredients to the nutribullet SmartSense blender's 32 oz cup, select speed 3 and blend for 20 seconds.
  2. Transfer the blended herb mixture to a small saucepan.
  3. Heat the mixture over medium-low heat for about 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly, until it becomes fragrant and the color intensifies. Be careful not to let it simmer or boil, as this can dull the color and flavor.
  4. After heating, immediately transfer the saucepan into a large bowl filled with ice water to rapidly cool the oil. Stir the herb oil while cooling to preserve its bright green color.
  5. Strain through a fine mesh strainer. Set aside until ready to serve.
  6. Drizzle the whipped feta dip with herb oil and sprinkle with za’atar.
