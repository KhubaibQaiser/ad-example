Mango Punch Cocktail

A refreshing cocktail for any group party that can be served non-alcoholic too!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 5 min
Servings: 16
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 cups frozen mango, thawed
  • 2 cups pineapple juice
  • 7 cups lemon-lime soda
  • 1/2 cup powdered lemonade mix
  • 4 cups cognac
  • Ice, for serving
  • Mint, for garnish
  • Pineapple, for garnish

Steps

  1. Put the mango and 1 cup water into a blender, and blend together to make a puree.
  2. In a large punch bowl, add the remaining ingredients, along with the mango puree, and stir well.
