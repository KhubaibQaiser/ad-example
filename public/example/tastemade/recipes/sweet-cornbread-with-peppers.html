Sweet Cornbread with Peppers

Chef Greg King feeds a crowd during the holidays with his easy, sweet cornbread recipe, which gets a nice kick from banana peppers.

By Greg King

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup chopped banana peppers with juice
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 stick butter
  • 1/4 cup grapeseed oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, sugar, salt, baking soda, and drained peppers. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs, melted butter, and drained peppers.
