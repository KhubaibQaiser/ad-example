Baked Brie Bites

These bite-sized brie bites are the perfect flavour bombs for your next gathering!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 24
Average
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Filo Tart Cases and Filling

  • 2 filo pastry sheets, cut into roughly 2 inch squares
  • 2 oz unsalted butter, melted

Filling

  • 6 tbsp red onion chutney
  • 5 oz brie, cut into ½ inch cubes

Serving

  • 2 small red apples, cored and cut into thin slices
  • ¼ cup walnuts, finely chopped
  • Thyme leaves
  • Black pepper
Get Ingredients

Steps

Filo Tart Cases and Filling

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Keep the filo pastry squares covered with a damp tea towel to stop them drying out.
  3. Using a pastry brush, lightly coat the mini tart tin holes with butter.
  4. Take three of the filo squares and place them on top of each other at a slight angle to the one underneath (forming a star shape), with a coating of butter in between layers.
  5. Repeat with the remaining pastry until all 24 holes are filled.

Filling

  1. Place a piece of brie at the bottom of the tart case, add some red onion chutney on top, then add a final piece of brie.
  2. Repeat until all the holes are filled.
  3. Place into a preheated oven, and bake for 10 minutes until the pastry is golden brown, and the brie is oozy.

Serving

  1. Remove from the oven, and allow the tarts to rest for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Place bites onto a platter, and top with a slice of apple, some chopped walnuts and a sprig of thyme. Enjoy!
