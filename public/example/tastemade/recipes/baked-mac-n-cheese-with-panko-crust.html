Baked Mac N' Cheese With Panko Crust

This creamy baked macaroni is topped with a crunchy panko breadcrumb crust.

By Derrell Smith

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Mad Good Food

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces skinny penne macaroni, cooked
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded gruyere cheese
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups panko crumbs
  • 4 tablespoons butter melted
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Lightly grease a large 3-quarts baking dish and set aside.
