Spinach Meatballs

Your next meatless Monday calls for these vegetarian meatballs made with spinach and tossed in a creamy tomato sauce.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

  • Oil, for frying
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 1/2 ounces spinach
  • 3/4 cup ricotta
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk

Steps

  1. In a saute pan over medium heat, add the oil and saute the onion, garlic and spinach until the spinach is dry. Set aside.
  2. In a food processor, add ricotta, salt and pepper along with the sauteed vegetables. Blend until it becomes a dough. Add the breadcrumbs and blend until combined.
