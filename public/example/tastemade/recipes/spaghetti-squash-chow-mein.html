Spaghetti Squash Chow Mein

Amazing recipe for Spaghetti Squash Chow Mein!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 3
Average
 :

Ingredients

Spaghetti Squash Chow Mein

  • 1/2 pound boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-size
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon shaoxing wine (chinese cooking wine), sake or dry sherry wine
  • 1/2 teaspoon chinese five spice
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch black pepper
  • 2 ounces shiitake or button mushrooms
  • 2 ounces julienne carrot
  • 3 ounces sugar snap peas, cut into halves
  • 4 ounces bean sprouts
  • 2 green onions
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds

Sauce

  • 3 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1/4 cup chicken stock
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 red chili, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 400°F.
  2. Cut spaghetti squashes into 2 halves in lengthwise, and scoop out seeds completely.
