Sweet and Spicy Chicken Quesadilla

Make your homemade quesadillas extra flavorful with Chef Derrell Smith's easy sweet & spicy marinade, which utilizes Dijon mustard, balsamic vinegar, and plenty of spices.

By derrell smith

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Sweet and Spicy Chicken Quesadilla

  • 2 large boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic

Marinade

  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon salt

To Assemble

  • 4 (12-inches) flour tortillas
  • 2 cups monterey jack cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Heat a large nonstick pan over medium-high heat. Drizzle oil into the pan to coat.
  2. Add the chicken to sear on all sides until browned. Add the garlic, stir to combine and cover with a lid. Continue to cook over medium-low heat until cooked through.
