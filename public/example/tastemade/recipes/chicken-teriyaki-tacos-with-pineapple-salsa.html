Chicken Teriyaki Tacos With Pineapple Salsa

Put a refreshingly sweet twist onto savory chicken soft tacos with spicy, fruity salsa.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 35 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Chicken Teriyaki Tacos With Pineapple Salsa

  • 3–4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, grated
  • 2 tablespoons ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper
  • White rice
  • Small flour tortilla
  • Butter lettuce

Pineapple Salsa

  • 2 cups pineapple, grilled & diced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/3 cup red pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon cilantro, chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced
  • Salt and pepper

Teriyaki Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 2 tablespoons sake
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine chicken, vegetable oil, garlic, ginger, salt and pepper. Set aside to marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken and fry until golden brown.
