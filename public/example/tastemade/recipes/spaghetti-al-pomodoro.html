Spaghetti Al Pomodoro

A simple but flavorful tomato sauce is the star of this pasta dish.

By daniele uditi

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 28 ounces san marzano tomatoes puréed
  • 4 leaves fresh basil
  • 12 ounces thick spaghetti
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 1/4 cup grated parmigiano reggiano
  • 2 tablespoons freshly grated parmigiano reggiano

Steps

  1. Heat the oil with the garlic in a large sautéed pan.
  2. Once the oil is perfumed with the garlic, remove the garlic.
