Grilled Sweet Potatoes with Soy Sauce & Maple Bacon

Perfect for a sunny holiday season, Chef Dale Talde's sweet potatoes with soy maple bacon are the perfect grilled addition to your festive feast.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 4 slices bacon
  • 1/2 cup reduced sodium-soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup sherry vinegar
  • 1 stick cold butter, cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced scallions

Steps

  1. Heat the grill to medium.
  2. Peel the sweet potatoes and quarter them lengthwise, then cut into 1 inch thick slices.
