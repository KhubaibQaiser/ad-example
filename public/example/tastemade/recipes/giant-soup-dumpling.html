Giant Soup Dumpling

People said this couldn't be accomplished. BOOM - a dumpling bigger than your face!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Average
Â :

Ingredients

Broth

  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 teaspoons agar agar powder

Dough

  • 1 cup bread flour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 7 tablespoons boiling water
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons canola oil

Filling

  • 3/4 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound ground shrimp
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chinese cooking wine
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup minced scallions
  • 3 tablespoons grated ginger
  • 3 tablespoons grated garlic
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Place chicken stock in a large stockpot and bring to a boil, reduce the heat and allow to simmer for 15 minutes.
  2. Add the agar agar to the pot and whisk, allow to simmer for an additional 5 minutes and take off heat.
