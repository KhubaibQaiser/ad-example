Lemon Chia Seed Protein Cookies

Satisfy your sweet tooth and fuel up after the gym with a batch of protein-packed, lemon chia seed cookies.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 8 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups spelt flour
  • 1 tablespoon black chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 scoops vanilla protein powder
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 egg white
  • 2 tablespoons pure maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Combine flour, black chia seeds, baking powder, and protein powder in a large mixing bowl.
