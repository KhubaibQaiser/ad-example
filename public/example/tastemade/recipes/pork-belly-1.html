Braised Pork Belly

This pork belly is pretty fancy.... and so good.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 2 hr 15 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 21 1/4 ounces pork belly, skin removed, sliced and chopped into 1 inch or less cubes
  • 12 quails eggs and/or 4 chicken eggs, hard boiled, peeled
  • 3 asian shallots or medium onions, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic - finely chopped
  • 2 birds eye chillies, whole
  • 14 fluid ounces coconut water
  • 7 fluid ounces cider
  • 2 tablespoons coconut caramel
  • 5 tablespoons premium quality fish sauce, to season
  • 1 pinch black pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the pork belly into bite sized pieces with skin. Hard boil and peel the eggs and set aside.
  2. Sweat off the shallots in a medium saucepan for about 5 minutes then add half the meat to colour off then the other half. Pour into the saucepan the coconut juice and coconut caramel (for colouring and a smoky flavor). Bring to a gentle boil. Remove scum and season with premium quality fish sauce and a generous pinch of black pepper.
