Cream Cheese-Filled Banana Bread

Perfect for brunch or dessert, this easy banana bread recipe comes together in a crockpot.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 3 hr 15 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Banana Bread

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt

Cream Cheese Filling

  • 1 large egg
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Make the banana bread: In a large bowl, mash bananas.
  2. Next, add egg, brown sugar, granulated sugar, butter, greek yogurt, and vanilla then mix together.
