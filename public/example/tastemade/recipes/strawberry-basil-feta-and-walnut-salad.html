Strawberry, Basil, Feta and Walnut Salad

This simple side salad pairs ripe strawberries with a sweet strawberry jam dressing.

By carolyn mcneil

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 6 min
Servings: 1
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Strawberry, Basil, Feta and Walnut Salad

  • 1 cup arugula
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, halved
  • 12 ounces feta cheese, cut into chunks
  • Handful of fresh basil, gently hand torn or roughly chopped
  • 1 cup fresh ripe strawberries, hulled and sliced

Dressing

  • 1 1/2 tablespoon strawberry jam
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon whole grain mustard
  • Large pinch salt and pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed and finely ground

Steps

Strawberry, Basil, Feta and Walnut Salad

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Place walnuts on a baking tray and pop into the oven for 5-6 minutes so they gently toast and become more flavorful. Take out and leave to cool.
  3. When you are ready to serve, in a large bowl gently toss all the ingredients along with the dressing. Lay salad on a large platter and place flowers on top.

Dressing

  1. Mix all the ingredients for the dressing in a small bowl or jar and leave to one side. (This keeps well in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.)
