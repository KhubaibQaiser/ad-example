Uni Carbonara

Take your creamy noodles to the next level with sea urchin and roe.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Uni Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons uni
  • 4 1/4 fluid ounces cream
  • 5 1/4 fluid ounces water
  • 3 tablespoons shiro dashi (dashi stock)

Uni Carbonara

  • 3 tablespoons sea urchin
  • 3 tablespoons cooked crab meat
  • 2 tablespoons ikura (salmon roe)
  • 1 package udon noodles
  • Green onion, chopped
  • Gold leaf (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine 2 tablespoons uni, cream, water and shiro dashi stock in a blender and mix until smooth.
