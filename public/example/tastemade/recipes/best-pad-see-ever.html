Pad See Ew

This Pad See Ew recipe, a classic Thai street food of stir-fried noodles, will become your go-to easy weeknight dinner.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 7 ounces flat rice noodles
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 1 sliced sliced garlic clove
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 1–2 pak chois

Steps

  1. To activate your noodles, simply soak them in water according to the packet.
  2. Heat your pan and add in your sliced onions and garlic. Cook till they start to turn a golden brown.
