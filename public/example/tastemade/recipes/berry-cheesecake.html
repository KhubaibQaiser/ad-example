Berry Cheesecake

This simple Berry Cheesecake is perfect for this time of year because it's light, it's chilled, and you can use all of the fresh berries that abound.

By katie quinn

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 28 ounces whole milk ricotta
  • 8 ounces reduced fat cream cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 4 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 cup confectioner's sugar
  • Fresh berry, to top (use your favorite berries/fruit!)

Steps

  1. Blend all of the ingredients together -- except the berries! Blend until smooth.
  2. If you do cut the recipe in half, as I suggest, just one 9x12-inches baking pan should do the trick here. Spread it evenly in the pan.
