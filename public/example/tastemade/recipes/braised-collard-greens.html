Braised Collard Greens

Chef Derrell Smith's smokey, slightly spicy collard greens are quick and delicious.

(1)

By Derrell Smith

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Mad Good Food

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 bunches collard greens
  • 1 jalapeño
  • 1 cup sliced onions
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1–2 teaspoons of liquid smoke, to taste
  • 1–2 cups of chicken broth or stock, as needed
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large saute pan, melt butter and saute minced garlic, sliced onions for about 2–3 mins.
  2. Add the collard greens, and jalapeño to the pot, but be sure not to cut or pierce, it will make the collards too spicy. Sauté for 5 mins.
