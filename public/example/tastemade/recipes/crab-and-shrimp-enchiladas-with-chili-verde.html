Crab and Shrimp Enchiladas with Chili Verde

Change up your enchilada game by adding seafood and chili verde along with some melty cheese for an ideal Mexican-inspired meal.

By Alex Hill

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Spice Spice Baby

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Crab and Shrimp Enchiladas

  • 8 ounces jumbo lump crab meat
  • 1 pound jumbo shrimp
  • 1 cup mexican shredded cheese
  • 6–10 flour tortillas

Chili Verde

  • 5–6 large green tomatillos
  • 2 cloves whole garlic cloves
  • 1 medium white onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, cut in half
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Kosher salt & black pepper

Garnish

  • Mexican crema
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Bring water to a gentle boil, seasoned with a little salt. Add shrimp to the water and let cook for 3 minutes.
  2. Remove heat and set aside.
