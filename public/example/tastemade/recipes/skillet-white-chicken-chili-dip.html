Skillet White Chicken Chili Dip

Baptize your tortilla chips in the holiness that is thick, cheesy white chicken chili dip.

(2)Recipe Notes (1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups white beans, drained
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup shredded cooked chicken breast
  • 1 cup canned corn
  • 1/4 cup diced green chiles
  • 1/2 cup diced red bell peppers, divided
  • 2 tablespoons chopped chives, plus more for garnish
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella, divided
  • 1 cup shredded sharp white cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1/3 cup salsa verde

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Grease a 10-inches cast-iron skillet with olive oil. Reserve 1/4 cup of the white beans and set aside.
