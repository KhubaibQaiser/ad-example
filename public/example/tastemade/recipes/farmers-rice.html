Farmer’s Rice

The “mother of Puerto Rican Cuisine,” Chef Giovanna Huyke, puts a spin on her mother’s famous farmer’s rice in this simple yet flavorful recipe.

By Monti Carlo

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 30 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Rice

  • 2 cups medium-grain rice
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon bijol
  • 3 cups water

Sauce

  • 4 ounces minced bacon
  • 1/2 cubanelle pepper diced
  • 1/2 medium onion (yellow) diced
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground garlic cloves
  • 1/2 piece large diced tomato
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 pound of fresh mushrooms, in quarters
  • 1 chicken stock cubito crumbled
  • 1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 medium tin diced roasted bell peppers, drained well
  • 5 ounces defrosted but not boiled frozen green peas

Chicken and Pork

  • 3/4 pound of skinned diced chicken (the thighs are perfect but can be done with breasts or with both)
  • 1/2 pound of pork tenderloin, cut into 1 1/2-inches cubes
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 1/2 cloves ground garlic
  • 1/2 tablespoon of poultry seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup dark puerto rican rum

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil slightly and saute the rice with the salt until coated with oil.
  2. Add the bijol and water and stir well. Let it come to a boil; lower the heat but keep simmering until dry.
