Classic Cornish Pasties

Nothing beats one of our Classic Cornish Pasties.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr 25 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 6
Easy

Ingredients

Classic Cornish Pasties

  • 450 grams plain flour
  • 125 grams butter
  • 2 2 yolks
  • 125 milliliters cold water

Filling

  • 400 grams potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 150 grams swede
  • 400 grams beef skirt
  • 9 grams plain flour
  • 35 grams butter
  • 1 egg, beaten

Steps

  1. To make the pastry place the flour, butter, egg yolks and generous 1 pinch of salt into a food processor and blitz until the mixture becomes crumb-like.
  2. Slowly add the water until a ball of pastry appears - you may not need all the water.
