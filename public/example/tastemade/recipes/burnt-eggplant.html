Burnt Eggplant

This succulent, caramelized eggplant makes the perfect side dish to delicious grilled meat and a fresh salad.

By Eduardo Garcia

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant
  • 1 cup strained yoghurt
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • 3 sprigs roughly chopped parsley
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt
  • Pinch cracked black pepper
  • ¼ cup white sesame seeds, lightly toasted
  • 1 tablespoon wildflower honey
  • Pinch Montana Mex seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to the high Broil setting on your oven.
  2. Line a small baking sheet with aluminum foil and place washed eggplant on a sheet pan.
