Coquito French Toast Bites

Sweet individual French toast bites with a dulce de leche topping and fresh blueberries

By Alex Hill

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Spice Spice Baby

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pack king hawaiian rolls
  • 1/2 cup half and half
  • 1/2 cup coquito mixture
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 pint blueberries
  • Powdered sugar, for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine 1/2 cup half & half, coconut milk, cinnamon, nutmeg, vanilla and egg in a bowl and whisk together.
  2. Dip Hawaiian rolls to fully submerge.
