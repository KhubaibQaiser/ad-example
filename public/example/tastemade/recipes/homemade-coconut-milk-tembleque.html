Homemade Coconut Milk & Tembleque

Today's ingredient: Coconut - and we mean ALL of it.

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 1 hr 30 min
Servings: 2
Average
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

For the Coconut Milk

  • 1 coconut
  • Flaxseed oil

For the Tembleque

  • 75g golden granulated sugar
  • 30g cornstarch
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Use a screwdriver to poke a hole into the soft eye of the coconut and drain the liquid. Then hold the coconut over a bowl and use a hammer or the back of a steady knife to knock firmly onto the centre of the shell, turning the coconut to go around the equator.
  2. After a few firm hits a crack will appear (you will also notice the sound change) and you can carefully open it to catch any remaining coconut water in the bowl.
