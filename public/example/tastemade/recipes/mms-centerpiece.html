Scary Movie Night M&M’S® Centerpiece

A Halloween scary movie night with friends calls for sweet & salty treats — like these DIY scary M&M’S® centerpieces filled with chocolate and popcorn!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/1/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Servings: 10
Easy
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • M&M’S® Ghoul’s Mix
  • Multi-colored construction paper or card stock
  • Bat stencil
  • Scissors
  • Pencil
  • Double sided tape or hot glue gun
  • Branches, in various sizes
  • 3 plastic cauldrons, in various sizes
  • 3 vases, in various sizes

Steps

  1. Use a pencil to trace the bat template onto black construction paper. Repeat the process, depending on the size of your branch. Cut the traced bats out of the black construction paper. Crease each one in the center to make them look like they're mid-flight. Apply glitter to some bats, and glue googly eyes onto each one. Use double-sided tape or hot glue to attach the bats along the length of the branches.
  2. Place the vases in the middle of each of the cauldrons. Fill the empty space in each cauldron with popcorn until it's almost spilling out. Fill the vases with M&M’S® Ghoul’s Mix.
  3. Place the larger bat branches inside the caldron, and place the smaller branches inside the vases with the M&M’S® Ghoul Mix.
  4. Add paper ghosts or other spooky decor to fill out the centerpiece. Top the vases with more M&M’S® Ghoul’s Mix. Set out at your next scary movie night and enjoy!
