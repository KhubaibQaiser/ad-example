Beet Hummus

Blend up the brightest hummus you've ever seen with a combination of chickpeas, boiled beets, and tahini.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 10
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas, cooked
  • 2 medium beets, boiled
  • 1 tablespoon tahini
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1 small garlic clove
  • Salt, to taste
  • 3 1/3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • Pita bread
  • Fresh ground black pepper, to taste
  • Thyme leaf
  • 1 tablespoon white sesame seeds, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a food processor, grind the chickpeas, beets, tahini, lime juice, garlic, salt and 1 tablespoon of olive oil into a paste. Set aside.
  2. Cut the bread into triangles, sprinkle with olive oil, season with salt and thyme, and bake in preheated oven at 350°F for approximately 20 minutes, or until toasted.
