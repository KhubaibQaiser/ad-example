Quesabirria Fire Bites

Psst, make your next party a hit with the ultimate finger food: quesabirria fire bites. We're talking TOSTITOS® Scoops! Multigrain loaded with tender birria and melty quesadilla cheese for a crunchy, one-bite appetizer you'll turn to again and again.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 3 hr
Servings: 10
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Birria

  • 1-1 ½ pound chuck roast, cut into 1 inch chunks
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon neutral oil
  • 1 tablespoon Mexican oregano, or regular
  • ½ tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ancho chili powder
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 Cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 jar TOSTITOS® Chunky Salsa, Mild or Medium
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Salt pepper, to taste

Quesabirria Bites

  • TOSTITOS® Scoops!® Multigrain Chips
  • Quesadilla cheese, shredded
  • Cilantro and onion, chopped

Steps

Birria

  1. Season meat on all sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a dutch oven or high walled frying pan over medium high heat with 1 tablespoon of neutral cooking oil. Sear the beef on all sides.
  3. Lower the heat to medium. Add the oregano, cumin, chili powder, and ground cloves, and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add the TOSTITOS® Chunky Salsa, broth, cinnamon, and bay leaf. Bring to a simmer, lower heat to lowest setting and partially cover with lid.
  5. Simmer for 1-2 hours or until the beef is falling apart. Allow to cool before shredding the beef and adding back into the sauce.

Quesabirria Bites

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Line a nonstick or parchment lined baking sheet with TOSTITOS® Scoops!®, fill each chip with beef mixture and top with cheese.
  3. Bake until the cheese is melted, top with cilantro and chopped white onion and enjoy!
