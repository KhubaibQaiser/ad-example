French Fries Au Gratin

These fries will blow your socks off.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 small carton heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup ricotta
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • 3 potatoes, peeled, boiled and cut into french fries

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 390°F.
  2. In a bowl, mix the heavy whipping cream, ricotta, milk and Parmesan. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg. Stir to combine.
