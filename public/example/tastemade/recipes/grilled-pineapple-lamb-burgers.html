Grilled Pineapple Lamb Burgers

Fire up the grill! Chef Derrell Smith is showing you how to make juicy lamb burgers, topped with charred pineapple and creamy goat cheese.

By derrell smith

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons ricotta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
  • 1 tablespoon diced white onion
  • 2 teaspoons worcestershire sauce
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 pounds ground lamb
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 pineapple, peeled, cored and sliced crosswise into 1/2-inch rings
  • 6 brioche hamburger buns, toasted
  • 6 ounces goat cheese, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat a large grill pan over high heat.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the egg, mustard, ricotta cheese, parsley, onion, Worcestershire sauce and salt. Add the lamb and use your hands or a large fork to combine. Form the lamb into 6 pieces patties. Brush both sides of each patty with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
