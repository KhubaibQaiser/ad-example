Tofu Chicken McNuggets

Crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside, these tofu nuggets with dijon honey mustard may just blow your mind.

By jerrelle guy

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr 20 min
Cook Time: 6 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • Block extra-firm tofu, cut into 14 cubes, and trimmed into nugget shapes
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 5 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 3 tablespoons arrowroot starch or cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon white miso paste
  • 1 splash water
  • Oil, for frying
  • 1/4 cup country mustard
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 1 pinch salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Drain the tofu block, then pat it dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture. Cut it into 14 pieces. Cut the pieces into nugget shapes if you like.
  2. Lay them out on a sheet pan lined with paper towels, and sprinkle with salt on both sides. Cover tofu with a couple more paper towels, place another sheet pan on top, then place something heavy on top of the sheet pan to help press out extra water. Let it sit for 20 minutes or so.
