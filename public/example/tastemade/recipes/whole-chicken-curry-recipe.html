Whole Chicken Curry

A curried take on your classic roast chicken! A great way to spice up your Sunday roast.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 20 min
Servings: 5
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • For chicken marination
  • 3 heaped tablespoons yoghurt
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 123 1/2 ounces whole chicken, skinless with slits cut into it
  • To make the curry
  • 5 tablespoons ghee/oil
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 2 inches cinnamon stick
  • 6 small onions, roughly diced
  • 3 teaspoons garlic paste
  • 3 teaspoons ginger paste
  • 5 tablespoons tomato passata
  • 2 bird eye green chillies, finely chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 handful fresh coriander, roughly chopped

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven at 390°F. Now combine the yoghurt and spices listed under “marination” in a bowl. Rub this marination all over the chicken and refrigerate for 4 hours.
  2. Heat ghee/oil in an ovenproof pan. Add bay leaf, cardamom and cinnamon. Then add the onions and cook until soft.
