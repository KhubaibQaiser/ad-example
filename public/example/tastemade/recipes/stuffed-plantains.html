Stuffed Plantains

Laz brings some flava to our day with roasted plantains stuffed with ground Italian sausage and topped with queso fresco and cilantro. Yum!

By lazarus lynch

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 2
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

  • 2 whole ripe yellow yellow plantains, (slightly dark-brown-spotted skin, preferably)
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • Kosher salt
  • 4 italian sausages, casing removed and crumbled
  • 1/4 cup chipotle in adobo sauce
  • 1/2 cup queso blanco
  • 2 tablespoons freshly chopped cilantro
  • Tabasco sauce or green chili hot sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven at 400Â°F.
  2. Peel plantains and place on a baking sheet and brush with oil. Sprinkle with salt. Roast the plantains until it starts to brown, about 45 minutes.
