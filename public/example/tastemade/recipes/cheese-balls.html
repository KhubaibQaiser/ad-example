Cheese Balls

Pão de Queijo, or Brazilian cheese bread, is like a delicious chewy cheese puff.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 18
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons oregano
  • Bread crumb
  • Oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the parmesan and the mozzarella.
  2. Add eggs and mix again.
