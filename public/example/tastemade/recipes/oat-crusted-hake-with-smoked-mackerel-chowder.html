Oat-Crusted Hake with Smoked Mackerel Chowder

This fish-forward dish features crusted hake and a mackerel chowder.

By Numra Siddiqui

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

If These Meals Could Talk

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

For the Hake

  • 600 grams hake fillets, skin left on
  • 1 egg white
  • 30 grams jumbo oats
  • 10 grams coriander seeds
  • 30 grams sesame seeds
  • Salt

For the Chowder

  • 50 grams butter
  • 1 garlic clove, bashed with the side of a knife
  • 1 x thyme sprig
  • 1 onion, peeled and chopped
  • 250 milliliters milk
  • 50 milliliters double (heavy) cream
  • 2 x fillets smoked mackerel
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon

For the Vinaigrette

  • 100 milliliters white wine vinegar
  • 100 milliliters vegetable oil
  • 100 milliliters extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1/4 shallot, peeled and finely diced
  • 1 thyme sprig, leaves picked
  • 4 grams sugar
  • 1 pinch salt

For the Spinach

  • 500 grams spinach
  • 30 grams butter

Steps

  1. To make the hake, preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Toast the coriander seeds in a dry frying pan until fragrant.
