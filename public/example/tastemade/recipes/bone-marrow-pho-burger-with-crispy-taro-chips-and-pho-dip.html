Bone Marrow Pho Burger with Crispy Taro Chips and Pho Dip

Savor the exquisite Bone Marrow Pho Burger paired with crispy taro chips and a pho-inspired dipping sauce, combining rich flavors and textures for a gourmet culinary adventure.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 4
Hard
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, cut into matchsticks
  • 1 quart high quality beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rock sugar
  • 1 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 big bone marrows
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin
  • 8 ounces very thinly sliced rib eye steak (korean style)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup thai basil
  • 1/2 cup minced cilantro
  • 4 large brioche buns
  • Taro chip, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat a medium sized pot over medium heat and add the oil.
  2. Saute the onion and ginger until soft, about 5–7 minutes.
