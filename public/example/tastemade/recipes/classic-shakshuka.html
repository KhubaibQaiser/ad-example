Classic Shakshuka

Chef Mac Elliot Jarvis serves up shakshuka and speaks about the kitchen being a safe space. He and Ingrid cook comfort food and chat about him using cooking to find confidence in transitioning.

By Ingrid Nilsen

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Cooking With Pride

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Shakshuka

  • 1 each onions, diced
  • 5 eaches garlic, minced
  • 1 cup jalapeños (canned), chopped
  • 1 each fresno chilies, minced
  • 1 each red bell peppers, diced
  • 32 ounces fire roasted tomatoes, puréed
  • 4 tablespoons cilantro, stems saved/leaves chopped
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 4 eggs

Pita Bread

  • 2 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Purée canned tomatoes with cilantro stems in a blender until smooth and set aside.
  2. Sweat in a large wide pot (12" is preferred) with oil the onions, garlic, chili and bell pepper. It takes about 15 minutes until all of the vegetables are melty, almost caramelized.
