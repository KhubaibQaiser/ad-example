Mofongo

This Puerto Rican dish stars fried plantains topped with crispy pork skins.

By Monti Carlo

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 very green plantains
  • Frying oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup pork cracklins, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons chicken stock, hot
  • High-quality olive oil

Steps

  1. Peel the plantain. Cut both ends of the plantain. Score the skin lengthwise 3 times. Work your fingers under the skin and peel it off. Cut in 1-inch pieces.
  2. Soak in salted water for 15 minutes
