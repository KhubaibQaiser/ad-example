Clam Chowder

Enjoy a hearty bowl of clam chowder with minimal effort thanks to this easy slow cooker recipe!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 6 hr 50 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces bacon, sliced
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons minced fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 3/4 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream, divided
  • 1 cup bottled clam juice
  • 13 1/2 ounces chopped clams, drained and juices reserved
  • 4 medium red potatoes, chopped into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • Sliced chive, for garnish
  • Chopped parsley, for garnish
  • Oyster cracker, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add bacon and cook while stirring until crisp. Remove to slow cooker. To the skillet, add the onion, celery, garlic and thyme. Cook while stirring until the onion is translucent. Remove to the slow cooker.
  2. To the slow cooker, stir in the milk, 1/4 cup heavy cream, all the clam juice, potatoes, salt and pepper. Cook on low for 6 to 8 hours, until the potatoes are tender.
