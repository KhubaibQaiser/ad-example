Salted Caramel Tea Cakes

A salted caramel twist on a classic treat

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 6 digestive biscuits or 12 mini similar biscuits
  • 200 grams giant marshmallows
  • 150 grams dark chocolate
  • Salted caramel sauce
  • 60 milliliters water
  • 200 grams light brown sugar
  • 100 milliliters double cream
  • 30 grams unsalted butter
  • 6 grams sea salt flakes

Steps

  1. Start by making the caramel sauce. Add the sugar to a pan with the water. Place on a medium heat, and leave for the sugar to melt away and it starts to bubble. You don't mean to stir the mix. Once the sugar has darken in color slightly stir in the butter. Take off the high and stir through the cream. Set this aside in the fridge to cool. There will be enough for you to have it another day too.
  2. Temper the chocolate and leave to cool slightly. When as a pourable consistency but not too runny, spoon inside the domed silicone moulds. Let it cool slightly so the chocolate doesn't all sink to the bottom of the mould.
