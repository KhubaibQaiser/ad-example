Ingredients

Potato Pizza

  • 6 medium potatoes
  • Olive oil, for cooking
  • Salt and pepper, for seasoning
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 12–14 slices slices of mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 5–7 smoked provolone cheeses
  • 1 cup tomato sauce

Toppings

  • For toppings:
  • 5–8 pepperoni slices
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. On a tray, place the potatoes, and rub with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
