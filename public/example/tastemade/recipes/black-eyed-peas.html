Black Eyed Peas

Ring in the New Year with Chef Derrell Smith's traditional black-eyed pea recipe, meant to bring good luck, wealth and fortune in the new year!

By Derrell Smith

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Mad Good Food

Prep Time: 3 hr
Cook Time: 3 hr
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces black-eyed peas, rinsed
  • 8 ounces bacon, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, cook chopped bacon, until fat is rendered. Add minced garlic and saute until lightly brown.
  2. Add peas to the pan along with all dried seasonings (hold on to the salt & pepper till the end), stir together, and add water to cover peas.
