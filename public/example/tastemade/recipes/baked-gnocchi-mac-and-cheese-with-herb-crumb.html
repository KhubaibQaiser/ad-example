Baked Gnocchi Mac and Cheese with Herb Crumb

Basic macaroni and cheese gets a luxurious upgrade when you replace noodles with gnocchi.

By Conor Maynard

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Recipe for Love

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 50g Butter
  • 50g Plain flour
  • 500ml Milk
  • 100g Mature Cheddar
  • 50g Grated mozzarella
  • 2tsp Dijon mustard
  • 120g Thick cut Pancetta
  • 600g Gnocchi
  • 1 Ciabatta roll
  • 10g Basil
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 30g Parmesan
  • 2tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Pre heat your oven to 200c, 180c fan, gas mark 6.
  2. Slice up the pancetta into thin slices, halve any large pieces. Heat a large frying pan on a medium - high heat.
