Tomato Rice

Try Tomato Rice featuring savory garlic, onions, tomato paste and KRAFT Garlic Aioli.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups long grain rice​
  • 3 tablespoons oil​
  • ½ onion, minced​
  • 1 clove garlic, minced​
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste​
  • Salt and pepper, to taste​
  • KRAFT Garlic Aioli​
  • 1 scallion, sliced into rings

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill.​
  2. ​In a cast iron pan on the grill over high heat, add oil and rice toast for 4-5 minutes. ​
  3. ​Add onion and garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add tomato paste, season with salt and pepper, add 2.5 cups of water and cook for 10-15 minutes, covered with a lid.
  5. Once finished top with KRAFT Garlic Aioli.
