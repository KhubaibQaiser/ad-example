Pilaf Rice

This savory rice gets flavored with cumin and topped with crispy shallots.

By Einat Admony

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Pilaf Rice

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 3 cups boiling water

Crispy Shallots

  • 6 medium (round) shallots, very thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup rice flour
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear. Drain and let dry for a couple of minutes.
  2. Pour the oil in non-stick pan and heat at medium heat.
