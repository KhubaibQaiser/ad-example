Alien Pasta

Made with veggies and sausage, this tasty spaghetti dish is out of this world.

(1)Recipe Notes (1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 mini sausages
  • 1 eggplant
  • 3 eryngii mushrooms
  • 70 grams spaghetti
  • 250 grams tomato sauce
  • 5 grams bouillon granules
  • 300 milliliters water
  • 100 grams mozzarella cheese
  • Pepper
  • Basil pesto
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut the sausages, eggplant and eryngii into chunks.
  2. Break spaghetti in half and insert 10 sticks of spaghetti into each vegetable chunk.
