Best Ever Pavlova

We've taken things back to basics with this classic fruit pavlova.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 3 hr
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 8
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Meringue

  • 4 egg whites
  • 250 grams caster sugar
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon cornflour

Decoration

  • 500 millilitres double cream
  • 2 tablespoons icing sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla bean paste
  • 500 grams mixed summer fruit: a mix of raspberries, blackberries, strawberries, peach slices, redcurrants
  • Mint leaves, to decorate
  • Icing sugar, to dust

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 140C / 120C fan. Cut a piece of baking paper to fit a large baking tray. Use a pencil to draw around a 22 centimetre plate onto the paper.
  2. Lightly grease the baking tray and stick the paper - pencil side down - onto the tray. Put to one side.
