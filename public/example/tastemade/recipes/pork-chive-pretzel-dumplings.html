Pretzel Pork and Chive Dumplings

Ground pork belly filled dumpling wrappers are fried to pretzel perfection and served with a tahini garlic dipping sauce

By Dale Talde

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 2 hr 40 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Pretzel Pork Filling

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • ¾ cup thinly sliced chives
  • 1 ½ pounds ground pork belly
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped ginger
  • 1 tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Asian sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp chicken bouillon
  • ½ tsp ground white pepper

Chive Dumplings

  • 36 wonton wrappers “shanghai style”
  • 4 quarts water
  • 3 tbsp baking soda
  • 3 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 stick unsalted butter, melted
  • Pretzel salt

Tahini Mustard Sauce

  • ½ cup hot Chinese mustard
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp Asian sesame oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a medium pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chives and cook, stirring until they have just wilted.
