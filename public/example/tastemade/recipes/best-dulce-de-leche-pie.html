Dulce De Leche Pie

This indulgent dulce de leche pie will satisfy all your sweet cravings

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 4 hr
Servings: 8
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Crust

  • 175 grams butter, melted
  • 150 grams pretzels, crushed into a fine crumb
  • 38 grams demerara sugar

Custard

  • 1 vanilla pod, split down the middle
  • 100 grams caster sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 40 grams cornflour
  • 200 milliliters dulche de leche

Assembly

  • Crust
  • Custard
  • 1 banana, sliced lengthways
  • 50 grams caster sugar

Steps

  1. Mix together the pretzels, butter and sugar and line the base and sides of your pie dish.
