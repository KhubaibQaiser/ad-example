Diwali Party Snacks

Hosting a party to celebrate Diwali, the Hindu festival of lights? Try these non-traditional snacks inspired by classic Indian dishes, like Italian arancini served with coriander chutney or Mexican spiced Bhel Puri.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 6
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Arancini

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • 1 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 cup chopped dill leaves
  • 2 tablespoons chopped mint leaves
  • 3 tablespoons cheddar
  • 1/2 cup water, if required for cooking
  • Salt, to taste
  • 15–20 raisins
  • 2 tablespoons corn-starch
  • 3 tablespoons cold water
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil for deep frying

Gravy

  • 6 tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato purée
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon kashmiri red chilli powder
  • 1 inch ginger, roughly chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon kasoori methi / dried fenugreek leaves
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cream

Tandoori patatas bravas with Mirchi mayo

  • 8 large potatoes, peeled and cut into small chunks (bite sized)
  • 4 tablespoons tandoori masala powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 4–5 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 6–8 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 4 tablespoons greek yoghurt
  • 4 tablespoons green chilli pickle (readymade)
  • Coriander chutney (readymade) or made at home
  • Red onion slice, for garnish

Mexican Bhel Puri

  • 2 cups puffed rice
  • 2 packets spicy doritos / nachos
  • 2 medium potatoes, chopped in small cubes, deep fried or sautéed
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons tinned sweet corn
  • 1 red pepper and 1 green pepper, finely chopped
  • 3 tablespoons pomegranate seeds
  • 1 south american variety, chopped
  • 2–3 red and green chillies, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons jalapeno peppers, chopped
  • 1 bunch spring onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • Salt, to taste

Gulab Jamun Shrikhand Parfait

  • 8 small gulab jamuns (readymade from asda), cut into small pieces
  • 15 1/4 fluid ounces greek yoghurt
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 teaspoon rose water
  • 1/4 cup pistachios – finely chopped
  • 1 pinch salt
  • Rose petal, to garnish

Steps

  1. Cook rice with plenty of water. Drain. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan, add the onions and sauté until light brown.
