Coffee Grounds Cookies

Time to put your coffee grounds to use and make your new favorite cookies!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 18
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 180 grams bread flour
  • 2 1/2 grams baking soda
  • 100 grams granulated sugar
  • 100 grams dark brown sugar
  • 40 grams espresso grounds
  • 80 grams extra virgin olive oil
  • 45 milliliters hazelnut milk
  • 5 grams miso
  • 5 milliliters vanilla extract
  • 140 grams dark chocolate

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flours, baking soda and 3 grams salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugars, espresso grounds, olive oil, hazelnut milk, miso and vanilla extract until smooth.
