Salmon and Rice Bites

Salmon and Rice Bites are a delicious and healthy snack or appetizer made with cooked salmon, rice, green onions, and seasonings, formed into bite-sized balls and baked until crispy and golden.

By derrell smith

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Salmon and Rice Bites

  • 3 sheets nori paper
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked sushi rice (or basmati)
  • 1 pound salmon, skin removed + cubed small
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil (or olive oil)
  • 1 tablespoon low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 2 scallions, diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt, to taste

Toppings

  • 1/2 avocado, diced small
  • 2 teaspoons black sesame seeds

Spicy Mayo

  • 1/4 cup kewpie mayo
  • 2 teaspoons sriracha sauce
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F.
  2. Grab a scissor and cut your nori sheets into 4 equal squares.
