Raw Corn Chips & Guacamole

This healthy corn chip recipe uses a dehydrator, or a conventional oven, for the ultimate chips & dip combination.

By Laura Miller

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Raw. Vegan. Not Gross.

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 4 hr
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Raw Corn Chips

  • 1 1/2 cups flax meal
  • 3 cups corn
  • 2 yellow bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Guacamole

  • 4 ripe avocados
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 lime
  • Salt
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1/4 cup cilantro

Steps

  1. Grind flax in high-speed blender add to mixing bowl.
  2. Add all remaining ingredients into a food processor and blend.
