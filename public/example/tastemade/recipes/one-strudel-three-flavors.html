One Strudel, Three Flavors

Once you learn how to make strudel dough from scratch, your options are endless! Flavor this crispy, buttery dessert with sour cherry and cheese, apple, or Nutella.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

Dough

  • 6 sheets phyllo dough, thawed if frozen
  • 1 stick unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup of raw sugar

Sour Cherry Cheese

  • 1/2 package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup cherry jam

Apple

  • 1 granny smith apple, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon dried fine breadcrumbs

Nutella

  • 1/2 cup nutella
  • 2 tablespoons each of raisins, dates, pecans and walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Brush 1 sheet phyllo with butter and sprinkle over a small amount of the raw sugar.
