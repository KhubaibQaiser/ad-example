Crab Fries & Spicy Pimento Cheez Wiz

Channel the flavors of Philly with this satisfying snack. Homemade French fries topped with fresh crab are slathered in a spicy pimento cheese spread for an ideal munchies meal.

By Ingrid Nilsen

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Cooking With Pride

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 9 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Crab Fries

  • 4 russet potatoes
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 4 teaspoons of old bay seasoning
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of chopped parsley
  • 1 can fresh lump crab meat
  • Vegetable oil

Spicy Pimento Cheez Wiz

  • 3/4 cup cheese wiz cheese dip
  • 4 ounces pimento peppers (drained and chopped)
  • 2 jalapeño peppers (seeded and finely chopped)
  • 3 green onions (chopped)s
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Peel and slice the potatoes into 1/4 thick planks.
  2. Add the potatoes to to a large bowl filled with cold water & the vinegar for 30 mins.
