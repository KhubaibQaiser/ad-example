Merguez Hot Dog

Meet the standout dish at your next barbecue: Merguez sausage hot dogs. Grilled to perfection and topped with a tangy yogurt sauce and crispy fried onions, this easy recipe will be on rotation all summer long.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Hummus sauce

  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1/2 clove garlic
  • 1/4 cup tahini paste
  • Salt, to taste
  • Olive oil, to taste
  • Ice cold water, as needed
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice

Yogurt sauce

  • 3 1/2 fluid ounces greek yogurt
  • Cilantro leaf
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Fried onion

  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 teaspoon ground red chili
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground garlic
  • Salt, to taste

Merguez sausages

  • Hummus sauce
  • Yogurt sauce
  • Parsley, to taste
  • Fried onion
  • Red onion finely diced
  • Green sprout
  • Hot dog bun

Steps

  1. The fast way is to add a little water and olive oil to an already made hummus, to get the texture of a sauce.
  2. The delicious way is to make it yourself. Place the chickpeas in a food processor, while processing add the rest of the ingredients.
