Crispy Potato Rosti

Forget bacon and eggs — your next brunch calls for Chef Frankie Celenza's crispy potato rosti, a Swiss-inspired crispy potato pancake with a sour cream dill sauce.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Rosti

  • 3 pounds russet potatoes
  • 2 teaspoons fresh thyme leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flake
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 6 tablespoons butter

Dill Sauce

  • 1 garlic clove, grated
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped dill
  • 1 tablespoon minced chives
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Scrub the potatoes clean. Grate the potatoes using a box grater. Wrap the grated potatoes in a clean kitchen towel and squeeze to release excess water. Add the potatoes to a large bowl and stir in the thyme leaves, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper.
