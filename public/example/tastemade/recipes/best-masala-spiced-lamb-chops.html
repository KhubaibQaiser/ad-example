Masala Spiced Lamb Chops

These lamb chops are lip-smackingly good!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 3 1/2 fluid ounces yogurt
  • Squeeze lemon juice
  • 8 lamb chops

Steps

  1. Mix marinade together and coat chops, leave to marinade for 20 minutes minimum.
  2. Preheat oven to 355°F.
