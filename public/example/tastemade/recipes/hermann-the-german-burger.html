Hermann-The-German Burger

Stacked high with bacon and a potato-fried egg, this burger is sure to keep you full all morning long.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 4 buns
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3 1/4 tablespoons mustard
  • 7 ounces sauerkraut
  • 8 slices bacon
  • 4 boiled potatoes
  • 4 slices allgäuer mountain cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 pickle slices

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 285°F.
  2. Season the ground beef with salt, pepper and 1/2 portion of mustard. Form into patties the size of the buns. Pan-cook, flipping to cook both sides. Add a slice of cheese to top and keep warm in the oven.
