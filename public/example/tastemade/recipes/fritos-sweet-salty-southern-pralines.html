Fritos® Sweet & Salty Southern Pralines

Classic Southern pralines get a salty twist with the help of crunchy Fritos.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 16
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 tablespoons butter, room temp and cut into cubes
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 cup pecan halves, toasted
  • 1 1/4 cup Fritos® Original Corn Chips, gently crushed
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Line a sheet tray with parchment and spray with cooking spray.
  2. Add both sugars, baking soda, and heavy cream to a medium heavy bottomed saucepan and heat over medium heat while stirring.
  3. Continue cooking while stirring until the mixture reaches 236 degrees Fahrenheit.
  4. Turn off the heat and stir in the butter, vanilla, salt, pecans, and Fritos® Original Corn Chips.
  5. Remove from heat and continue stirring, until it cools and thickens slightly.
  6. Drop tablespoon size spoonfuls of the mixture onto the prepared sheet tray.
  7. Allow the pralines to set and harden for at least one hour.
