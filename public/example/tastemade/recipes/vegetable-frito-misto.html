Vegetable Frito Misto

Fennel, onion, and eggplant get fried to crispy perfection for this savory appetizer.

By tiffani faison

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 4 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 237 grams fennel, layers separated and cut into petals
  • 122 grams spanish onion, layers separated and cut into petals
  • 237 grams eggplant, cut into dredge
  • 230 milliliters buttermilk
  • 300 grams wondra flour
  • 5 grams ground coriander
  • 5 grams ground fennel seed
  • 4 grams ground szechuan peppercorns
  • 4 grams ground dry thai chili
  • Fennel frond

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot 2/3 third full with canola oil. Using a thermometer bring to 190°C.
  2. Combine the eggplant, onion, and fennel.
