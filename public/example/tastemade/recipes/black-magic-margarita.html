Black Magic Margarita

If you want a tequila recipe that goes beyond the classics, grab a bottle of smooth, rich Tequila Mi CAMPO and craft up this colorful twist on the classic margarita.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Servings: 1
Easy
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Blanco Tequila Mi CAMPO
  • 1 oz triple sec
  • 1 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • ½ oz red vermouth
  • A few dashes bitters
  • ½ oz Simple Syrup or agave
  • ¼ teaspoon activated charcoal
  • 1 teaspoon Tajin/chili or black lava salt and lime salt
  • Lime wedge
  • Edible gold leaf
  • Orange Slice

Steps

  1. Sprinkle the salt on a saucer, run a lime wedge around the rim of a tumbler and dip to coat in the salt/chili.
  2. Add the cocktail ingredients to a shaker filled with ice and shake. Double strain into a tumbler filled with ice. Garnish with an orange slice and lime wedge.
