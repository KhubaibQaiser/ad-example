Fruit Bacon

Using fruit to make bacon? Believe it! This vegan bacon recipe tries everything from coconut, watermelon, jackfruit and banana peels for the best fake bacon results.

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Fruit Bacon

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon liquid smoke
  • 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Coconut

  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut chips

Watermelon

  • 5 ounces watermelon

Jackfruit

  • 5 ounces canned jackfruit in brine

Banana Peels

  • 3 whole banana peels
