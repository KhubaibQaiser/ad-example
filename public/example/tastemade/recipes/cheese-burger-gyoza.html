Cheese Burger Gyoza

All the flavors of a delicious cheeseburger are stuffed inside this easy homemade gyoza, which is made using store-bought gyoza wrappers. The best appetizer for a party!

By julie nolke

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 6
Average
Â :

Ingredients

Cheese Burger Gyoza

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup dill pickle, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup raw onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper
  • 12 squares processed cheese
  • Gyoza wrapper

Mac Sauce

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons sweet pickle relish
  • 1/2 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon french dressing
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon finely minced white onion

Steps

  1. Place the ground beef in a medium-sized bowl and to it add the dill pickle, raw onion, salt & pepper and 3 tablespoons of the Mac sauce. Stir the ingredients until well incorporated.
  2. Rip up the squares of processed cheese and place one smaller square into the center of one of your gyoza wrappers.
