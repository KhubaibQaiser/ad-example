Focaccia Three Ways

Homemade focaccia is easier than you think. Chef Julie Nolke is sharing her foolproof recipe, plus three ways to customize your focaccia: rosemary, pesto, and balsamic!

By julie nolke

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 cups lukewarm water
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons dried yeast
  • 1 3/4 fluid ounces olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 5 cups all purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons salt
  • Sea salt flake
  • Rosemary leaf
  • 2 tablespoons jarred pesto
  • 6 cherry tomatos, halved
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Smoked salmon

Steps

  1. Combine the sugar and 1 cup of the water. Sprinkle over the yeast and set aside for 5 minutes to let the yeast bloom and become foamy.
  2. Add remaining water, olive oil and flour. Mix with a wooden spoon until it is too difficult to stir and then use your hands to continue to mix.
