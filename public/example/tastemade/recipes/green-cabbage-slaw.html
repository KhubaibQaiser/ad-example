Green Cabbage Slaw

This fresh, spicy slaw featues peanuts, serrano chile, and lime.

By Daisy Ryan

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 head green cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 white onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup raw peanuts, chopped
  • 1/2 serrano chile, seeded and minced
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a bowl, toss to coat. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
