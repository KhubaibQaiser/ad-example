Chicken and Beer Steamed Rice

Take basic chicken and rice to another level with a can of dark beer.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 360 milliliters uncooked rice
  • 350 milliliters dark beer
  • 100 grams chicken thigh
  • 30 grams edamame
  • 30 grams corn
  • 20 grams cheese
  • 5 milliliters instant japanese soup stock (dashi)
  • 10 grams butter
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cut off fat from chicken thigh and set aside.
  2. Thoroughly wash and drain rice.
