Noah's Chopped Salad

Noah's take on the classic Chopped Salad.

By Noah Galuten

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Don't Panic Pantry

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Salad Mix

  • 2 handfuls each of chopped radicchio, arugula and iceberg or romaine
  • 1 handful of diced vegetables
  • 1 hearty pinch of finely chopped parsley leaves
  • Some diced, blanched and shocked green vegetables, like green beans or potato
  • 1 can beans, drained -- red kidney, cannellini, garbanzo, pinto -- any bean you like!
  • A 1 small petite handful of diced salami (optional), or even mortadella could be nice

Salad Dressing

  • 1 garlic clove, grated
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 pinch of oregano
  • 1 dab mustard
  • 1 squirt of honey
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine the salad ingredients in a bowl! Combine the dressing ingredients in another bowl or container, then stir it well and season it to taste with salt.
  2. Toss as much dressing as you want with as much salad as you want. Place in a bowl (ideally a big goofy cereal bowl). Top with cheese and black pepper.
