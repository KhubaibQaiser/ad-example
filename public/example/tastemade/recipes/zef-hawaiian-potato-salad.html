ZEF Hawaiian Potato Salad

This potato salad features three types of potatoes, salty bacon, and a vinegary dressing.

By Logan Sandoval

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound baby purple potatoes
  • 1/2 pound yukon gold potatoes, large dice
  • 1/2 pound sweet potatoes, large dice
  • 1 pound slab
  • 1/3 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup japanese mayonnaise
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 bunch chives, chopped

Steps

  1. Dice bacon into 1/4-inch lardons, place in a dry medium saute pan over medium heat, and render bacon until it becomes crisp (8 minutes).
  2. Strain rendered fat from bacon and reserve both.
