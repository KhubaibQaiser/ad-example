Queso Dip Nachos

Melty queso dip tops crispy tortilla chips in this classic nacho recipe.

By erwan heussaff

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Queso dip

  • 1 block velveeta
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1 can green chilies

Nachos

  • Tortilla chip
  • 1/2 cup black beans
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 7 ounces grilled chicken, pulled and shredded
  • 1 cup pico de gallo
  • 1/2 cup guacamole

Steps

  1. In a medium sauce pot over medium heat, add cream.
  2. Once warm, add Velveeta and green chilies.
