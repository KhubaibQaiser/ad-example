Lettuce and Tofu Balls

When you're craving a quick, easy and healthy meal, make these lettuce-wrapped chicken and tofu balls.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 8 min
Servings: 7
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 10 1/2 ounces tofu
  • 7 ounces ground chicken
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 teaspoon instant dashi powder
  • 3 tablespoons potato starch
  • 8 lettuces
  • 1 tablespoon potato starch
  • Minced ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Wash the lettuce and drain well of excess moisture.
  2. Microwave tofu for 2 minutes at 500°F. Then place on a flat plate and let it rest for 20 minutes. Drain of excess liquid. Mince the ginger and set aside.
