Crispy Squid Montadito with Two Aiolis

This classic Spanish tapas dish features crispy squid, squid ink aioli, and saffron aioli.

By jamie lauren

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound squid, small
  • 1/3 pound flour, all-purpose
  • 1/3 pound flour, rice
  • 1/3 pound cornstarch
  • 4 eggs, yolks
  • 8 cloves garlic, peeled, minced
  • 2 lemons
  • 1 tablespoon saffron
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup grapeseed oil
  • 2 small brioche hot dog buns
  • 1 can smoked paprika, sweet
  • 1 tablespoon squid ink
  • 1 quart buttermilk
  • 1 jar pipparra peppers
  • 1 gallon fryer oil
  • Kosher salt/pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat fryer oil to 350°F in a skillet over medium heat. Clean squid by removing the beaks and the insides, then cut into rings, separating the tentacles from the body. Soak squid in buttermilk for 20 minutes and set aside.
  2. Make Aioli Base: In a small bowl, combine 2 eggs of egg yolks with 1 tablespoon of dijon and whisk until foamy. Slowly whisk in 1 cup of olive oil and 1 cup of grapeseed oil until an emulsion begins to form. Add lemon juice to thin as needed. Fold in 4 cloves of garlic and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. Divide evenly into two small bowls.
