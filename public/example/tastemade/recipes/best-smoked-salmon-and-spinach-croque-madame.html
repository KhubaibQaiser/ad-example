Smoked Salmon and Spinach Croque Madame

The OG grilled cheese gets a delicious twist

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • Makes 1 great sandwich
  • 40 grams unsalted butter
  • 40 grams baby spinach
  • 1 dash double cream
  • Seasoning
  • 1 handful of meltable handful of meltable cheese, like grated mozzarella, like grated mozzarella
  • 2 good pieces smoked salmon
  • 2 pieces white bread
  • 1 egg
  • 30 grams parmesan, grated

Steps

  1. Place half the butter into a pan on a medium heat. Once melted, add the spinach and stir so the butter covers the leaves, and it begins to wilt. Quickly add in the cream and stir, add in the cheese, season and take off the heat. In the same pan add the remaining butter and place on a medium heat.
  2. Place one piece of bread in the pan to start to crisp and then you can build the sandwich with an even layer of spinach mix and smoked salmon. Close the sandwich with the other piece of bread and top with the grated parmesan.
