Meatballs Makhani

Spaghetti and meatballs but not as you know it!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 8 3/4 ounces pork mince
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon chilli powder

Makhani Sauce

  • 4 tablespoons oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 inches cinnamon stick
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 4 tablespoons tomato passata
  • 2 tablespoons cashew paste
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cream
  • 1 handful fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • 1 bird eye green chilli, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Place all meatball ingredients in a bowl and combine. Slightly wet your hands and form small golf sized balls. Keep aside.
