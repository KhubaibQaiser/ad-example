Ceviche and Patacones

Patacones, or fried green plantains, are a popular Latin American recipe that pair well with fresh ceviche. Use the crispy plantains to scoop up a bite of cold ceviche — made with the best quality fish you can find! — and enjoy all summer long.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Ingredients

Leche de tigre

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 small piece of ginger
  • 1/2 piece celery stalk
  • 1 chili pepper
  • 1/4 onion
  • Cilantro leaves and stalk, to taste
  • Fish trimming
  • Salt, to taste
  • 6 limes
  • Water or ice to balance the acidity

Patacones

  • 1 green plantain
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Salt, to taste

Ceviche

  • 300 grams white fish fillet
  • 1/2 red onion
  • Cilantro
  • 1 chili pepper
  • 1 lime
  • Leche de tigre
  • Patacone

Steps

  1. Put the vegetables and the fish trimmings in a bowl and add salt generously so the internal water comes out of the vegetables.
  2. Pound lightly so the flavors start mixing.
