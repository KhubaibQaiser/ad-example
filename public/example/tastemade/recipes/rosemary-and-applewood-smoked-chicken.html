Rosemary and Applewood Smoked Chicken

You don't need any fancy gadgets to make a perfect smoked chicken! This recipe relies on tinfoil and a wire rack to achieve a great smoky flavor, and you can experiment with any other herbs you may have on hand.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 3 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 1–4 pounds chicken, spatchcocked
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup soaked applewood chips
  • 6 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 1 tablespoon black peppercorns

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, grated garlic, and chili flakes. Stir to combine.
