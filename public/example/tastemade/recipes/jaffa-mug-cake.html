Jaffa Mug Cake

British Jaffa cakes are transformed in this easy microwave mug cake recipe.

By Arron Crascall

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Gamer Snacks

Prep Time: 3 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup self raising flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Zest 1/2 orange
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 jaffa cakes, chopped up
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Begin by mixing the dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Add the wet ingredients and mix thoroughly until no lumps remain.
