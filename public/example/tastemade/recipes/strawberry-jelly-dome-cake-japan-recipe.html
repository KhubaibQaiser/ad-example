Strawberry Jelly Dome Cake (Japan)

Delicious japanese dome cake

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr 40 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 45 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 90 grams sour cream
  • 150 grams yoghurt
  • 200 milliliters of fresh cream
  • 50 grams granulated sugar
  • 12 grams of gelatine
  • 30 milliliters water
  • 200 grams strawberry
  • 30 grams of granulated sugar
  • 5 grams of gelatine
  • 15 milliliters of water
  • 4 strawberries
  • 20 grams of blueberries
  • 50 grams biscuits

Steps

  1. Sprinkle the gelatine in water and let it bloom for 5 minutes.
  2. Finely chop strawberries and crush the biscuits.
