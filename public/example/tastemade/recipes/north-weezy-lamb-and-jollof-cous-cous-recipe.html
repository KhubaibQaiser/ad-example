North Weezy Lamb & Jollof Cous Cous

North and West African food come together in this awesome lamb dish!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr 30 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 piece rack of lamb
  • 3 crushed garlic cloves
  • Moroccan spice medley
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 sprigs of fresh rosemary (chopped finely)
  • 2 sprigs of fresh coriander (chopped finely)
  • Jollof couscous/bulgar wheat
  • Tomato mix
  • 1 tin plum tomatoes
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 scotch bonnet
  • 2 red onions
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • (blend all the above to fine sauce)
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 sprigs rosemary
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 3 tablespoons rapeseed olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon all seasoning
  • 10 1/2 ounces couscous or bulgar
  • 2 chicken stockpots
  • Salt and pepper
  • Diced mixed pepper and spinach, to garnish

Steps

  1. Season lamb cutlet moderately with herbs and spices and leave to marinade in fridge for 20mins to several hours. Preheat oven to 390°F.
  2. Bring out lamb to allow meat to come to room temperature.
