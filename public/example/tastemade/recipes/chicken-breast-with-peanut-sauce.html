Chicken Breast With Peanut Sauce

Rich and creamy, this chicken is totally dreamy!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Chicken Breast

  • 2 chicken breasts, skin off
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon thai red curry paste
  • 1/2 can coconut milk (save the remaining ½ for the
  • Add 1/2 teaspoon of chopped onions to the mixture then cook and stir for 4 minutes
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Sesame oil, for frying
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, for taste

Peanut Sauce

  • 1–2 inches of ginger, peeled and grated
  • 1 small garlic clove, peeled and grated
  • 1 teaspoon thai red curry paste
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter, smooth
  • 1/2 can coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon light brown sugar
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1/4 cup peanuts, roasted, salted, and roughly chopped

Garnishes

  • Coriander
  • Lime wedge
  • Red chili, sliced
  • Scallion, sliced
  • Peanut, roughly chopped
  • Cooked rice

Steps

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, mix all the ingredients for the peanut sauce together. Once well combined, add the chicken breasts.
  2. Cover with Saran wrap, and marinate for at least 30 minutes. Optional to leave the chicken marinating overnight.
