Filipino Pork Blood Stew

Dinuguan, a Filipino pork blood stew, is a comforting dish made of pork belly and aromatic spices, simmered together in a rich gravy!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups pork blood
  • 2 pounds pork butt or pork belly, cut into small chunks
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Chili pepper, to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Oil for pan
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Heat a drizzle of oil in a deep pan.
  2. Add garlic and onion and saute for a few minutes.
