Pesto Twist Tree

A festive and savory puff pastry appetizer filled with a vibrant pesto.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Pesto

  • 1/2 cup basil leaves
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup walnuts
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Puff Pastry Tree

  • 1 tomato
  • 1 package puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Lay the basil and garlic in a pile on a cutting board and season with salt. Use a sharp knife to mince the basil and smash the garlic together into a paste. Add it to a bowl and whisk in the olive oil.
  2. Very finely chop the walnuts, then add them to the basil mixture. Whisk in the parmesan cheese.
