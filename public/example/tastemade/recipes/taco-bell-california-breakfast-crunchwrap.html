Taco Bell California Breakfast Crunchwrap

Forget breakfast burritos and try this breakfast crunchwrap instead, filled with eggs, bacon, guac and more!

By zoe kelly

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 hash brown patty
  • 2 strips bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 (10-inches) tortilla
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons pico de gallo
  • 2 tablespoons guacamole
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook hash brown according to package instructions. Set aside.
  2. Over medium heat, cook the bacon strips. Remove from pan.
