Supreme Fried Rice with Shrimp, Chicken, Bacon, Pickled Raisins & Jalapenos

Fabulous fried white rice with chicken, shrimp, bacon and a bit of added sweetness and spice

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Pickled Raisins

  • 1/2 cup golden raisins
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Supreme Fried Rice with Shrimp, Chicken, Bacon, Pickled Raisins & Jalapenos

  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 cups day-old rice
  • 3 eggs, scrambled
  • 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled & deveined
  • 8 ounces raw chicken breast, diced
  • 8 strips bacon, raw
  • 1 jalapeno, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 scallions, sliced

Sauce

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat the golden raisins, white vinegar, sugar, water, salt and set aside to pickle for at least one hour.
