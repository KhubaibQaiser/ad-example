Avocado Fudgesicles

The Force is strong with these avocado pops.

By katie quinn

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 8 hr
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 6 pits removed dates
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 3 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 cup canned coconut milk (or other non-dairy milk)
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut (optional)

Steps

  1. Soak the dates for about 30 minutes in the 3/4 cup water until they soften a bit (to make them easier to blend).
  2. Then add in the remaining ingredients and blend!
