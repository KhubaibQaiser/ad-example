Charred Watermelon Salad with Grilled Halloumi, Mint, Chilis & Lime

Experience the vibrant flavors of Charred Watermelon Salad featuring grilled halloumi cheese, fresh mint, spicy chilis, and zesty lime, creating a refreshing and dynamic medley of tastes and textures.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pound halloumi
  • 2 lemons, cut in half
  • 2–3 pounds watermelon, sliced into large thick pieces
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 shallots, thinly sliced
  • 2 chilis minced
  • 1/4 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup yellow and red cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup thai basil
  • 1 cup mint leaves
  • Mini watermelon

Steps

  1. Heat the grill to medium high.
  2. Dry the halloumi very well.
