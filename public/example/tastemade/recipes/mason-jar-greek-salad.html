Mason Jar Greek Salad

This super easy Greek salad in a mason jar is perfect for lunch.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt, plus more to season the chicken
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper, plus more to season the chicken

Salad

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup grape tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, peeled and diced
  • 1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/3 cup pitted kalamata olives
  • 1/3 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 1 cup baby romaine lettuce

Steps

  1. Combine the ingredients for the dressing and then set aside.
