Grandma's Pizza

Brooklyn style grandma pizza

By Noah Galuten

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Don't Panic Pantry

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 ball grandma-style pizza dough
  • 10–12 ounces freshly grated low-moisture mozzarella
  • 1 cup quick and easy pizza sauce
  • 1 handful torn basil leaves
  • Drizzle olive oil
  • 7 grates parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the hottest temperature (ideally about 500°F).
  2. Once your dough is proofed on the counter and pliable, grease your pizza pan or sheet pan with a drizzle of olive oil.
