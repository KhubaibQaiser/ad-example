Homemade Chili Oil

Here's how to make and use your own homemade chili oil! This easy condiment recipe will make everything you cook instantly taste better.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/30/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces dried whole thai chilis
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons sichuan peppercorns
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 green cardamom pods
  • 1 whole star anise

Steps

  1. Toast peppercorns in a pan just until fragrant.
  2. Remove and grind in a mortar and pestle or spice grinder.
