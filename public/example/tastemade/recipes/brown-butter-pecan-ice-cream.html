Brown Butter Pecan Ice Cream

This ice cream captures all of the joys of a classic butter pecan scoop in a keto-friendly recipe.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 4 hr
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 13 1/2 ounces coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup swerve sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon xanthan gum
  • 1/3 cup browned butter
  • 1 tablespoon bourbon
  • 2 cups heavy cream, whipped
  • 1 cup pecans, toasted and chopped

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine coconut milk, sugar, and xanthan gum, and cook until thickened, approximately 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat, and stir in browned butter and bourbon.
