Chicken Burrito Bowls

Flavorful shredded chicken and saucy beans and corn are the perfect building blocks to an easy weeknight dinner: slow cooker burrito bowls!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 6 hr 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Chicken Burrito Bowls

  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 14 1/2 ounces diced tomatoes, drained well
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 15 ounces black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup frozen yellow corn

Toppings

  • 2 cups cooked brown rice
  • 2 cups finely shredded romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup pico de gallo
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Lime wedge
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. In the slow cooker, combine the chicken breasts, diced tomatoes, garlic, chili powder, salt and cumin. Cover with the lid and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours. Stir in beans and corn, and cook for 30 more minutes.
  2. Use forks to shred the chicken directly in the slow cooker.
