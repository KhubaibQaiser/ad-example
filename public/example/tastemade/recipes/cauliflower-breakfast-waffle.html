Cauliflower Breakfast Waffle

Cauliflower...for breakfast? Better believe it! This powerhouse ingredient blends easily into waffle batter for a savory and nutritious start to the day.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Cauliflower Breakfast Waffle

  • 3 cups riced cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup shredded swiss or gruyere cheese
  • 2 green onions,,
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • Cooking oil spray

Toppings

  • Fried egg
  • 6 slices center-cut applewood-smoked bacon, cooked and coarsely chopped
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Paprika, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle iron on medium heat.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, add all ingredients except cooking spray. Stir until thoroughly combined.
