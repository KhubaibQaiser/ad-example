Pumpkin Spice Latte Cake

Yummy little cakes made to look like burning candles to create a festive holiday spread

By Mia Starr

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Cake

  • 3 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  • 3/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup espresso powder or instant espresso
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/3 cups light brown sugar
  • 2 cups canned pumpkin puree
  • 4 eaches eggs

Vanilla Buttercream

  • 4 sticks unsalted butter
  • 8 cups powdered sugar
  • 4 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons almond extract
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Water Ganache

  • 1 1/2 cups melting candy
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons warm water

Assemble and Decorate

  • Buttercream
  • Ganache
  • Birthday candle
  • Skewer

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F and line and grease a jelly roll pan or high sided cookie sheet or sheet tray.
  2. In a medium bowl whisk together the flour, baking powder, pumpkin pie spice, cinnamon and salt.
