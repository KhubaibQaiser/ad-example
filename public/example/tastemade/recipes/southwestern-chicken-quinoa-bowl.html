Southwestern Chicken Quinoa Bowl

Spice up your week with our Southwestern-style chicken and quinoa bowl, packed with protein and ideal for a week of lunches.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 4
Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 chicken breasts, cubed
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 jalapeno, diced
  • 1 1/2 cups quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 15 ounces black beans, 1 can, drained and rinsed
  • 15 ounces fire-roasted diced tomato, not drained
  • 1 1/2 cups corn
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 avocados, diced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped, extra for garnish
