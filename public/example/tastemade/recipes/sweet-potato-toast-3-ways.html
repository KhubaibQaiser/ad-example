Sweet Potato Toast 3 Ways

Looking for a toast alternative? Here are three ways to add a sweet twist to an old favorite.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 1 avocado, mashed
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper chili flakes
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1/2 sliced banana
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 egg, fried
  • 1/8 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Slice sweet potato into 1/4 inch slices, lengthwise.
  2. Turn your toaster to 750°F.
