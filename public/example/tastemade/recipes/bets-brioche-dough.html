Basic Brioche Dough

This dough can be made into a brioche scroll, doughnuts, fruit loaf, or hot cross buns and can also be used to make savory scrolls, burger buns, or a simple plain brioche loaf

By Bernadette Gee

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

The Unruly Baker

Prep Time: 20 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 50 grams sugar
  • 15 grams salt
  • 240 milliliters milk
  • 10 grams instant dry yeast
  • 170 grams salted butter
  • 560 grams plain flour

Steps

  1. In your mixer bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar and salt.
  2. Pour the milk into a suitable bowl, and heat in your microwave (in short bursts) until lukewarm.
