Roasted Pumpkin, Pickled Mushrooms and Bruschetta

Celebrate fall with a vegan, autumn-themed bruschetta, packed with roasted pumpkin, pickled mushrooms, and plenty of herbs.

By Laura Miller

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Raw. Vegan. Not Gross.

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4–6 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup fresh mushroom, roughly chopped + juice of 1 lemon, 1 tablespoon olive oil and 1/2 cup fresh mixed herb leaves; mint, parsley, oregano
  • 2 slices vegan bread, thinly sliced + grape tomatoes, to garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Transfer butternut squash cubes and garlic cloves to parchment-lined baking sheet, drizzle with oil.
