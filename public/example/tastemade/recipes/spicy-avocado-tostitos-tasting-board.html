Spicy Avocado TOSTITOS® Tasting Board

The snack board of your dreams is here and it includes crunchy veg, a spicy and creamy avocado dip, and TOSTITOS® Bite Size Rounds and Black Bean & Garlic chips. Dig in!

(1)Recipe Notes (1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 10
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Spicy Avocado Dip

  • 4 small or 3 medium ripe avocados
  • ¼ cup sour cream
  • 1 large or 2 small cloves garlic, grated or finely minced
  • ⅛-¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper, depending on desired spice level
  • ½ lime, juiced
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt, or more to taste

Tasting Board

  • TOSTITOS® Black Bean and Garlic Flavored Chips
  • TOSTITOS® Bite Size Rounds
  • TOSTITOS® Chunky Salsa, Mild or Medium
  • 1 cup crunchy toasted corn
  • 1 cup pickled peppers
  • 1 pound mini sweet peppers, seeded and halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 bunch radishes, sliced
  • 1 Fresno chili, diced with some rounds reserved for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon cilantro
  • Lime wedges, for garnish

Steps

Spicy Avocado Dip

  1. In the bowl of a blender add the avocados, sour cream, garlic, cayenne, lime juice and salt.
  2. Blend until smooth. If it’s too thick add more lime juice and if it’s too thin add more avocado. The texture should be light and fluffy!

Tasting Board

  1. Spread the spicy avocado dip onto a cutting board and garnish with Fresno chilis and cilantro.
  2. Surround with crunchy corn, pickled peppers, TOSTITOS® Black Bean and Garlic Flavored Chips, TOSTITOS® Bite Size Rounds, and mini sweet peppers, cucumbers, radishes and limes!
