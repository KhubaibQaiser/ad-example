Apple Pie Dough

Want to make a delicious homemade apple pie? Start by making your pie dough from scratch.

By Monti Carlo

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 30 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 12.5 12 1/2 ounces all-purpose flour, frozen and divided in half
  • 2 sticks salted european style butter, frozen
  • 1/4 cup of water, chilled
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar, chilled

Steps

  1. Place 1/2 half of flour in a food processor.
  2. Using the grater blade of a food processor (or a hand grater and a whole lot of elbow grease) grate 1 stick of the frozen butter into the flour.
