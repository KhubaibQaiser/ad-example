Keto Lava Cake

This lava cake captures all of the chocolatey joy of the classic dessert in a keto-friendly recipe.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 4 tablespoons swerve powdered sugar
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon grass-fed butter
  • Special equipment - 4 ramekins

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Prepare 4 ramekins greased with grass-fed butter.
