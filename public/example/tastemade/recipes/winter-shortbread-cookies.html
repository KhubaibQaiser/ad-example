Winter Shortbread Cookies

These buttery, melt in your mouth cookies are sure to please a crowd!

By Mia Starr

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 24
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 2/3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/2 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 drops blue gel color
  • 1 each egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup silver crystal sugar
  • 1/4 cup white crystal sugar

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and salt to combined.
  2. In the stand of an electric mixer, beat together the butter and sugar and powdered sugar until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes.
