Pea & Ricotta Striped Ravioli

It's easier than you think to make pasta from scratch. This two-tone dough is filled with ricotta and peas, for a homemade ravioli recipe that's sure to impress.

By julie nolke

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 14 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

White Pasta

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1–2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Green Pasta

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons frozen spinach, thawed
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Filling

  • 1 cup ricotta
  • 3/4 cup fresh peas, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • Butter for sautéing

Steps

  1. To make fresh pasta, make a well in the flour and add in the wet ingredients. Combine with your hands and then knead to bring together dough. Continue to knead for 5 minutes. Wrap with plastic wrap and let rest in the fridge for 30 minutes before rolling. Repeat with green dough the only difference being that you will have to blend the egg, spinach and oil in a blender together prior to adding it to your flour.
  2. Using a pasta maker, roll out each color dough to level 7°F thickness. Flouring the dough slightly as you go. Cut the green dough into thin strips and place over top the white dough to make a striped pattern, pressing down. You may need to use water to help it stick. Continue to roll out the striped dough until level three thickness.
