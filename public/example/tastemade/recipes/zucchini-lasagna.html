Zucchini Lasagna

Satisfy your pasta cravings with this healthy lasagna recipe, which incorporates plenty of vegetables, brown rice noodles, and skim cheeses.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 small carrot, diced
  • 1 stalk celery, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1–1 1/2 cups tomato sauce
  • 6 basil leaves, torn
  • 6 sheets brown rice lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces skim ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups grated part-skim mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350°F. Butter a 9x9-inch baking dish.
  2. Slice zucchini into long 1/4-inch ribbons using a mandoline.
