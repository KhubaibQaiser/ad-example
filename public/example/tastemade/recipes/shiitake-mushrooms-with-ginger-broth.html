Shiitake Mushrooms with Ginger Broth

Broth with dashi and ginger infused with grilled mushroom caps.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup sake
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon dashi powder
  • 6 large shiitake mushrooms
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 scallions, sliced

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, heat the sake, mirin, soy sauce, water, ginger and dashi for 10–15 minutes.
  2. Remove the stems from the mushrooms and drizzle with oil and salt and pepper.
