Watercress Salad

Chef Eduardo Garcia channels the wild flavors of Montana in his fresh watercress salad.

By eduardo garcia

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds washed and cleaned watercress
  • 1/4 cup hazelnuts, toasted, peeled, crushed
  • 1/4 cup montana mex, avocado oil, extra virgin
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon whole grain mustard
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Cracked black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. In an oven set at 350°F, toast Hazelnuts for 10 min. Remove from oven and using a dry kitchen towel, massage exterior paper/skin away from the inner nut.
  2. In a small bowl whisk together Honey, Thyme, Lemon juice, and Mustard. Slowly add Avocado Oil while whisking. Check for seasoning and add salt and black pepper to taste.
