Stone Ground Grits with Scallions & Garlic Chili Oil

Weeknight comfort food doesn't have to be boring. Try Chef Logan Sandoval's creamy stone-ground grits with scallions and a flavorful garlic chili oil as a base for whatever protein you happen to have on hand.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 8 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1 fresno or serrano chile, thinly sliced
  • 2 teaspoons crushed red pepper flakes
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 cup stone-ground grits
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup shredded white cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped scallions

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic, chile, and pepper flakes, stirring constantly until the garlic is golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes. Transfer the garlic-chile oil to a jar and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, bring 1 teaspoon of water seasoned with salt to a boil. Slowly pour in the grits, whisking constantly to prevent any lumps. Reduce the heat to medium-low and cook, stirring constantly, until the water is absorbed, about 10 minutes.
