Whole Roasted Smoked Chicken with Potatoes, Sausage and Salsa Verde

Dive into the smoky delight of a whole roasted chicken, nestled with savory potatoes and sausage, all topped off with a zesty, fresh salsa verde for a mouthwatering feast.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 3 hr 20 min
Servings: 8
Hard
 :

Ingredients

Whole Roasted Smoked Chicken

  • 3 small whole chickens
  • 1/2 cup avocado oil
  • Salt and pepper

Salsa Verde

  • 2 lemons, sliced in half
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 bunch parsley
  • 3 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup agave nectar
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Potatoes and Sausage

  • 5 pounds fresh chorizo or spanish sausage links tied together
  • 8 large leeks, cleaned
  • 8 russet potatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 bunch parsley
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the fire under the trestle: light the briquettes and wood chips and let burn for 20–25 minutes.
  2. Rub the chicken with avocado oil, salt and pepper and hang it from the top of the trestle over the fire pit.
