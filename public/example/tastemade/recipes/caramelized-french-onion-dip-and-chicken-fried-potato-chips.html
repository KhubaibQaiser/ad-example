Caramelized French Onion Dip and Chicken Fried Potato Chips

If you love chicken fried steak you will love these chips paired with a classic dip fit for a crowd.

By danni rose

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 35 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Caramelized French Onion Dip

  • 4 slices thick cut bacon, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons bacon fat, reserved from cooked bacon
  • 1 large sweet onion, thinly sliced
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley

Chicken Fried Potato Chips

  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 2 large russet potatoes, peeled
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 tablespoon accent
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon paprika
  • 1/2 tablespoon kosher salt, plus more for sprinkling
  • 1/2 tablespoon black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon according to package directions, and reserve 2 tablespoons of bacon fat.
  2. Pat bacon with paper towels to soak up any extra oil.
