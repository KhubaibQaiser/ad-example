Peanut Butter Chocolate Cupcakes

A rich chocolate cupcake with salty & sweet peanut butter buttercream frosting.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 1 hr 5 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Chocolate Cupcakes

  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Peanut Butter Frosting

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Chocolate Webs

  • 2 ounces semi-sweet chocolate bar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Line a 12 count muffin pan with paper cupcake liners or spray with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk the flour and cocoa powder until smooth. Whisk in the white and brown sugar, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
