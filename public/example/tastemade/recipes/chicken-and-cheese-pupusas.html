Chicken and Cheese Pupusas

Learn how to make the national dish of El Salvador: pupusas. Paola shares her homemade recipe for the masa dough cakes stuffed with chicken and cheese, served with a carrot and cabbage curtido.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Curtido

  • 2 cups cabbage, shredded
  • 2 carrots, shredded
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Chicken and Cheese Pupusas

  • 1 1/2 cups masa harina
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 cups water, plus more for shaping
  • 1/4 cup oil, plus 2 tablespoons for frying

Filling

  • 1/2 cup queso fresco, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, shredded

Steps

  1. Make the curtido: Combine ingredients and allow to sit for at least 30 minutes.
