Apple Pie Waffles with Drunken Maple Syrup

When dessert meets brunch for the ultimate treat. And its vegan!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 6
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Vegan Waffles

  • 1 ¾ cups non-dairy milk
  • 2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons pure cane sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¾ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 4 tablespoons non-dairy butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Apple Pie Topping

  • 1 frozen vegan pie crust
  • 4 tablespoons non-dairy butter
  • 4 granny smith apples, peeled, cored and sliced ¼” thick
  • 1 cup pure cane sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¾ cup vegan heavy whipping cream

Drunken Syrup

  • 2 cups maple syrup
  • ½ cup cognac

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle maker to desired temperature.
  2. In a measuring cup, whisk together the non-dairy milk and vinegar.
