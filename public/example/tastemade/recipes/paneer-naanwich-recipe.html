Paneer Naanwich

This paneer naanwich is your new favourite lunch

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Paneer Naanwich

  • 3 1/2 ounces paneer, cut into 3/16 inch
  • Cubes 2 tablespoons ghee/butter
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • Black cardamom 1 green cardamom
  • 2 cloves peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 2 tablespoons tomato passata 2 birds eye green chillies
  • 2 tablespoons cashew paste 2 tablespoons greek yoghurt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric, handful baby spinach
  • 1 handful fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • Finely chopped

Kachumer Salad

  • 1/2 red onion, finely sliced
  • 1 lime, juiced, finely diced
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 extra ghee/butters
  • 2 popadoms, broken into 3–4 pieces

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a non-stick pan. Once hot, add the cumin seeds. When they begin to splatter, add the paneer and salt. Cook for 2–3 minutes or until golden all over.
  2. Remove from pan and keep aside.
