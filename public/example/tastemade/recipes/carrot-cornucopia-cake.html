Carrot Cornucopia Cake

Moist and delicious carrot cake made into a cute fall harvest decorated cake

By Mia Starr

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 12
Average
 :

Ingredients

Carrot Cake

  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground clove
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 1/4 cups vegetable or canola oil
  • 6 eaches eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups light brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 cups grated carrots

Buttercream

  • 4 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 2 pounds powdered sugar
  • 4 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons almond extract
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 pinches salt

Assemble and Decorate

  • Buttercream
  • 1 each waffle cone
  • 1 bunch green grapes
  • 1 basket orange gooseberries
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line 3 8 inches cake pans with parchment and spray.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, clove and salt until well combined.
