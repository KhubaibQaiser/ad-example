Moroccan Chicken Tagine

Ingredients

Chicken Tagine

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 chicken thighs, with skin and bone on
  • 1 tablespoon ras el hanout
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 4 shallots
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 thumb size piece of ginger
  • 2 tablespoons rose harissa
  • 1 preserved lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 4 small carrots
  • 2 3/4 ounces violet olives
  • 15 1/4 fluid ounces chicken stock
  • Coriander

Cous Cous

  • 3 1/2 ounces cous cous
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 ounce parsley
  • 1 3/4 ounces pomegranate seeds
