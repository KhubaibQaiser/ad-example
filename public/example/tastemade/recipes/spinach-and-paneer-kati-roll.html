Spinach and Paneer Kati Roll

Nadiya Hussain shows us how to create the perfect dishes to complement any kind of day; like her vegetarian spinach and paneer kati rolls.

By nadiya hussein

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

Spinach and Paneer

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 ounces block paneer cheese, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 5 1/4 ounces spinach leaves
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Kati Rolls

  • Olive oil, for frying
  • 2 free-range eggs
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 4 small tortilla wraps

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of the oil in a saucepan over a medium heat.
  2. Add the paneer and fry for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. You want to color the paneer as much as possible.
