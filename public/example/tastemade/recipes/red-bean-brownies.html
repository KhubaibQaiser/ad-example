Red Bean Brownies

Red bean paste lends caramely sweetness to these vegan dessert bars.

By Nyesha Arrington

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pound mochiko (or sweet rice flour)
  • 1/2 cup gluten free flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, himalayan
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups almond milk
  • 2 ounces vegan butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup grapeseed oil
  • 18 ounces sweetened red bean paste
  • Cooking spray, for spraying pan
  • Dairy free vanilla ice cream, for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients in a large bowl. Add wet ingredients and red bean paste and whisk until thoroughly combined.
