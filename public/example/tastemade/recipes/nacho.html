Healthy Homemade Nachos

Try a lighter twist on classic nachos with this creative recipe! Chef Erwan Heussaff swaps store-bought tortilla chips for a homemade, baked tortilla chip made with almond flour.

By Erwan Heussaff

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Lightened Up

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 2
 :

Ingredients

Nacho

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 3/4 pound ground chicken
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • Season with salt and pepper
  • 15 ounces black beans, rinsed
  • 1/2 small head iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • 3/4 cup fresh salsa
  • 1 avocado, chopped
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Pickled jalapeño
  • Greek yogurt

Tortilla Chips

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. While the chips bake, Heat oil in a skillet. Cook the ground chicken with onion and garlic.
  2. When chicken is cooked and colored, add in beans to warm. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
