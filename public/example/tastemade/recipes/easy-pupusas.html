Easy Pupusas

Learn how to make authentic pupusas, a masa flatbread stuffed with refried beans and cheese. This traditional dish from El Salvador is similar to an arepa, and will quickly become your favorite easy lunch recipe!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 2
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 cups masa harina
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2.5 cups warm water

Curtido

  • 1/2 cabbage
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 onion
  • 1 jalapeño
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2,5 cups boiling water
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp salt

Pupusas

  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (50 g) - fresh if not, mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup refried beans (100 g)
  • 1/2 tablespoon vegetable oil, for frying

Steps

  1. Mix the masa harina and water in a bowl.
  2. Mix it with your hands until you get a soft and pliable dough.
