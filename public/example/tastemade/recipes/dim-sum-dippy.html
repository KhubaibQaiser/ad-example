Dim Sum Dippy

This mustardy, vinegary sauce makes the perfect dip for crispy fried veggies.

By tiffani faison

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces mayonnaise
  • 4 teaspoons diluted red vinegar (available in asian markets)
  • 3/16 ounce oriental mustard powder
  • 1 tablespoon tamari
  • 1 tablespoon sambal calabrian chili oil, to taste

Steps

  1. Rehydrate the mustard powder in the red vinegar by whisking them together.
  2. Fold in the remaining ingredients until combined.
