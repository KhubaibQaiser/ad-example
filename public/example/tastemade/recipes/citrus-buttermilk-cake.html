Citrus Buttermilk Cake

Finish off any meal on a sweet note with Chef Logan Sandoval's citrus buttermilk cake, infused with a lemon and orange syrup and covered in a zesty citrus glaze.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Cake

  • Softened butter, for greasing
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 3 teaspoons grated lemon zest
  • 2 teaspoons grated orange zest

Syrup

  • 3 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fresh orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Glaze

  • 3/4 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tablespoon buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon fresh orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • Lemon slices and/or zest, for serving (optional)
  • Orange slices and/or zest, for serving (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Lightly butter a 8x8-inch baking dish and line with parchment paper, leaving a 1 inch overhang on two sides.
  2. Butter the parchment.
