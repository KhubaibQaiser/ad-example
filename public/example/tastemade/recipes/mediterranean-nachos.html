Mediterranean Nachos

Serve a bed of crispy pita chips loaded with texture-filled toppings like cucumber, banana peppers, and crispy chickpeas, along with with hummus, tzatziki, and fresh herbs.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Crispy Chickpeas

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and patted dry
  • 1½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Tzatziki

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1 tablespoon dill, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ½ cucumber, grated
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon salt, or more to taste

Mediterranean Nachos

  • Pita chips
  • ½ English cucumber, chopped
  • ¼ cup Kalamata olives, chopped
  • ¼ cup banana peppers or pepperoncini
  • ½ cup hummus
  • 2 tablespoons Calabrian chili paste
  • 1 tablespoons dill, chopped
  • 1 tablespoons parsley, chopped

Steps

Crispy Chickpeas

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. To a nonstick or lined baking sheet add the drained, rinsed, and dried chickpeas, coat them in olive oil, and bake at 350 degrees for 40-50 minutes.
  3. As soon as the chickpeas come out of the oven, season with all the spices and toss to coat, then allow to cool.

Tzatziki

  1. For the tzatziki, put the grated cucumber in a cheese cloth or clean tea towel and squeeze as much liquid out as possible.
  2. Add the drained cucumber to a medium mixing bowl, then add the rest of the ingredients and mix well.

Mediterranean Nachos

  1. To assemble the nachos, dollop hummus and tzatziki in the middle of the plate, surround with pita chips, and add additional toppings as desired. Enjoy!
