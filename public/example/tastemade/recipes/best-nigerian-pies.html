Nigerian Meat Pies

Pasties ain't got nothing on these Nigerian meat pies!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 10
Average
 :

Ingredients

Pastry

  • 14 ounces plain flour
  • 7 ounces unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt
  • Smoked paprika
  • Water

Filling

  • 10 1/2 ounces mince meat
  • 1 diced potato
  • 1 diced onion
  • 2 diced garlic cloves
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 beef stock cube
  • Thyme (optional)
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 splash cold milk

Steps

  1. Mix in your butter turmeric, flour, and salt together. It should get to a crumble looking consistency.
  2. Add water gradually and keep kneading until you can roll out, or it can be rolled into a ball.
