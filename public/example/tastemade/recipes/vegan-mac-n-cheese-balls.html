Vegan Mac N Cheese Balls

Made with cashew cheese, these bite-sized vegan cheese balls are crispy on the outside and gooey within.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 2 hr
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Vegan Mac N Cheese Balls

  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 8 ounces quinoa macaroni pasta

Crispy Coating

  • 1 cup almond meal
  • 1/4 cup cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup hemp seeds
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt

Sauce

  • 1 cup raw cashews
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk

Steps

  1. Meanwhile, cook the quinoa pasta in boiling water for 6–8 minutes, or until al dente. Drain well.
  2. Add the cooked pasta to a large mixing bowl and top with the sauce. Mix well.
