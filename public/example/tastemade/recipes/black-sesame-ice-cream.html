Black Sesame Ice Cream

A backyard barbecue feast isn't complete without dessert! Chef Logan Sandoval's easy black sesame ice cream is the perfect light finish to a heavy meal.

By Logan Sandoval

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 6 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons black sesame seeds
  • 14 ounces sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 vanilla bean, split and scraped
  • 2 cups heavy cream

Steps

  1. Place a medium-size loaf pan (9x5-inch) in the freezer until cold, 30 minutes.
  2. Add sesame seeds to medium non-stick saute pan and toast over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally and making sure the seeds do not burn 4–6 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool on a separate plate.
