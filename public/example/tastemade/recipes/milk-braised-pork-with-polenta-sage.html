Milk Braised Pork With Polenta & Sage

This herby braised pork and polenta makes the perfect centerpiece on a holiday table.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 3 hr
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Braised Pork

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 pounds pork butt
  • Salt and pepper
  • 5 cups of milk
  • 3 sprigs sage
  • 3 sprigs thyme
  • 1 head of garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flake
  • 3 strips lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley leaves

Polenta

  • 1 1/2 cups stone ground cornmeal
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F. Season the pork butt with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a large dutch oven over medium high heat. Add the vegetable oil. Sear the pork until deep golden brown on all sides.
