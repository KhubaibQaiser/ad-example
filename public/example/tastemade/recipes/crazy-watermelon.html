Crazy Watermelon

Loaded with watermelon, cucumber, mango and assorted Mexican candies, this refreshing dessert truly is crazy.

By priscilla gonzales

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy

Ingredients

  • 1/2 small, seedless watermelon
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1 mango, chopped
  • 1 handful peanuts
  • Salsaghetti candy
  • Chaca-chaca candy, chopped
  • 1 medium skwinkle
  • Marimbas lollipop
  • 4â€“5 sour gummy worms
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Chamoy sauce
  • Tajin

Steps

  1. Use a melon baller to scoop little balls out of the watermelon half, being careful not to pierce through the rind. Once it's is balled out, place most of the balls back in the watermelon half.
  2. Add the the cucumber and mango. Top with peanuts, Salsaghetti and Chaca-Chaca. Place Swkinkles on skewers and stick them into the watermelon, followed by lollipops. Add 2 pieces gummy worms, then squeeze lime juice over the watermelon.
