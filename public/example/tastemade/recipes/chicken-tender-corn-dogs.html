Chicken Tender Corn Dogs

Your two favorite foods just joined forces in these chicken tender corn dogs! Everyone will love this fun dinner recipe, which is served alongside a sweet and spicy dipping sauce.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Corn Dog Batter

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cornmeal
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten

Chicken Tender

  • 1/2 gallon vegetable oil, for frying
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut lengthwise into 2-inches-thick strips

BBQ Dipping Sauce

  • 1/2 cup store-bought bbq sauce
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, cornmeal, sugar, salt and baking powder. Add the milk and eggs and whisk to combine.
