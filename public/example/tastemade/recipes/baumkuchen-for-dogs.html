Baumkuchen for Dogs

Roll up all your love for your pup into one very special birthday cake.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Baumkuchen for Dogs

  • Sweet potato
  • Blueberry
  • Apple
  • Chervil
  • 1 banana

Pancake Batter

  • 1/2 cup rice flour pancake mix for dogs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons soy milk, unprocessed
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons water

Strawberry Cream

  • 1 cup yogurt, unsweetened
  • Powdered strawberry, freeze-dried

Steps

  1. Cut the banana to fit the width of a tamagoyaki rolled egg pan.
  2. Pour the pancake batter little by little into the tamagoyaki pan, and roll it up around the sliced banana in the center (if it sticks to the frying pan, add a little water to adjust).
