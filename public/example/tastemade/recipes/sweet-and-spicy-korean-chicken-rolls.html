Sweet and Spicy Korean Chicken Rolls

This Korean-style twist on chicken rollups is a protein-packed dinner recipe you'll love — and you don't even need to turn on the oven.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 1
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken thigh, boneless
  • 1 6-inches welsh onion
  • 1 small bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 ounce kimchi
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sake
  • 1/2 teaspoon doubanjang (chili bean paste)
  • 1 tablespoon ground sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 clove garlic, grated

Steps

  1. Butterfly chicken and flatten open onto a sheet of plastic wrap.
  2. Roll the chicken up and twist the ends to ensure a tight wrap.
