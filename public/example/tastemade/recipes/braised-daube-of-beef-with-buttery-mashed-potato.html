Braised ‘Daube’ of Beef with Buttery Mashed Potato

Your next elegant dinner party calls for Chef Michelle Trusselle's tender braised beef, served atop buttery mashed potatoes. This surprisingly simple recipe will stun your guests with its rich flavors!

By Numra Siddiqui

Updated: 9/30/2024

Recipe from:

If These Meals Could Talk

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 5 hr
Servings: 2
Hard
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

For the beef cheeks

  • 2 beef cheeks, trimmed of sinew
  • 50 grams butter
  • 3 onions, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and chopped
  • 1 bunch thyme, leaves picked
  • 600 milliliters red wine
  • 400 milliliters beef stock

For the mashed potatoes

  • 400 grams maris piper potatoes
  • 158 milliliters double cream
  • 160 milliliters whole milk
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 5 thyme sprigs, leaves picked
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 100 grams unsalted butter

Garnish

  • Flat leaf parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. To make the beef cheeks, first preheat the oven to 200°C/Gas mark 6.
  2. Season the beef cheeks with salt and pepper and sear in a casserole so that they are browned on all sides.
