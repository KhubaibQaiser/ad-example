Cricket Cupcakes

Cricket flour is the secret ingredient in this homemade cupcake recipe, which also incorporates sweet candied crickets as a topping! If you've ever wanted to eat bugs, this is the dessert recipe for you.

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Cricket Cupcakes

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup cricket flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup salted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temp
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla paste or extract
  • 1/2 cup whole milk

Frosting

  • 1 cup salted butter, softened
  • 4 cups powdered sugar, sifted
  • 2 tablespoons whole milk
  • 2 teaspoons pandan flavoring paste or extract

Candied Crickets

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons corn syrup
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 3 tablespoons dry roasted crickets (for human consumption)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a bowl, sift both flours and baking powder together.
