Omurice

This twist on a classic Japanese omurice uses pork meatballs, making it a filling and easy dinner recipe!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 8 3/4 ounces pork mince
  • 1 garlic clove, grated
  • 2 shallots, grated
  • 3/4 inch ginger, grated
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1 teaspoon light soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons groundnut oil

Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tonkatsu sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon hot water

Omu

  • 1 egg
  • 1 dash cream
  • 1 teaspoon groundnut soy bean oil, for frying
  • 1 cup pork jok

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all the meatball ingredients except for the groundnut oil. Roll into 8 approximately 1-inch meatballs. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. To cook the meatballs, heat the groundnut oil in a frying pan over a medium-high heat. Fry the meatballs in batches until golden brown all over and cooked through.
