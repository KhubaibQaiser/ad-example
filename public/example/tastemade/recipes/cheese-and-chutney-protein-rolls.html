Cheese and Chutney Protein Rolls

For a puff pastry snack or crowd-pleasing appetizer that offers a boost of protein, dig into these easy cheese and chutney protein rolls with chef Gabe Kennedy.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 8
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Chutney

  • 1 cup packed cilantro leaves, washed and dried
  • ½ cup packed mint leaves, washed and dried
  • 2 serrano chiles
  • 2 green onions
  • 2 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp virgin olive oil
  • 2 scoops Isopure Zero Carb Protein Powder, Unflavored
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Cheese and Chutney Protein Rolls

  • 1 13-14oz puff pastry sheet, defrosted
  • 1 cup extra sharp cheddar cheese or muenster cheese, shredded
  • ½ cup small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 egg, beaten with 1 tbsp of water

Steps

Chutney

  1. On a cutting board, finely chop cilantro and mint leaves, set aside on the cutting board.
  2. Finely chop serrano chiles and green onion, add to herbs then chop while mixing together.
  3. Add mixture to medium bowl with lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic, cumin, salt and oil.
  4. Gently mix until all combined.
  5. Add one scoop of protein powder at a time and stir until fully combined, mixture will look dry.
  6. Set aside.

Cheese and Chutney Protein Rolls

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees. Line a sheet tray with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk egg and water in a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Lightly dust a work surface with flour, unroll or unfold puff pastry on the surface, roughly 13in x 15in.
  4. Dollop herb chutney mixture onto puff pastry, covering all but an 1in strip along one of the short ends.
  5. Sprinkle it with cheese and red onion.
  6. Brush uncovered strip with egg wash, this will help seal the puff pastry after it is rolled.
  7. Begin rolling on the opposite end of the uncovered strip.
  8. Roll tightly and evenly, trying not to press down while rolling.
  9. Cut into 1 ½ in pieces and place 2 inches apart on sheet tray.
  10. Brush each roll with egg wash.
  11. Bake in the oven on a lower rack for 20-25 minutes until golden brown and caramelized.
  12. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for 5 minutes before eating.
