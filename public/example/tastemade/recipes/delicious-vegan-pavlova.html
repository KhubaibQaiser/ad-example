Vegan Pavlova

You don't have to miss meringue just because you're vegan!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 4 hr
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Pavlova

  • 140–150 milliliters aquafaba (juice from 1 can chickpeas)
  • 1 pinch cream of tartar
  • 180 grams caster sugar
  • 3 1/2 grams xantham gum
  • 1/2 vanilla pod, seeds scraped

Cream

  • 400 milliliters coconut cream, well chilled
  • 38 grams icing sugar

Assembly

  • Pavlovum
  • Cream
  • 250 grams mango
  • 250 grams pineapple
  • 2 kiwis
  • 2 passionfruits
  • 1 lime
  • Coconut flake, toasted

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 100°C.
  2. Pour the aquafaba into the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the whisk. Add the cream of tartar and whisk on medium speed for 3–5 minutes, until foaming. Increase the speed to high and whisk for 5–10 minutes to stiff peaks. Add the sugar 13 grams at a time, whisking between each addition to a stiff meringue. Add the vanilla seeds and sift the xanthum gum over the meringue. Whisk for a further 30 seconds.
