Smash Fried Brussels Sprouts with Lemon Gremolata

Transform basic Brussels sprouts into a savory, addictive side dish with this simple lemon gremolata recipe.

By Dini Klein

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds brussels sprouts
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 1/3 cup parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Fill a large pot 3/4 full of the way full with water. Bring to a boil. Add in Brussels sprouts and boil for 10 minutes until bright green and tender. Drain well.
