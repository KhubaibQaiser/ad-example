Sweet Red Bean Paste

Forget store-bought — learn how to make sweet red bean paste at home, to be used in Asian dessert recipes.

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried red beans (adzuki or kidney)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Submerge and soak dried beans in a bowl overnight.
  2. Drain beans and transfer to a pot. Fill the pot with water until the beans are submerged.
