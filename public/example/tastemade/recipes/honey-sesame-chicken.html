Honey Sesame Chicken

This nutritious honey sesame chicken wrap is a perfect easy lunch.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Honey Sesame Chicken

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
  • 1/4 cup raw honey
  • 2 tablespoons plain greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 3 tablespoons sliced almonds
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

Wraps

  • 4 large whole wheat large tortillas
  • 1 cucumber, cut into matchsticks

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine all ingredients for the honey sesame chicken.
  2. Lay out the tortilla on a flat surface. Place honey sesame chicken mixture in a line in the center of the wrap.
