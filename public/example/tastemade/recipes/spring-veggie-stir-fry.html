Spring Veggie Stir Fry

Make the most of fresh spring produce with Chef Frankie Celenza's budget-friendly, bright seasonal stir fry with a nutty sauce.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 5
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Veggie Stir Fry

  • 2 cups long grain white rice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 broccoli stems
  • 1 bunch swiss chard stems
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 1 bunch radishes
  • 8 ounces snap peas
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flake

Tahini Sauce

  • 1/2 cup tahini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons minced parsley leaves
  • 2 tablespoons mint
  • 2 tablespoons chives
  • 1 garlic clove, grated
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the rice according to package instructions.
  2. Use a vegetable peeler to peel the broccoli stalk and cut into small bite size pieces. Chop the chard stems into 1/2 inch pieces. Trim the woody ends off the asparagus and cut each stalk into thirds. Remove the radish tops and store them in the refrigerator for another recipe. Quarter the radishes. Trim and discard the ends off of the peas.
