Breakfast Burrito Collard Wraps

This paleo and gluten-free breakfast burrito filled with eggs, bacon, and veggies still solidly satisfies.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 large collard green leaves
  • 4 slices nitrate free bacon, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yellow onion, diced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce

Steps

  1. Lay the collard green leaves face down.
  2. Use a sharp knife to cut down the stems to be the same thickness as the leaves and remove the part of the stem that extends past the leaves. Set aside.
