Arroz Con Pollo

Indulge in a fiesta of flavors with Chef Alex Hill's Arroz Con Pollo recipe. Check out her feature on SpringHill's Recipe for Change, at youtube.com/MakeSpringhill.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 5
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Sofrito

  • 1⁄2 red pepper, finely chopped
  • 1⁄2 green pepper, finely chopped
  • 1⁄2 Spanish onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1⁄2 cubanelle green pepper (Italian Pepper)
  • 1⁄4 cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped

Arroz Con Pollo

  • 1⁄2 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite size pieces
  • 1 cup sofrito, roughly
  • 1 cup white rice (uncooked & parboiled white rice, like Carolina Gold)
  • 1-2 tablespoons sazon con coriander y achiote
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 1/2 to 2 cups chicken stock
  • kosher salt & pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Season your boneless, skinless chicken thighs with salt, pepper & some everyday seasoning like adobo - this has garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cumin & coriander in it. (Tip: Season the chicken at least 4 hours before cooking)
  2. Over medium heat, cover pot with one tablespoon or so with extra virgin olive oil
