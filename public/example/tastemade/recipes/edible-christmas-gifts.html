Edible Christmas Gifts

Here are 3 recipes perfect for last-minute gifting: infused vodka, homemade sugar cubes and a creamy hot chocolate mix!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 8 hr 10 min
Servings: 5
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Homemade Sugar Cubes

  • 1 cup granulated sugar (or brown sugar)
  • 2 teaspoons water
  • Food colouring, if desired

Infused Vodka

  • 50 3/4 fluid ounces of your favourite vodka
  • 1/2 cup fresh blackberries
  • 1 1/2 fresh figs, sliced into quarters
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cardamom pods
  • 1 vanilla bean, split down the center
  • Pickles and pickle juice

Creamy Hot Chocolate Mix

  • 1/2 cup powdered coffee creamer
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup skim milk powder
  • 1/2 cup confectioner's sugar
  • 1 candy cane

Steps

  1. If colouring your sugar cubes, combine the water and food colouring.
  2. Add the water to the sugar and mix well, until the sugar resembles wet sand.
