Aperol Spritz with Gooseberries

Refreshing twist on an Aperol Cocktail

By Alex Hill

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Spice Spice Baby

Prep Time: 5 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • 3 ounces aperol
  • 3 ounces dry prosecco
  • 1 splash lemon lime soda
  • 1 pint fresh gooseberries

Steps

  1. Add ice to a wine glass until it is nearly full.
  2. Pour in the Aperol (I usually eyeball this and pour until I’ve filled about 1/3 glass of the glass).
