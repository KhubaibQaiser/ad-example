Succulent Vegetarian Fajitas

These veggie-packed fajitas get wrapped into a spinach tortilla for the perfect portable meal.

By Greg King

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
¬†:

Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes, sliced
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 1 red pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow pepper, sliced
  • 1 italian squash, sliced
  • 1 bunch thin asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 beyond meat patties, defrosted
  • 10 spinach wraps
  • 1 pinch lawry's seasoning salt
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup grapeseed oil
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa (see recipe below)
  • Lime wedge, for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat cast iron skillet to medium-high heat and add oil. Fry the beyond patties for 2 minutes on each side. Once cooked, remove them from the pan and and slice into thin strips. Set aside.
  2. Bring the cast iron skillet to medium-high heat and add oil. Add the sliced potatoes and cook. Then add asparagus, peppers, and squash. Saut√© for 10 minutes or until desired. Add back the Beyond Meat and simmer a few more minutes.
