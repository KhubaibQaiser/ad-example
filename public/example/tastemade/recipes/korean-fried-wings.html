Air Fried Korean-Style Wings

Perfect for parties, chef Erwan Heussaff's healthy spin on Korean wings brings plenty of flavor — and crispy texture, thanks to the use of potato starch and an air fryer.

By Erwan Heussaff

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Lightened Up

Prep Time: 7 min
Cook Time: 37 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Korean "Fried" Wings

  • 1 pound chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup potato starch
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon gochujang paste
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hot water
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Garnish

  • 2 teaspoons white sesame seeds
  • 1/4 cup scallions, sliced

Steps

  1. Set air fryer to 380°F.
  2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl toss wings with potato starch, salt and pepper. Shake off any excess potato starch and place wings inside air fryer basket. Cook for 12 minutes and flip wings over. Cook for an additional 12 minutes, then raise the fryer temperature to 400°F and cook for 6 more minutes.
