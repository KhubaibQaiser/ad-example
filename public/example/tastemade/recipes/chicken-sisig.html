Healthy Chicken Sisig

Sisig, a classic Filipino dish, gets a revamp in chef Erwan Heussaff's recipe, which swaps fatty pork for chicken.

By Erwan Heussaff

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Lightened Up

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 8 hr 25 min
Servings: 4
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Chicken Sisig

  • 1/3 cup chicken liver
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup white onion, chopped
  • 2 red chiles, sliced
  • 4 calamansis, cut into wedges

Marinade

  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups calamansi juice
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 pound assorted chicken portions

Steps

  1. In a small blender, puree the chicken liver with salt and pepper and set aside.
  2. Remove the chicken from the marinade and place on a medium-high grill, char and cook through. Take chicken from the grill and chop into pieces and set aside.
