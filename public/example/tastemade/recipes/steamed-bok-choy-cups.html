Steamed Bok Choy Cups

Give bok choy some love with these cups stuffed with chicken and served with a peanut butter dipping sauce.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1/4 cup soy
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes

Steamed Bok Choy Cups

  • 8 baby bok choys, divided
  • 1/2 pound ground chicken
  • 2 scallions, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds (whites and greens)
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup water chestnuts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup bean sprouts, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated
  • Vegetable oil for cooking
  • Black sesame seed, for garnish
  • Cilantro, for garnish

Peanut dipping sauce

  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine all of the sauce ingredients and mix together until well combined. Set aside.
