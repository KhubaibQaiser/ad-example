Pac-Man Dim Sum

Dim Sum inspired by your favourite retro game... Pac Man!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 ounces wheat starch
  • 1 3/4 ounces tapioca starch
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon melted lard
  • 5 3/4 fluid ounces boiling water
  • 20 prawns - peeled, washed and dried
  • 1 3/4 ounces bamboo shoot, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt for marinating
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt, for seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3/16 ounce corn starch
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
  • 1 tablespoon shaoxing wine
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon melted lard

Steps

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients for the dough, then pour in boiling water (it must be boiling!) and mix quickly.
  2. Add the oil, which should bring the dough together, then knead until soft and silky. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it stand while you prepare the filling.
