Apple and White Chocolate Rocky Road

Nadiya Hussain shows us how to create the perfect dishes to complement any kind of day; like her apple and white chocolate rocky road, an autumnal twist on a classic sweet treat.

By nadiya hussein

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 18
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 200 grams white chocolate, roughly chopped (or use chocolate chips)
  • 30 milliliters golden golden syrup
  • 130 grams unsalted butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 1/2 grams ground cinnamon
  • 200 grams cinnamon biscuits (or digestives)
  • 100 grams white mini marshmallows (if you can only find big marshmallows, use scissors to chop them into small pieces)
  • 100 grams dried apples, chopped into small pieces
  • 50 grams raisins
  • 13 grams icing sugar, for dusting

Steps

  1. Lightly grease the inside of a 23-centimeters square baking tin. Line the base and sides of the tin with baking paper.
  2. Put about 2.5-centimeters of water into a medium saucepan. Find a heatproof bowl that will fit the top of the pan snugly. Make sure the base of the bowl doesn’t touch the water.
