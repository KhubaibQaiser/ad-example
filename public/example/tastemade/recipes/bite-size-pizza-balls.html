Bite-Size Pizza Balls

These bite-size sausage and pepperoni pizza balls make for the perfect party snack, and are easy to make thanks to the use of store-bought pizza dough!

By Arron Crascall

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Gamer Snacks

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 10
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Sausage & Pepperoni

  • 600 grams shop-bought pizza dough
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 2 Italian sausages, squeezed out of their skins
  • 150 grams pizza sauce
  • 1 pepperoni, thinly sliced
  • 200 grams mozzarella, grated
  • Dried oregano

Ham & Pineapple

  • 600 grams shop-bought pizza dough
  • 150 grams pizza sauce
  • 200 grams mozzarella, grated
  • 6 slices ham, chopped into small pieces
  • 3 pineapple rings, chopped into small pieces
  • Olive oil
  • Dried basil

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan and fry the sausage meat off until cooked through, stirring often so the mixture doesn’t clump.
  2. Preheat the oven to its hottest setting.
