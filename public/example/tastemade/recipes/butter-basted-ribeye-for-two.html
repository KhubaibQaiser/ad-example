Butter Basted Ribeye For Two

DIY a steakhouse dinner with this butter basted ribeye recipe.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 12 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces thick ribeye steak
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 sprigs rosemary
  • 2 sprigs thyme
  • 3 cloves of garlic, smashed
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Remove your steaks from the refrigerator 20–30 minute before cooking to bring them to room temperature.
  2. Heat a cast iron skillet over high heat. Once the pan is hot, add the vegetable oil.
