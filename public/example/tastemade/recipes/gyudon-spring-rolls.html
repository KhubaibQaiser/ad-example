Gyudon Spring Rolls

Enjoy all the flavors of Japanese gyudon (a rice bowl topped with simmered beef and onions) inside of a spring roll! This homemade spring roll recipe will quickly become your favorite weeknight dinner.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound beef, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sake
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mirin
  • Grated ginger
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • Red pickled ginger
  • 3 spring roll wrappers
  • Flour dissolved in water
  • 1 soft-boiled egg
  • Sesame seed

Steps

  1. In a saucepan over medium-high heat, add the onions, sugar, sake, soy sauce, mirin, ginger and water.
  2. Bring to a boil, then add the beef. When thoroughly cooked, add the rice and the red pickled ginger.
