Pecan Crusted Chicken with Yeast Roll Biscuits

The classic slider gets a facelift with pecan crusted chicken and yeast roll biscuits.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Yeast Roll Biscuits

  • 2 1/2 cups bread flour, plus more for dusting
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 heaping teaspoon rapid rise yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 8 tablespoons cold unsalted butter, diced, plus more for brushing
  • 3 tablespoons cold butter flavored shortening, diced
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon thyme
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
  • Flakey salt, for sprinkling
  • Brown butter honey, for drizzling

Pecan Crusted Chicken

  • 1 cup self-rising flour, divided
  • 3/4 cup pecans, finely ground
  • 1 pound chicken breast tenders, cut into 1 1/2 inches pieces
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder, divided
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder, divided
  • 2 teaspoons paprika, divided
  • 4 teaspoons salt, divided
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper, divided
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the bread flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, yeast and salt.
