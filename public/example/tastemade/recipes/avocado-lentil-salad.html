Avocado Lentil Salad

This nutritious salad is the perfect side-dish for just about any meal.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 large tomato
  • 1/4 cup red onion
  • 1 can (15 ounces) lentils
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Cilantro
  • Sea salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Dice avocado, tomato and onion and place in a bowl.
  2. Add the lentils and mix together.
  3. Drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil.
  4. Season with cilantro, salt and pepper.
