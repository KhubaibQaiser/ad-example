Margarita Station with Fresh Fruit Purees

Impress your guests with this DIY margarita station including toppings and fruit puree additions

By Alex Hill

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Spice Spice Baby

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Margarita Station with Fresh Fruit Purees

  • Margarita ingredients
  • 1 3/4 ounces tequila
  • 3/4 ounce fresh lime juice
  • 3/4 ounce orange liqueur
  • 1/4 ounce agave

Strawberry Fruit Puree

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 lemon juiced

Mango Fruit Puree

  • 1 cup fresh mango
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 lemon juiced

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice and shake!
  2. Rim glass with chamoy, tajin, salt, or whatever you'd like
