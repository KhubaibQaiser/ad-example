Raw Vegan Cheesecake Cups

This may be the easiest way to indulge in cheesecake guilt-free.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 4 hr
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almonds
  • 1 cup sultanas
  • 1 cup soaked cashews
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. Add the almonds, sultanas, and 1 pinch of sea salt to a food processor to make the crumb.
  2. Press the crumb into the bottom of the silicone cups.
