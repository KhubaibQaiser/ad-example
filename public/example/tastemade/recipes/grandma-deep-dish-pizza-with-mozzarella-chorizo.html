Grandma's Deep Dish Pizza with Mozzarella & Chorizo

Take store-bought pizza dough and sauce to new levels with this easy, cheesy deep-dish chorizo pizza recipe perfect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

Grandma Deep Dish Pizza with Mozzarella & Chorizo

  • 16 ounces store-bought pizza dough
  • 1 jar store-bought or prepared marina sauce
  • 1–2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pack dry cured chorizo
  • 1 piece pizza pan or baking pan
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

Homemade Marinara Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves whole garlic cloves
  • 28 ounces whole roma tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500°F.
  2. Pour 1/4 cup EVOO into your pizza pan & spread out the dough. Stretch the dough to fit the pan with your fingers and add dimples, as if you're making focaccia.
