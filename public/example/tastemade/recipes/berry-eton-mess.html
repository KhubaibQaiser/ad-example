Berry Eton Mess

If you're going to make a delicious mess, make one with grilled mixed berries and cream.

By megan mitchell

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 4 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pint blackberries
  • 1 pint blueberries
  • 1 pint raspberries
  • 1 pint strawberries
  • 1 pint heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 box vanilla meringues

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to 375°F. Place a grill basket on the grates.
  2. While the grill heats up, make the whipped cream. With a hand mixer or in a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment, beat the cream until soft peaks form. Add in the powdered sugar and vanilla; whip to incorporate. Place in the refrigerator until needed.
