Halloumi And Grape Skewers

These Halloumi and grape skewers are a great vegetarian alternative to your meat kebabs. The fruit adds a touch of sweetness that you didn't think you needed. Why not serve with a side of hummus for a complete summer mezze?

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 2
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 300g halloumi cheese, cut into rough 1 inch squares
  • 18 - 20 red grapes
  • Olive oil
  • Black pepper
  • 75g honey
  • 25g salted butter
  • 1 tbsp fresh oregano leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220 C / 200 C fan and line a baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Skewer the halloumi and grapes onto soaked wooden skewers until all the ingredients have been used up and then toss in a little olive oil and season with black pepper.
