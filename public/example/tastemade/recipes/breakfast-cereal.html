Breakfast Cereal

Grab a seat, Laura's making breakfast. These groats, with a bonus apple crumble and cream, are a great alternative to those humdrum smoothies and can be served warm during cold winter months.

By Laura Miller

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Raw. Vegan. Not Gross.

Prep Time: 1 hr 30 min
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Breakfast Cereal

  • 2 cups buckwheat groats, soaked 1 hour
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon stevia

Baked Apples

  • 2 apples
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Cashew Cream

  • 2 cups cashews
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil

Steps

  1. Soak buckwheat groats for at least one hour, then rinse
  2. Combine all remaining ingredients in a medium-sized bowl
