No Bake Apple Pie Protein Bar

The perfect post-workout no-bake treat.

By lazarus lynch

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 40 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups oat flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 1/2 cup vanilla vegan or paleo protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon apple pie spice
  • 1 cup almond butter, divided
  • 1/2 cup grade b maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup applesauce
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons almond milk
  • Sliced apple

Steps

  1. Line a 1/2-baking tray baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the oat flour, coconut flour, protein powder, and apple pie spice.
