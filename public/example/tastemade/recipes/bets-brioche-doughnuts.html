Brioche Doughnuts

Delicious doughnuts created using a sumptuous brioche dough.

By Bernadette Gee

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

The Unruly Baker

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 basic brioche dough
  • 800 milliliters canola or rice bran oil
  • 250 grams castor sugar
  • 20 grams ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Flour your benchtop thoroughly with plain flour. Remove your pre-made dough from the fridge and give it a little knead until it's smooth
  2. Roll the dough out with a rolling pin until it's about 2 centimeters thick - because the dough is chilled this will be a good work-out, so by the end of it you'll feel justified in eating that doughnut.
