Bacon Caramel Corn

Forget Cracker Jacks — with bacon and caramel, this is our new sweet and salty popcorn addiction.

By vijaya selvaraju

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 8 pieces bacon, chopped
  • 3/4 cup popcorn kernels
  • 3 tablespoons canola/vegetable oil
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup light corn syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon coarsely black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F, and prepare 2 baking sheets with slightly greased aluminum foil.
  2. In a cold frying pan, add chopped bacon. Turn heat up to medium, and cook bacon until crispy. Remove from pan, and drain on paper towels.
