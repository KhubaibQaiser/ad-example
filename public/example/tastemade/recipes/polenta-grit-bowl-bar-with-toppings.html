Polenta Grit Bowl Bar with Toppings

This polenta bowl bar lets everyone customize their toppings to their liking.

By Derrell Smith

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Mad Good Food

Prep Time: 3 hr
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Grits

  • 6 cups water
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cups corn grits or polenta
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 8 ounces sharp yellow cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 4 ounces parmesan cheese, grated
  • 3–6 tablespoons unsalted butter

Bang Bang Shrimp

  • 1 1/2 pounds jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined with tail off
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sriracha
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons grape seed oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Beer Braised Pulled Pork

  • 2 pounds boneless pork shoulder, cut into large pieces
  • 6 cloves garlic, sliced in half lengthwise
  • 6 ounces light beer of choice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon
  • Mustard powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Chili Oil Eggs

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup chili oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • Garnish
  • 1/2 bunch of scallions, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 bunch dill, leaves chopped

Garnish

  • Chive
  • 1 cup cherry tomato
  • 2 tablespoons pomegranate syrup
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons grapeseed oil

Steps

  1. In a large pot or dutch oven, bring the water to a boil. Stir in the grits.
  2. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let cook, stirring occasionally to prevent the grits from sticking to the bottom of the pot.
