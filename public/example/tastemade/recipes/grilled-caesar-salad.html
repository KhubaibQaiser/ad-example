Grilled Caesar Salad

With lightly charred lettuce and homemade dressing, this tasty salad would make Caesar proud.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/24/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 3 min
Cook Time: 8 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Salad

  • 1–2 romaine lettuce hearts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Bread of your choice
  • Parmesan shaving, to serve

Dressing

  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon anchovy paste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 large pasteurized egg yolk
  • 1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Cut bread into cubes, toss with some olive oil, salt and pepper. Bake for about 8 to 10 minutes or until golden. Set aside.
