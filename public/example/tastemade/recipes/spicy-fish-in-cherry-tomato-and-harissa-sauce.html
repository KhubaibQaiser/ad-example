Spicy Fish in Cherry Tomato and Harissa Sauce

Harissa, jalapeno, and paprika spice this flaky fish.

By einat admony

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 10 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 jalapeno chile, cored, seeded, and thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon harissa, store-bought or homemade
  • 3 tablespoons sweet paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground caraway
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 pints cherry tomatoes
  • Kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 large bunch fresh cilantro
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • 28 ounces flakey white-fleshed fish (grouper, bass, snapper, and halibut are all nice), skin off if possible

Steps

  1. Pour the vegetable oil into a deep large skillet.
  2. Immediately add smashed garlic cloves and cook on low heat just until fragrant, 3–4 minutes. Watch the pan closely to make sure the garlic doesn't brown, or it will become bitter.
