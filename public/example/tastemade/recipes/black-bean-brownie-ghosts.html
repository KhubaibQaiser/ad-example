Black Bean Brownie Ghosts

Dark rich mini brownies topped with royal icing and marshmallows for a fun and festive treat!

By Mia Starr

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 24
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Brownies

  • 15 ounces black beans
  • 2 eaches eggs, large
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons strongly brewed coffee
  • 3/4 cup dark cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips, semi sweet or milk chocolate

Royal icing and Marshmallow decorations

  • 1 each egg white, large
  • 1/2 pound powdered sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 24 eaches standard size marshmallows
  • 1 each edible black marker

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F and spray a mini muffin pan well with baking spray in each cavity.
  2. In a blender, add the black beans, eggs, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and coffee and blend to as smooth as possible.
