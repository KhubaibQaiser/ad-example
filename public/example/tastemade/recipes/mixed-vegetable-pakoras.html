Mixed Vegetable Pakoras

These veggie-packed pakoras are crispy and perfect dipped in a mint chutney.

By Jamie Lauren

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Mixed Vegetable Pakoras

  • Avocado, canola, or sunflower oil to fry in
  • 1 cup chickpea flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon indian chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 3/4 cup sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt + extra to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 cups curly kale, torn into small pieces
  • 1 zucchini, grated
  • 1 cup yellow onion, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1 serrano chili, minced
  • Coconut-mint chutney

Coconut-Mint Chutney

  • 1 cup mint leaves, picked
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 2 cups cilantro, leaves and stems
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 serrano chilies, cut into large chunks
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Water, as needed
  • Kosher salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat about 2-inches of oil in a heavy-bottomed sauté pan over medium heat until it reaches 350°F.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large bowl, whisk together the chickpea flour, spices, salt, and baking powder. Stream in water to form a thick batter. Fold in the curly kale through the serrano chili until everything is well covered.
