Chocolate Espresso Stuffed Cookies with Kahlua Glaze

Rich and decadent chocolate cookies.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 24
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Cookies

  • 8 ounces salted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup dark brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup plus 1 tablespoon dark cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons instant espresso powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 1/4 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Caramel candy, lightly flattened

Glaze

  • 1 cup powdered sugar, sifted
  • 1 tablespoon light corn syrup
  • 3 tablespoons coffee liqueur
  • 1/2 tablespoon water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line two baking sheets with parchment.
  2. Using an electric mixer, beat the butter until smooth, about 1 minute, then add the sugar and dark brown sugar.
