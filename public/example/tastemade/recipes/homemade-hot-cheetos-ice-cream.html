Homemade Hot Cheetos Ice Cream

Homemade Hot Cheetos ice cream is the surprising dessert you didn't know you needed! This fun ice cream recipe uses everyone's favorite store-bought chips as well as an ice cream maker for the best results.

By julie nolke

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 6
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup Flamin Hot Cheeto Dust
  • 1/2 cup Flamin Hot Cheetos, chopped

Steps

  1. Freeze ice cream maker base as recommended by manufacturer.
  2. In the saucepan, combine milk, cream, vanilla and half of the sugar. Set over medium heat and cook, stirring occasionally, until mixture simmers, about 5 minutes.
