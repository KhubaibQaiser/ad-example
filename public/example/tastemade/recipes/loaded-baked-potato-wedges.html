Loaded Baked Potato Wedges​

Serve your favorite grilled entrées with a side of loaded baked potato wedges topped with sour cream, cheese, chopped bacon, scallions, and zesty KRAFT Garlic Aioli for maximum flavor.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 6
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes, cut into 1/8​th's
  • 2 tablespoon neutral oil​
  • Salt and pepper, to taste​
  • 1/2 cup cubed butter​
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar​
  • 1/2 cup scallions​
  • 1/2 cup crumbled bacon​
  • 1/2 cup KRAFT Garlic Aioli

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill. ​
  2. ​Toss the potato wedges in oil, salt, and pepper. Place on a medium hot grill and cook potatoes, until crispy and golden, about 15-20 minutes. ​
  3. ​Remove and toss with butter. Once melted, plate the wedges and top with KRAFT Garlic Aioli, crumbled bacon, scallions, cheddar cheese. Serve immediately. ​
