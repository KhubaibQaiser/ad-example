Eva’s Roasted Branzino stuffed with Tomato-Ginger Sofrito

Dig into perfectly roasted fish this summer by grilling branzino atop your barbecue. The skin gets perfectly crisp, while a tomato-ginger sofrito stuffing ensures that the inside of the fish stays moist and tender!

By Dale Talde

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe plum tomatoes, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 knob ginger, minced
  • 1 bunch scallions, sliced
  • 1/4 tablespoon fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 whole branzinos, gutted
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro
  • 1/2 bunch scallions, sliced

Steps

  1. Season the tomato, garlic, onions, ginger and scallions with fish sauce and place in a large cast iron skillet on the grill over high heat.
  2. Leave one side of the grill on high and turn off the other side.
