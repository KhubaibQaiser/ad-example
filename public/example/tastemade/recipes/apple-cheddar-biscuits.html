Apple Cheddar Biscuits

Apple and cheddar make a surprisingly perfect pair, especially with a sweet honey butter spread.

By jerrelle guy

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Apple Cheddar Biscuits

  • 1 apple, cut into small pieces
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons pure cane sugar
  • 1 stick cold butter, diced into pea-sized chunks
  • 3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup iced milk

Honey Butter

  • 1 stick butter, softened
  • 3 tablespoons raw honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. In a small skillet, heat the coconut oil, and sauté the diced apples until tender. Season with cinnamon, nutmeg and sugar and cook until browned.
  2. Add the apple cider vinegar to deglaze the pan, loosening all the bits still stuck to the pan. Remove from the heat and allow to cool completely.
