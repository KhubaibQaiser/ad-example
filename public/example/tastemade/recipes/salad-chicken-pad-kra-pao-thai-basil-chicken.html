Salad Chicken Pad Kra Pao (Thai Basil Chicken)

This easy Thai basil chicken will save your weeknight.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 1
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 steamed chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/4 onion, diced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 eggplant, diced
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons thai fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • Powdered chicken stock, as needed
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 chilli pepper, finely chopped
  • 10 basil leaves
  • 7 ounces rice
  • 1 fried egg
  • 2 basil leaves, for decoration

Steps

  1. Finely chop the cooked chicken breast.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan. Cook diced onions until softened. Add in chicken, red bell pepper, and eggplant.
