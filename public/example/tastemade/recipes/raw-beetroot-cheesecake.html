Raw Beetroot Cheesecake

You Can Do That With Beets?!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 12
Average
 :

Ingredients

Crust

  • 5 1/4 ounces walnuts
  • 8 dried figs
  • 2 tablespoons raw cacao
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 pinch sea salt

Beetroot Layer

  • 10 1/2 ounces raw beetroot, peeled and finely grated
  • 7 ounces coconut butter or creamed coconut
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup

Chocolate Drizzle

  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
  • 2 tablespoons cacao powder
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 3 tablespoons pistachios, crushed

Steps

  1. Prepare a 9-inches square cake tine by lining it with baking paper. Set aside.
  2. Place the walnuts into food processor, pulse few times until they are broken down.
