Bright Citrus Salad

Fresh orange, pink grapefruit, arugula & radicchio form a fresh base for this summery salad dressed with chilli, cilantro seed & roast hazelnut salsa. Makes a great appetiser, paired with Casa Noble Blanco.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Salad

  • 2 oranges, segmented
  • 2 pink grapefruits, segmented
  • 2 nectarines, cut into wedges
  • 4 large handfuls of chicory & radicchio
  • 2 handfuls of arugula
  • 2 cups roasted hazelnuts, roughly chopped

Dressing

  • 1 ½ tsp coriander seeds
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp sherry vinegar
  • 2 tbsp agave syrup
  • Surplus juice from grapefruit and orange

Steps

Salad

  1. Combine the salad ingredients in a bowl aside from the hazelnuts. Pour over the salad dressing, toss and plate up. Sprinkle over the roasted hazelnuts, a dash of olive oil and season to taste.
  2. Serve immediately and enjoy.

Dressing

  1. Toast the coriander seeds in a pan over a medium heat. Take off the heat then add the rest of the dressing ingredients. Mix well, add salt and pepper and leave for a few minutes to infuse while you prepare the salad.
