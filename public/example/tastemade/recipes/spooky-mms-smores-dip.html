Spooky M&M’S® S'mores Dip

For a Halloween treat with a haunted twist, whip up this easy and spooky M&M’S® s'mores dip — made with milk chocolate, melty marshmallows, and candy eyeballs.

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 16
Easy
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup M&M’S® Ghoul’s Mix
  • 11.5 ounces milk chocolate chips
  • 48 regular sized marshmallows
  • 5 teaspoons orange sanding sugar
  • 5 teaspoons purple sanding sugar
  • 5 teaspoons black sanding sugar
  • 5 teaspoons green sanding sugar
  • Candy eyeballs
  • 24 graham crackers, divided in half
  • Toothpicks

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. In a 10 inch cast iron skillet, evenly sprinkle chocolate chips in the pan. Top with 1/4 cup of M&M’S® Ghoul’s Mix, then set aside.
  2. Set up a dipping station with a shallow bowl of water and separate bowls of the sanding sugars. Using a toothpick, pierce a marshmallow and dip one side into the water, shaking off any excess. Immediately dip into sanding sugar and roll to coat the wet marshmallow. Only the top and sides of the marshmallow need to be coated.
  3. Starting on the outer edge of the cast iron skillet, place dipped marshmallows on top of the chocolate, sanding sugar side up. Repeat with other colors, placing in the skillet in a bullseye pattern, making sure not to have the same color next to each other.
  4. When the chocolate chips are covered with marshmallows, place the skillet in the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until chocolate is melted and marshmallows have grown in size and filled in any gaps. Meanwhile, break graham crackers in half into rectangular pieces.
  5. Remove the skillet from the oven and top with candy eyeballs in varying sizes and remaining M&M’S® Ghoul’s Mix. Serve immediately with graham crackers for dipping. Enjoy!
