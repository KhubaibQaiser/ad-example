Suya Spice Brisket Cuban Sandwich

Suya Spice Brisket Cuban Sandwich is a fusion of African and Cuban cuisine, featuring tender and flavorful brisket marinated in a spicy Suya rub, layered with pickles, ham, Swiss cheese, and mustard, and served on a toasted roll.

By derrell smith

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Suya Spice Mix

  • 2 cups roasted peanuts
  • 4 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 3 tablespoons ground ginger
  • 2 tablespoons ground cayenne pepper
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 3 tablespoons onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon chicken bouillon

INSTAPOT SUYA BRAISED BRISKET

  • 1 1/2–2 pounds beef brisket, 2 inches thick
  • 3–5 drops liquid smoke
  • Suya spice mix
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 2 tablespoons acv
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar

CUBAN SANDWICH

  • 1 loaf french bread
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons yellow mustard
  • 1 pound suya brisket
  • 4 ounces picante provolone
  • 4 ounces sliced dill pickles

Steps

  1. Roughly blend the roasted peanuts using the pulse button of your blender. You can also use a spice grinder or a food processor. Be careful not to blend it into peanuts butter.
  2. Transfer the ground peanuts onto a clean kitchen towel.
