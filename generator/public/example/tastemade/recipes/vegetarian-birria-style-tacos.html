Vegetarian Birria Style Tacos

This vegetarian take on birria tacos uses jackfruit "birria" in place of meat but doesn't skimp on spice or flavor.

(1)Recipe Notes (1)

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

Braising Liquid

  • 3 dried guajillo chiles, stems and seeds removed
  • 1 dried ancho chile, stem and seeds removed
  • 1 dried arbol chile
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobos
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 28 ounces whole or crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper

Jackfruit Birria

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 40 ounces jackfruit

Tacos

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 12 small corn tortillas
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 white onion, diced

Steps

  1. Remove the stems and seeds from the chiles by cutting them down the middle length-wise, and scraping the seeds out with the knife.
  2. Heat a cast iron skillet over medium heat and add the chiles. Toast for 2–3 minutes until fragrant, then place in a medium sized heat proof bowl.
