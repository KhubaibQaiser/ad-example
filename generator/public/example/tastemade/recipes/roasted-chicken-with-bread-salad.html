Roasted Chicken with Bread Salad

Get extra crispy skin, minus the grease when you cook your chicken vertically. Want it? Click here: http://taste.md/2frncoZ

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 50 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Roasted Chicken

  • 4–5 pounds whole chicken, rinsed and patted dry
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • 8 garlic cloves
  • 1 handful fresh thyme
  • 1 handful fresh oregano
  • 3 sprigs fresh rosemary

Bread Salad

  • 1 loaf pane rustica, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • Pan drippings from roasted chicken
  • Extra-virgin olive oil (as needed), plus 3 tablespoons
  • 1 large shallot, sliced
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups wild arugula
  • 1 1/2 cups heirloom cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/3 cup pitted green or castelvetrano olives, quartered
  • 1/3 cup roasted, unsalted almonds, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup currants
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1/3 cup shaved parmesan cheese, for garnish
  • Fresh oregano, for garnish
  • Basil leaf, for garnish

Special Equipment

  • Vertical roaster
  • Butcher’s twine

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F, leaving one rack on the lowest setting (and removing additional racks if necessary).
  2. Line a baking sheet with foil. Assemble the vertical roaster according to package instructions and place on the foil-lined baking sheet.
