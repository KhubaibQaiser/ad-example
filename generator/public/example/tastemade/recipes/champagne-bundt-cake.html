Champagne Bundt Cake

Why simply sip on Champagne when you can bake it into a zesty bundt cake?

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 8
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Champagne Bundt Cake

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup Champagne
  • 1 teaspoon Champagne extract
  • 1 tablespoon grapefruit zest
  • 2 tablespoons grapefruit juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour

Champagne Glaze

  • 1/4 cup Champagne
  • 2 cups powdered sugar, sifted
  • Pink food coloring, as needed (optional)

Steps

Champagne Bundt Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Prepare a bundt pan greased with butter and dusted with flour.
  2. In a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, beat softened butter until creamy and begins to lighten in color. Sprinkle in sugar. Continue to cream mixture until fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, scraping down the sides after each addition.
  4. Next, add in sour cream and mix until homogenous. In another bowl, mix together dry ingredients and, alternating with the remaining liquids, add to the mixing bowl.
  5. Pour into prepared bundt pan and bake for 45 minutes to an hour, or until a skewer comes out clean.
  6. Once bundt cake is cooled to room temperature, turn out on a wire rack and prepare your Champagne glaze.

Champagne Glaze

  1. Whisk champagne into powdered sugar until smooth.
  2. Glaze will harden as it sits. For best results, pour over cake right away or cover directly with plastic wrap so a skin doesn't form.
