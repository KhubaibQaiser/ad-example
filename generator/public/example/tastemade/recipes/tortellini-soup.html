Tortellini Soup

A classic tortellini soup with homemade chicken broth

(1)Recipe Notes (1)

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Broth

  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 2 celery stalks
  • 2 whole carrots
  • 1 chicken carcass
  • 1 chicken backbone
  • Giblets from 1 chicken
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 sprigs thyme
  • 2 tablespoons parsley
  • 1 teaspoon of whole black peppercorns

Tortellini Soup

  • 12 ounces frozen tortellini
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chopped chives

Steps

  1. Peel the onion and cut it in half. Cut celery stalks and carrots into 3 inches pieces.
  2. In a large stock pot, add the chicken carcass, backbone and giblets, saving the liver for another recipe. Add the onion, celery, carrots, thyme, parsley and peppercorns into the pot. If you have additional vegetable scraps saved for stocks, add them to the pot.
