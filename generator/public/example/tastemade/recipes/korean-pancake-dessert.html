Korean Pancake Dessert

This sweet Korean pancake is filled with sesame seeds, walnuts, brown sugar, and cinnamon.

By Derrell Smith

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Mad Good Food

Prep Time: 1 hr 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Pancake/Hotteok

  • 1 1/3 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dry active yeast
  • 1 tablespoon neutral oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sweet rice flour

Pancake Filling

  • 1/4 cup roasted sesame seeds
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, ground
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon

Steps

  1. In a large liquid measuring cup, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast until completely dissolved.
  2. Let it sit for 5 minutes or until the yeast activates and creates some foam on the surface.
