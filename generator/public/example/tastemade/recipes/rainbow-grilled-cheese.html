Rainbow Grilled Cheese

All the cheesy goodness of traditional grilled cheese, but now with crazy rainbow cheese pulls.

(1)

By priscilla gonzales

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated mozzarella cheese
  • Food coloring (red, blue, green, purple)
  • 4 slices slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Rainbow sprinkle

Steps

  1. Place even amounts of cheese in 4 separate bowls. Pour different food coloring in each bowl, and mix until cheese reaches desired color.
  2. Butter one side of a slice of bread, and place on pan butter side down. Quickly place cheese in rows (red, green, blue, purple).
