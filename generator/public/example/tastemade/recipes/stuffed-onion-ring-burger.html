Stuffed Onion Ring Burger

Whether it's lunch, dinner, or a weekend barbecue, this hamburger recipe is sure to impress. Combine your love of onion rings with a perfect burger by stuffing the patty inside of a thick, battered onion ring!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 4 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Stuffed Onion Ring Burger

  • 1/2 gallon vegetable or peanut oil, for frying
  • 1 large white or yellow onion, peeled and cut crosswise into 4, 1 1/2 inches-thick slices
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1 teaspoon granulated onion
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon golden brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 pounds 80/20 ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices american cheese
  • 1 tablespoon whole grain mustard
  • 4 butter lettuces
  • 12 slices dill pickles
  • 4 slices center-cut thick bacon, cooked
  • 4 slices tomato
  • 1/2 avocado, thinly sliced

Spicy Mayo

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon sambal chili sauce

Steps

  1. In a large, heavy pot, heat the oil over medium heat until temperature reaches 350°F.
  2. In a small shallow baking dish, add 1 cup of flour. In a second baking dish, add the buttermilk and eggs and mix to combine. In a third baking dish, add the remaining 1 cup of flour and all of the spices and sugar. Mix to combine.
