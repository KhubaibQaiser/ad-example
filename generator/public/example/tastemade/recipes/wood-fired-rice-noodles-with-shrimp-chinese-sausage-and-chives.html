Wood Fired Rice Noodles with Shrimp, Chinese Sausage and Chives

Savor the sizzling goodness of grilled wok-fired rice noodles, tossed with juicy shrimp, savory Chinese sausage, and fresh chives for an irresistible smoky delight.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 10/16/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4

Ingredients

Wood Fired Rice Noodles with Shrimp, Chinese Sausage and Chives

  • 1 package xl rice noodles
  • 1/2 cup avocado oil, divided
  • 1 pound shrimp
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 3 shallots, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1 cup sliced chinese chives or scallions
  • 1 cup chinese sausage, sliced into rounds
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup finely chopped peanuts

Sauce

  • 1 cup shaoxing wine
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce

Steps

  1. Soak the noodles in hot water for 10–15 minutes, until soft.
  2. Heat the wok in the charcoal.
