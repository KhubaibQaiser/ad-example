Root Vegetable Gratin

A colorful and creamy seasonal root vegetable dish topped with buttery breadcrumbs.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 1 hr 25 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 5 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dijon
  • 3 sprigs thyme, leaves only
  • 2 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flake
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups grated mozzarella cheese
  • 2 large red beets
  • 2 large golden beets
  • 2 orange sweet potatoes
  • 1 large purple sweet potato
  • 1 rutabaga
  • 1 turnip
  • 1 large russet potato
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 1/2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons parsley
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. In a small pot, heat the heavy cream, dijon, thyme, garlic and red pepper flakes just to a simmer. Season with salt and pepper. Turn off the heat and set aside.
