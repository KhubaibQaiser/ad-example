Mofongo

Experience the savory delights of mofongo, a Puerto Rican classic made from mashed green plantains, seasoned with garlic, and accompanied by crunchy pork cracklings, immersed in a flavorful broth for an authentic taste sensation

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 head of garlic
  • 1/4 cup olive oil, divided
  • 3 ripe plantains, peeled
  • 1/2 pound thick cut applewood smoked bacon
  • 1 lime, halved
  • 1/2 bunch of scallions, sliced
  • 1/2 cup minced cilantro
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the grill.
  2. Cut the top off of the head of garlic and drizzle with oil and salt and pepper.
