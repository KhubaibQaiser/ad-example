Fresh Fruit Spring Rolls

Tired of boring fruit salad? Turn it into beautiful spring rolls instead!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Servings: 3
Average
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Fresh Fruit Spring Rolls

  • Rice paper
  • Filling 1: pomegranate seeds, kiwi, pineapple
  • Filling 2: strawberries, mango, peach
  • Filling 3: mint leaves, raspberries, mango

Dipping sauce

  • Juice from 1/2 lemon
  • Juice from 1/2 lime
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Steps

  1. First, slice all of the fruit into thin rounds or long sticks.
  2. Working with one sheet at a time, soak a rice paper sheet in warm water for 5-10 seconds, until it softens. Lay flat on a plate and place your desired fillings in the center. Wrap into a spring roll, making sure to pull the rice paper taught - this will give you a tightly-rolled spring roll. Arrange on a plate.
