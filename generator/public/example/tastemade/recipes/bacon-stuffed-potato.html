Bacon Stuffed Potato

Meet the ultimate side dish for your weeknight dinner: A perfect baked potato, stuffed with bacon and cheese!

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons clarified butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Rosemary
  • 2 sirloin strip steaks
  • Cream cheese
  • 1 cup saffron rice, cooked
  • 1 cup grated gruyere cheese
  • 8 slices smoked bacon

Steps

  1. Clean the potatoes and brush with butter. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and rosemary.
  2. Wrap with aluminum foil and bake in the oven at 350°F for 35 to 40 minutes until cooked through.
