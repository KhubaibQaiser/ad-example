Chocolate Blackout Cake

Chocolate lovers, we're looking at you! Sink your teeth into this decadent chocolate masterpiece for a heavenly dessert.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 10 min
Servings: 12
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

Ganache

  • 21 1/4 ounces dark chocolate
  • 10 1/4 fluid ounces thickened/whipping cream

Chocolate syrup

  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Chocolate Blackout Cake

  • 1 stick butter, softened, plus more for pan
  • 2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon bicarb soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup coffee
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup semi-sweet mini choc chips

Steps

  1. Add chocolate and cream into a large microwave safe bowl. Microwave for 20 seconds at a time, mixing each time until smooth.
  2. Cover with plastic wrap and set aside to set.
