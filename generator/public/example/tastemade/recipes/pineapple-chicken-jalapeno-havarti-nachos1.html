Pineapple, Chicken and Jalapeño Havarti Nachos

The perfect recipes to share with friends and family

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Nachos & Toppings

  • 1 piece chicken breast, butterflied
  • 2 teaspoons paprika salt
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/2 piece small pineapple, peeled and cut into rings
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 9 ounces plain tortilla chips
  • 1 x small red onion, finely sliced
  • Pickled jalapeño
  • 5 ounces castello jalapeño havarti, grated
  • 1 bunch coriander, chopped
  • Guacamole
  • Sour cream
  • Lime

Cheese Sauce

  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 2 jalapeños, deseeded and finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons plain flour
  • 1/2 cup skimmed milk
  • 1 ounce castello creamy havarti, grated
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Sprinkle the paprika salt over the chicken and pineapple slices and drizzle with olive oil for 4–5 minutes on each side on the barbecue or on a hot griddle pan.
  2. Preheat the grill to high. Place the tortilla chips on a large baking tray and drizzle 4 tablespoons of the cheese sauce over them. Chop both the chicken and the pineapple into small pieces, scatter over the tortilla chips along with the sliced onion and jalapeños. Grate over the Castello Jalapeño Havarti and cook under the grill for 4–6 minutes until melty.
