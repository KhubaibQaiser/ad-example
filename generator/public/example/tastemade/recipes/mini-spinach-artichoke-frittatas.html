Mini Spinach Artichoke Frittatas

Make your breakfast for the week in one sitting and still be healthy!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 12
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 14 1/2 ounces artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 5 cups loosely packed baby spinach
  • 8 eggs
  • 4 slices bacon, crispy/crumbled
  • 1/3 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/3 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Grease a 12-counts muffin tin with olive oil.
