Sriracha Brined Fried Chicken

This take on fried chicken has sriracha stirred into its buttermilk brine.

By Derrell Smith

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Mad Good Food

Prep Time: 4 hr
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds bone in-thighs, legs, wings
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup sriracha
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons coarse ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce, optional
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup corn starch
  • Canola oil, for frying

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chicken pieces, buttermilk, sriracha, salt, pepper and hot sauce and refrigerate for at least four hours.
  2. Mix the paprika, garlic powder, with the flour, cornstarch in a medium bowl with a baking sheet set up next to it.
