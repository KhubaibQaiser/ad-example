Creamy Pasta with Prawns

A simple and satisfying pasta dish with juicy shrimp and rich flavors, sure to satisfy every single time.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups penne pasta, cooked al dente
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1 pound peeled shrimp
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of milk cream
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of dried tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground chili bell pepper
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • Chopped basil, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, sauté garlic in olive oil for about 2 minutes. Add shrimp and cook for 3 minutes on each side. Season with 1/2 portion salt, remove from pan and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add the cream and bring to a boil. Reduce to low heat and add mozzarella, sun-dried tomatoes, basil and ground chili.
