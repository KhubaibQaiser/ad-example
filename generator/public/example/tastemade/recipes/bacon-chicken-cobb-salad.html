Bacon Chicken Cobb Salad

Dig into a fresh salad in no time with the help of quality store-bought ingredients like crispy bacon, rotisserie chicken, and a pre-made bottled vinaigrette.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups Freshness Guaranteed Rotisserie Chicken, breast meat, cubed
  • 1 bag Marketside Chopped Romaine Mix
  • ½ red onion, sliced
  • 1 cup Grape tomatoes, halved
  • 6 pieces Great Value Thick Sliced Bacon, cooked crispy and chopped
  • 2 Soft boiled Marketside Organic Cage Free Brown Eggs, chopped or halved or quartered
  • 1 Avocado, cubed
  • 2 Tablespoons Chives, sliced
  • ½ cup Marketside Blue cheese crumbles
  • ½ cup Great Value Light Raspberry Vinaigrette & Marinade
  • 1 Tablespoon Great Value Red Wine Vinegar

Steps

  1. In a large bowl toss together Chopped Romaine Mix, red onion, salad dressing, and red wine vinegar. Transfer to a serving bowl.
  2. Arrange the rest of the ingredients atop the salad in rows, toss before serving. Enjoy!
