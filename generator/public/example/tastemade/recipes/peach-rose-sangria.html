Peach Rosé Sangria

Light, fruity, and floral, this boozy punch is the perfect summery beverage for an afternoon in the sunshine.

By jamie lauren

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 3 peaches, quartered
  • 1 bottle rosé wine, spanish
  • 1 pint strawberries, quartered
  • 1 pint raspberries, quartered
  • 1 persian cucumber sliced
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice, fresh
  • 1/2 cup orange juice, fresh
  • 6–8 thyme sprigs
  • 1/4 cup simple syrup
  • 3/4 cup hendricks gin (or a more floral forward gin)
  • 1 cup sparkling water
  • Ice

Steps

  1. In a pitcher, combine all ingredients over ice and top with sparkling water. Adjust sweetness as needed.
