Japanese Sando 3-Ways

Make your own trendy Japanese sandwiches at home with these three easy recipes! A Japanese egg sando, Japanese pork katsu sando, and fruit salad sando will all make you feel like you just visited an authentic Japanese mini mart.

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Egg Salad Filling

  • 5 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons kewpie mayo
  • 1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh chive, to taste

Pork Katsu Filling

  • 1 boneless pork chop
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup panko
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Tonkatsu sauce, to taste
  • Kewpie mayo, to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded green cabbage

Fruit Salad Filing

  • 1 strawberry, hulled and halved lengthwise
  • 1 kiwi, peeled and halved lengthwise
  • 1 canned peach slice
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tablespoon mascarpone cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon powdered sugar
  • Japanese or korean milk bread slice

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with 1 inch of water, insert a steamer basket, cover and bring to a boil.
  2. Add the eggs, cover, and steam.
