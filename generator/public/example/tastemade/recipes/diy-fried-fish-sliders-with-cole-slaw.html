DIY Fried Fish Sliders with Coleslaw

A Southern style fish fry but bite sized.

By danni rose

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Fried Fish Sliders

  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 2 pounds whiting or catfish, cut into 3-inches pieces
  • 1/2 cup yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon old bay seafood seasoning
  • 2 cups yellow cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup self-rising flour
  • 1/4 cup garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup onion powder
  • 1/4 cup paprika
  • 1/4 cup chicken bouillon powder
  • 1/4 cup kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon accent
  • Hawaiian roll slider bun
  • Coleslaw

Coleslaw

  • 2/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1 bag cabbage slaw mix
  • 1/4 cup chopped scallions, optional

Steps

  1. Fill a cast iron skillet nearly 2 inches up the sides with vegetable oil.
  2. Heat to 375°F.
