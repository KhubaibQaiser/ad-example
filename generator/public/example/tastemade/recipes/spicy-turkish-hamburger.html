Spicy Turkish Hamburger

Here's a simple summer dinner that you can cook outdoor on the grill or on the stovetop. The spiciness of the ground beef is tempered by fresh herbal garnishes, so you get a perfectly balanced bite.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Spicy Turkish Hamburger

  • 8 tortillas
  • 1/2 red leaf lettuce
  • Vegetable oil, as needed

A

  • 14 ounces ground beef
  • 1/2 large onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 ounce bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 5 italian parsley stems
  • 1 tablespoon potato starch
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

B

  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 2 teaspoons nutmeg powder
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons cumin powder
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Red Cabbage Marinade

  • 1/8 red cabbage
  • Salt, as needed
  • 1/4 lemon juice

Yukari (dried shiso leaves) Onion

  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 teaspoon yukari

Garnish

  • Mini tomato, as needed
  • 1/4 lemon

Steps

  1. Grate the onion and garlic cloves from A.
  2. Take the leaves off of Italian parsley and mince.
