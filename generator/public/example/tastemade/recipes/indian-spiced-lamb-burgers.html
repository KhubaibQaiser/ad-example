Indian Spiced Lamb Burgers

Classic Indian spices flavor this succulent lamb burger.

By Jamie Lauren

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :

Ingredients

Indian Spiced Lamb Burgers

  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated on a microplane
  • 4 tablespoons yellow onions, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint, leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, leaves, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 pinch cloves
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • 2 brioche buns, split
  • 4 tablespoons clarified or melted unsalted butter, warmed
  • Yogurt sauce
  • Onion chutney

Onion Chutney

  • 1 yellow onion, minced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon indian chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1–2 tablespoons leaves of fenugreek plant, dried
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin, ground
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • Kosher salt, to taste

Yogurt Sauce

  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, whole
  • 1 cup full-fat greek yogurt
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cilantro, chopped
  • Kosher salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients and mix together well.
  2. Form into two 8-ounces patties. Cook over high heat on a grill until medium, about 4 minutes per side.
