Kimchi

Ever attempted kimchi at home? It's easier than it seems! Chef Justin Horne shows us a simple method for homemade kimchi.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Servings: 1
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 chinese cabbage / 2 cauliflower leaves
  • 6 cloves of garlic, peeled
  • 1 thumb-sized piece ginger
  • 2 teaspoons caster sugar
  • 1 birds eye chilli
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang
  • 7 ounces daikon
  • 6 spring onions

Steps

  1. Quarter and remove the stalks and leaf spines from the cabbage and julienne, then chop it into 1 1/2-inches pieces and place in a large bowl. Sprinkle over 4 tablespoons of sea salt and massage it in with your hands.
  2. Cover the cabbage with cold water, set aside for 2 hours.
