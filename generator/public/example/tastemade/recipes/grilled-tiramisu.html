Grilled Tiramisu

For a smoky take on an Italian classic, grill pound cake and top it with tiramisu fillings.

By megan mitchell

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pound cake
  • 1/2 cup of whipped cream
  • 1/2 cup of mascarpone cheese, room temp
  • 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar
  • 4 tablespoons of brewed coffee, cold
  • 1/2 teaspoon of granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground coffee
  • Small chocolate bar

Steps

  1. Slice 4 slices from pound cake about 1/2 inch thick.
  2. On a low heat, place on grill and heat for 2 minutes each side. Meanwhile in a bowl add mascarpone, 2 tablespoons brewed coffee, ground coffee, and powdered sugar. Mix well.
