Pineapple-Coconut Upside Down Cake

Tropical flavors of pineapple and coconut scent this decadent upside down cake.

By Keith Corbin

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks + 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 2 1/2 cups + 2 tablespoons sugar, divided
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup shredded coconut, unsweetened
  • 1 can pineapple rings
  • Maraschino cherry

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Cream 2 sticks butter and 2 1/2 cups sugar in a mixer with paddle attachment.
