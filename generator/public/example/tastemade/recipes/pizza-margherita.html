Pizza Margherita

Make your own Margherita pizza from scratch with this recipe.

By daniele uditi

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 3 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Dough

  • 4 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon active yeast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt
  • 2 cups lukewarm water

Sauce

  • 28 ounces san marzano tomatoes
  • 2 pinches salt

Assembly

  • 1/2 cup fresh mozzarella or fior di latte
  • 6 leaves fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup parmigiano reggiano
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Dissolve the yeast in the water.
  2. In a large bowl add the flour and add the yeasty water and olive oil.
