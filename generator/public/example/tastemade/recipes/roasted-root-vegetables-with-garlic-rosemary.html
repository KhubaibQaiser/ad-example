Roasted Root Vegetables with Garlic & Rosemary

Seasoned warm and delicious roasted root vegetables that make the perfect side dish all year round

By Alex Hill

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Spice Spice Baby

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • Root vegetable
  • 2 russet potatoes, skin removed & chopped
  • 2 sweet potatoes, skin removed & chopped
  • 2–3 carrots, chopped
  • 3 sprigs rosemary, removed from stem & chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, zested
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2–3 tablespoons evoo

Steps

  1. Combine marinade in a bowl and whisk together, taste as you go to adjust seasonings (set some aside for the root vegetables)
  2. Preheat oven to 425°F.
