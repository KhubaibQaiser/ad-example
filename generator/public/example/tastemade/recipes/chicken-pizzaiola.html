Chicken Pizzaiola

Juicy, pan fried chicken breast prepared pizza style, with tomato sauce and mozzarella

(2)Recipe Notes (1)

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella ball
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 28 ounces tomato purée
  • 4 fresh basil leaves
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper

Steps

  1. On a plastic cutting board, cut each breast in half lengthwise. Season each cutlet on all sides with salt, pepper and garlic powder. Dredge the chicken in flour and set aside. Slice the mozzarella ball into thin rounds and set aside.
  2. In a large saute pan, melt the butter over medium high heat and add the olive oil. Add the chicken to the pan one at a time and pan fry until golden brown, 3–5 minutes. Remove to a paper towel lined plate.
