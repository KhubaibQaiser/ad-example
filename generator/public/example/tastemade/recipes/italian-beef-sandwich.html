Italian Beef Sandwich

Roast beef, pickled vegetables and sweet sauteed peppers on a hoagie roll

(1)

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 3 hr 10 min
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chuck roast
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 beef bouillon cubes
  • 2 sprigs thyme
  • 2 green bell peppers
  • 4 hoagie rolls
  • 1 cup minced giardiniera

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F.
  2. Season the chuck roast with salt and pepper. Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a large dutch oven over high heat. Add the roast and sear for 7–8 minutes per side. Remove to a plate. Turn the heat down to medium.
