Vegetable and Spare Ribs Paella

The one-dish meal to prepare when you want to look fancy and cultured.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Vegetable and Spare Ribs Paella

  • 10 1/2 ounces pork spare ribs
  • 1/3 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/3 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 1/4 onion
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon powdered bouillon
  • 7 fluid ounces water
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Sauce

  • 1 teaspoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon potato starch

Steps

  1. Heat some oil in a frying pan and sear the ribs on all sides.
  2. Add bell peppers, eggplant slices and salt and saute for a minute. Transfer to a plate.
