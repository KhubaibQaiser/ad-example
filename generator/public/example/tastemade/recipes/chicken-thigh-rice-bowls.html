Chicken Thigh Rice Bowls

Creamy yogurt rice topped with crispy chicken thighs will be your new favorite comfort meal.

(1)

By Daisy Ryan

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Chicken Thighs

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 3/4 cup olive oil, divided
  • 1/2 serrano, minced
  • 1/2 tablespoon chili flakes
  • 1 lime, zest
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • Yogurt rice
  • Coconut cilantro broth
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 bunch scallions, sliced

Yogurt Rice

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 1 cup goat milk yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chili flakes
  • Kosher salt, to taste

Coconut Cilantro Broth

  • 2 cans coconut milk
  • 1 bunch cilantro, leaves and tender stems
  • 1 serrano, chopped
  • Kosher salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Put all the chicken thighs in a bowl and toss in 1/2 cup olive oil, serrano, chili flakes, lime zest and salt and pepper.
  2. Let sit in seasoning for at least 30 minutes.
