Vodka Martini With Boursin Cheese Stuffed Olives

Shaken or stirred, the perfect dirty martini isn’t complete without a garnish. Olives stuffed with Boursin® Garlic & Fine Herbs Cheese take your classy cocktail to the next level, so you can sip and snack like a true adult.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons Boursin Garlic & Fine Herbs cheese
  • 6 green Spanish olives
  • 5 ounces vodka
  • 1 ounce dry vermouth
  • 1 ounce olive brine

Steps

  1. Using a small cheese knife, fill each olive with approximately 1/4 teaspoon of Boursin cheese. Set aside while you prepare the martinis.
  2. Fill a shaker three-fourths of the way with ice. Add vodka, vermouth and olive brine.
  3. Shake until canister is very cold to the touch. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
  4. Pierce three Boursin stuffed olives with a toothpick and garnish each martini.
