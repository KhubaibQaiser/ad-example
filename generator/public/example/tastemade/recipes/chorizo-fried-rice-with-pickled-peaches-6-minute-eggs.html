Chorizo Fried Rice with Pickled Peaches & 6 Minute Eggs

Sweet & savory chorizo fried rice with jammy eggs and tangy stone fruit

By Dale Talde

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Chorizo Fried Rice and Eggs

  • 1/4 cup grapeseed oil
  • 1 cup diced spanish chorizo
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 scallions, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 3 cups day old rice
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1/2 cup cilantro leaves
  • 3 eggs

Pickled Peaches

  • 1 unripe peach, sliced
  • 1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander seeds

Steps

  1. Heat a large cast iron pan on the grill, add grapeseed oil and chorizo and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  2. Add the eggs and then onion, garlic, scallion, and ginger and saute for 2–3 minutes.
