Smoky Spinach Shakshuka

Weekend brunch calls for a savory, shareable main course. Chef Nadiya Hussain's shakshuka recipe incorporates smoky spices like paprika and cumin, plus spinach for added creaminess.

By Nadiya Hussein

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon tomato purée
  • 4 tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 2 teaspoons cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 7 ounces baby spinach leaves
  • 4 free-range eggs
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli flakes
  • Greek-style yoghurt
  • Toasted sourdough

Steps

  1. Place a large, lidded frying pan over a high heat. Add the oil, and as soon as it is warm, add the garlic.
  2. Reduce the heat and add the spring onions.
