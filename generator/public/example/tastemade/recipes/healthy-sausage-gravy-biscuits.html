Healthy Sausage Gravy & Biscuits

Who says you can't enjoy a flaky whole wheat biscuit topped with creamy turkey sausage gravy guilt free?

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/24/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 18 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Sausage Gravy

  • 1 pound lean ground turkey
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried sage
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne powder
  • 1/3 cup whole wheat flour
  • 3 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Biscuits

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 tablespoons butter, grated and chilled
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup almond milk

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix ground turkey and seasonings.
  2. Heat a pan on medium-high.
