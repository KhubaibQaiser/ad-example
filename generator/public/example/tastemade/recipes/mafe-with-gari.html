Mafe With Gari

This spicy stew made with sweet potatoes, carrots, ginger and more may just put you in the mood.

Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4

Ingredients

Mafe With Gari

  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 1 sweet potato with orange flesh
  • 1 sweet potato with white flesh
  • 2 carrots
  • Garlic powder
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • 5–6 cups vegetable broth
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2–3 cups peanut butter
  • Maca root powder
  • 1 red cabbage
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 lemon
  • Cilantro
  • Gari powder

Hot sauce

  • 5 habanero peppers
  • 3 grains djassan
  • Fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 little bit of onion
  • 1/2 tomato
  • Magic cube
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut orange sweet potatoes in a batonnet cut (thin sticks), white sweet potatoes in a large dice cut, and carrots in circles.
