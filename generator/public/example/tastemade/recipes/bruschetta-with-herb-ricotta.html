Bruschetta with Herb Ricotta

This decadent toast stars oregano and parsley-packed ricotta.

By daniele uditi

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 2 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough slices
  • 3 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup fresh whole milk ricotta
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of freshly ground black pepper
  • 5–7 oregano leaves, to decorate

Steps

  1. In a medium sauté pan heat the oil and toast the bread until golden and brown.
  2. Cut the garlic in half and rub the bread from the crispy side with the garlic.
