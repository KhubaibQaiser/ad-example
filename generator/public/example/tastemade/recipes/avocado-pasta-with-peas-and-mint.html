Avocado Pasta with Peas and Mint

Spicy and refreshing at the same time, chef Nadiya Hussain's easy pasta recipe is perfect for busy weeknights. Boxed pasta, cooked al dente, is tossed in a creamy avocado sauce for a surprisingly luscious dish.

By Nadiya Hussein

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
