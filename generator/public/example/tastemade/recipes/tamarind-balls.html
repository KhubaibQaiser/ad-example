Tamarind Balls

Sweet and tangy tamarind balls are popular all over with this recipe using a spicy Scotch Bonnet pepper to dial up the flavor.

By vijaya selvaraju

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups brown sugar, divided
  • 1/2 scotch bonnet pepper, de-seeded
  • 8 ounces tamarind pulp
  • 4 tablespoons white sugar
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. In a coffee grinder, blend 1/4 cup brown sugar with the Scotch bonnet until the pepper is finely ground.
  2. Transfer to a mixing bowl along with the remaining brown sugar, white sugar, the tamarind pulp and salt to taste.
