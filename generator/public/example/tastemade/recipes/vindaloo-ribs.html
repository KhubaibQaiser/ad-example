Vindaloo Ribs

Spice up your ribs with a sweet, citrusy Indian marinade and cucumber yogurt sauce.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 3 hr 15 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 2 slabs baby back ribs, membrane removed
  • 8 garlic cloves
  • 2 serrano chilies
  • 3 inches piece of ginger

Rub

  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 5 cloves cloves
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seed
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 4 dried chilies
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 10 peppercorns

Raita

  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 1/2 persian cucumber, julienned
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Zest and juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt and pepper and pepper

Glaze

  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1/4 cup onion, grated
  • 3 inches piece of ginger, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 tablespoon red chili flakes
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/3 cup tamarind paste
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup orange juice

Garnish

  • Lime
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Grind garlic, ginger and serranos in food processor.
  2. Rub into ribs, wrap tightly with plastic wrap and marinate for 2–24 hours.
