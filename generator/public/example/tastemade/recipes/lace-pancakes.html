Lace Pancakes

The key to a beautiful day is a lovely breakfast — start off your morning with pretty hotcakes.

By vijaya selvaraju

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 1 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Butter for greasing pan
  • Powdered sugar
  • Maple syrup
  • Nutella
  • Fruit jam

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, salt and sugar. Create a well in the middle of the dry ingredients, and add eggs and milk.
  2. Begin to whisk the wet ingredients, and slowly pull in the dry ingredients from the side, until batter comes together. Pour into a squeeze bottle.
