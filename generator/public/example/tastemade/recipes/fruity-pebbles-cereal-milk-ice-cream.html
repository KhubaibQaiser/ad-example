Fruity Pebbles Cereal Milk Ice Cream

Fruity Pebbles cereal milk ice cream counts as breakfast, right?

By laura miller

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 6 hr
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 1 1/4 cups fruity pebbles (or any cereal of your choice)
  • 14 ounces condensed milk

Steps

  1. Combine 1 cup of cereal and heavy cream. Let steep for at least 20 minutes. Pour cereal and milk through a strainer into another bowl, squeezing the cereal in all its soggy glory to get all the fruity flavor. Discard soggy cereal. (You should be left with at least 2 cups of cereal-flavored heavy cream.)
  2. Beat on medium speed with a hand mixer for about 3–5 minutes until soft peaks form. Add condensed milk. Fold in remaining 1/4 cup of cereal.
