Mom's Curry Chicken

Chef Rashida Holmes shares her mother's curry chicken recipe, a flavorful recipe featuring chicken legs marinated in a spicy, bright turmeric paste and cooked until it's falling off the bone.

By Rashida Holmes

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 4 hr
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Fenugreek Curry Powder

  • 2 teaspoons whole fenugreek
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons whole cumin
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons whole coriander
  • 2 eas allspice whole
  • 1 teaspoon whole mustard seed
  • 2 eas whole clove
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seed
  • 10 eighths fresh curry leaves or bay leaves

Turmeric Curry Paste

  • 10 cloves garlic
  • 2 3/4 ounces fresh ginger
  • 2 1/2 ounces fresh turmeric
  • 2 1/2 ounces scallions
  • 3 1/2 fluid ounces orange juice
  • 1 each scotch bonnet pepper or habanero

Chicken

  • 3–4 whole chicken legs with thighs
  • 2 white onions
  • 1 quart chicken stock
  • 1 cup canola oil

Steps

  1. Toast all the spices in saute pan until they begin to smell, remove from heat and let cool.
  2. Toast the curry leaves until the get hard about 30–45 second, remove from heat and let cool
