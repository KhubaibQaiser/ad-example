Deep Fried Sweet Chinese Buns

Super sweet fried buns of nom. You NEED to give these Chinese buns a try!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 5
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 8 3/4 fluid ounces whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon veg oil
  • 1/4 ounce active dry yeast
  • 8 1/2 ounces plain flour
  • 4 1/4 ounces sponge flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine salt
  • 4 tablespoons caster sugar
  • 2 tablespoons custard powder
  • Oil, for deep frying
  • Ice cream
  • Caramel sauce
  • Sea salt flake

Steps

  1. Warm the milk and oil in a saucepan over a low heat until lukewarm, then pour into a jug and sprinkle over the yeast. Allow to sit for 5 - 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile sift together the dry ingredients and mix well to combine. Make a well in the centre and pour the yeast mixture into the well, stirring all the time, until everything is incorporated. You are aiming for a 1 dough slightly sticky dough.
