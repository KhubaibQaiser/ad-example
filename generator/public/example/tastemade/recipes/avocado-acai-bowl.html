Avocado Acai Bowl

Save money with this cheap and easy acai bowl.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 large banana, sliced
  • 1 medium avocado, diced
  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened frozen acai
  • 1/4–1/2 teaspoon powdered stevia or agave nectar
  • 1 pinch wasabi powder
  • 1/4 cup freeze-dried raspberries
  • 2 tablespoons roasted pumpkin seeds
  • 1 lemon for zesting, for garnish
  • 1 piece dark chocolate, for garnish

Steps

  1. Blend banana, avocado, acai, stevia and wasabi in a blender — start with the banana and avocado on the bottom, and the acai, stevia and wasabi powder on top.
  2. Immediately spoon into serving containers. Top with raspberries and pumpkin seeds. Add a pinch of lemon zest, and grate some dark chocolate on top with a microplane for a fabulous texture.
