Frankie's Tiramisú

A classic, rich Italian dessert favorite with strong notes of chocolate, coffee and sweet mascarpone

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 12
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 16 ounces mascarpone cheese
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups strongly brewed coffee
  • 14 ounces lady fingers
  • 1 ounce chocolate, for grating

Steps

  1. Take eggs and mascarpone out of the refrigerator and allow to come to room temperature. Separate the eggs and place the yolks in a medium bowl and the whites in another.
  2. Add the sugar to the yolks and whisk until smooth. Add mascarpone and the vanilla extract to the yolks. Whip until smooth and slightly pale in color, about 3 mins.
