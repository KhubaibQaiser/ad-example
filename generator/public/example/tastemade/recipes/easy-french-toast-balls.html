Easy French Toast Balls

French toast just got a whole new look. These deliciously satisfying french toast balls are the perfect twist on a beloved classic.

(3)Recipe Notes (1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups bread crumbs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk
  • Maple syrup, for topping

Steps

  1. In a bowl mix the bread crumbs, sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, and milk until well combined.
  2. Add batter to waffle maker (takoyaki or aebleskiver pan) and cook for about 20 minutes or until browned, turning toast balls halfway through with a chopstick or wooden skewer.
