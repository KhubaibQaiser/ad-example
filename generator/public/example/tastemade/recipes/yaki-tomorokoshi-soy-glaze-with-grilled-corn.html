Yaki Tomorokoshi Soy Glaze (with Grilled Corn)

Fresh corn on the cob simply glazed with Japanese flavors makes for the ultimate summer side dish.

By Guy Turland

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Sourced

Prep Time: 1 hr 30 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 corn cobs
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 4 tablespoons mirin
  • 4 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 3 shallots, sliced

Steps

  1. In a pot on medium heat add all ingredients, cook till sugar dissolves and reduce by 1/3.
  2. Soak corn and skewers in water for about 30 minutes.
