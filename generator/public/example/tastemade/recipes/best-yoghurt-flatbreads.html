Yogurt Flatbreads with Lentil Meatballs

Try this vegetarian twist on British street food right at home! Whip up your own flatbreads from scratch, and top with crunchy lentil "meatballs" and a tangy yogurt sauce.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 20 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Flatbreads

  • 350 grams self-raising flour plus extra for dusting
  • 5 grams baking powder
  • 350 grams natural yoghurt plus
  • 50 grams for serving
  • 25 grams dill, chopped
  • 50 grams butter
  • 1 handful dill, finely chopped

Lentil Meatballs

  • 200 grams brown lentils
  • 1 onion, diced
  • Olive oil spray
  • 100 grams mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 23 grams oat flour
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 1 red chilli, finely chopped
  • Salad
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 pinch sumac
  • Dill, picked
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients to a mixing bowl and mix together with a spoon, then use clean hands to pat and bring everything together.
  2. Dust a clean work surface with flour, then tip out the dough. Knead for a minute or so to bring it all together.
