Peperonata (Sweet Peppers Side Dish)

This sweet, spicy side consists of caramely roasted bell peppers bathed in olive oil and dotted with salty olive, spicy Calabrian chili, and sharp garlic.

By daniele uditi

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 7 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 eaches large red bell pepper
  • 2 large yellow bell peppers
  • 1 larger garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/3 cup kalamata olives
  • 1/3 cup capers
  • 1/2 fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon calabrian chili
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 2 pinches black pepper

Steps

  1. In a half sheet tray, place the bell peppers and drizzle a little bit of olive oil. Roast them in a 500°F degrees oven for 6–7 minutes (or until the skin blisters).
  2. After cooked and while they’re still hot, place them in a bowl. Cover immediately with plastic and let it steam (this will make the skin detach easier).
