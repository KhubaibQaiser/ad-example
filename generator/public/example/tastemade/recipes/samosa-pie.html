Samosa Pie

Nadiya Hussain shows us how to create the perfect dishes to complement any kind of day; like her flavorful and flaky samosa pie filled with hearty lamb, potatoes, veggies, and aromatic herbs and spices.

By nadiya hussein

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 8
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Filling

  • 5 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 small onions, finely chopped
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp garlic granules
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tsp dried red chilli flakes
  • 500g/1lb 2oz lamb mince
  • 500g/1lb 2oz potatoes, peeled and cut into small cubes
  • 200g/7oz frozen peas
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • A large handful fresh coriander, finely chopped

Hot Water Pastry

  • 265g/9½oz plain flour
  • 55g/2oz strong bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 135ml/4½fl oz boiling water
  • 65g/2¼oz vegetable fat
  • 1 free-range egg, beaten, for glazing

Steps

  1. For the filling, heat the oil in a large saucepan over a medium heat. Add the onions and salt and cook until soft and translucent. This will take about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the ground ginger, garlic granules, cumin seeds and chilli flakes and cook for a few minutes. Add the lamb mince and cook until it is browned.
