Spring Chicken Tagine

Braised with tons of spices like cumin and turmeric, this Moroccan chicken dish is packed with bold flavors.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs, bone-in
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced and divided
  • 2 teaspoons cumin, toasted and ground
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon, ground
  • 2 teaspoons coriander, ground
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 shallot, sliced thin
  • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced diagonally into 1 inch lengths
  • 1 cup marinated artichoke hearts
  • 1 cup fava beans, shelled and blanched (can substitute lima beans)
  • 2 tablespoons preserved lemon, pulp removed
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken stock (or vegetable)
  • 1/2 cup ripe green olives, pitted
  • Crumbled feta
  • Fresh mint
  • Couscous

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. In a small bowl, combine: 1/2 cup the garlic, cumin, cinnamon, coriander, salt and pepper, stirring to combine. Season chicken liberally on both sides with spices, and marinate for at least an hour, preferably overnight.
