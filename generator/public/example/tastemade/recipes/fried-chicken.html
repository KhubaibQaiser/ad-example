Fried Chicken

Perfectly seasoned, perfectly juicy, homestyle buttermilk fried chicken

(1)

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/30/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Average
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 pounds bone-in skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 pounds drumsticks
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup cornstarch
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 3 ½ cups vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Add the buttermilk, egg, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt and pepper to a bowl and whisk to combine.
  2. Place the chicken pieces in a large resealable plastic bag and pour the buttermilk mixture over the top. Refrigerate for 6 hours or overnight.
