Poisoned Candy Apples

Poisoned Candy Apples to tempt your Halloween guests

By Mia Starr

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 12
Average
Â :

Ingredients

  • 12 eaches small, unwaxed apples
  • 4 2/3 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons black gel color
  • 12 eaches sticks for the apples

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the apples, and remove the stems from the apples. Place a stick in each apple and set spread out onto a lightly sprayed parchment lined sheet tray.
  2. In a medium sauce pan mix the granulated sugar, boiling water, cream of tartar and white vinegar until the sugar starts to dissolve.
