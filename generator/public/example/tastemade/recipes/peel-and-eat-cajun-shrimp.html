Peel and Eat Cajun Shrimp

Loaded with garlic and spices, peel-and-eat shrimp is great for parties.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
¬†:

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of divided butter
  • 2 pounds of large shrimp
  • 2 heads garlic, chopped
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 tablespoons of canola oil
  • 1 celery stalk, white parts discarded, green parts diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup of white wine
  • 1/2 cup amber lager
  • 2 tablespoons of worcestershire sauce
  • Juice of 1 orange
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 5 sprigs thyme, broken in half
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • French bread, torn
  • 1 scallion, sliced on a bias, for garnish

Steps

  1. Add 1 tablespoon butter to a large saut√© pan and place on medium-high heat. Once the butter has melted, add the garlic and cook for one minute.
  2. Add onions and another 1 tablespoon of butter, along with salt and canola oil, then celery and bell pepper. Cook for 5 to 10 minutes, until soft.
