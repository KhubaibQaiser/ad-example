Banana Pudding Cheesecake

Banana Pudding

  • 1/4 cup cornstarch

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
