Cheesy Chicken Verde Nachos

Take your nacho party platter to the next level with TOSTITOS® Original Restaurant Style smothered in cheesy chicken verde.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • TOSTITOS® Original Restaurant Style Chips
  • TOSTITOS® Chunky Salsa, Mild or Medium
  • 5 tomatillos
  • 1/2 large white onion
  • 1 Serrano peppers
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 bunch of cilantro, tops only
  • 1 4-ounce can green chiles
  • 1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon Mexican oregano
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 1/2 cups rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • 1 1/2 cups quesadilla cheese, or to taste
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • ¼ cup white onion, chopped
  • Crema, for serving
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In a medium saucepan, boil the tomatillos, onion and Serrano until tender, about 10 minutes.
  3. To the bowl of a blender add the boiled vegetables, garlic, cilantro, green chiles and salt. Blend until smooth.
  4. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and add a tablespoon of neutral cooking oil.
  5. Once oil is hot add the oregano and fry until fragrant.
  6. Pour the contents of the blender into the pan, add cumin and shredded chicken and simmer for a few minutes. This is a good point to check for seasoning, add a little salt if you like.
  7. On a nonstick or parchment lined cookie sheet, lay down a layer of TOSTITOS® Original Restaurant Style chips, top with a layer of chicken, then cheese and transfer to the oven for 5 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
  8. Garnish with avocado, onions, crema, and jalapeños, serve with TOSTITOS® Chunky Salsa and enjoy.
