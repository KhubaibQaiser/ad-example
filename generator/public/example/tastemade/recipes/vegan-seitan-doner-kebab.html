Vegan Seitan Doner Kebab

This vegan doner kebab uses seitan instead of meat for a satisfying late-night snack!

By Arron Crascall

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Gamer Snacks

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Doner

  • 1 kilo seitan log
  • 5 fluid ounces olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • Pita breads (oval, white)
  • Iceberg lettuce, chopped
  • Red cabbage, finely sliced
  • Turkish hot sauce
  • Long green chili pepper (turkish)

Garlic Sauce

  • 6 3/4 fluid ounces vegan yoghurt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper

Tabbouleh

  • 1 lemon, juice only
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 ounces fresh mint
  • 1 3/4 ounces parsley
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 1 dice white onion

Steps

  1. Preheat grill pan.
  2. Whisk together olive oil, garlic, cumin, salt and pepper.
