Jamaican Johnnycake Dumplings

Laz's Johnnycake dumplings are so light on the inside and crisp on the outside. Perfect for sopping up that gravy, baby!

By lazarus lynch

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • Canola oil, for deep frying
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/3 cup whole milk

Steps

  1. Heat canola oil in a deep skillet over medium-high heat until it registers 325°F.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt. Using your fingers, rub the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles pea-size crumbles.
