Salted Caramel Ganache

Sweet and savory caramel perfect as that extra special touch in your dessert creations!

By Bernadette Gee

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

The Unruly Baker

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 1
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 225 grams white sugar
  • 120 milliliters full fat cream
  • 90 grams salted butter cubbed
  • 12 grams sea salt flakes

Steps

  1. Put a deep pot over a low heat.
  2. Sprinkle 1/3 of the sugar in an even layer over the bottom of the pot and let it start to melt.
