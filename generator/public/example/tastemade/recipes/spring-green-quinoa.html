Spring Green Quinoa

Embrace fresh spring produce with Chef Frankie Celenza's vibrant, herby quinoa salad with crisp peas, arugula and toasted pumpkin seeds.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon olive oil, divided
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper
  • 8 ounces snap peas
  • 3 scallions, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley leaves and tender stems
  • 3/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint
  • 2 tablespoons chopped chives
  • 2 1/2 ounces arugula leaves
  • 1/4 cup pistachios
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1 lemon, juiced

Steps

  1. Place the quinoa in a fine mesh strainer and rinse under cold water. Add the quinoa to a medium sized saucepan with 2 cups of water and bring to a boil. Cover and turn down to simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat a large skillet and add 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Slice the onion and garlic. Add the onion, garlic and cumin to the pan and cook for 5–7 minutes until the onion is golden brown.
