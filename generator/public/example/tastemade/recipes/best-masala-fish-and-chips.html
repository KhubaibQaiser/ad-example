Masala Fish & Chips

Classic British fish and chips get a flavorful upgrade in this recipe thanks to the addition of masala, for an Indian-style fish and chips that will make your taste buds sing.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Fish Marination

  • 2 fillets white fish, each cut into 3 pieces, skinless
  • Boneless
  • 2 teaspoons ginger paste
  • 2 teaspoons garlic paste
  • 2 green birds eye chillies mashed into a paste
  • 2 tablespoons tandoori powder
  • Salt, to taste

Masala Fish & Chips

  • 1/4 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 pinch himalayan pink salt
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 potatoes (desiree/maris pipers work well),
  • Washed & cut into chip
  • Mustard oil (can use any oil if you don't have)
  • Mustard

Batter

  • 5 1/4 ounces plain flour, sifted
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons coriander seeds, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon sunflower oil
  • 6 3/4 fluid ounces chilled beer
  • Sunflower oil for frying
  • Plain flour, combined with salt to taste & a sprinkle
  • Of chilli powder

Steps

  1. For the fish marination, place fish fillets on a plate.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and massage into the fish pieces. Keep aside.
