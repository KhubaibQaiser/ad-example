Baked Eggs with Ham & Cheese and Clodagh’s Daily Happy Juice

Start your day with chef and food writer Clodagh McKenna's baked eggs with ham and cheese alongside her nutrient-packed "Daily Happy Juice."

By Numra Siddiqui

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

If These Meals Could Talk

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

For the baked eggs

  • 15 grams butter
  • 4 medium free range eggs
  • 75 grams cooked ham, shredded
  • 50 grams gouda cheese, grated
  • 5 milliliters dijon mustard
  • 1 1/2 grams grated fresh nutmeg
  • 2 1/2 grams thyme leaves,
  • 30 milliliters double (heavy) cream
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • To serve
  • Soda bread, toasted
  • Thyme leaf

For Clodagh’s daily happy juice

  • 30 grams baby leaf spinach
  • 100 grams pineapple or mango chunks
  • 1/2 apple, halved, cored and sliced
  • 1/4 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 celery stick, sliced
  • 1 1/4 centimeters root ginger, peeled and sliced
  • 59 milliliters natural yoghurt
  • 3–4 ice cubes

Steps

  1. To make the baked eggs, first preheat the oven to 180°C/Gas Mark 4 (fan oven).
  2. Grease two small ovenproof dishes with the butter and crack two eggs into each one. Divide the shredded ham between the two dishes.
