Chicken Thighs with Sake Mirin Marinade

Lots of homecooks sacrifice overcooking it for the char but you can make juicy chicken thighs without overcooking.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken legs
  • 1/2 cup sake
  • 1/2 cup mirin
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 big skewers

Steps

  1. Butcher the thighs, square them off and cut 1/2 inch x 2–3 inch strips, leaving the skin on.
  2. Skewer the legs.
