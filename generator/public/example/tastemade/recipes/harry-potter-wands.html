Harry Potter Wands

Bring magic to the kitchen with these chocolatey cookie wands covered in a delightfully glowing glaze.

By jerrelle guy

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 12
Easy

Ingredients

Chocolate Donut Wands

  • 3 cups whole wheat pastry flour, plus more for dusting
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground espresso powder
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup neutral oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar or white vinegar

Glow Glaze

  • 1/4 cup cornstarch or arrowroot powder
  • 1/2 cup tonic water, plus more for thinning
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • White glitter sprinkle, optional

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F, and prepare a sheet tray with parchment paper.
  2. Make the donut wands: In a large bowl, stir together the flour, cocoa powder, espresso powder, baking powder, and salt.
