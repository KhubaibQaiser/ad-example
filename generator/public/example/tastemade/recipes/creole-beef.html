Creole Beef

This hearty tomato and beef stew is packed with yummy veggies.

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 35 1/4 ounces beef
  • 5 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 choppeds chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 1 red pepper, chopped into pieces
  • 1 sliced carrot
  • 1 cup peas
  • 4 plum tomatoes
  • 1 package tomato sauce
  • 3 sliced potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of oregano
  • 1 tablespoon of ground pepper
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Chopped parsley and pepper
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a pan with olive oil, add the chopped garlic.
  2. Before garlic browns, add the onions, pepper and carrot. Sauté a few minutes.
