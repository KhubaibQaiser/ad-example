Green Mango With Sweet and Spicy Dipping Sauce (Nam Pla Wan)

This sweet and spicy Thai dipping sauce pairs perfectly with sour, crunchy fruits like green mangoes.

By vijaya selvaraju

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • Firm sour green mango, peeled and cut into slices
  • 1/2 cup dried shrimp
  • 3 bird's eye chilis
  • 3/4 cup palm sugar
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried red chili flakes

Steps

  1. In a food processor, pulse together dried shrimp and bird's eye chilis until coarsely ground.
  2. In a saucepan, add palm sugar, white sugar, red chili flakes, and fish sauce. Bring to a simmer and cook, stirring frequently, until thickened.
