Cream of Mushroom Mac & Cheese

Chef Keith Corbin's decadent mac & cheese channels a nostalgic favorite, cream of mushroom soup, for extra umami.

By Keith Corbin

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Cream of Mushroom Mac & Cheese

  • 1 pound dried elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 2 cups mushroom cheese sauce
  • 1/2 cup smoked cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, grated

Mushroom Cheese Sauce

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup crimini mushrooms, minced
  • 3/4 cup shiitake mushrooms, minced
  • 1/3 cup cello (white) mushrooms, minced
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup vegetable stock
  • 2 jalapenos, minced
  • 1 cup smoked cheddar, shredded
  • 1/2 cup sharp white cheddar, shredded
  • 3/4 cup mozzarella, shredded
  • 1/4 cup monterey jack, shredded
  • 1/2 cup smoked gouda, shredded
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. In a pot, salt 6 quarts of water and bring to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni until al dente. (A minute or two shy of what the box recommends.)
  2. Drain and rinse in cold water.
