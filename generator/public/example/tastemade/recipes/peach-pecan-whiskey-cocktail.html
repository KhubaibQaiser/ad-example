Peach Pecan Whiskey Cocktail

A Southern twist on the French 75.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 12 hr
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Peach & Pecan Whiskey

  • 1/2 cup raw pecan halves, toasted
  • 8 ounces frozen sliced peaches, thawed and dried
  • 2 1/2 cups whiskey
  • 1/2 cup honey

Cocktail Assembly

  • 4 ounces peach pecan whiskey
  • 2 ounces cognac
  • 1 ounce lemon juice
  • Prosecco, for topping
  • Candied pecan, for garnish

Steps

  1. Pour the pecans, peaches, whiskey and honey into an airtight container.
  2. Allow to steep in the fridge for at least 12 hours, or up to five days.
