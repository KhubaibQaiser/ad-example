Buttercream Sticker Sugar Cookies

A twist of the classic royal icing sugar cookie using a buttercream sticker technique. Easy to make, quick to bake, delicious to eat!

By Mia Starr

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 12 min
Servings: 24
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Sugar Cookies

  • 3 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs, room temperature
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract

Buttercream

  • 2 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 pound powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 pinch salt

Decoration

  • 1 drop assorted gel colors
  • 1 small small amount assorted sprinkles
  • Printed or drawn stencils of your choosing
  • Parchment paper

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, salt and cornstarch and set aside.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment.
