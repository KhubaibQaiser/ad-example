Chicken Gyoza

Whether you're eating them for dinner or serving them as an appetizer, these homemade gyozas — stuffed with chicken, ginger, and onion — are sure to be a hit.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 20
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 6 spring onions, roughly chopped
  • 3/4 inch ginger, roughly chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce + more for dipping
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 5 1/4 ounces minced chicken
  • Gyoza skin
  • Cornflour

Steps

  1. Blitz the spring onions, garlic and ginger in a food processor. Tip into a bowl and add the soy and oyster followed by the chicken. Mix well.
  2. To assemble the gyoza, hold the dumpling skin in the palm of one hand and put a heaped 1 teaspoon of the the filling onto the centre of the skin. Dip your finger in some water and wipe around the edge of the skin,then bring the edges of the skin together. Pinch pleats along one side, then press each pleat against the opposite flat side of the skin. With each pinch make sure that you are sealing the parcel and keeping the filling in the centre.
