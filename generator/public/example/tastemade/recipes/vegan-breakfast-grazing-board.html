Vegan Breakfast Grazing Board

A creative, vegan spin on the classic breakfast.

Updated: 9/28/2024

Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 4

Ingredients

Maple Glazed Vegan Bacon

  • 1/2 cup maple syrup

Board Assembly
