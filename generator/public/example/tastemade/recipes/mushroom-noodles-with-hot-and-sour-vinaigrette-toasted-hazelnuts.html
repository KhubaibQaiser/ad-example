Mushroom Noodles with Hot and Sour Vinaigrette & Toasted Hazelnuts

Perfectly prepared stir fried noodles with tangy vinaigrette and a hazelnut crunch on top

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 12 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Mushroom Noodles with Hot and Sour Vinaigrette & Toasted Hazelnuts

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 pound oyster and shitake mushroom mix
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 4 stalks scallions, sliced into 1 inch batons
  • 1/2 cup hazelnuts, toasted and crushed
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, sliced in half
  • 1 pound pre-cooked lo mein noodles (like fresh egg noodles)

Hot & Sour Vinaigrette

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sherry vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 teaspoons white pepper

Steps

  1. On the cast iron hibachi flattop, add oil and mushrooms.
  2. Add butter to the mushrooms and saute them for 5–6 minutes.
