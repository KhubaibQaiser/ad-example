Yogurt Chicken

Marinating chicken in spiced Greek yogurt is a foolproof way to keep it succulent and flavorful; pair it with your grain of choice and an herby salad for the perfect summer meal.

By tiffani faison

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds whole frying chicken, butterflied and scored
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard seed
  • 1 tablespoon whole cumin
  • 1 tablespoon whole coriander
  • 1 cup full-fat greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon madras curry powder
  • 1 whole jalapeno
  • 1 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 4 eaches garlic cloves, peeled and grated on microplane
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 ounces ginger, peeled and grated on microplane

Steps

  1. Toast spices and grind, leaving the mixture a little coarse.
  2. Add all ingredients, except for chicken into a blender and blend until completely combined.
