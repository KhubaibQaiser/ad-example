The Best Profiteroles

Chef Casey Corn shares her secret on how to make the best profiteroles. Though these cream puffs can seem intimidating, it's actually not too hard to make choux pastry from scratch!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 25
Average
 :

Ingredients

Choux Pastry

  • 1 cup water
  • 7 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup flour
  • 4–5 eggs
  • 1 egg, for egg wash

Mascarpone Filling

  • 1 cup mascarpone
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons heavy whipping cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Assembly

  • 1 cup strawberry jam, in a piping bag
  • 1 cup blueberry jam, in a piping bag
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. In a medium pot, add water, butter, salt and sugar and heat gently, just enough for butter to melt, and sugar and salt to dissolve.
