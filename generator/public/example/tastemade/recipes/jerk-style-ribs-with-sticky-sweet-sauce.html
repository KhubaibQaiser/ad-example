Jerk-Style Ribs with Sticky & Sweet Sauce

Jamaican inspired ribs that bring some heat.

By Danni Rose

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 3 hr
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Ribs

  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground thyme
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons allspice
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 teaspoon scotch bonnet powder (or cayenne pepper) (or cayenne pepper)
  • Zest of 1 lime (reserve for juice in sauce)
  • 3 tablespoons dark brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 2 racks baby back ribs

Sticky & Sweet Sauce

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 1/3 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons dark rum
  • Juice of 1/2 lime (about 1 tablespoon)
  • 3/4 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup chopped scallions, plus more for topping
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill to 300°F. To help regulate temperature, turn off one or two of the burners while cooking.
  2. For the ribs, remove the membrane from the back of the rack.
