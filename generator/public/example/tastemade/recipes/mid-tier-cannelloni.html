Mid-Tier Cannelloni

For chefs looking for a bit more of a challenge, this delicious cannelloni recipe is just a step above store-bought.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 2
Average
Â :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 3 cups ricotta
  • 1/4 teaspoon grated nutmeg
  • Zest of 1/2 lemon
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup parmesan, grated, divided
  • 10 cannelloni tubes
  • 1 can tomato pasta sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 pinch chili flakes
  • 1 splash red wine
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Blanch the spinach in boiling water for 30 seconds then submerge in ice water. Drain and squeeze out as much water as possible by placing into a clean tea towel and wringing out. Chop finely and place into a bowl with the ricotta, nutmeg, lemon zest, black pepper and 1/2 cup Parmesan. Mix to combine.
