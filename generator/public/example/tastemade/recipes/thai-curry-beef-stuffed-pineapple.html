Thai Curry Beef Stuffed Pineapple

Perfectly balanced the heat with a little sweet by baking your Thai beef in a pineapple.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Beef Curry

  • 14 ounces full-fat coconut milk, divided 1/4 and 3/4 cups
  • 1/2 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 (2-inches) fresh ginger, peeled and grated
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 fresh lime leaves
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 handful fresh cilantro, tender stems included, minced
  • 3 tablespoons store-bought, prepared red curry paste
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 5 ounces shredded low-moisture mozzarella cheese, divided

White Rice

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice

Garnish

  • Chopped roasted peanut
  • 1 red fresno chile, thinly sliced
  • Cilantro leaf, with tender stems

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F, and place an oven rack in the middle.
  2. In a large stainless steel or cast-iron pan over medium heat, add 1/4 cup of the coconut milk fat from the top of the can.
