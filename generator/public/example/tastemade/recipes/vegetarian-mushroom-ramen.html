Vegetarian Mushroom Ramen with Bok Choy & Jammy Egg

Making restaurant-worthy ramen at home is easier than you think.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 2
Easy
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Broth

  • Splash of cooking oil
  • 1/2 small red onion (reserve other half to pickle), sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1 carrot, peeled and rough chopped
  • Salt
  • 1 cup Holland House Marsala Cooking Wine
  • 4 cup dry shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 quart veggie broth

Pickled Onions

  • 1/2 small red onion, sliced
  • 1 serrano or fresno chili, sliced thin (stem removed)
  • 1/2 cup seasoned rice wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water

Jammy Eggs

  • 2-4 eggs

Mushrooms

  • Splash cooking oil
  • Salt
  • 8 ounce wild mushrooms (such as shiitake and oyster)
  • 1/4 cup Holland House Marsala Cooking Wine

Noodles & Garnish

  • 1 head baby bok choy, quartered then thinly sliced Cilantro, rough chopped
  • Spicy chili crunch - spoonfuls to taste
  • 3 packs instant ramen noodles or other ramen noodles

Steps

Broth

  1. Heat a soup pot over medium high heat. Add in splash of oil. Add in onion, garlic and carrot, season with a couple pinches of salt. Cook for 5 minutes until onions start to become translucent. Deglaze with Holland House Marsala Cooking Wine and reduce by half. Add in shiitake and veggie broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and simmer for 30 minutes. Should reduce by about 1/3. Strain, pushing to get all of the flavor out of the mushrooms. Taste and adjust seasoning.

Pickled Onions

  1. Put onion and chili in a 2 cup jar. Bring vinegar and water to a boil then pour over onion and chili, let cool until ready to eat. Can cover and hold in the fridge for a week if do not use it all.

Jammy Eggs

  1. Bring small pot of water to a boil carefully lower in eggs and boil for 6 minutes. Remove from water and run under cold water to cool to room temperature. Peel carefully. When ready to eat, cut in half and season with salt.

Mushrooms

  1. Heat a large sauté pan over high heat. Add in a splash of cooking oil. Add in mushrooms and sauté until get a little color. Season with salt and deglaze with Holland House Marsala Cooking Wine. Let cook until wine is absorbed and evaporated.

Noodles & Garnish

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Divide noodles among 4 bowls and ladle in broth. Top in sections with mushrooms, bok choy, pickled onions (removed from liquid), jammy egg half (or halves). Spoon garnish of chili crunch and top with cilantro.
