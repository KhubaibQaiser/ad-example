Porchetta Street Sandwich

This decadent sandwich stars herby pork belly porchetta.

By daniele uditi

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 45 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Pork Belly Porchetta

  • 5 pounds pork belly, skin attached
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 3/4 cup fresh sage
  • 3/4 cup fresh rosemary
  • 3/4 cup fresh dill
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Dipping Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 4 celery stems
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 glass wine
  • Porchetta dripping
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 pound pork bones
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon black pepper

Sandwich Assembly

  • 1 pizza dough or store bought baguette
  • 4 slices smoked mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup sautéed rapini
  • 2 cups roasted porchetta
  • 1 cup dipping broth

Steps

  1. Place all the herbs in a robot coupe along with the garlic and the oil.
  2. Blend until it becomes a paste.
