Lamb Kofte Mini Pita Burgers

Regular burgers are so passé — give this lamb köfte pita version a go instead!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Lamb Kofte Mini Pita Burgers

  • 14 ounces minced lamb
  • 1 small onion, coarsely grated
  • 2 garlic cloves finely chopped or grated
  • 2 tablespoons garam masala
  • 1 bunch cilantro, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes

To Serve

  • 4 pitta breads
  • 2 tomatoes, halved and sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced (optional)
  • 1 piece small pot plain yogurt

Steps

  1. Place the mince into a large bowl with all the the onion, garlic, garam masala, cilantro and chili flakes and a good pinch of salt. Mix well with your hands. Pat the mix into small burgers.
  2. To cook, heat grill to its highest setting and lay the burgers in a single layer on a baking tray (you may need to do this in batches, depending on how big your tray is).
