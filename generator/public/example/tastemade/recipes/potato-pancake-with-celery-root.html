Potato Pancake with Celery Root

For a fresh take on a classic potato pancake, mix in celery root.

By megan mitchell

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet baking potatoes
  • 1/2 piece of small celery root, whole
  • 1 small red onion, grated
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of chives, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 2 teaspoons of kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of freshly ground pepper

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes. Grate potatoes on a box grater.
  2. Place potatoes in a large bowl of cold water. Rinse and drain.
