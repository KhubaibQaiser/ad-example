Salmon And Pesto Rice Bowl

A salmon sushi bowl fused with Italian-inspired flavors is a game-changing meal.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 ounces cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon pesto
  • Black pepper
  • 1/2 avocado
  • Salt
  • 1/4 lemon
  • 3 1/2 ounces sashimi-grade salmon
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • Salmon roe
  • Black olive
  • Fresh basil leaf
  • Parmigiano reggiano

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine cooked rice, pesto and black pepper and mix to combine.
  2. Cut avocado into 3/4 inch cubes and combine with salt and lemon juice. Set aside and let it marinate.
