Banh Mi Sandwiches

Homemade banh mi sandwiches are packed full of flavor and nutritious chicken and veggies.

By Dini Klein

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 teaspoons ginger, minced
  • 1/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cups carrots, peeled and grated
  • 2 cups daikon, peeled and grated
  • 1/2 jalapeño, seeded and thinly sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups rice wine vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4–6 baguettes, halved
  • 1 bunch fresh mint
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. In a zip-top bag, add the ginger, brown sugar, garlic, sesame oil, vinegar and soy sauce.
  2. Let marinate for 15–20 minutes or overnight.
