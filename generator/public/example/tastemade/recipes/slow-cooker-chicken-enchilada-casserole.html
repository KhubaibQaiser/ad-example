Slow Cooker Chicken Enchilada Casserole

A cheesy chicken enchilada casserole is sure to please the family. Even better, this weeknight dinner comes together easily in a slow cooker — just set it and forget it!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 4 hr
Servings: 2
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 28 ounces red enchilada sauce
  • 8–10 corn tortillas, sliced into strips
  • 1 can black beans
  • 2 cups mexican blend cheese, divided
  • 1 jalapeño, sliced
  • Sour cream
  • Scallion
  • 1 avocado

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts in slow cooker. Season with salt, pepper, cumin and chili powder.
  2. Add onions, garlic and enchilada sauce. Cover and cook on high for 4 hours.
