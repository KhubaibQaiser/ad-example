Huevos Divorciados

Vegetarian, gluten free, nutritious, hearty, and deeply satisfying!

By Noah Galuten

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Don't Panic Pantry

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ripe tomatoes
  • 1 pound tomatillos, husks removed
  • Some neutral oil
  • Salt
  • 1–2 serranos (de-seed them if you want it milder)
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1 white onion, diced
  • Corn tortilla
  • Egg
  • Cooked black bean, warmed through

Steps

  1. Preheat your broiler, or preheat the oven to the highest temperature. Place the tomatoes, tomatillos, serranos and garlic in a large mixing bowl. Toss them just lightly to coat in oil and a hefty pinch of salt.
  2. Place them on a foil-lined sheet pan in a single layer and roast or broil, until they are blackened on top, then rotate the pan and flip the tomatoes and tomatillos. Return to the oven until they are charred but not mushy.
