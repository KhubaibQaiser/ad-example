Reno Cupcake Christmas Tree

This filling will give those grocery store cupcakes a new lease on life! And don't stop there make a clever design

By Mia Starr

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 24
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Cupcake Filling

  • 2 cups white chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 zest or orange
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Buttercream

  • 2 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 pound powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 pinch salt

Assemble and Decorate

  • 24 grocery store cupcakes, any flavor
  • 1 cup red sanding sugar
  • 1 cup gold sanding sugar
  • 1 cup blue sanding sugar
  • 1 cup purple sanding sugar
  • 1 cup white sanding sugar
  • 2–4 drops yellow gel color
  • 2–3 drops purple gel color
  • 2–4 drops blue gel color
  • 5 eaches square candy pieces
  • 1 tablespoon gold dragees
  • 1 tablespoon silver dragees
  • 1/4 teaspoon silver luster dust
  • 1/4 teaspoon gold luster dust
  • 1 drop green gel color
  • 2 drops brown gel color
  • 2–4 drops red gel color
  • Piping tip #2
  • Piping tip #3
  • Piping tip #4
  • Piping tip #823
  • Piping tip #864
  • Piping tip #1m

Steps

  1. Place the white chocolate chips in a medium heat proof bowl.
  2. Place the heavy cream, orange zest and cinnamon into a small bowl and microwave for 2 to 3 minutes or until the cream mixture is hot but not boiling.
