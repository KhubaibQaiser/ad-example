Mocha Swirls

Similar to cinnamon rolls or monkey bread, chef Nadiya Hussain's mocha swirls recipe is the perfect baked good to make in advance and share with guests all weekend long.

By nadiya hussein

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 9
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Filling

  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp instant espresso powder (or coffee granules)
  • 65g/2¼oz soft light brown sugar
  • 100g/3½oz dark chocolate, finely chopped (or chocolate chips)

Dough

  • 450g/1lb self-raising flour, plus extra for dusting
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp caster sugar
  • 100g/3½oz unsalted butter, melted, plus extra for greasing
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 2 medium free-range egg yolks
  • 200ml/7fl oz whole milk, plus extra for glazing

Icing

  • 2 tbsp boiling water
  • 2 tsp instant espresso powder (or coffee granules)
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tbsp full-fat cream cheese
  • 125g/4½oz icing sugar, sifted

Steps

  1. Mix together the melted butter, cocoa powder, espresso powder (or coffee granules), brown sugar and chocolate. Set aside.
  2. Grease a loose-bottomed round 20cm/8in cake tin. Preheat the oven to 180C/160F Fan/Gas 3.
