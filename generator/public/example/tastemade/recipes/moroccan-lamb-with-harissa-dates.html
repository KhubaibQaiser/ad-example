Moroccan Lamb with Harissa & Dates

Warm and cozy pull-apart tender lamb with a bit of sweet and spice for your table

By Alex Hill

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Spice Spice Baby

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 lamb shanks
  • 1 preserved lemon, chopped
  • 2 heaping tablespoons of harissa
  • 1 cup pitted dates
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 1/2 teaspoon saffron threads added to a bowl with 1 tablespoon of hot water (from the tap is fine)
  • 1 small onion, thickly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine (a good sauvignon blanc is good)
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon any moroccan spice blend
  • 1 dash of rose water & orange blossom water (this is optional but i really like the added depth of flavor to this!)
  • Note: if you can’t find moroccan spice blend, season the lamb with cumin, coriander and cinnamon, turmeric, garlic powder, smoked paprika, kosher salt & cracked black pepper, to taste
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Season lamb shanks with all the spices, salt, pepper, turmeric, moroccan spice blend, and smoked paprika
