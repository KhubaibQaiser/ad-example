California Cheese and Veggie Grain Bowls

A healthy, yummy snack that's anything but ordinary.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Easy
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup freekeh
  • 1 (15 1/2 ounces) can cannelini beans, rinsed and drained, divided
  • 3 tablespoons Real California sour cream or plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary leaves
  • 1 small clove garlic
  • Sea salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 cups arugula or baby kale blend greens
  • 1 cup Real California Gouda, baby bella mushroom or Sicilian Jack cheese, in small cubes
  • 1 cup roasted red pepper strips
  • 1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Bring water to a simmer in a medium saucepan. Stir in freekeh and cook for 10 minutes then remove from heat and keep warm.
  2. While freekeh is cooking, set aside 1/2 cup of beans and place the rest in a small food processor with yogurt, lemon juice, rosemary and garlic. Puree until smooth and season with salt and pepper.
  3. To assemble bowls, place equal amounts of warm freekeh into 4 bowls and top with greens, cheese, red peppers, onion and reserved beans. Top each with spoonful of white bean puree.
  4. Note: Freekeh (free-kha) is roasted green wheat. It's high in protein and has almost twice the fiber and is higher in protein than quinoa. It's also high in iron, calcium and zinc.
