Sabich Salad

This salad features eggplant, boiled eggs, and a citrusy aioli.

By einat admony

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Sabich Salad

  • 1 large or 2 small globe eggplant
  • Kosher salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 1/2 cup amba aioli
  • 1 cup drained and rinsed canned chickpeas
  • 1/2 pint cherry tomatoes halved
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro leaf, for garnish

Eggplant

  • 1 large or 2 small globe eggplant
  • Kosher salt
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Boiled Eggs

  • 4 large eggs

Amba Aioli

  • 2 tablespoons fresh orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon amba paste
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced or grated

Steps

  1. Spread a thin layer of aioli over a serving platter.
  2. Layer the fried eggplant rounds on top of the aioli.
