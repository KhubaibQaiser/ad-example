Peppermint Ice Cream Cone Cake

A peppermint stick ice cream cake with a chocolate layer and a crunchy sugar cone crust.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 8 hr
Servings: 16
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Cake

  • 20 ice cream cones
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 8 ounces semi sweet chocolate bars
  • 2 1/2 cups heavy cream, divided
  • 1 1/2 quarts pink peppermint ice cream, softened
  • 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 6 candy canes

Ice Cream Cone Topping (Optional)

  • 1/2 quart pink peppermint ice cream
  • 3 ice cream cones

Steps

  1. Line a 9x13-inch pan with parchment, leaving 2 inches hanging over the edge on either side.
  2. Set aside three ice cream cones for decoration. Place the rest of the ice cream cones in a large resealable plastic bag and use a rolling pin to crush into fine crumbs. Pour the crushed ice cream cones into a bowl and mix with the melted butter. Press into the bottom of the prepared pan in an even layer.
