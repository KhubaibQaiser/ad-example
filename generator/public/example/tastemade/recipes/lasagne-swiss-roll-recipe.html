Lasagne Swiss Roll

An easy homemade Swiss Roll, minus the chocolate. Get ready to be comforted by carbs with this classic Italian-inspired lasagna recipe video that the kids will love.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 3 hr
Servings: 8
Average
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Ragú

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 150g pancetta, cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 500g pack beef mince
  • 500g pack pork mince
  • 100ml milk
  • 2 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 rosemary sprig
  • 2 thyme sprigs
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 500ml red wine

Bechamel

  • 50g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 750ml milk
  • 100g mozzarella, grated
  • 100g parmesan, grated
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Assembly

  • 6/8 sheets lasagne
  • 150g cheddar cheese, grated
  • 100g mozzarella, grated
  • 50g parmesan, grated, plus 50g for serving

Steps

  1. Put the oil and pancetta in a large saucepan. Gently cook until the pancetta is crispy and golden. Add the onion and turn the heat down, cooking for about 10 minutes, until the onion is softened, then add the garlic and cook for a further 3 minutes.
  2. Tip in the beef and pork minces and cook, breaking down the mince with the back of a wooden spoon then add the milk and chopped tomatoes. Stir in the bay leaves, rosemary, thyme, oregano, beef stock cube and wine, and bring to a simmer.
