Indian Cashew Brittle

For a confection with extra rich flavors, look no further than this cashew brittle made with jaggery, an unrefined cane sugar.

By vijaya selvaraju

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon clarified butter
  • 1 cup raw cashews
  • 3/4 cup jaggery or gur (unrefined cane sugar)
  • 3 teaspoons water
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger

Steps

  1. Grease a baking sheet with ghee.
  2. In a frying pan on medium heat, toast cashews until golden brown and crisp. Set aside and allow to cool completely.
