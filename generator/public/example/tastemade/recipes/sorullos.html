Sorullos

Chef Monti Carlo celebrates her grandmother with this classic Sorrullos recipe. Sorrullos, a cheesy Puerto Rican corn meal fritter, will quickly become your new favorite appetizer!

By Monti Carlo

Updated: 9/28/2024

Servings: 20

Ingredients

Sorullos

  • Canola oil for frying
  • 1 3/4 cups boiling water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup finely ground cornmeal
  • 1 cup loosely packed grated edam cheese

Sorullo Dipping Sauce

  • 1/4 cup mayo
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon chopped italian pickled hot peppers

Steps

  1. Bring water and salt to a boil in a heavy-bottomed 2-quarts saucepan.
  2. Lower the heat to 300°F.
