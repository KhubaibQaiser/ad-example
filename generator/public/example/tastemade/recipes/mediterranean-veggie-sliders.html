Mediterranean Veggie Sliders

Yummy vegetarian sliders with a healthy, Mediterranean-inspired twist.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Mediterranean Veggie Sliders

  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 1 cup red quinoa, cooked
  • 1/4 cup red onions, diced
  • 1/2 cup parsley, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons kalamata olives, pitted
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • Slider bun, toasted
  • Cucumber slice
  • 4 slices red onion
  • Green leaf lettuce

Yogurt Sauce

  • 1/2 cup nonfat greek plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dill
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a food processor combine chickpeas, quinoa, red onion, parsley, garlic, lemon juice, Kalamata olives, flour and salt and pepper. Divide the mixture into 4 patties. Firmly press together.
  2. Pan fry the patties over medium-high heat until both sides are browned and crispy, about 3 minutes per side.
