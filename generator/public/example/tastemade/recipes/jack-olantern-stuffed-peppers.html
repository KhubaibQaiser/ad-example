Jack O'Lantern Stuffed Peppers

Hearty and smokey sausage-stuffed peppers carved to mimic mini Jack-O-Lanterns.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 1 hr 5 min
Cook Time: 55 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 cups grated cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound pork sausage
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn
  • 14 1/2 ounces fire roasted tomatoes, drained
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons hot sauce
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 8 large orange bell peppers

Steps

  1. Add the rice and 2 cups of water to a saucepan and cook according to package instructions.
  2. Peel and dice the onion into small pieces. Shred the cheddar cheese and set aside.
