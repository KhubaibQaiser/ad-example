Lazy Cooking Hacks

Too lazy to cook? These easy dinner recipes will save your weeknight! Use the microwave to whip up a quiche in no time, or make the fastest-ever chicken noodle soup. A one-pan baked mac 'n' cheese is sure to be a hit! And for dessert? Easy stovetop chocolate chip cookies will make everyone smile.

Ingredients

Microwave Quiche

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 slice ham, chopped
  • Raw spinach, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon scallions, chopped
  • 1 sun-dried tomato, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon shredded cheddar
  • Salt & pepper to taste

On-The-Go Noodle Soup

  • 1 small package bean thread noodles
  • 1/4 cup cooked shredded chicken
  • 1 tablespoon scallions
  • 2 tablespoons frozen peas & carrots
  • Chicken bouillon powder, to taste
  • Sesame oil, to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups boiling water

Mac & Cheese Bake

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cups evaporated milk
  • 16 ounces shredded cheese
  • 8 ounces elbow pasta
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup cheesy crackers, crushed

Stovetop Cookies

  • Premade chocolate chip cookie dough

Steps

  1. Coat a microwave-safe bowl with cooking spray, add eggs and scramble, mix in the rest of the ingredients, and microwave for 1 minute.
