Movie Snacks

Skip the popcorn and try these easy, homemade movie snacks as a healthy alternative!

By Laura Miller

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Raw. Vegan. Not Gross.

Prep Time: 50 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Brussels Sprouts

  • 1 big bag brussels sprouts
  • 2–3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Apple Rings

  • 4–5 apples
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg

Almond Butter Cups

  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil
  • Almond butter for filling

Steps

  1. Separate the leaves of the brussels sprouts.
  2. Toss leaves in a bowl with olive oil.
