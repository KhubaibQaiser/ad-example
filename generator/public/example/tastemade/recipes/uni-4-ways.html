Uni 4-Ways

Here's how to use uni in your own cooking, from fresh sushi and ceviche, to uni pasta and creamy uni risotto. These uni recipes will make you feel like you're at a restaurant in your own home!

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Uni Sushi

  • 1 roasted nori sheet, sliced into strips
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked & seasoned sushi rice
  • 4 ounces fresh uni
  • 2 tablespoons seasoned salmon roe
  • Water for hand
  • Soy sauce for dipping

Scallop & Uni Ceviche

  • 1/4 pound sea scallops, sliced into halves
  • 3 ounces fresh uni
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed grapefruit juice
  • 1/8 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Red chili, to taste, thinly sliced
  • Kosher salt, to taste

Uni Pasta

  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 1 egg
  • 4 tablespoons melted butter
  • 4–6 ounces fresh uni
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Grated parmesan, to taste
  • Shredded nori

Uni Risotto

  • 1/4 pound pancetta, diced
  • 4–6 ounces fresh uni
  • 2 tablespoons shallots, minced
  • 3/4 cup arborio rice
  • 3/4 cup sake
  • 3 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons softened butter
  • Furikake, to taste

Steps

  1. Wet hands with water and shape a small ball of rice into an oval.
  2. Wrap with a strip of nori leaving space on top.
