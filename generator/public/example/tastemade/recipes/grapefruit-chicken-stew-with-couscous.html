Grapefruit Chicken Stew with Couscous

Savory and tart, chef Nadiya Hussain's chicken stew is perfect for feeding a crowd — especially when piled atop steaming, fluffy couscous.

By Nadiya Hussein

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Stew

  • 35 1/4 ounces skinless boneless chicken thighs, diced
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil, plus a little extra
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 large grapefruit, juice and zest
  • 17 3/4 fluid ounces hot water
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour mixed with 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 large handful fresh flat leaf parsley, finely chopped

Couscous

  • 14 ounces couscous
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 19 1/2 fluid ounces boiling water

Steps

  1. Put the chicken into a bowl.
  2. Add 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric and mix until all the chicken is covered.
