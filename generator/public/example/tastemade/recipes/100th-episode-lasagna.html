Celebration Lasagna

Get ready to dig into chef Frankie Celenza's hearty sausage lasagna with layers of homemade pasta, herbed ricotta, and creamy bechamel filling.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

Sausage Tomato Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 pound bulk italian sausage
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons fresh oregano

Pasta Dough

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 5 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Ricotta Mixture

  • 2 cups whole milk ricotta
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ounces parmesan cheese

Bechamel

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 2 ounces parmesan cheese

Lasagna Assembly

  • Sausage tomato sauce
  • Pasta dough
  • Ricotta mixture
  • Bechamel
  • 4 ounces mozzarella, shredded

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat. Peel and finely chop the onion and carrot. Add to the pot. Sprinkle in 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon of pepper, and red pepper flakes, if using. Add in the sausage, breaking up any larger chunks.
  2. Mince the garlic. Stir into the pot. Cook until the vegetables have softened, about 7 minutes, stirring occasionally with a wooden spoon.
