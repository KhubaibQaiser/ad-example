Fish & Grits

Chef Keith Corbin takes Southern cuisine to new heights with his well-seasoned, crispy fish atop creamy grits.

By Keith Corbin

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 2
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Fish

  • 1 pound rockfish fillet, skinless
  • Fish seasoning spice mix, recipe follows
  • 2 cups cornmeal
  • 2 eggs, whites only
  • 4 cups frying oil (canola or vegetable)
  • Fish sauce, recipe follows
  • 2 scallions, sliced, for garnish

Fish Seasoning Spice Mix

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon paprika
  • 1/2 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 2 tablespoons old bay seasoning
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cumin

Fish Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup yellow onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup carrot, diced
  • Reserved fish trimming
  • 2 tablespoons spices
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup tomato juice

Fish Sauce Spices

  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1/2 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne

Grits

  • 1 cup white corn grits
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 cup aged white cheddar, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons cream

Steps

  1. Cut fish into 2 portions, trimming off the thinner sides for a more uniform shape and even thickness. Reserve trimmings for Fish Sauce.
  2. Mix Fish Seasoning Spice mix and cornmeal.
