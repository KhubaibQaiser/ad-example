Elote Couscous Salad

Elote Couscous Salad is a delicious and colorful side dish made with Israeli couscous, grilled corn, cherry tomatoes, avocado, cotija cheese, and a creamy and tangy dressing of lime juice, mayo, and spices.

By derrell smith

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 corn (husked)s
  • 1 1/2 cups pearl couscous
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup fresh lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons salt (divided)
  • 1/2 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 small red onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 5 chopped green onions
  • 1 diced (and seeded if less heat desired) jalapeno pepper
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin, ground
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup cotija cheese (or feta) (or feta, crumbled)

Steps

  1. Prepare the couscous. In a saucepan combine the broth, water, 1/4 cup lime juice and 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, bring the mixture to a boil, and stir in the couscous. Cover the pan, and let the couscous simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Remove the lid, fluff the couscous with a fork and let it cool in the pan.
