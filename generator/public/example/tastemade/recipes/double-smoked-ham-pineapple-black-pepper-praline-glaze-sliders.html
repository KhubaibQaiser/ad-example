Double Smoked Ham & Pineapple Black Pepper Praline Glaze Sliders

Whether you're celebrating Christmas outside under the sun, or simply looking to try a twist on ham, Chef Dale Talde has the recipe for you. His double-smoked ham sliders are slathered with cilantro mayonnaise and pineapple for a crowd-pleasing meal that's perfect for any occasion.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Servings: 10
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 10–12 pounds cooked bone-in ham
  • 1 pineapple
  • 1 jar maraschino cherries
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 cup dark brown corn syrup
  • 1/2 pound butter
  • 3 tablespoons fresh cracked black pepper
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • Cooking spray
  • Cilantro mayonnaise
  • 1 1/2 cups mayonnaise
  • 3/4 cup cilantro leaves
  • 2 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1 lime, juiced and zested
  • 1 cup candied mixed nuts
  • 1/2 cup grated truffled pecorino
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  • 1 package hawaiian slider buns

Steps

  1. Heat one side of the grill and fill the pellet tube with wood chips.
  2. Grill should be at 615°F.
