Caribbean Coconut Curry Chicken with Rice

Chef Michelle Trusselle shares the recipe to her mother's Caribbean chicken curry, which packs a punch of flavor thanks to cumin, thyme, cloves, and fiery Scotch bonnet peppers.

By Numra Siddiqui

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

If These Meals Could Talk

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 4
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 44 grams myristica curry powder
  • 4 1/2 grams ground cumin
  • 5 thyme sprigs, leaves picked
  • 1 1/2 grams cracked black pepper
  • 1 kilogram skinless and boneless chicken thighs, roughly diced into 4-centimeters pieces

Curry

  • 9 grams ground cumin
  • 1 x medium onion, diced
  • 2 x garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 x red pepper, diced
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 10 x thyme sprigs, leaves picked
  • 1 piece scotch bonnet chilli
  • 400 milliliters coconut milk
  • 60 milliliters tomato ketchup
  • 300 grams agria potatoes, peeled and chopped into 4-centimeters chunks
  • 100 grams agria potatoes, peeled and grated
  • 1 lime, halved and juiced
  • 500 milliliters water
  • Vegetable oil

Rice

  • 800 grams basmati rice
  • 1 3/10 liters water
  • 2 spring (green) onions, washed
  • 2 small scotch bonnet chillis
  • 10 grams myristica everyday seasoning
  • 40 grams unsalted butter
  • 1 pinch of salt

To Serve

  • Coriander, leaves chopped
  • Lime, cut into wedges
  • Chilli, sliced

Steps

  1. Combine the curry powder, cumin, thyme and black pepper.
  2. Mix the chicken in this mixture and leave to marinate in the fridge for at least 1 hour, or ideally overnight.
