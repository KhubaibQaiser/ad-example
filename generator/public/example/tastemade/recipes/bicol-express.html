Bicol Express

A deliciously creamy and nutty pork stew from the region of Bicol in the Philippines

By erwan heussaff

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Lightened Up

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • 8 3/4 ounces sliced pork loin
  • 2 teaspoons of shrimp paste
  • 1 tablespoon of grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of grated garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 sliced red chili
  • 1 sliced banana pepper
  • Top with some lime

Steps

  1. Fry off the onions and the pork together in a pan. Give the vegetables and the meat some color.
  2. Add the shrimp paste, garlic, ginger, peanut butter.
