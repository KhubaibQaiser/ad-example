Chori Pan Burger with Pickled Papaya Slaw, Maggi Mayo and Fried Egg

It’s burger day! Chef Dale tackles three classics with a full flavor spin. Game changing Pho Burgers with bone marrow, toasted milk bread Ahi Tuna patty melts, and Chori Pan Burgers with a fried egg and shrimp chips.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 1 day
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Chori Pan Burger with a Fried Egg

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound longanisa, removed from casing
  • 4 slices american cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pan de sal
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro leaves

Pickled Papaya Slaw

  • 1 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 cups shredded green papaya
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, cut into matchsticks
  • 2 fresno chiles, sliced into rings

Maggi Mayo

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoons maggi seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat a large cast iron griddle on the grill.
  2. Divide the beef into four 4 oz balls and flatten them to form burger patties.
