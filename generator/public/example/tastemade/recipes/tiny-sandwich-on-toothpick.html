Tiny Sandwich on Toothpick

This tiny sandwich on a stick is the perfect snack for your holidays!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 mini loaves bread
  • 1/4 teaspoon mayonnaise
  • 2 mini cheese slices
  • 2 mini ham slices
  • 1/2 piece mini sliced cooked carrot

Steps

  1. Lay a mini layer of bread on a board, cover with the mayonnaise and the cheese.
  2. Add another layer of bread, cover with the mayonnaise and the ham.
