Baked Then Fried Chicken

Chef Keith Corbin shares his secret to perfectly crispy fried chicken that's still juicy on the inside.

By Keith Corbin

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 2 eas chicken thighs, legs and wings, bone-in, skin on
  • Chicken seasoning mix
  • Canola or vegetable oil, for frying
  • Ranch hot sauce

Chicken Seasoning Mix

  • 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 3 tablespoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 cup old bay seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

Ranch Hot Sauce

  • 1 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 teaspoons fresh parsley leaves, minced
  • 2 teaspoons fresh chives, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh chervil, minced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh tarragon, minced
  • 2 teaspoons serrano peppers, seeded and minced
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Hot sauce, to taste

Steps

  1. Coat chicken very well in chicken seasoning and tap off any excess.
  2. Place all the chicken pieces on a sheet tray fitted with a baking rack.
