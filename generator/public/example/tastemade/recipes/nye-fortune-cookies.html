New Years Eve Fortune Cookies

You don't have to wait for take out, these are easy and you can personalize them!

By Mia Starr

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 16 min
Servings: 16
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 eaches egg whites
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup white chocolate melting wafers or white chocolate chips
  • 1 cup dark chocolate melting wafers or chocolate chips
  • 1 cup assorted sprinkles
  • Paper and pen for fortune

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F. Line a baking sheet with a silicone mat and lightly grease.
  2. In a medium bowl, beat the egg whites, vanilla extract and oil until foamy.
