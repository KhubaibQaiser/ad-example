Savory Eclairs

Welcome to eclairs like you've never seen before. This savory twist on a classic sweet treat will make you rethink everything you know about French pastries.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/30/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 1/2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cubed
  • 2/3 cup strong white flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 camembert wheels
  • Caster sugar, to sprinkle

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F.
  2. Put the water, milk, butter, and salt into a saucepan and bring to the boil, then remove from the heat. Sieve the flour, then tip it into the milk mixture and mix it vigorously with a wooden spoon, to bring it together.
