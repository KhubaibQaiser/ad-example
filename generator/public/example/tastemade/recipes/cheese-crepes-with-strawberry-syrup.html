Cheese Crepes with Strawberry Syrup

Is it a crepe or is it a blintz? Either way, Chef Derrell Smith has a recipe for a perfect ricotta filling and an easy strawberry syrup to drizzle on top.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Batter

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Filling

  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese, drained
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • Zest from 1 lemon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/7 cup diced strawberries
  • Butter, for browning

Strawberry Compote

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups quartered strawberries

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  2. In a blender, add all batter ingredients and mix until smooth. Allow to rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.
