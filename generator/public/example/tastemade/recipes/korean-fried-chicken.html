Korean Fried Chicken

Get ready to transport your tastebuds all the way to Korea with our insane Korean Fried Chicken recipe. This flavorful and easy recipe is perfect for any occasion (lunch and dinner included), and we're certain that it will be love after first bite.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 1 hr 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 6
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Korean Chilli Sauce/Glaze

  • 6 tablespoons light soy sauce
  • 4 tablespoons honey
  • ½ teaspoon mustard powder
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Korean Gochujang paste
  • ½ tablespoon minced garlic
  • ½ tablespoon minced ginger

Flour Coating/Batter

  • 3 tablespoons plain flour
  • 5 tablespoons rice flour
  • 1.5 tablespoons cornflour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons mild curry powder
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 100-125ml water
  • Vegetable oil for deep frying

Chicken

  • 500g chicken thighs, boneless, cut into large chunks
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon Korean chilli flakes (optional)
  • Sesame seeds
  • Spring onions
  • Kimchi slaw

Steps

  1. Place all the ingredients into a small saucepan and bring to the boil, simmer for around 5-10 minutes until the sauce becomes sticky and glossy.
  2. Remove from the heat, but keep in the saucepan ready to quickly warm through once the chicken has been fried.
