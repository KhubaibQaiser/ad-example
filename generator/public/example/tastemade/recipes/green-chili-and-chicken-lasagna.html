Green Chili and Chicken Lasagna

Green chili chicken lasagna is a hybrid food dream come true.

By lazarus lynch

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 5 cups shredded rotisserie chicken, skin removed
  • 1 tablespoon chipotle powder
  • 2 teaspoons smoked chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 10 ounces fire roasted green chilies
  • 1 1/2 cups salsa verde
  • 12–16 no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces cacique crema mexicana
  • 16 ounces mozzarella cheese
  • 8 ounces monterey jack cheese
  • 2 jalapeños, sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the chicken, chipotle powder, chili powder, cumin, salt, pepper, green chilies, and salsa verde.
