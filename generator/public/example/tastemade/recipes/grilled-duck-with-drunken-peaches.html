Grilled Duck with Drunken Peaches

Indulge in the decadent flavors of grilled duck, perfectly paired with sweet and boozy drunken peaches for a luxurious, mouthwatering treat.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Grilled Duck with Drunken Peaches

  • 3 duck breasts
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 inches piece of ginger, sliced into matchsticks
  • 2 firm peaches, pitted and sliced
  • 1 teaspoon chinese 5 spice
  • 1 cup basil leaves, chopped

Honey Bourbon Sauce

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup bourbon
  • 1/2 cup shaoxing wine
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce

Steps

  1. Score the fat side of the duck.
  2. Place on grill grates, fat side down and cook just to render the fat, but keep the meat raw, about 4–5 minutes.
