Smokey Mushroom Queso Tacos with Truffle Mole Negro

Looking for a vegetarian taco recipe? Crispy, cheesy flour tortillas are filled with smokey mushrooms and grilled with a truffle mole sauce.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Smokey Mushroom Queso Tacos with Truffle Mole Negro

  • 1 1/2 pounds mushroom mixture (shiitake, oyster, trumpet), stemmed
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon truffle oil
  • High-quality flour tortilla
  • Oaxacan string cheese

Mole Negro

  • 2 tablespoons grapeseed oil
  • 1/2 cup mole negro base
  • 1 cup vegetable stock

Steps

  1. Toss the mushrooms with oil and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Place on the grill over medium high heat to char.
