White Chocolate Holiday Bark

Nothing better than some chocolate holiday bark than chocolate holiday bark done 3 ways!

By Mia Starr

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 20 min
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces dark chocolate chips or melting wafers
  • 22 ounces white chocolate chips or melting wafers
  • 10–15 pretzel sticks broken in half
  • 8–10 mini peanut butter cups
  • 12–20 candy googly eyes
  • 1 teaspoon orange sprinkles
  • 2 tablespoons red hot cinnamon candy decor
  • 1/4 cup crushed peppermint candy canes
  • 1/4 cup blue and white sprinkles/sanding sugar decor

Steps

  1. Line a 1/2 sheet cookie sheet with parchment.
  2. In a medium microwave safe bowl, melt the 2 bags of dark chocolate chips, heating for 30 seconds at a time and stirring between each 30 seconds, until completely melted.
