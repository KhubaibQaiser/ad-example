Grilled Vegetable Sandwiches with Cheddar Pepper Spread

A vegetarian sandwich with cheesy spread.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Spread

  • 1 1/4 cups finely shredded yellow cheddar
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1/3 cup chopped cherry peppers
  • 3 tablespoons cherry pepper brine
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Fried Shallots

  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 3 medium shallot bulbs, thinly sliced
  • Kosher salt, for sprinkling

Sandwich

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3/4 large eggplant, sliced 1/4
  • 1 medium orange bell pepper, cut into 2–3 inches planks
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, cut into 2–3 inches planks
  • 1 medium yellow bell pepper, cut into 2–3 inches planks
  • 1/2 medium yellow squash, sliced 1/4-inch
  • 1/2 medium pound zucchini, sliced 1/4-inch
  • 3 ciabatta demi-baguettes (or any loaf about 10" long)s
  • Fried shallot, for topping

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a small bowl.
