Tomato Soup with Bacon Grilled Cheese Croutons

With bacon grilled cheese croutons, tomato soup will never be the same.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 35 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Tomato Soup

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 56 ounces whole tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme, finely chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Bacon Grilled Cheese Croutons

  • 4 slices breads
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 6 strips bacon, cooked
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Parsley, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a pot, add butter and saute onion and garlic, until softened.
  2. Transfer the onion and garlic to a blender, along with the tomatoes.
