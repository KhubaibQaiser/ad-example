Gilded Tuxedo Cake

Transform store-bought cake into a tiered tuxedo masterpiece for New Year's Eve or any celebration that calls for a white, black, and gold slice!

By Mia Starr

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 24
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Cake

  • 2 large grocery store sheet cakes

Cake Filling

  • 3 cups shortbread cookies, chopped
  • 1 small jar caramel
  • 1 package chocolate pudding
  • 2 cups whole milk

Buttercream

  • 2 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 pound powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 pinch salt

Assembly and Decorations

  • 6 inches cake board
  • 8 inches cake board
  • 6–8 cups vanilla buttercream
  • Gel food coloring
  • Gold luster dusk
  • 1 teaspoon vodka
  • White chocolate ganache
  • Store-bought cookies and truffle - macaroons, truffles (white and chocolate), wafer cookies
  • Various gold and silver sprinkles and dragée

Steps

  1. In a medium sized bowl, mix together 1 package instant chocolate pudding and 2 cups cold milk. Whisk together and then set it in the fridge to set.
  2. Chop shortbread cookies into small pieces, so you get a little chunk of cookie in every bite of cake.
