Tater Tot Hotdish

A savory combination of ground beef with crispy tater tots and melted cheddar cheese.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 50 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion
  • 8 ounces button mushrooms
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flake
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 bag frozen peas and carrots, thawed
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 pounds frozen tater tots

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Heat a 12 inches cast iron skillet over medium heat and add the butter. Peel and mince the onion and add it to the pan. Slice the mushrooms and add them to the pan. Season with salt and pepper, and saute for 5–7 minutes.
