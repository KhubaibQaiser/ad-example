Crab Rangoon Nachos

Crab Rangoon Nachos are a fusion of Asian and Mexican cuisine, featuring crispy wonton chips topped with a creamy crab and cream cheese mixture, melted cheese, and green onions, drizzled with sweet and spicy sauce.

By derrell smith

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 packets wonton wrappers
  • 8 ounces crab meat, imitation crab meat
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1 large clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
  • 4 scallions, chopped divided in half
  • 1 1/4 cups pepperjack cheese
  • Thai chili sauce

Steps

  1. Cut wonton wrappers in half.
  2. Keep wontons covered with a towel while you finish heating the oil.
