Curry Cauliflower Fritters with Cilantro Yogurt Sauce

You can't go wrong with these curry cauliflower fritters.

By lazarus lynch

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 4 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

Fritters

  • Peanut oil, for frying
  • 1 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/2 garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon curry or turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup cornstarch
  • 1 cup club soda
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 small cauliflower, cut into florets

Sauce

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro leaves

Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven or heavy-bottomed pot, heat oil (enough for deep-frying) over medium-high heat until oil reaches 360Â°F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the ginger, garlic powder, curry powder, cumin, coriander, flour, cornstarch, club soda, salt and pepper. Let stand for 5 minutes.
