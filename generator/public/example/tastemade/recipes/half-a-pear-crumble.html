Half-a-Pear Crumble

Everyone gets the perfect serving in chef Nadiya Hussain's pear crumble recipe, which features halved poached pears topped with a sweet, crispy crumble.

By Nadiya Hussein

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 6
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Pears

  • 3 large pears, peeled and halved
  • 500ml/18fl oz apple juice
  • 1 vanilla pod, split
  • 150g/5½oz demerara sugar
  • 75g/2½oz golden marzipan
  • Ice cream, to serve

Crumble

  • 50g/1¾oz plain flour
  • 25g/1oz unsalted butter
  • 25g/1oz demerara sugar
  • 100g/3½oz granola

Steps

  1. Put the pears into a medium saucepan with the apple juice and vanilla pod, making sure to scrape out the vanilla seeds into the apple juice. Boil the pears in the juice for 20–30 minutes, or until they are tender.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200C/180C Fan/Gas 6 and have a roasting dish at the ready, large enough to fit all 6 pear halves.
