New York-Style Bacon, Egg, & Cheese with Black Pepper Bacon

LA spin on a NY-style bagel sandwich

By Alex Hill

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Spice Spice Baby

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2–4 of your favorite plain bagels
  • 1/2 pound applewood smoked bacon
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup & fresh cracked black pepper, for brushing of the bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices american cheese
  • 1 avocado
  • 1–2 tablespoons butter
  • Kosher salt & black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Align bacon on a parchment paper lined baking sheet.
