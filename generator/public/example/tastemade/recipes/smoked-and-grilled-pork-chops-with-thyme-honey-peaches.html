Smoked and Grilled Pork Chops with Thyme-Honey Peaches

Summertime seasonal fruit pampers your tastebuds. Chef Dale grills blistered grapes with ham wrapped Trout, Peaches with smoked Pork Chops, Watermelon with halloumi, and Chargrilled Cherries with chocolate Mousse.

(2)

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 1 day
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 4 thick bone in pork chops
  • 2 cups water
  • ¼ cup kosher salt
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 3 peaches, cored and cut into thick slices
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ¼ cup sherry vinegar
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup mint
  • ¼ cup Thai basil

Steps

  1. Heat the water in a small saucepan and stir in the salt and sugar to dissolve.
  2. Add ice to cool the water mixture.
