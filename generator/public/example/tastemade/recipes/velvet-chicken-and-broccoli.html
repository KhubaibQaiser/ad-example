Chicken and Broccoli

Ever wanted to replicate your favorite Chinese takeout order? Chef Jen Phanomrat shares the secret to making perfect chicken and broccoli right at home.

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :

Ingredients

Velvet Chicken and Broccoli

  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, sliced
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 tablespoon white wine
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Stir-Fry Sauce

  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 2 teaspoons ginger root, grated
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • Oil for pan

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine egg white, wine, cornstarch, and salt. Add the sliced chicken and coat well.
  2. Cover and place in the fridge for at least 1 hour or preferably overnight.
