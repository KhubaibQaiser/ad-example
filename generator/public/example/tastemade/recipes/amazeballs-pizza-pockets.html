Amazeballs Pizza Pockets

Amazeballs Pizza Pockets are delicious bite-sized snacks made with pizza dough, tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and pepperoni, rolled into balls and baked until golden and crispy.

(1)

By derrell smith

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, defrosted per package instructions
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Amazeball meatball, diced
  • 1/4 cup amazeball sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon italian seasoning, divided
  • 1 tablespoon water + egg
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon water

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Roll out dough and cut into 2-rectangles.
