Green Tomato Salad with Sesame Seeds and Peanuts

Celebrate tomato season with this simple green tomato salad, which gets added texture from sesame seeds and peanuts.

By Daisy Ryan

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds green tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons black sesame seeds
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons white sesame seeds
  • 1/2 tablespoon aleppo pepper (or cayenne if aleppo unavailable)
  • 2 tablespoons cilantro leaves and tender stems, chopped
  • 1/2 cup unsalted peanuts, toasted and chopped
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange tomatoes on a serving platter and dress with lemon juice and olive oil.
  2. Garnish with black and white sesame seeds, peanuts, aleppo pepper, and cilantro.
