Foil Packet Greek Steak and Potatoes

Steak and potatoes get slathered in spices and baked in a foil packet in this no-mess meal.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 1
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound sirloin steak, cut into 1 1/4-inches cubes
  • 1/2 cup baby red potatoes
  • 1/4 cup carrot, sliced in 1/3-inch rounds
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon chopped thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon chopped oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon feta, crumbled
  • 1 tablespoon parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, toss the meat, potatoes, carrot, olive oil, garlic and spices together. Season with salt and pepper.
