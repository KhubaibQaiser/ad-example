Fried Eggplant with Pecorino & Spicy Tomato Sauce

Crispy eggplant gets paired with Calabrian chili-studded tomato sauce in this spicy vegetarian main.

By jamie lauren

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy

Ingredients

Eggplant

  • 1 italian eggplant, partially peeled and sliced into 1/4 ring rings
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 cups italian breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup flour, ap
  • 1/2 cup pecorino, locatelli, grated
  • 1 quart olive oil blend for frying for frying (or canola oil)
  • 2 lemons
  • 12 cloves garlic, peeled, smashed
  • Kosher salt/black pepper, to taste

Tomato Sauce

  • 24 ounces crushed san marzano tomatoes, canned
  • 8 cloves garlic, peeled, minced
  • 1 yellow onion, minced
  • 3 calabrian chilies, chopped
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 3 tablespoons basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons parsley, chopped
  • 4 tablespoons italian olive oil
  • Kosher salt/pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a heavy bottom skillet over medium heat until it reaches 350Â°F. Place the eggplant slices on a sheet tray with a rack and lightly salt them on both sides. Allow to sit for 30 minutes to remove some of the bitter. Crack the eggs in a small mixing bowl and add 2 tablespoons of water to make an egg wash. In a small bowl, combine pecorino and Italian breadcrumbs and set aside. Dredge eggplant in flour, then egg wash, then evenly coat with breadcrumb mixture. Cook eggplant in hot oil until golden on one side, flip over, add the garlic to the pan and continue cooking until golden on the other side. Place eggplant on a rack lined sheet tray and season with salt, squeeze lemon juice over the eggplant and reserve.
