Char Kway Teow

This Singaporean street food classic is your new favourite stir-fry. Just add some wok hei

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 8 3/4 ounces fresh flat rice noodles
  • 2 fresh red chillies
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 4 shallots, peeled
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 3/4 ounces pawns, shelled & de-veined
  • 2 stalks chinese chives
  • 1 stalk chinese cabbage
  • 1 cup bean sprouts, tailed
  • 2 teaspoons light soy sauce
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon kecap manis (sweet soya sauce)

Steps

  1. Wash the noodles and drain well. If using dried, pour (just under) 210°F water over them for a few minutes before using.
  2. Make the spice paste by blending the chillies, garlic and shallots in a food processor, until a paste is formed.
