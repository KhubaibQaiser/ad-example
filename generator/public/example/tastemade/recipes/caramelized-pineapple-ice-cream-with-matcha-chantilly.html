Caramelized Pineapple Ice Cream with Matcha Chantilly

Make weeknights exciting with Chef Logan Sandoval's homemade caramelized pineapple ice cream, served with a matcha chantilly cream for a balanced dessert.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/16/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Ice Cream

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 14 ounces sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 16 ounces crushed pineapple drained
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 1/2 ounces brown sugar

Matcha Chantilly

  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 vanilla bean pod
  • 2 teaspoons matcha green tea powder

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet and allow it to start browning slightly.
  2. Add brown sugar and stir until nappe.
