Pfeffernüsse

Pfeffernüsse, a simple spiced cookie, are the ultimate holiday treat! Try Chef Mia Starr's recipe, packed with the warming flavors of cinnamon, allspice, and nutmeg.

By Mia Starr

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 18
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground all spice
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, room temperature
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1 egg, large
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 cups powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Mix flour, spices, and soda in a medium bowl.
  2. Cream the butter, brown sugar and molasses in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment.
