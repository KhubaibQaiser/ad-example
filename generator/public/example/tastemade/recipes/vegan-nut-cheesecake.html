Vegan Nut Cheesecake

Satisfy your sweet tooth, minus the dairy. Chef Erwan Heussaff's vegan cheesecake recipe mimics a classic cheesecake's velvet texture thanks to raw cashews and coconut cream.

By Erwan Heussaff

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Lightened Up

Prep Time: 2 hr 20 min
Cook Time: 5 hr
Servings: 8
Hard
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 cup roasted almonds
  • 1/4 cup roasted green pumpkin seeds
  • ¼ cup pistachios
  • 2 Tbs coconut oil
  • 1 Tbs molasses
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1-2 Tbs water

Cheesecake filling

  • 2 cup raw cashews – soaked overnight or 2 hours in hot water.
  • 1/2 cup Lakanto maple syrup - or pure maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil - melted
  • 1/4 cup juice & + 1 Tbs zest of an orange
  • 1/4 cup fresh or canned coconut cream
  • 3/4 tsp cinnamon
  • dash of nutmeg
  • pinch of salt

Garnish

  • Coconut whipped cream (very cold)
  • orange zest
  • pistachio dust
  • cinnamon

Steps

  1. Pulse nuts and seeds in a food processor.
  2. Add coconut oil, molasses and salt, to form a sticky dough.
