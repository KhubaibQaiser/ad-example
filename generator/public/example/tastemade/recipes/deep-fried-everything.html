Deep Fried Everything

Want to deep-fry your favorite snacks? Here's the secret to a perfect batter.

By Arron Crascall

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Gamer Snacks

Prep Time: 45 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 100 grams plain flour
  • 100 grams cornflour
  • 10 grams baking powder
  • 150 milliliters milk
  • 150 milliliters sparkling water
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Pickle
  • Twirl / flake
  • Hamburger
  • Panko breadcrumb
  • Haribo
  • Vanilla
  • Skittle
  • Bubblegum sauce
  • Vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Combine the flour, cornflour and baking powder in a large bowl and mix.
  2. Gradually pour the milk and sparkling water into the bowl, stirring until you have a smooth, lump-free batter.
