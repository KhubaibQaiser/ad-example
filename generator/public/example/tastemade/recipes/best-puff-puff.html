Puff Puff

This classic Nigerian street food is a delicious piece of spiced, fried dough stuffed with sweet mango jam and dusted with powdered sugar. You won't be able to eat just one!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water (lukewarm)
  • 1/2 ounce quick rise yeast
  • 12 1/4 ounces all purpose flour
  • 6 1/2 ounces sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Mango jam (for filling)
  • Powdered sugar (for dusting)

Steps

  1. Mix lukewarm water with yeast in a mixing bowl.
  2. Combine all dry ingredients, whisking them to allow even dispersion.
