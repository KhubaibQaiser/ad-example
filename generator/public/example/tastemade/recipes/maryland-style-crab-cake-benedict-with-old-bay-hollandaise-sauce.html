Maryland-Style Crab Cake Benedict with Old Bay Hollandaise Sauce

Planning an at-home brunch with friends? Add Alex Hill's creamy and tangy eggs Benedict with an Old Bay twist to your menu.

By Alex Hill

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Spice Spice Baby

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 6
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

Maryland-Style Crab Cake Benedict

  • 1 can buttermilk biscuits
  • 4 eggs
  • 8 ounces jumbo lump crab meat
  • 1 teaspoon old bay seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1/3 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoons flat leaf parsley, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • Kosher salt & black pepper

Old Bay Hollandaise Sauce

  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 8 tablespoons hot, melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon old bay

Steps

  1. Cook biscuits according to the package instructions.
  2. In a mixing bowl, add all ingredients for the crab cake and combine. Be sure not to over mix.
