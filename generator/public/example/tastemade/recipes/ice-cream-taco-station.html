Ice Cream Taco Station

Ice Cream Taco Station is a fun and interactive dessert buffet, featuring crispy waffle taco shells filled with a variety of ice cream flavors, toppings such as sprinkles, candies, and syrups, and sauces like chocolate and caramel for guests to build their own custom ice cream tacos.
