Three Flavors of Polvoron

Maple bacon means they're acceptable to eat for breakfast, right? Polvorón in chocolate, almond and bacon-maple flavors for your snacking needs.

By eduardo garcia

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 cups toasted all purpose flour
  • 2 cups powdered milk
  • 1 1/3 cups butter, melted
  • 1 cup caster sugar
  • 3 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 4 slices bacon, small diced, fried
  • 3 tablespoons cacao powder
  • 6 tablespoons peanuts, crushed
  • 2 tablespoons palm sugar
  • 1/3 cup almond flour

Steps

  1. In a pan, toast the flour until cooked.
  2. In a bowl, combine the flour, powdered milk and caster sugar.
