Sweet Potato Kibbeh

Our Sweet Potato Kibbeh is a really easy but super impressive dish. Would you give that awesome decoration a try?

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 50 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 30 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 38 3/4 ounces sweet potatoes
  • 10 ounces fine bulgur wheat
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, grated
  • 1 large handful of chopped coriander
  • 1 ounce gram flour
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 355°F, place the sweet potatoes on a baking tray and bake in the hot oven for 30 - 40 minutes or until soft. Set aside to cool.
  2. Prepare the bulgur wheat by soaking it in 195°F water for 20 minutes, then drain it in a sieve and let all excess water drip away.
