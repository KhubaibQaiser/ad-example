Homemade Bao

If you've ever wanted to make bao from scratch, this homemade bao recipe is the perfect place to start. Delicious, savory pulled pork is used as a nontraditional stuffing to these fluffy bao buns!

By julie nolke

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 5
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil, plus extra

Steps

  1. Begin by adding 1 pinch of the sugar into the warm water.
  2. Sprinkle over the yeast and let bloom for 5–7 minutes until foamy.
