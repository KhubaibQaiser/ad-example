Watermelon Wellington

This vegetarian twist on beef Wellington uses an unexpected ingredient: watermelon! Try this vegetarian recipe for your next dinner party for a fun main course.

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Watermelon Wellington

  • 1 watermelon
  • 2–3 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh rosemary, to taste
  • 14 ounces puff pastry, thawed
  • Egg + water

Marinade

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Filling

  • 1 1/2 pounds mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Fresh rosemary, to taste
  • 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Butter for the pan

Steps

  1. Cut away all watermelon rind. Carve the fruit to resemble a beef tenderloin shape. In a bowl, combine marinade ingredients and transfer to a Ziploc bag.
  2. Place watermelon in the marinade and chill for 2 hours.
