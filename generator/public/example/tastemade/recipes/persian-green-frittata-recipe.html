Persian Green Frittata

Eaten hot or cold, served for lunch or at picnic, this recipe will quickly become one of your favourite go-to dishes. Packed full of flavour and goodness, it will be a hit with everyone of all ages. Perfect to celebrate Persian New Year.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 6 spring onions, finely chopped
  • 1 small leek, white and pale-green parts only, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 3 1/2 ounces flat leaf parsley, finely chopped
  • 3 1/2 ounces coriander, finely chopped
  • 3 1/2 ounces dill, finely chopped
  • 1 lemon, zest only
  • 4 free-range eggs
  • 2 tablespoons plain flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill or grill function in your oven.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large oven-proof skillet. Gently saute the onions and leeks for 10 minutes with the turmeric and salt, until soft and just starting to color.
