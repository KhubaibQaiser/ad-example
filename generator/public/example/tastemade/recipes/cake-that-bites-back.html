The Cake that Bites Back

Two tiers of red velvet and chocolate goodness that might bite back!!

By Mia Starr

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 1 hr 5 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 18
Hard
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Red Velvet Cake

  • 4 cups ap flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 3/4 cup salted butter
  • 2 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup oil
  • 2 ounces red food color
  • 3 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white vinegar
  • 2 cups buttermilk

Chocolate Buttercream

  • 6 sticks unsalted butter
  • 3 pounds powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups black or dark cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 pinches salt
  • 6 tablespoons strong black coffee
  • 2–4 drops black gel color

Vanilla Buttercream

  • 1 1/2 pounds unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 3 pounds powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon almond extract
  • 3/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 pinches salt
  • 2–3 drops purple gel color
  • 2–4 drops red gel color

Bloody Ganache

  • 1 1/2 cups red melting candies
  • 1/4 cup milk chocolate melting wafers
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Assembles & Decorate

  • 1 1/2 cups purple rock candy
  • 1 cup white jordan almonds
  • 2–3 tablespoons silver dragees

Steps

  1. NOTE: you will have to make this cake 2 1/2 times to have enough cake.
  2. Preheat oven to 350°F and line and grease 3 each 6-inches cake pans and 3 each 10-inches cake pans.
