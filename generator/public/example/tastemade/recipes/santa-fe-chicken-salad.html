Santa Fe Chicken Salad

Spice up regular chicken salad with this flavorful, nutritious recipe.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Santa Fe Chicken Salad

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup fire roasted corn, thawed
  • 1 cup black beans, cooked, drained and rinsed
  • 3/4 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cilantro, chopped

Wraps

  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 4 large whole wheat large tortillas

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine all ingredients for the santa fe chicken salad.
  2. Lay out the tortilla on a flat surface. Place chicken salad mixture in a line in the center of the wrap.
