Korean Short Ribs

Step up your barbecue game with Chef Logan Sandoval's juicy Korean short ribs marinated in a pineapple huli huli sauce.

By Logan Sandoval

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 2 min
Cook Time: 8 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Korean Short Ribs

  • 2 1/2 pounds flanken short ribs
  • Huli huli sauce
  • Coconut jasmine rice
  • Szechuan fried green bean
  • 1 tablespoon white sesame seeds, garnish
  • 1 tablespoon black sesame seeds, garnish
  • 1/2 cup scallion greens, sliced for garnish

Pineapple Huli Huli Sauce

  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup shoyu
  • 3 teaspoons chili paste
  • Scallion, reserved white parts

Coconut Jasmine Rice

  • 14 ounces coconut milk
  • 1 1/4 cups cold water
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 cups jasmine rice

Szechuan Fried Green Beans

  • 1 pound green beans, ends trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons shaoxing wine
  • 3 tablespoons shoyu
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons red szechuan peppercorns
  • 5 whole dried chile de arbols
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, sliced
  • 1/4 cup scallion, white part

Steps

  1. Marinate short ribs in half of Huli Huli sauce for a minimum of 2 hours and maximum overnight.
  2. Reserve the other half of the Huli Huli sauce for plating.
