Chicken Breast with Wasabi and Lime

Yaki means “grilled” and tori means “chicken”, so Chicken Breast with Wasabi and Lime, Chicken Wings with spicy pepper is a beautiful example of simplicity at its best.

(1)

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 bunch scallions, white part only sliced
  • 1 tablespoon nambi wasabi
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 6–7 skewers, soaked

Steps

  1. Break down the whole chicken.
  2. Cut the breasts into 1 inch pieces and skewer.
