Chinese Broccoli with Bacon, Oyster Sauce and Sesame

Treat your palate to Wok grilled Chinese broccoli, deliciously crisp and smoky with bacon, drizzled in savory oyster sauce, and topped with a sprinkle of nutty sesame seeds.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chinese broccoli
  • 1 pound applewood smoked bacon
  • 1 red fresno chili, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Steps

  1. Light the charcoal grill and allow to heat for 20 minutes.
  2. Slice the Chinese broccoli stems into 1/2 inch diagonal slices and leave the leaves whole.
