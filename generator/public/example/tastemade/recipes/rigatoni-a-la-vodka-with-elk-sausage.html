Rigatoni A La Vodka with Elk Sausage

Spicy, meaty sauce dresses up chewy noodles in this fresh take on a classic pasta dish.

By eduardo garcia

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pound rigatoni
  • 1/2 pound herb or italian elk sausages cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 1 pint plum tomatoes, crushed by hand
  • 2 cloves garlic sliced
  • 1/8 cup montana mex avocado oil
  • 1 cup montana mex habanero sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon montana mex jalapeño seasoning

Steps

  1. Bring pot of water to boil.
  2. Add pasta and give a good stir immediately after adding.
