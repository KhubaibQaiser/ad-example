Savory Cheese & Herb Crackers with Pepper Jelly Spread

Cheesy, savory crackers with cream cheese topping.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 24
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Savory Cheese & Herb Crackers

  • 1 1/3 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon parsley, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons thyme leaves, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons rosemary leaves, chopped
  • 4 ounces unsalted butter, diced
  • 2 cups shredded yellow cheddar
  • 4–5 tablespoons cold water
  • Flakey salt, for sprinkling
  • Pepper jelly spread, for serving

Pepper Jelly Spread

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 3 tablespoons pepper jelly
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Pour the flour, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, salt, pepper, parsley, thyme and rosemary into a food processor.
  2. Pulse to combine.
