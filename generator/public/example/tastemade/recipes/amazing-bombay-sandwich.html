Bombay Sandwich

This Bombay sandwich has a twist you never knew you needed!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Spiced Potato Filling

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 small potato, boiled & mashed
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 small handful fresh coriander

Sandwich

  • Spiced potato filling
  • 2 slices slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Mint chutney
  • 1 red onion, cut into thin round slices
  • 1 tomato, cut into thin round slices
  • 2 large handfuls cheddar cheese, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon chaat masala

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a non stick pan. Now add the cumin and mustard seeds. When they splatter, add the remaining potato filling: ingredients and mix. Remove from heat and allow to cool.
