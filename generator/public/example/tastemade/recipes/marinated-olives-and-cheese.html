Marinated Olives and Cheese

Dress up store-bought olives and cheese with this herby marinade.

By daniele uditi

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 1 hr
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped thyme
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped rosemary
  • 1 orange zest
  • 1 lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon toasted fennel seeds
  • 2 cups of mixed olives
  • 1 cup of provolone cheese
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch of freshly ground pepper

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl add the vinegar and the sugar along with the garlic, the herbs, the zests, the salt and pepper, and fennel seeds.
  2. Whisk everything and add the oil while whisking.
