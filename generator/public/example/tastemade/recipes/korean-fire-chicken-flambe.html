Korean Fire Chicken Flambé

This spicy Korean chicken is topped with cheese and set on fire for an amazing main course that your dinner party guest will love!

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 8 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1/2 cup korean hot pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons ground thai chili flakes
  • 2 tablespoons korean hot pepper paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup agave
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Korean Fire Chicken Flambé

  • 2 pounds chicken thighs, cubed
  • 4 ounces rice cake squares
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/8 cup scallions
  • Sesame seed
  • 2 ounces soju
  • Oil for the pan

Steps

  1. Mix sauce ingredients in a bowl.
