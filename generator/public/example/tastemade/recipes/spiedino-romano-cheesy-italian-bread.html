Spiedino Romano (Cheesy italian bread)

A cheesy, buttery Italian loaf with a bright capers and a zingy anchovy sauce

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 shallot
  • 4 anchovy filets
  • 1/4 cup capers, drained
  • 6 large basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 clove garlic, cut in half
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella
  • 1 italian country loaf

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500°F.
  2. Peel and finely chop the shallot. Drain and roughly chop anchovies. Drain the capers. Stack basil leaves, roll them together and chop. Chop parsley and set aside.
