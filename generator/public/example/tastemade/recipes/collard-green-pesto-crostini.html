Collard Green Pesto Crostini

A new take on pesto with collard greens and pecans.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces collard greens, de-stemmed, blanched and shocked
  • 1/4 cup pecans, toasted
  • 1 small garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Baguette, sliced 1/4 inch and baked
  • 3 slices prosciutto, cooked and broken into shards

Steps

  1. Place the collard greens into a food processor, along with the pecans, garlic, Parmesan, apple cider vinegar, lemon juice, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper. Blend until chunky. Stream in the olive oil, and blend until the mixture is combined.
  2. Spread over sliced, baked baguette, and top with prosciutto.
