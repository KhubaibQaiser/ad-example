Spice-Roasted Squash with Dried Cherries and Tahini

Caramely roasted squash gets topped with tart cherries and creamy tahini in this decadent side.

By Numra Siddiqui

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

If These Meals Could Talk

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 42 1/4 ounces butternut squash
  • Olive oil
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 heaped tablespoon tahini
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 3/4 fluid ounces yoghurt
  • 2–3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 6 spring onions (green onions)s
  • 1/2 bunch mint, leaves roughly chopped
  • 2 ounces rocket (arugula)
  • 1 1/2 ounces dried cherries
  • Sumac (optional)

Steps

  1. To make the squash, first preheat oven to 390°F/Gas mark 6.
  2. Cut the squash in half lengthways, then slice into 3/4–1 1/4 inch wide wedges. Clean out and discard the orange membranes, but keep the seeds, giving them a little wash.
