Tropical Smoothie Bowl

Brighten up your morning with a colorful smoothie bowl!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 frozen bananas
  • 1/2 cup frozen pineapple
  • 1/2 cup papaya
  • 1/2 cup mango
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1/2 banana, for topping
  • 1/4 cup strawberries, for topping
  • 1/4 cup blueberries, for topping
  • 1/2 handful of kale, finely chopped, for garnishing
  • Granola, for garnishing
  • Shredded coconut, for garnishing

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the bananas, pineapple, papaya and mango. Combine in a blender together with almond milk and peanut butter. Blend until texture is creamy and smooth.
  2. Pour in a serving bowl and top with sliced strawberries, sliced banana and blueberries. Garnish with finely chopped kale, granola and shredded coconut.
