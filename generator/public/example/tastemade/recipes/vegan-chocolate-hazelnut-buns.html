Vegan Chocolate Hazelnut Buns

These Nutella-inspired buns with a banana glaze are so deliciously rich and chocolatey, you'll completely forget they're vegan.

By jerrelle guy

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 2 hr
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plant-based milk (coconut, cashew or flax milk)
  • 1 packet fast acting yeast
  • 3 tablespoons canned coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup packed dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil plus more for oiling
  • Salt, to taste
  • 3 1/2 cups white whole wheat flour, plus more for dusting
  • 8 ounces chocolate hazelnut spread (dairy-free)
  • 6 ounces chocolate chips
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar or maple syrup (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the milk over the stove or in the microwave to bring to 115°F. Add to a large mixing bowl and pour the packet of yeast overtop. Allow to yeast to activate, about 5 minutes.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the canned coconut milk and baking powder with a fork. Once the yeast is activated, add the coconut milk mixture, the brown sugar, vegetable oil, and salt. Mix, then add the flour.
