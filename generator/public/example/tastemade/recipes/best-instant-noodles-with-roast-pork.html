Instant Noodles with Roast Pork

Jazz up a bowl of instant ramen noodles by adding in leftover roasted pork, a boiled egg, and aromatics like fresh coriander.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 portion instant ramen noodles
  • Leftover roast pork
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 1 handful coriander with stalks
  • Pak choi leaf
  • Chopped spring onion
  • Bean sprout

Steps

  1. Boil a small pan of water and cook the instant noodles as per packet instructions with its flavor contents. Then pour into a bowl and place your favorite leftover ingredients, such as roast pork or duck from a takeaway.
  2. Top with bean sprouts and coriander.
