Potato-Cauliflower Mash

Chef Eduardo Garcia channels the wild flavors of Montana in this simple potato and cauliflower mash, the perfect base for a big ol' bison steak.

By eduardo garcia

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1–2 large russet potatoes, peeled and cut in 5 large slices, about 0 cups cut up.
  • 1/2 head cauliflower, florets removed from the stem
  • 1/4 cup montana mex extra virgin avocado oil
  • 2–3 teaspoons montana mex jalapeno seasoning
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 1 teaspoon thyme, chopped
  • Cracked white pepper and salt, to taste

Steps

  1. In a med stock pot full of water, simmer Potato and Cauliflower for 20–30 minutes until tender and completely cooked through. Strain in a sieve.
  2. Add cooked Potato and Cauliflower back to the pot. Add avocado oil, lemon zest, thyme, and jalapeno seasoning. Mash with a hand masher, spoon, or fork until the mash is as smooth as you like it.
