Mini Vegan Blackberry Cheesecakes

This is a sweet, creamy vegan dessert that can fit in the palm of your hand.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 12
Average
 :

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 cup raw pecans
  • 1 cup pitted dates, packed
  • 1/8 teaspoon sea salt

Filling

  • 1 1/2 cups raw cashews
  • 1 cup fresh blackberries
  • 1/3 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (approximately 2 lemons)
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 2/3 cup full-fat coconut milk, refrigerated
  • 1/3 cup pure maple syrup

Steps

  1. In a food processor or heavy duty blender, add the raw pecans and process into an even meal consistency, then remove and set aside. Add the pitted dates and sea salt into the same food processor and process until a small ball forms.
  2. Return the pecans back into the food processor and process until the mixture resembles a loose dough.
