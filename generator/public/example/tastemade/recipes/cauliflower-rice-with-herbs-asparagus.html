Cauliflower Rice With Herbs & Asparagus

This easy cauliflower rice is the perfect side dish for pizza!

By bobby parrish

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 12 min
Servings: 3
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 head white cauliflower
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 sweet onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly cracked pepper
  • 1 pound asparagus, tips only, cut on the bias
  • 3 tablespoons Italian flat leaf parsley, chopped
  • 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Use a knife to cut the cauliflower into large florets, removing most of the stalk. Add to a food processor, using the grater attachment. Set aside.
  2. Preheat a large non-stick pan or skillet over medium heat with 2 tablespoons of oil.
  3. Add the onions, garlic, and thyme, along with 1/2 teaspoon of salt and a couple cracks of pepper. Cook for 5 minutes, then add the asparagus tips and cook for 2 minutes more.
  4. Add the grated cauliflower; season with another 1/2 teaspoon of salt and a couple cracks of pepper.
  5. Place a lid on the pan and let it cook and steam for 5 minutes, or until the raw crunch is gone from the cauliflower.
  6. Turn the heat off and add the chopped parsley and the juice of half a lemon. Check for seasoning; you may need more a little more salt or lemon juice.
