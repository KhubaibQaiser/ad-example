Caramel Apple SNICKERS® Nachos

Your Halloween guests will scream with delight when they get a taste of these sweet, spooky, and crowd-pleasing SNICKERS® nachos, featuring crisp apple slices drizzled with thick, gooey caramel and topped with bite sized SNICKERS® pieces.

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 2 large apples
  • ¾ cup milk chocolate chips
  • 16 caramel squares
  • ½ teaspoon milk
  • 4 SNICKERS® Fun Size bars

Steps

  1. Slice the apples into ¼ inch pieces, discarding the core, and arrange slices on a plate. Set aside.
  2. Place milk chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave in 15 seconds intervals until melted, stirring in between. Drizzle over the apple slices, making sure to slightly cover each apple piece with some chocolate.
  3. In a microwave-safe bowl, add caramels and milk. Microwave for 45 seconds or until melted, stirring halfway through. Drizzle over the apple slices, again making sure to cover as many pieces as possible.
  4. Sprinkle with chopped SNICKERS® Fun Size bars. Enjoy!
