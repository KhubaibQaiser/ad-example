Rich Tartar Chicken Nanban

Chicken nanban, or fried chicken served with tartar sauce, is an authentic Japanese recipe similar to chicken karaage. This homemade chicken nanban recipe will satisfy all of your cravings!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Rich Tartar Chicken Nanban

  • 1 lemon
  • 5 eggs
  • Boiling water
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 pickles, chopped
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 chicken breast
  • Tempura powder mixed with water
  • Frying oil

Nanban Vinegar

  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup kombu soup stock
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • Cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs and lemon in boiling water for 12 minutes.
  2. Dip eggs in ice water before peeling.
