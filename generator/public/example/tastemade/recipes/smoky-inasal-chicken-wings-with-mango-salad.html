Smoky Inasal Chicken Wings with Mango Salad

Grilled chicken wings come alive thanks to Chef Dale Talde's flavorful sweet and sour marinade. Pair with a fresh, simple mango salad and your summer barbecue is complete!

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Chicken Wings

  • 2 pounds whole chicken wings
  • 2/3 cup coconut vinegar
  • 1/2 cup calamansi or lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup lemon-lime soda
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 6 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon fresh cracked black pepper
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, minced
  • Salt

Mango Salad

  • 4 scallions, sliced
  • 1 sweet mango, julienned
  • 1 green mango, julienned

Brushing Oil

  • 1 tablespoon annatto seeds
  • 1/2 cup grapeseed oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Blend together all of the marinade ingredients.
  2. Pour the marinade over the chicken and marinate for at least 4 hours and up to overnight.
