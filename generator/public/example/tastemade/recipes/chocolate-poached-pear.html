Chocolate Poached Pear

Rich, decadent, chocolate-covered pears are the perfect sensual dessert.

By gladys nyouth

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 whole pears, peeled
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salted butter
  • Semi-sweet baking chocolate
  • 1/2 cup half and half
  • 1/4 cup confectioners sugar
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup cream of coconut
  • 1 vanilla bean

Steps

  1. In a pot, boil enough water so that it covers 1/3 piece of each pear. Once water reaches a boil, reduce heat and place each peeled pear inside the pot.
  2. Layer each pear with lemon juice and brown sugar and steam the pears until the flesh is tender.
