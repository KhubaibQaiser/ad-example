Chamoy Grilled Fruit Skewers with Honey Lime Sauce

A street food classic gets a tasty topping and dip.

By Danni Rose

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 12
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Fruit Skewers

  • 8 ounces cubed mango, 3/4 thick
  • 8 ounces watermelon, cut into 3/4-inch cubes
  • 8 ounces pineapple, cut into 3/4-inch cubes
  • 8 ounces jicama, peeled and cut into 3/4-inch cubes
  • 8 ounces persian cucumbers, cut into 3/4-inch rounds
  • 8 ounces cantaloupe, cut into 3/4-inch cubes
  • 10 inches wooden skewers, soaked in water
  • 1/4 cup chamoy
  • Vegetable cooking spray
  • Chile lime seasoning such as tajin, for sprinkling

Honey Lime Sauce

  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • Zest and juice of 1 lime (about 2 tablespoons juice)

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill pan over a high flame.
  2. Thread the fruit and vegetables onto the skewers.
