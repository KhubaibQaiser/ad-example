Tostones Grilled Cheese

Grilled cheese gets a whole new ride in delicious tostones.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Tostones Grilled Cheese

  • 1 green plantain, peeled and sliced into six 1 inch pieces
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3–4 pepper jack cheeses
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Sauce

  • 1/3 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • Chopped 1 garlic clove
  • Minced 1 teaspoon red jalapeño
  • Minced 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons red wine vinegar
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet or deep fryer.
  2. Fry plantain pieces for about 3 minutes.
