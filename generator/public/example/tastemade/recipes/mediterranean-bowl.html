Mediterranean Bowl

Feta-stuffed meatballs and smoky chickpeas make this zesty zucchini noodle bowl a nutritious yet satisfying meal.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Feta-Stuffed Meatballs

  • 1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons chopped mint
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 ounces feta, cut into 1/2-inch cubes

Spiced Chickpeas

  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 shallot, sliced
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 pinch red chili flakes
  • 30 ounces chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

Zucchini Noodles

  • 4 spiralized zucchinis
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 pinch kosher salt

Assembly

  • Grated carrot
  • Grape tomato, sliced
  • Persian cucumber, sliced

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°F. Line a sheet tray with parchment paper.
  2. Combine beef, green onions, garlic, egg, mint, cumin, salt and pepper in a large bowl. Roll 1 golfball-sized meatballs with your hands and pop a cube of feta inside. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes.
