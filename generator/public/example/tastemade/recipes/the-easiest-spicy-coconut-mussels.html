The Easiest Spicy Coconut Mussels

Stella Star Ingredient: Coconut Milk

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 shallot, thinly sliced
  • 2 teaspoons sliced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon chopped lemongrass
  • 2 teaspoons chopped thai chili
  • 2 pounds mussels
  • 1/3 cup cold water
  • 1/4 cup rice wine
  • 1/2 teaspoon fish sauce
  • 1 handful basil
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Lime
  • Crusty bread

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat; add coconut oil. When oil is hot, add shallot and fry them until lightly golden, about 2 to 3 minutes. Remove fried shallot from skillet and place on a paper towel lined dish and set aside.
  2. In same skillet with shallot infused coconut oil; add garlic, lemon and chili. Saute until they soften, about 2 to 3 minutes. Add mussels, water, rice wine and fish sauce. Increase heat to high heat and stir to combine everything. Cover and cook until muscles are fully cooked, about 6 to 8 minutes. Discarded un-opened mussels.
