Tequila Chocolate Pots

This decadent chocolate pot is the perfect way to utilize smooth Tequila Mi CAMPO outside of traditional cocktail recipes.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Tequila Chocolate Pots

  • ¾ cup finely chopped milk chocolate or chocolate chips
  • ½ cup finely chopped dark chocolate or chocolate chips
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup condensed milk
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • Seeds from ½ a vanilla bean pod and the pod or ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 oz Reposado Tequila Mi CAMPO
  • ¼ stick butter, cut into cubes
  • Pinch cinnamon

Optional serving accompaniments:

  • Chocolate flakes, lime zest, sea salt flakes, biscuits, frozen grapes, mango and papaya slices

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate in a microwave or in a heatproof bowl set over a pan of gently simmering water. Heat the cream, condensed milk and salt in a small saucepan until very warm.
  2. Pour the cream mixture into the chocolate along with the vanilla, tequila, butter and cinnamon. Fold with a spatula until smooth and glossy.
  3. Pour or spoon into the serving pots. Leave to set in the fridge for 2 hours or overnight. Add a pinch of salt, chocolate flakes and lime zest to serve. Eat at room temp or blast for a few seconds in the microwave.
