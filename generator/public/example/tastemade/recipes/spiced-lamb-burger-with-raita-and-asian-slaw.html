Spiced Lamb Burger with Raita and Asian Slaw

Spice up a dinner date with a homemade lamb burger slathered in mango chutney, raita, and a crunchy Asian-style slaw.

By Conor Maynard

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Recipe for Love

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Lamb Burger

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 30 grams sultanas
  • 250 grams lamb mince
  • 15 grams garam massala
  • 1 egg
  • 20 grams plain flour
  • 45 milliliters olive oil
  • 1 handful spinach
  • Mango chutney
  • 2 brioche burger buns

Cabbage Slaw

  • 100 grams red cabbage
  • 1 small carrot
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 5 grams nigella seeds
  • 20 grams coriander
  • Lime

Cucumber Raita

  • 1/4 cucumber
  • 1 handful mint leaves
  • 100 grams greek yogurt
  • 2 1/2 grams ground coriander
  • 1 lime

Steps

  1. Finely chop the garlic, sultanas and coriander.
  2. Place the lamb mince, garlic, sultanas and coriander into a mixing bowl, add 15 grams garam masala and some salt and pepper.
