White Wine Sorbet Station

A delicious twist on a sundae station with tropical passion fruit, lemon & lime, elderflower & apple, and grapefruit flavors that complement Kim Crawford’s Sauvignon Blanc. Laid out with a range of fresh, delicious, and healthy toppings; it’s the perfect adult pick and mix!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 8 hr
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Sugar White Wine Syrup

  • ⅔ C water
  • 1 C sugar
  • 1¼ C Kim Crawford Sauvignon Blanc

For Passion Fruit Sorbet

  • 1 ¼ C passion fruit puree

For the Grapefruit Sorbet

  • 1 C grapefruit Juice

For the Apple and Elderflower Sorbet

  • ½ cup elderflower cordial
  • ¾ cup apple juice

For the Lemon and Lime Sorbet

  • Juice and zest of 2 lemons
  • Juice and zest of 2 limes

Toppings

  • Candied Citrus
  • Toasted Coconut Flakes
  • Dried Mango Slices
  • Mint
  • Fruit Preserves
  • Dehydrated Pineapple Chunks
  • Granola
  • Chopped Nuts

Steps

Sugar White Wine Syrup

  1. Add the water, sugar and Kim Crawford Sauvignon Blanc to a medium pan, and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and simmer for 3-5 mins until the sugar has dissolved and the liquid has thickened slightly. Remove from the heat and allow to cool.
  2. Combine the sugar syrup and desired flavorings. Mix well.
  3. Pour mixtures into shallow, freezer-proof containers and freeze for 8 hours. The ice crystals will need to be broken down, so every hour for the first four hours, remove the container from the freezer and, using an electric whisk on low, break the surface of the sorbet, stir and place back in the freezer.
  4. Remove from the freezer after completely frozen (about 8 hours) and allow to soften slightly before serving.

For Passion Fruit Sorbet

For the Grapefruit Sorbet

  1. Combine the sugar syrup and desired flavorings. Mix well.

For the Apple and Elderflower Sorbet

For the Lemon and Lime Sorbet

  1. Combine the sugar syrup and desired flavorings. Mix well.

Toppings

  1. Sprinkle the sorbet with your favorite toppings and enjoy!
