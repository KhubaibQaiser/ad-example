Poke Bowl with Seared Tuna Steak

Save money by creating your own impressive poke bowl at home, complete with toppings like edamame, mango, and a beautiful seared tuna steak.

By Conor Maynard

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 2
Easy

Ingredients

Poke Bowl

  • 7 ounces sushi rice
  • 1/2 mango
  • 2 3/4 ounces edamame beans
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 small sheets nori
  • 2 tuna steaks
  • 2 tablespoons white sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1/4 ounce pickled sushi ginger
  • 1 large handful coriander
  • 1 teaspoon black sesame seeds

Dressing

  • 3 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce

Steps

  1. Add the rice to a saucepan. Cover with water and swirl the water around to wash the rice. Put the water out and repeat 4â€“5 times until the water is clear.
  2. Cover the rice with 9 3/4 fluid ounces fresh cold water. Bring to a boil then turn down the heat to low and cover with a lid.
