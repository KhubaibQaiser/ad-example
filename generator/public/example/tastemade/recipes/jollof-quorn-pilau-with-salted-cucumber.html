Jollof Quorn Pilau with Salted Cucumber

Chef Nadiya Hussain's one-pot jollof rice recipe starts with a homemade spice mixture that'll come in handy for future dishes. Paired with salted cucumber, this comforting dish is perfect for feeding a crowd.

By Nadiya Hussein

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Jollof Spice Mix

  • 30 grams ground ginger
  • 30 grams garlic granules
  • 45 grams chilli flakes
  • 20 grams dried thyme
  • 20 grams ground cinnamon
  • 15 grams ground nutmeg
  • 20 grams ground coriander
  • 19 grams salt
  • 8 grams ground black pepper

Pilau

  • 500 grams basmati rice
  • 75 milliliters olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 12 grams salt
  • 300 grams quorn pieces
  • 60 grams jollof spice mix (see above)

Salted Cucumber

  • 1 large cucumber, cut in half lengthways and thinly sliced on an angle
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 20 grams rock salt

Steps

  1. Start by making the jollof spice mix.
  2. Put all the spices into a large jar. We only need 1 small pinch for this recipe - the rest can be covered and put away from another occasion.
