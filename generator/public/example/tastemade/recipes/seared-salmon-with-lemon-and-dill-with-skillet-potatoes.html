Seared Salmon with Lemon and Dill, with Skillet Fried Potatoes and Onions

This meal of seared salmon with a classic lemon, dill and caper pan sauce, and a side of crispy skillet-fried potatoes and onions is the perfect thing to cook over live fire.

(1)

By Andrew Zimmern

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Andrew Zimmern's Wild Game Kitchen

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 8 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 6–8 ounces salmon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 5 tablespoons grapeseed or canola oil
  • 2 cups of diced yellow yukon gold or idaho potatoes
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 1 handful chopped dill
  • 2/3 cup white wine
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 4 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Place both skillets over high heat on a grill top set over a moderately deep 3-feet-wide coal bed.
  2. Season the salmon pieces with salt and pepper.
