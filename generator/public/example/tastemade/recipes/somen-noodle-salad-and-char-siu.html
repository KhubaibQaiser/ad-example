Somen Noodle Salad and Char Siu

This hearty somen noodle salad features an array of vegetables and Chinese BBQ pork, and can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch or dinner.

By Lani Nishiyama

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Somen Noodle Salad

  • 1 package somen noodles
  • 1 cucumber, seeded and sliced thin
  • 1 head green lettuce, shredded
  • 1 egg, fried and sliced into strips
  • Char siu, chilled and cut into thin strips
  • 1 kamaboko (typically pink and white), sliced thin
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 garlics, minced
  • 1–2 tablespoons sesame seeds, pulverized

Char Siu — Chinese BBQ Pork

  • 1 pork tenderloin, pierce with a fork or knife all over
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon red bean paste (chinese, found in asian section or stores)
  • 1 large gallon ziplock bag

Steps

  1. Boil somen, rinse, drain and plate (make individual swirls with a fork/chopstick on the salad to make it easier to serve and eat).
  2. Layer the somen with lettuce, cucumbers.
