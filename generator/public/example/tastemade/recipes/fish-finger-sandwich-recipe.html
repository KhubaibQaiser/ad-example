Fish Finger Sandwich

Name something more British than our Fish Finger Sandwich recipe, we'll wait. This quick, simple and easy recipe is perfect for a BBQ, lunch or dinner. Trust us when we say that the kids will be asking for this whenever they get the chance.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 5
Average
 :

Ingredients

Tartare Sauce

  • 6 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 8 cornichons, drained and roughly diced
  • 1 heaped tablespoon capers, drained and roughly chopped
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 tablespoon dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Fish

  • 21 1/4 ounces cod loin, skinned
  • 2 medium sized eggs
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon mustard powder (optional)
  • 2 heaped tablespoons plain flour
  • 3 1/2 ounces fresh breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • Vegetable oil

Mushy Peas

  • 1 ounce butter
  • 2 echalion shallots, finely chopped
  • 7–8 ounces frozen peas
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 1/4 fluid ounces water (room temperature)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Serving

  • 5 brioche/burger buns
  • Pea shoots/watercress leaf
  • Chunky chip
  • Lemon wedge

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl and place in the fridge until you serve.
