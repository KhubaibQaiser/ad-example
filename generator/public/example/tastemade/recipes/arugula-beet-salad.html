Arugula Beet Salad

Brighten up any meal with this fast and easy side salad, loaded with sweet beets.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 4 beets (about 8 ounces)
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 ounces arugula
  • 3/4 cup walnuts
  • 12 ounces feta, crumbled

Steps

  1. Quarter the beets.
  2. Whisk together the mustard, vinegar and olive oil to make the dressing.
  3. Place some arugula on a plate.
  4. Top with the beets, walnuts, feta and dressing.
