Frozen Yogurt Dot Candy

These giant frozen yogurt candy buttons are as cute as, well, a button.

By dini klein

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 3 hr
Servings: 3
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon freeze-dried blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon freeze-dried strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon freeze-dried bananas
  • 9 tablespoons greek yogurt
  • 6 teaspoons honey

Steps

  1. Measure 6 rectangles rectangles, 4x6 inches each, on a piece of parchment paper and cut out. Place on baking sheet.
  2. Using 3 sandwich-sizes Ziploc bags, place blueberries in one, strawberries in another, and bananas in the last. Squeeze out the air and seal each bag.
