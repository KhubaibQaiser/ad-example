Greek Turkey Protein Burgers with Tzatziki Sauce

This low-carb turkey burger with tzatziki sauce is loaded with Greek flavors.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

Greek Turkey Protein Burgers

  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup sun dried tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 small red onion, diced
  • 1 cup baby spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta
  • 1/4 cup almond meal
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Tzatziki Sauce

  • 1 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • 1 garlic clove, whole
  • 1 english cucumber, diced
  • 2 tablespoons hemp seed hearts
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk, plus more as needed
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill
  • 4 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Toppings

  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, for buns
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 large tomato, sliced

Steps

  1. To make the patties, combine the ground turkey, sun-dried tomatoes, red onion, spinach, feta, oregano, almond meal, and egg in a medium bowl and mix until fully combined.
  2. Shape into patties using your hands.
