Coconut Tres Leches Cake

This tres leches cake keeps the evaporated and condensed milk but switches things up with coconut milk.

By zoe kelly

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 6
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

Cake

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 6 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Milk Mixture

  • 14 ounces condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • Butter to cover baking pan

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325Â°F, and move the rack to the middle of the oven.
  2. Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan until well coated. Sift the flour into a mixing bowl, making sure to remove any lumps. Add the cinnamon and set aside.
