Cheats Macarons

Did you know you can make amazing macarons without the effort of a sugar syrup?

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 20
Hard
 :

Ingredients

  • 125 grams icing sugar, sieved
  • 75 grams fine ground almonds, sieved
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 50 grams caster sugar
  • 1–2 drops of pink food colouring gel
  • Vanilla buttercream
  • Strawberry jam
  • Pink and blue sprinkle

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 120°C.
  2. With an electric hand mixer, whisk the egg whites until the mixture is foamy. Add a pinch of salt, then add the caster sugar half at a time. Whisk the mixture until soft peaks are formed.
