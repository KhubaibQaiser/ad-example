Cheesy Spinach Artichoke Dip & Pizza

Hosting a game night or having a few friends over? Chef Dini Klein shares her super simple recipe for cheesy spinach artichoke dip, and shows you how to use the leftover dip as a start to amazing homemade pizza.

By Dini Klein

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 32 ounces chopped spinach, thawed and drained
  • 2 cans quartered artichokes, drained
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup labne (or sour cream)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400Â°F. Spray the bottom of a cast-iron pan or baking dish with oil and then add in remaining ingredients. Mix together and then spread out in an even layer.
  2. Bake for 20 minutes until golden and bubbly. Serve with chips or vegetable sticks.
