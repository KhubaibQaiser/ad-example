S'mores Cookies

A campfire classic you can make anytime.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 12 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups plain (all-purpose) flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 16 ounces dark chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 20 regular marshmallows
  • 2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 9 ounces cooking chocolate, roughly chopped, or choc chips
  • 6 graham cookies, roughly chopped
  • 3 1/2 ounces dark chocolate, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda and salt. Set aside.
