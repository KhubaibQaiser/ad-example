Fusilli Lunghi alle Vongole

In the first episode of SOURCED, we take a journey to Sicily, where the best lemons in the world are rumored to grow. Guy Turland from Bondi Harvest explores the centuries-old groves along the Mediterranean coast, at the foot of a smoking Mount Etna.

By Guy Turland

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Sourced

Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fusilli lunghi
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 chili, minced
  • 1 cup vongole
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 cup parsley, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a pot of salted boiling water cook fusilli lunghi as per instructions on packaging.
  2. In a separate pan on medium to medium-high heat, add olive oil, then garlic and chili, and cook until tender.
