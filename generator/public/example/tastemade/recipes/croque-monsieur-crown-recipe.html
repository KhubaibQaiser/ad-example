Croque Monsieur Crown

Croque Monsieur is an epic dish, but we've made an easy version of this classic dish that is perfect for sharing!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 25 grams butter
  • 25 grams plain flour
  • 300 milliliters milk
  • 1 block ready-made puff pastry
  • 100 grams cheddar cheese, grated
  • 100 grams gruyere cheese, grated
  • 8 thick slices good quality ham
  • 100 grams butter
  • Sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. For the béchamel sauce, melt the butter in a small saucepan over low heat. Stir in the flour using a wooden spoon, then cook gently for 1 minute, still stirring. Remove the pan from the heat and gradually whisk in the milk until smooth.
  2. Return the pan to the heat and continue to cook, stirring constantly with the wooden spoon, until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper, then remove from the heat and leave until cold.
