Cereal Milk Ice Cream

Vijaya is showing us her take on Momofuku's incredibly decadent cereal milk ice cream.

By vijaya selvaraju

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 4 hr
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Ice Cream

  • 2 cups cornflakes
  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 cup condensed milk

Cereal Brittle

  • 2 cups crushed cornflakes
  • 1/4 cup milk powder
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 5 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F.
  2. On a lined baking tray, add 2 cups cornflakes and bake for 12 to 15 minutes.
