Vegan Banana Bread

The best ways to use overripe bananas? Whip up homemade banana bread, studded with gooey chocolate chips, for the ultimate breakfast or afternoon snack.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 10
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup muscovado sugar
  • 1/2 cup oatmeal
  • 1 1/3 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/3 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons rolled oats, for garnish

Steps

  1. Cut off 1/3 third of banana and set it aside for garnishing.
  2. In a bowl, mash the rest of the ripe bananas with a fork until completely smooth.
