Pan De Bono

Pan de bono is a delicious Colombian cheese bread that's surprisingly easy to make! Serve warm and top with guava jam for a delicious breakfast or afternoon snack.

By zoe kelly

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 8
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups tapioca flour
  • 2 teaspoons precooked white cornmeal
  • 2 cups queso fresco or shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 teaspoons granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons butter, room temperature
  • Milk, as needed
  • Guava jam, to serve

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F.
  2. In a food processor, add all the dry ingredients and pulse until combined. While mixing, add one egg at a time then add the butter. Dough should start coming together away from the sides. If not, add 3 tablespoons of room temperature milk until it comes together.
