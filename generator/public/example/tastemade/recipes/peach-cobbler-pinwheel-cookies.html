Peach Cobbler Pinwheel Cookies

Lazarus Lynch turns the popular Southern peach cobbler into delicate bite-sized pinwheel cookies.

By lazarus lynch

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 12
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 1/2 ounces plain cream cheese, softened
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons peach preserves
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. In a standing mixer, beat together the butter, cream cheese, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Add the vanilla and mix until light and fluffy. Stir in the flour and salt.
