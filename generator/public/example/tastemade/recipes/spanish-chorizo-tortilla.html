Spanish Chorizo Tortilla

This Spanish-style tortilla, a potato and egg dish, includes chorizo, a flavorful Spanish sausage.

By Guy Turland

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Sourced

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 5 1/4 ounces sliced chorizo
  • 3 cloves garlic (sliced)
  • 3 red potatoes, (peeled and sliced thinly)
  • 1 minced chili
  • 1 onion (sliced finely)
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 8 eggs
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a pan on medium heat add olive oil then chorizo, garlic, onion and chili. Cook till tender and fat is rendered, then pour into a bowl.
  2. In the same pan add potato and fry till tender and golden, then add chorizo mix, back to the pan and toss.
