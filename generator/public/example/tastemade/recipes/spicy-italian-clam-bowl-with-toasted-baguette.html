Spicy Italian Clam Bowl with Toasted Baguette

Calabrian chili spices briny clams in this hearty dish.

By Daniele Uditi

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more for finishing
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 tablespoon calabrian chili, minced
  • 1/2 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons parsley leaves, plus more for garnish
  • 1 1/2 pounds manila clams
  • 1/2 tall glass dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup fish stock
  • 1 slice slice of baguette, toasted

Steps

  1. Heat up a medium-sized saucepan over medium-heat, add the olive oil.
  2. Add the garlic, chili and tomato paste and cook.
