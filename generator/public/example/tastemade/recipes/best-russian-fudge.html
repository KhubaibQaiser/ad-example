Russian Fudge

A delicious Caramel Fudge, made with butter, golden syrup and sweetened condensed milk.

By Bernadette Gee

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

The Unruly Baker

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 64
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram caster sugar
  • 155 grams butter
  • 70 grams golden syrup
  • 155 grams milk
  • 6 grams salt
  • 250 grams sweetened condensed milk
  • 5 milliliters vanilla essence

Steps

  1. Prep a 20-centimeters square tin by lightly spraying it with cooking spray and lining it with baking paper.
  2. Put all the ingredients except the vanilla in a large pot. Heat on LOW, stirring occasionally. The mixture will melt together and then start to slowly boil with large, rolling, popping bubbles. Be careful when you stir it, as these bubbles can pop and splash you-and trust me, that is not ideal and burns instantly.
