Crispy Rice Balls

Onigiri is traditional crispy rice ball glazed with tare yet still tender and chewy.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Average
Â :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup rice wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce

Steps

  1. Add the rice and water to a saucepan and bring to a boil.
  2. Turn down the heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
