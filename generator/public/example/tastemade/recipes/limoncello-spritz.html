Limoncello Spritz

Reminiscent of summer evenings, this refreshing limoncello spritz features prosecco, club soda, and mint to perfectly balance the limoncello's sweetness.

By daniele uditi

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 5 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces limoncello
  • 3 ounces prosecco
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 squeeze lemon rind
  • 1 ounce club soda
  • 1 slice lemon
  • 2–3 cubes ice
  • 2–3 mint leaves, for garnish

Steps

  1. Place the ice in the glass.
  2. Mix the sugar with the limoncello and add to the glass.
