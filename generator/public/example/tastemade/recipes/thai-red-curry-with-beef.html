Thai Red Curry with Beef

The deep flavors of Thai red curry pair perfectly with seared beef. Though this recipe takes a bit of time, the complex flavors are worth the effort.

By Conor Maynard

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Recipe for Love

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 5 1/4 ounces brown basmati rice
  • 1 tablespoon sunflower oil
  • 1 large brown onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 thumb size piece of ginger
  • 1 tablespoon red thai curry paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 teaspoon dark brown sugar
  • 2 lime leaves
  • 1 red pepper
  • 5 1/4 ounces green beans
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 lime
  • 1 red chilli
  • 7 ounces beef rump steak
  • Coriander, for garnishing

Steps

  1. Wash the rice in a large saucepan until the water runs clear.
  2. Add 10 1/2 fluid ounces cold water to the washed rice along with some salt.
