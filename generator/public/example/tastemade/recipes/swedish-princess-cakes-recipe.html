Swedish Princess Cakes

Take your shop brought cupcakes and take them to the next level with our awesome princess cakes recipe!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 50 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 10
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 65 grams caster sugar
  • 15 grams plain flour
  • 15 grams cornflour
  • 350 milliliters whole milk
  • 2 1/2 milliliters vanilla extract
  • 100 grams fresh raspberries
  • 13 grams icing sugar sugar
  • Zest of 1 lemon, finely grated
  • 400 milliliters double cream
  • 13 grams icing sugar
  • 500 grams white marzipan
  • Green food coloring, if desired
  • Pink food colouring, if desired

Steps

  1. For the pastry cream, in a large mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar, flour, cornstarch and vanilla and set aside.
  2. Place the milk in a saucepan and bring to a gentle simmer. Remove the pan from the heat and let cool for 1 minute.
