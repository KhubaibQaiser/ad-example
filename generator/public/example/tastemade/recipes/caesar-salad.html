Spicy Caesar Salad

This decadent Caesar features crispy fried capers and a Calabrian chile-spiced dressing.

By Daniele Uditi

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 large anchovies filets under oil
  • 1 1/2 eaches garlic cloves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sea salt
  • 1/2 cup parmigiano reggiano finely grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon calabrian chili paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
  • 3/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cups baby honey gem lettuce leaves
  • 1 cup shaved parmigiano reggiano
  • 1/2 cup of bread croutons
  • 1/4 cup fried capers
  • 1/4 cup finely grated parmigiano reggiano
  • 3/4 cup caesar dressing

Steps

  1. In a blender, add the anchovies and the garlic and the egg yolks.
  2. Start blending at low speed and add the Calabrian chili and Worcestershire sauce.
