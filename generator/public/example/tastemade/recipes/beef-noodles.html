Beef Noodles

These savory beef noodles are packed with veggies, steak, and a delicious sauce.

(1)

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 1 hr 30 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce, divided
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce, divided
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 10 ounces skirt steak
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • Salt
  • 16 ounces lo mein noodles
  • 10 ounces baby bok choy
  • 4 ounces button mushrooms

Steps

  1. Slice the steak into thin pieces.
  2. In a large bowl, add 3 tablespoons soy sauce, 1 tablespoon oyster sauce and 1 teaspoon sesame oil. Whisk to combine. Add the steak and toss to coat. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
