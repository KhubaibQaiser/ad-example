Pretzel Chicken Schnitzel

A crispy, pretzel-breaded chicken cutlet served with a side of tangy, quick sauerkraut.

(2)Recipe Notes (1)

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4

Ingredients

Quick Sauerkraut

  • 1/2 head green cabbage
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 2 teaspoons salt

Schnitzel

  • 2 cups vegetable oil
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 5 cups pretzels
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons dijon
  • 2 tablespoons minced parsley leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Thinly slice the cabbage. Add the white vinegar, water, honey and salt to a medium sized saucepan and bring to a boil. Turn down to a simmer, add the cabbage and cook for 15 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and allow to cool to room temperature. Pour into a jar or container with a tight fitting lid. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 2 weeks.
