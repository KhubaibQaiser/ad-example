Mediterranean Flatbread

Load up a gluten-free flatbread with vibrant and delicious Mediterranean ingredients like hummus, roasted cherry tomatoes, fresh vegetables, dairy-free tzatziki, and a delicious and easy-to make olive gremolata. This quick lunch is full of color and flavor!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Oven Roasted Cherry Tomatoes

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons thyme, roughly chopped
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Olive Gremolata

  • 1 cup Cerignola olives, pitted
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ¼ piece preserved lemon, minced
  • ⅓ cup parsley, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons pine nuts, toasted and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons mint, chopped
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar

Flatbreads

  • Store-bought gluten-free pizza doughs
  • Hummus
  • Fresh dill and parsley
  • Avocado, sliced
  • Radish, shaved
  • Red onion, shaved
  • Oven roasted cherry tomatoes
  • Olive Gremolata
  • Tzatziki
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps

Oven Roasted Cherry Tomatoes

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. For the tomatoes, toss all ingredients in a small mixing bowl and place on a parchment lined sheet tray.
  3. Slow roast until the tomatoes shrivel and caramelize.

Olive Gremolata

  1. For the gremolata, mix all ingredients together in a small bowl.

Flatbreads

  1. To assemble, toast the gluten-free pizza dough in the oven until warmed.
  2. Spread hummus over the flatbread, then spoon olive gremolata over the hummus.
  3. Top with fresh ingredients and oven roasted cherry tomatoes, then drizzle with tzatziki and olive oil.
  4. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes over the flatbread to taste. Enjoy!
