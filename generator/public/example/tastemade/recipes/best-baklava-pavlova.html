Baklava Pavlova

Enjoy all the flavors of a delicious baklava — like pistachio, walnuts, and honey — in this unique pavlova recipe! This is the ultimate fusion dessert that's sure to impress your guests.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 3 hr 20 min
Cook Time: 3 hr 5 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

aOrange Nut Filling

  • 2 unwaxed oranges
  • 60 milliliters of honey
  • 25 grams walnuts, toasted and roughly chopped
  • 25 grams pistachios, toasted and roughly chopped
  • 1 gram ground cardamom seeds (seeds removed from the pod)

Meringues

  • 6 egg whites
  • 350 grams golden caster sugar
  • 3 grams corn flour
  • 10 milliliters orange blossom water

Syrup

  • 75 milliliters honey
  • The juice of 1/2 orange
  • 30 milliliters orange blossom water

Filo Discs

  • 250 grams filo pastry
  • 75 grams unsalted butter, melted
  • Syrup

Cream Filling

  • 600 milliliters double cream
  • 250 grams mascarpone cheese
  • 20 milliliters orange blossom water

Assembly

  • Meringue
  • Filo disc
  • Cream filling
  • Orange nut filling
  • 25 grams pomegranate seeds, for garnish
  • 25 grams pistachios, toasted and roughly chopped for garnish
  • 25 grams walnuts, toasted and roughly chopped for garnish

Steps

  1. Place the oranges in a saucepan with water, make sure they are completely covered. Bring to the boil and the down to a simmer, cook for an hour until completely soft. Set aside to cool slightly.
  2. Cut the oranges in half, remove the seeds and place in a food processor puree until smooth. Scoop the puree out in to some cheesecloth or muslin, squeeze out as much liquid as possible over a bowl. Reserve this juice for use in the syrup.
