Popcorn Grits and Shrimp

For the purists out there, please keep an open mind - popcorn grits are delicious!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :

Ingredients

Grits

  • 1 cup water
  • 4 cups plain popcorn, popped
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper

Shrimp

  • 1/2 pound shrimp
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil, divided

Garnish

  • Bacon
  • Parsley
  • Chili flake
  • Chili oil

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, bring water to a simmer.
  2. Add popcorn to a large food processor. Pour water over kernels and blend until smooth. Using a mesh strainer, press the grits though, catching all of the large kernels. Add grits to saucepan, stir in milk and cheese. Simmer until mixture begins to thicken. Remove from heat, stir in butter and salt. Set aside.
