Cauldron Cake

Red velvet cake with a black cocoa buttercream

By Mia Starr

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 12
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Red Velvet Cake

  • 4 cups ap flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 3/4 cup salted butter
  • 2 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup oil
  • 2 ounces red food color
  • 3 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white vinegar
  • 2 cups buttermilk

Chocolate Buttercream

  • 2 sticks unsalted butter
  • 1 pound powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup black or dark cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons strong black coffee

Vanilla Buttercream

  • 1 1/2 pounds unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 3 pounds powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon almond extract
  • 3/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 pinches salt
  • 2–3 drops purple gel color

Assemble & Decorate Cake

  • 1/4 pound green modeling chocolate
  • 1/4 pound white modeling chocolate
  • 1/8 pound pink modeling chocolate
  • 1/8 pound white modeling chocolate
  • 1 tablespoon black modeling chocolate
  • 4 skewers
  • 4–5 white chocolate truffles
  • 2–4 drops red gel color
  • 1 teaspoon gold glitter stars
  • 1/4 cup sweetapolita candy apple sprinkles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line and grease 3 each 8-inches cake pans.
  2. In a medium bowl whisk together the flour, cocoa powder and baking soda.
