Green Curry Shrimp Patties

Brighten up boring weeknight dinners with Chef Rashida Holmes' bold island flavors! These turmeric shortcrust patties are stuffed with an herbaceous, spicy shrimp mix for a handheld patty that works as an appetizer or a main course.

By Rashida Holmes

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Shrimp Filling

  • 2 pounds shrimp
  • 4 anaheim peppers
  • 3 green bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 4 scallions
  • 1 bunch parsley
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 1 bunch chives
  • 10–12 culantros
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced thin
  • 2 habanero or scotch bonnet peppers
  • 2 tablespoons chopped ginger
  • 2 tablespoons your favorite curry powder
  • 4 yukon gold potatoes, medium
  • 1 lemon halved

Turmeric Shortcrust Pastry

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground turmeric
  • 7 ounces butter
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 1/2 ounces crisco shortening

To Assemble

  • Shrimp filling
  • Turmeric shortcrust pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup ap flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 fork

Steps

  1. Place potatoes in a 4 quarts pan, cover with water, season with 2 pinches of salt, and simmer until potatoes are soft. Drain the water and let cool
  2. In another 4 quarts pot, bring water up to a boil. Blanch the scallion greens, parsley, cilantro, and chives for 10 seconds and then place in ice water to cool
