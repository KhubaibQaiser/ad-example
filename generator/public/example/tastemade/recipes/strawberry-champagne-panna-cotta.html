Strawberry Champagne Panna Cotta

A delicious, bubbly, sweet treat for the holidays.

(1)Recipe Notes (1)

By Mia Starr

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon unflavored gelatin
  • 1 each vanilla bean
  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 cups sparkling pink champagne
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup pureed strawberries
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons powdered gelatine
  • 2 cups de-stemmed fresh strawberries

Steps

  1. In a medium sauce pan, add the whole milk and sugar and stir.
  2. Sprinkle the top with the powdered gelatin and let set for 5 minutes to bloom.
