Greek Moussaka

Chef Erwan Heussaff's take on traditional Greek Moussaka includes a creamy cauliflower bechamel sauce. Try this hearty casserole for dinner on a chilly weeknight!

By erwan heussaff

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Moussaka

  • 2 large eggplants, cut in 1/4 inch thick slices
  • 1 large sweet potato, cut in 1/4 inch thick slices
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Meat

  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium white onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 28 ounces crushes or whole tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon all spice
  • 4 tablespoons of fresh oregano leaves

Cauliflower Sauce

  • 1 medium cauliflower, cut in florets
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Pre heat your oven to 400°F.
  2. Slice up your eggplants and sweet potatoes lengthwise. You can season the eggplants with salt and let them rest for 30mins or just brush a baking dish with some olive and lay them on it, brush them with some oil and then season with salt. Do the same thing with your sweet potatoes.
