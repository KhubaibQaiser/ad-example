Beer And Honey BBQ Chicken Skewers

Happiness is only a bottle of beer and a delicious, flavor-packed chicken skewer away.

By lazarus lynch

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 10
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup beer
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 chicken breasts, cut into small cubes
  • Cilantro, for garnish

Steps

  1. Cut chicken in small pieces, about 1 1/2 inches cubes.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together all ingredients except chicken and cilantro. Add the chicken to bowl so marinade covers each piece of chicken. Cover with plastic wrap and place in fridge. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours to 24 hours.
