Chef's at Home: Daniele Uditi's Cacio e Pepe Pizza

Chef Daniele Uditi of Pizzana shows us how to make pizza like a pro, starting with a Cacio e Pepe twist everybody loves!

By daniele uditi

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Prep Time: 6 hr
Cook Time: 7 min
Servings: 12

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

  • 8 cups bread flour (64 oz)
  • 2 3/4 cups water, room temperature (22 oz)
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sea salt

Parmigiano crema

  • 2 cups cow ricotta (16 oz)
  • 1 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano (8 oz)
  • 1/2 cup regular heavy whipping cream (4 oz)

Cacio e Pepe Pizza

  • Flour or semolina
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Freshly cracked black pepper
  • Salt
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons regular heavy whipping cream, per pizza
  • 1 2/3 cups grated dry whole milk mozzarella (13 oz)
  • 2 1/2 cups grated fresh mozzarella (20 oz)
  • 5 tablespoons Parmigiano-Reggiano

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add the flour and water and combine until the mixture comes together. Let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Dissolve the yeast in a little warm water and add to flour and water mixture. Knead until all the yeast is absorbed. Add the salt and knead for 10 to 15 minutes to start developing some gluten.
