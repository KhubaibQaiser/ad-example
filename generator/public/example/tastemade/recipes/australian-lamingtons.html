Australian Lamingtons

Julie gives us her take on Australian Lamingtons.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 9
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Australian Lamingtons

  • 9 ounces white chocolate, chopped
  • 7 ounces butter
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder

Icing

  • 4 cups icing sugar
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup, plus 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 pound sweetened, dried coconut

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 315°F.
  2. Grease an 8-inches cake tin and line with parchment paper.
