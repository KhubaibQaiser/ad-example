Elote Style Chipotle Corn Ribs ​

Add some zing to your next cookout with elote chipotle corn ribs made with the creamy goodness of Kraft Sauces. No grill master experience necessary.

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 6
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Marinade

  • ¼ cup KRAFT Garlic Aioli​
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil​
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika​
  • ½ teaspoon salt ​
  • 1 lime, juiced ​
  • 4 cobs of fresh sweet Corn, cut into corn ribs​

Corn Ribs

  • ¼ cup KRAFT Chipotle Aioli​
  • ¼ cup cotija​
  • ½ bunch cilantro, finely chopped ​
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder ​
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Steps

Marinade

  1. In a small bowl whisk together the marinade ingredients.​
  2. ​Toss the ribs in the marinade and allow to hangout for at least 10 minutes. ​
  3. Tips: ​The easiest way to cut corn into “ribs" is to stand the corn up on the wider base and slowly slice the corn down the middle from tip to tip. Then repeat on each half you just created. ​

Corn Ribs

  1. Heat a grill pan to medium heat, and grill the corn, kernel side down, until charred and curled, about 6-8 minutes.​
  2. ​Transfer the grilled corn ribs to a serving platter, drizzle with KRAFT Chipotle Aioli, sprinkle with cotija cheese, cilantro, chili powder and serve with lime wedges. ​
