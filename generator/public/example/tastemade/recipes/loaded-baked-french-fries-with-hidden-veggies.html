Loaded Baked French Fries With Hidden Veggies

Loaded French fries don't have to be loaded with unnecessary calories. Here is a baked recipe loaded with veggies.

By michel janse

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Loaded Baked French Fries With Hidden Veggies

  • 2 large russet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast

Veggie Guacamole

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 lime
  • 1/2 cup pre-made pico de gallo

Toppings

  • 1 can black beans, rinsed
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 jalapeño, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons scallions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup broccoli micro-greens

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  2. Cut potatoes into pieces the size of steak fries. Place in a mixing bowl and toss with olive oil, salt, chili powder and onion powder.
