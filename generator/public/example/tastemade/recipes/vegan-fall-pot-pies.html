Vegan Fall Pot Pies

These gluten-free savory pot pies are perfect for a chilly autumn night.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 1/2 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup frozen green beans
  • 6 cremini mushrooms, quartered
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened plain almond milk
  • 2 cups low sodium vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt, plus more for topping
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, plus more for topping
  • 1/4 cup chickpea flour
  • 2 tablespoons arrowroot starch
  • 2–3 baby red potatoes, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Once hot, add onion, carrot, garlic, mushrooms, pepper, and salt, and then stir stir. Cook until soft, about 6–8 minutes.
