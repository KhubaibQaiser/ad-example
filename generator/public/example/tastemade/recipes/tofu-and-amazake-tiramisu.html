Tofu And Amazake Tiramisu

Tofu makes for a yummy dessert! Sweetened with a Japanese rice drink, this super silky tofu is layered with sponge cake and matcha syrup.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Servings: 3

Ingredients

Tofu And Amazake Tiramisu

  • 7 ounces castella sponge cake
  • Cream
  • Matcha sauce
  • Matcha powder
  • Gold leaf

Tofu Cream

  • 10 1/2 ounces soft tofu
  • 2 3/4 fluid ounces amazake (fermented rice drink)
  • 2 tablespoons cane sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  • Vanilla extract

Matcha Sauce

  • 1/2 teaspoon matcha powder
  • 1 teaspoon cane sugar
  • 3 1/2 fluid ounces hot water
  • 1/2 tablespoon rum

Steps

  1. Cut the castella cake into small pieces.
  2. Prepare the tofu cream.
