Back-To-School Smoothies

Cure back-to-school blues with these power-packed smoothies.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Concentration

  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup almond milk

Nerves

  • 1 teaspoon matcha green tea powder
  • 1 banana
  • 1/4 avocado
  • 1 teaspoon acai berry powder
  • 1/2 fresh peach
  • 1 cup peach juice
  • 1 drizzle honey

Emergy

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon instant coffee

Steps

  1. To make all smoothies, just pop the ingredients into a blender, pulse until smooth and enjoy!
