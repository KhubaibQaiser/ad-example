Sun Dried Tomato and Spinach Stuffed Mushrooms

A delicious vegetarian recipe, which can be served alongside a side salad for a quick weekday dish!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Sun Dried Tomato and Spinach Stuffed Mushrooms

  • 4 large portobello mushrooms, stem removed and finely chopped
  • 1 brown onion, finely diced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained and finely chopped
  • 3 ¾ cups spinach, washed
  • ½ cup dried breadcrumbs
  • 3 oz hard goat cheese, grated
  • 2 oz cheddar cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Serving

  • Green salad
Get Ingredients

Steps

Sun Dried Tomato and Spinach Stuffed Mushrooms

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp of the olive oil in a medium pan, add the mushrooms stems and onion then cook over a medium heat for 10 mins until softened and opaque.
  3. Meanwhile, place the whole mushrooms onto a wire rack over a baking tray. Drizzle with 1 tbsp of olive oil and season as desired.
  4. Place into the preheated oven and bake for 10 minutes.
  5. While the mushrooms are cooking, boil water and place the spinach into a colander.
  6. Once the water has boiled, pour over the spinach to wilt it then refresh it under cold water. Squeeze out any excess moisture using a tea towel or kitchen roll, then roughly chop.
  7. In a large bowl, add the sun-dried tomatoes, spinach, breadcrumbs, grated goat cheese, cooked onions, and mushroom stems. Mix to combine.
  8. Remove the mushrooms from the oven and evenly fill them with the mixture. Top with the cheddar, and place back in the oven until the cheese has melted and begins to turn golden brown.

Serving

  1. Serve immediately with salad and enjoy!
