Coconut Lime Truffles

Find paradise in these coconut lime truffles.

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 1 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 11 ounces white chocolate chips
  • 1/3 cup coconut cream
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. In a saucepan on medium-low heat, combine coconut cream and lime zest. Once it comes to a simmer, remove from heat. Stir in lime juice and salt.
  2. Place white chocolate chips in a heat-proof bowl. Pour heated infused coconut cream over the chocolate. Continuously stir until all the chocolate has melted. If there are lumps, melt in the microwave for a few seconds and stir again.
