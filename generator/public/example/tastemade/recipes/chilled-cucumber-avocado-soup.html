Chilled Cucumber Avocado Soup

Cool off on hot summer days with a chilled cucumber avocado soup with a subtle spicy kick.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 cucumbers
  • 3 avocados, divided
  • 3/4 cup cilantro leaves
  • 1/2 cup parsley leaves
  • 3 tablespoons roughly minced chives
  • 3 scallions, sliced
  • 1 jalapeno, seeded and diced
  • 1/2 cup full fat greek yogurt
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt & pepper
  • 4 radishes, sliced

Steps

  1. Peel the cucumbers and roughly chop them. Slice 2 avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop out the flesh. Add the cucumbers and 1 avocado to the blender and puree.
  2. Cut the stems off the parsley, 1/2 cup of cilantro, and roughly mince the chives. Slice the scallions into 1/4 inch slices. Remove the seeds from the jalapeno and dice.
