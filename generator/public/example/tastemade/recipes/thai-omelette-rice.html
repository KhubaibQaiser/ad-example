Thai Omelette Rice

Tomato fried rice packaged in a fluffy omelet is the tastiest gift of all.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Fried rice

  • 4 1/4 ounces cooked rice
  • 1 3/4 ounces ground pork
  • 1/3 cup onion, diced
  • 1/2 small tomato
  • 2 green beans
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Thai Omelette

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Chili sauce
  • Lettuce leaf
  • Green onion

Steps

  1. Heat some vegetable oil in a frying pan. Saute garlic and onions until translucent. Then add in ground pork, tomatoes and green beans and cook until done.
  2. Add in cooked rice, ketchup, fish sauce, oyster sauce and sugar and stir-fry for one minute. Turn off heat and set aside.
