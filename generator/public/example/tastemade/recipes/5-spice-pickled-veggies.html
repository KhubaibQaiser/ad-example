Five-Spice Pickled Veggies

An Asian flavored quick pickle recipe.

By danni rose

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 16 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • 1 small cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 2 carrots, peeled and cut into sticks
  • 4 radishes, sliced into rounds
  • 2 persian cucumbers, sliced into rounds
  • 1 fennel bulb with frond
  • 2 cups white vinegar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon szechuan peppercorn
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 4 whole cloves

Steps

  1. Thinly slice the vegetables, and place in a large mason jar.
  2. Add peppercorns, cinnamon sticks, cloves, and fennel seeds into the jar.
