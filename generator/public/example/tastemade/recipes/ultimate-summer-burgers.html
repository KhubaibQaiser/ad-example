Ultimate Summer Burgers

When it comes to grilling season, burgers just can't be beat. Kick of summer with chef Dale Talde's mouthwatering burgers and wedges loaded with the creamy goodness of Kraft Sauces.

By dale talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Burgers

  • 2 pounds ground chuck, formed into 4 8 oz patties​
  • 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard​
  • 4 slices American cheese​
  • 4 potato rolls​

Candied Bacon

  • 1 pound of bacon​
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup​
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper​

Grilled Onions

  • 1 onion, cut into rings​

Assembly

  • 1/4 cup of sliced dill pickles​
  • 1 ripe tomato, cut into slices
  • inch slices salt and pepper to taste​
  • 1 head of butter lettuce​
  • KRAFT Smoky Hickory Bacon Flavored Aioli​
  • KRAFT Burger Aioli

Steps

Burgers

  1. Preheat your grill. ​
  2. Next, season the patties with salt and pepper then brush with Dijon mustard.
  3. Add to the hot grill and sear them. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes each side, then rest on a rack.​

Candied Bacon

  1. On a baking tray lay out bacon, roast on the grill until 80% cooked, about 8 minutes.
  2. Drain bacon grease and reserve.
  3. Top bacon with maple syrup and black pepper and place back onto the grill to cook.
  4. Maple syrup will reduce and candy in about 4-5 minutes.
  5. Once done set the candied bacon aside to cool.​

Grilled Onions

  1. Take reserved bacon fat and brush on the cut side of your bun, toast cut side for 1 minute. Brush the onion with bacon fat, salt and pepper it, then grill 2-3 minutes on one side only.​

Assembly

  1. To assemble the ultimate classic burger, place bottom bun down, then add ½ tablespoon KRAFT Burger Aioli, burger patty then pickles, then butter lettuce, next bacon grilled onion and tomato, add ½ tablespoon KRAFT Burger Aioli and finish with the top bun.​
  2. ​To assemble the ultimate bacon burger, place bottom bun down, then add ½ tablespoon KRAFT Hickory Smoked Bacon Aioli, burger patty, then pickles, then butter lettuce, then top with 3 strips of candied bacon, bacon grilled onion and tomato, add ½ tablespoon KRAFT Hickory Smoked Bacon Aioli and finish with the top bun. ​
