Ghormeh Sabzi (Persian Herb Stew)

This Persian stew features beef, beans, leeks, and spinach flavored with Ghormeh Sabzi herbs.

By Ingrid Nilsen

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Cooking With Pride

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 4 hr
Servings: 6
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds stewing beef
  • 1 can red kidney beans
  • 1 package frozen black-eyed peas
  • 1 large yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large bunches flat-leafed parsley (washed & finely chopped)
  • 3 large bunches cilantro (washed & finely chopped)
  • 1 large thinly sliced leek
  • 1 package frozen chopped spinach
  • 1 can sadaf ghormeh sabzi herbs (available on amazon)
  • 4 dried limes (limoo amani)s
  • 3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Ghee
  • Water

Steps

  1. Soak the dried herb mix in a medium bowl with enough water to cover the herbs completely. After 10 minutes, drain herbs with a fine meshed sieve. Set aside.
  2. Heat 1/3 cup of ghee in a large skillet over medium heat and sauté the chopped fresh herbs & leek and 1 teaspoon of salt for about 10 minutes, stir frequently. Add the rehydrated dried herbs and keeping sautéing for 5 more minutes. Set aside.
