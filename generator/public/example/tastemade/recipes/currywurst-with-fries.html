Currywurst with Fries

Savory bratwurst and crispy french fries topped with a sweet and spicy ketchup.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 35 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Curry Ketchup

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 2 teaspoons coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 teaspoons worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/4 cup beef broth

French Fries

  • 2 pounds russet potatoes
  • 5 cups vegetable oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • 4 pre-cooked bratwursts
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Heat a saucepan over medium heat and add the vegetable oil. Peel and mince the onion. Add the onion to the pan and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 5–7 minutes until soft.
  2. Peel and smash the garlic. Add it to the pan with the curry powder, cumin, coriander, and paprika. Cook while stirring for 2–3 minutes.
