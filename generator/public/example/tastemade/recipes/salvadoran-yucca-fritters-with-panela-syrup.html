Salvadoran Yucca Fritters with Panela Syrup

Learn how to make a typical Salvadoran dish!

By karla t. vasquez

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Salvadoran Yucca Fritters with Panela Syrup

  • 2 yuccas, about 1 pound, peeled and grated
  • 1/2 cup salvadoran duro blando cheese, grated
  • 6 ounces milk, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil/ oil for frying

Panela Syrup

  • 12 ounces water
  • 2 cones piloncillo/panela
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 2 spice cloves

Steps

  1. Take half of the shredded yucca, half of the cheese, 3 ounces of milk and pulse in a food processor until fully combined. Mixture will look soft and pulpy, transfer to a mixing bowl. Repeat with remaining yucca, cheese, milk.
  2. Beat 2 eggs until well combined and foamy, add yucca mixture, sugar, salt and baking powder. Mix until well combined.
