Swirled Chocolate Pavlova With Whipped Cream and Berries

SAVE THE PLANET! - it's the only one with chocolate.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Pavlova

  • 1/2 cup finely chopped semisweet chocolate
  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Berry Topping

  • 2 cups mixed berries
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Chocolate Bark

  • 1/2 cup finely chopped dark chocolate

Whipped Cream

  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar, plus more for dusting
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F.
  2. On 2 separate pieces of parchment paper, use a pencil to draw four 4-inches circles. You should have 8 circles total. Turn papers upside down.
