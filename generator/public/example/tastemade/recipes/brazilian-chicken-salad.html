Brazilian Chicken Salad

Salpicão, or Brazilian chicken salad, is a textural delight thanks to crispy potato straws and a creamy, mayo-based dressing. Serve chilled it at your next backyard barbecue or picnic.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr 15 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 skinless chicken breast
  • 1 minced onion
  • 1 small red bell pepper, in strips
  • 1 small green bell pepper, in strips
  • 2 minced stalk of celeries
  • 1/2 cup of green peas
  • 1 grated carrot
  • Minced parsley
  • 1 cup of mayo
  • 1 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1 cup of straw potatoes
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken in boiling water for 30 minutes. Shred.
  2. In a bowl, add the mayo, yogurt, onion, red pepper, green pepper, celery, green peas, carrot and mix.
