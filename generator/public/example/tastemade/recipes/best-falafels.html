Falafel Pita Pockets

Enjoy this Middle Eastern-style street food right at home, starting with perfectly fluffy falafel. Packed into pita and drizzled with a bright tahini sauce, these will quickly become your new craving.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Falafel

  • 17 3/4 ounces dried chickpeas, left overnight (water should be changed a couple of times)
  • 1 pinch fresh parsley
  • 1 pinch dried coriander
  • 1 head garlic
  • 3 tablespoons cumin powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Accompanying Sauce

  • 5 tablespoons tahini
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 glug good olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Assembly

  • Pitta bread
  • Sliced pickle
  • Flat-leaf parsley
  • Hot sauce!
  • Falafel
  • Accompanying sauce

Steps

  1. You have to prepare the chickpeas overnight
  2. You need to put the mixture (chickpeas, garlic and spices) through a blender, or even better, a mincer
