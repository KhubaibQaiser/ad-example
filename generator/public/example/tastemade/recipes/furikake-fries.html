Furikake Fries

Perfect homemade French fries are even better when tossed in a Japanese-style furikake seasoning. Just follow Nadiya Hussain's recipe for a side dish that'll outshine any main course!

By Nadiya Hussein

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Seasoning Mix

  • 2 nori sheets
  • 6 tablespoons garlic granules
  • 2 teaspoons chili flakes
  • 3 tablespoons white sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon black sesame seeds

Fries

  • 2 1/4 pounds large baking potatoes
  • 53 fluid ounces vegetable oil, for frying

Sticky Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce

Steps

  1. Put the nori sheets, garlic and chili flakes into a food processor and blitz until the nori has broken up.
  2. Transfer into a bowl and add the white and black sesame seeds.
