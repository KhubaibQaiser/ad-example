Silky Chocolate Mousse with Chargrilled Cherries and Toasted Hazelnuts

Indulge in a decadent Chocolate Mousse paired with chargrilled cherries and toasted hazelnuts—an unexpected and luxurious dessert that adds a delightful twist to any barbecue gathering.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 pound milk chocolate, cut into pieces
  • 1 pound soft tofu
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 plus 2 cup cherry liqueur, divided
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pound cherries, pitted
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup turbinado sugar
  • 1 cup hazelnuts
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves

Steps

  1. Place the chocolate in a glass bowl over a pot of simmering water and melt.
  2. Set aside to cool.
