Honey Ginger Buddha Bowls

Veggies, ginger and avocado make this a healthy and delicious meal.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 16 min
Cook Time: 6 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Buddha Bowls

  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 1/2 cup quinoa, uncooked
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 1/2 avocado, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1 carrot, cut into matchsticks
  • 1–2 green onions, thinly sliced

Honey Ginger Sauce

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons raw honey
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce

Steps

  1. Heat sauce pan over medium heat and add the quinoa and water.
  2. Cook, covered, until quinoa absorbs all the liquid.
