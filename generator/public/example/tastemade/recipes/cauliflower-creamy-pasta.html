Cauliflower Creamy Pasta

This creamy fettuccine switches out the dairy with an incredible coconut, cashew and cauliflower combination.

By jenny barber

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 2
Average

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cashews
  • 16 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 1â€“2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons shallots, chopped
  • 1 1/4 cups canned full-fat coconut milk
  • 2 cups cauliflower, riced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground sage
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cherry tomato, sliced in half and oven roasted or sauteed in a pan
  • Fresh basil, chopped

Steps

  1. Soak cashews in boiling water for 1 hour; drain. Cook pasta according to box instructions.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a medium pan. Add garlic and shallots, stirring occasionally until garlic is fragrant and shallots are translucent.
