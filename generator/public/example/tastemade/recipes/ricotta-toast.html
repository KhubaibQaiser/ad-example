Ricotta Toast

Vijaya let's her inner fangirl out to try Sqirl's riccotta toast inspired by Kiernan Shipka's Instagram.

By vijaya selvaraju

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Ricotta

  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice

Strawberry Rose Jam

  • 3 cups chopped strawberries
  • 2 cups sugar
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3/4 teaspoon rose water
  • 1 loaf brioche

Steps

  1. In a sauce pot, add milk, cream and salt and stir continuously until it just begins to boil. Remove from heat and pour in lemon juice. Stir once and allow to sit for 1 minute.
  2. Line a colander with multiple layers of cheesecloth and place over a large bowl. Pour cream mixture into colander and allow to sit for approximately 60 minutes, or until you have a light and fluffy spreadable ricotta.
