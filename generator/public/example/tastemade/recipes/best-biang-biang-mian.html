Biang Biang Mian

If you love noodles, this biang biang mian recipe is sure to satisfy. Biangbiang noodles, a Chinese classic, are packed with flavor thanks to chili oil and black vinegar!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 35 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • Noodle
  • 10 1/2 ounces all purpose flour or bread flour
  • 3/7 teaspoon salt
  • 4 1/2–5 fluid ounces water
  • Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon black vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2–3 heaped tablespoons of homemade chilli oil (chilli flakes, crushed asian chilli pepper flakes, vegetable oil that has been infused with star anise, cinnamon and sichuan peppercorns)
  • Assemble
  • Pak choy
  • Green shallot

Steps

  1. Add ingredients in a bowl and incorporated. It will be a tough dough. Remove from the bowl and knead until it becomes smooth. Wrap and rest for 20 mins.
  2. Roll into a log, cut in half and divide into six.
