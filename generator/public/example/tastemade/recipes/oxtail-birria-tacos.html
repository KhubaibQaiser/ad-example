Oxtail Birria Tacos

Oxtail Birria Tacos are a savory and flavorful Mexican dish made with tender and juicy oxtail meat, slow-cooked in a rich and spicy broth of dried chilies, herbs, and spices, and served on warm corn tortillas with melted cheese, fresh cilantro, and lime wedges.

By derrell smith

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 4
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 6 pounds oxtails
  • Lime juice (to clean the oxtails)
  • 1/2 cup carrot, diced
  • 1/2 cup yellow onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup celery diced
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 bottle jalisto
  • Water
  • Oaxaca cheese
  • Small flour tortilla

Steps

  1. Clean oxtails with lime juice then season with salt and pepper. Pour on the mixture and marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight
  2. Preheat your oven to 380°F
