Grilled Loco Moco

Craft the iconic Grilled Loco Moco by grilling juicy hamburger patties to perfection, topping them with fried eggs and smothering them in rich brown gravy, a beloved Hawaiian dish that combines savory, hearty flavors into a satisfying meal.

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 pound shiitake mushrooms, stemmed and sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 quart beef stock
  • 2 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 6 eggs
  • Cooked short grain white rice for serving
  • Fried shallot, for serving

Steps

  1. Add the butter to a saucepan and melt it over medium heat.
  2. Stir in the onion, shiitake mushrooms, garlic and continue to cook for 3–4 minutes.
