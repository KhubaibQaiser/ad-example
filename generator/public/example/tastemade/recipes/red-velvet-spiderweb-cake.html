Red Velvet Spiderweb Cake

Red velvet cake with buttercream and marshmallow spider webs

By Mia Starr

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 12
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Red Velvet Cake

  • 4 cups AP Flour
  • 1/2 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda
  • 3/4 cup Salted butter
  • 2 1/2 cups Granulated Sugar
  • 3 each Eggs
  • 3/4 cup Oil
  • 2 ounces Red food color
  • 3 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon White Vinegar
  • 2 cups Buttermilk

Buttercream

  • 6 sticks (or 1 1/2 pounds) Unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 3 pounds Powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon Almond extract
  • 3/4 cup Sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 pinches Salt
  • a few drops Orange gel color
  • a few drops Black gel color

Assemble and Decorate

  • Orange Buttercream (see recipe)
  • Black Buttercream (see recipe)
  • 3 cups Standard sized marshmallows
  • 1/2 cup Chocolate chips
  • 4 to 5 Malt balls
  • 4 to 5 Chocolate truffles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line and grease 3 each 8 inch cake pans.
  2. In a medium bowl whisk together the flour, cocoa powder and baking soda.
