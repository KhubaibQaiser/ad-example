Chocolate Brownie Bavarois

A white chocolate barvois, raspberry coulis and whipped cream swirls on top with a brownie base.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 5 hr
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 12
Average
 :

Ingredients

Chocolate Brownie Bavarois

  • 25 grams plain flour
  • 25 grams cocoa
  • 100 grams brown sugar
  • 100 grams butter
  • 100 grams chocolate
  • 2 eggs

White Chocolate Bavarois

  • 90 milliliters milk
  • 90 milliliters double cream
  • 1/2 vanilla pod, split lengthwise
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 50 grams castor sugar
  • 2 gelatin leaves
  • 250 grams white chocolate, chopped

Raspberry Coulis

  • 102 grams sugar
  • 45 milliliters water or orange juice
  • 340 grams frozen raspberries thawed
  • 1 gelatine leaf

Whipped Cream

  • 200 milliliters double cream
  • 1/2 vanilla pod, split lengthwise

Steps

  1. Preheat a fan forced oven to 175°C. Spray an 20 centimeters springform pan with non stick cooking spray. Set aside.
  2. Add butter and chocolate to a microwave safe bowl.
