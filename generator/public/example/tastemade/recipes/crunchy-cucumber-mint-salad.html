Crunchy Cucumber Mint Salad

Perfect palate cleansing salad for any cozy comfort meal

(1)

By Alex Hill

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Spice Spice Baby

Prep Time: 40 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 english cucumbers, sliced thin on a bias
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons evoo
  • Fresh cracked black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint, to garnish

Steps

  1. Slice the cucumbers lengthwise and then into thin 1/2 slice slices.
  2. Place the cucumber in a small bowl and sprinkle it with salt. Let sit for 20–30 minutes.
