Grilled Chicken Parmesan

A grilled twist on your favorite chicken parm served warm and cheesy over pasta of course!

By Dale Talde

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Grilled Chicken Parmesan

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 cups buffalo or fresh mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound spaghetti noodles, cooked
  • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Curly parsley

Tomato Sauce

  • 10 really ripe, big tomatoes
  • 2 onions, halved
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add salt and pepper to the chicken breasts.
  2. Heat the grill with split heat levels - one side medium, one side high heat.
