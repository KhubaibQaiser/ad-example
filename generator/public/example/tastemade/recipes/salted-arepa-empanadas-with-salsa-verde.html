Salted Arepa Empanadas with Salsa Verde

When 2 popular Latin American street foods become one snack, expect deliciousness.

By lazarus lynch

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 12
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Salted Arepa Empanadas with Salsa Verde

  • 2 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups pre-cooked p.a.n. white cornmeal
  • 6 ounces crumbled queso fresco
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Salsa Verde

  • 5 tomatillos, husked and rinsed
  • 1 jalapeño pepper
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, stir water and salt together until salt dissolves. Using your fingers, gradually stir cornmeal into the water, adding 1/2 cup at a time. Work the mixture until it forms a soft, moist and pliable dough. Add more water if necessary, one tablespoon at a time.
  2. Shape dough into a log and cut the log to have workable pieces. Pat each one into a hockey-puck-shaped patty, into 4-inches round dish. Stuff with 1 ounce of queso fresco.
