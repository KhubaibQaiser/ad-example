Vegan Fried Avocado Tacos

These may sound a little out there, but trust us when we say these healthy avocado tacos will change your world!

By michel janse

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Vegan Fried Avocado Tacos

  • 2 semi-firm avocados
  • 4 gluten-free corn tortillas
  • 1/4 cup chopped red cabbage
  • 1/4 cup microgreens (alfalfa sprouts)
  • 1 lime
  • 1 radish, finely sliced

Chipotle Aioli

  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons liquid from can of garbanzo beans
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 pinch salt

Batter

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon potato starch
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 pinch pepper
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup coconut flakes
  • Avocado oil

Steps

  1. Cut avocados into long pieces.
  2. Dump avocado into batter, then into coconut.
