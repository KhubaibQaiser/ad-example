Quaker Oats Oatmeal Breakfast Board

Everyone can customize a bowl of oatmeal for breakfast when you lay out a fun oatmeal breakfast board full of fruit, jams, nuts, and other toppings!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion and nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Oatmeal

  • 2 packets/cups of Quaker’s Apples & Cinnamon Instant Oatmeal per person (total = 8 packets/cups)
  • 1 ½ cups of water or preferred milk per person, to cook the oatmeal
  • salt, a pinch

Fruit

  • 2 bananas, sliced
  • ½ cup cranberries or pomegranate seeds (or both!)
  • 2 cups of blueberries
  • 1 cup of pineapple chunks
  • ⅓ cup coconut flakes, toasted or raw
  • 5 apples of different varieties and colors (pink lady, gala, fuji, etc.), sliced and steamed

Jams & Spreads

  • ½ cup guava jam
  • ½ cup dulce de leche or cajeta
  • ½ cup of golden honey
  • ½ cup mascarpone whipped cream

Nuts & Spices

  • ½ cup sliced almonds
  • ½ cup pecans
  • ⅓ cup pumpkin seeds
  • ¼ cup chia seeds
  • ¼ cup hemp seeds

OTHER

  • 2 tbsps cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp nutmeg

Steps

Oatmeal

  1. Bring water or milk to a boil.
  2. Add instant oatmeal.
  3. Add a pinch of salt.
  4. Stir and cook at medium until consistency.

Fruit

  1. Add desired toppings to your oatmeal.

Jams & Spreads

  1. Add desired toppings to your oatmeal.

Nuts & Spices

  1. Add desired toppings to your oatmeal.
