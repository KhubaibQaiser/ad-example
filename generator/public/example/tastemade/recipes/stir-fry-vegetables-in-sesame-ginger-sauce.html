Stir-Fry Vegetables in Sesame Ginger Sauce

Level up your weeknight dinners with a flavorful stir-fry, which incorporates quality store-bought ingredients like riced cauliflower and pre-minced garlic.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 bag Great Value Riced Cauliflower
  • 1 bag Marketside Snow peas
  • ½ red bell pepper, cut into ¼ inch strips
  • ½ red onion, sliced
  • 1 cup Marketside Organic Shredded Carrots
  • 1 Tablespoon Sesame oil
  • 2 Tablespoons Great Value Less Sodium Soy sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon Fresh Ginger, minced or paste
  • 1 Tablespoon Great Value Minced Garlic
  • ¼ teaspoon Great Value Ground Black Pepper
  • ¼ cup Green Onions, sliced
  • 1 ½ Tablespoons Great Value Peanut Oil or other neutral cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat a large non-stick skillet over medium high heat. Add the peanut oil.
  2. Add the garlic, ginger, and onions and saute until fragrant.
  3. Add the snow peas, carrots and sesame oil and saute for another 3-5 minutes or until nice golden edges have formed on the vegetables.
  4. Add the cauliflower and red bell pepper and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Finish by adding the soy sauce and black pepper, stirring quickly to get everything fully incorporated. Remove from heat.
  6. Garnish with green onions and Enjoy!
