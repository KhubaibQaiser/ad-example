Huli Huli Chicken with Charred Pineapple & Scallion

Fire up the grill for Huli Huli Chicken, marinating the tender chicken in a tangy, sweet glaze, then grilling it to succulent perfection alongside caramelized charred pineapple and smoky scallions for an unforgettable tropical dish.

Recipe from:

All Up In My Grill

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 20 min
Servings: 6

Ingredients

Huli Huli Chicken

  • 2 cups pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • Zest of 2 limes
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 whole chicken
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Charred Pineapple and Scallions

  • 1/2 pineapple, peeled
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 tablespoons light soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons black sesame seeds
  • 2 limes, juiced

Steps

  1. In a large bowl whisk together the pineapple juice, 1/4 cup soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, lime zest paprika, black pepper, ginger and salt.
  2. To spatchcock the chicken, cut the backbone out of the chicken, turn it breast side up and press down firmly with the palm of your hands to flatten.
