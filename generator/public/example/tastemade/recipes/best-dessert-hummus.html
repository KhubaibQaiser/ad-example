Dessert Hummus

Everyone loves hummus and we've found a great way to eat it for dessert! Here are 3 awesome flavours to try including chocolate brownie and strawberry cheesecake.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Sweet Hummus

  • 35 1/4 ounces chickpeas
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 3 tablespoons oat milk
  • 3 tablespoons maple syrup

Brownie batter

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla paste
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • Shaved white chocolate, to serve
  • Shaved dark chocolate, to serve

Chocolate peanut butter

  • 3 tablespoons smooth peanut butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon icing sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salted peanuts, 2 tablespoons,
  • Chopped, to garnish
  • Shaved dark chocolate, to serve

Strawberry cheesecake

  • 1 tablespoon cream cheese
  • 1 tablespoon icing sugar
  • 2 strawberries, 1 for the hummus, 1 chopped, to garnish
  • Zest of 1/2 lemon
  • 1 digestive biscuit, crumbled, to garnish

Steps

  1. To make the basic sweet hummus, blitz the chickpeas in a food processor until broken down and nearly smooth. Add the rest of the ingredients and blitz again until completely smooth - this may take up to 5 minutes.
