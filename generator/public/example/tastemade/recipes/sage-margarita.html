Sage Margarita

Enter your cottagecore era with the earthy green notes of trending Herb Garden fragrances — fresh, woody, and aromatic, just like this herbacious take on a margarita, inspired by trending Herb Garden scents at Sephora.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 1
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Sage Simple Syrup

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 8 sage leaves

Margarita

  • 2 ounces tequila
  • 1/2 ounce orange liqueur
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1/2 ounce sage simple syrup
  • Lime salt for rim

Mocktail

  • 2 ounces lime juice
  • 1 ounce orange juice
  • 1/2 ounce sage simple syrup
  • 1/4 cup club soda
  • Lime salt for rim

Garnish

  • Lime peel
  • Sage sprig

Steps

Sage Simple Syrup

  1. Add sugar, water, and sage leaves to a small saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Whisk until sugar is dissolved.
  3. Bring to a boil and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat, strain, and let cool.

Margarita

  1. Wipe lime wedge around the rim of the glass. Roll in lime salt. Set aside.
  2. Add tequila, orange liqueur, lime juice, and sage simple syrup to a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  3. Shake vigorously for 1 minute. Strain into glass with ice.

Mocktail

  1. Wipe lime wedge around the rim of the glass. Roll in lime salt. Set aside.
  2. Add orange juice lime juice, and sage simple syrup to a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  3. Shake vigorously for 1 minute. Strain into glass with ice.
  4. Top with club soda.
