Pumpkin Dinner Rolls

They look like pumpkins, but the pumpkin flavor is just a suggestion!

By Mia Starr

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Baking the Holidays

Prep Time: 3 hr
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 8
Average
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons Bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon Active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup Whole milk, warmed to 100°F
  • 1 tablespoon Granulated sugar
  • 3 each Eggs (1 egg lightly beaten for egg wash)
  • 1/2 cup Canned pumpkin puree
  • 3 cups Bread flour
  • 10 tablespoons Unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Salt
  • 3 tablespoon, divided Oil
  • 4-6 each Pretzel sticks

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix the bread flour, yeast, warmed milk and sugar until well combined and set aside for 5 to 10 minutes or until the mixture starts to bubble.
  2. Pour the mixture into the bowl of a stand mixer and mix in 2 each eggs and pumpkin with a fork until it is mostly combine then add 1/2 cup of the flour to create a paste.
