Corn Cavatelli Cacio e Pepe

Chef Joe Sasto uses the entire ear of corn to create custom creamy cacio e pepe cavatelli. We’re talking cob, kernel, and husk - and for dessert, he’s making a sweet summery strawberry cobbler that you won’t soon forget.

By Joe Sasto

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 2
Hard
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Cavatelli Dough

  • 340 grams 00 flour
  • 5 grams corn husk powder, recipe below
  • 5 grams pepper
  • 1 gram salt
  • 65 milliliters whole milk
  • 50 milliliters water
  • 1 whole egg

Corn Pudding

  • 120 grams butter
  • 350 grams corn kernels (cobs reserved for corn stock, husks reserved for corn husk powder)
  • 1 gram xanthan gum
  • 100 grams mascarpone cheese
  • 59 milliliters corn stock, recipe below

Corn Stock

  • 5 reserved from corn puddings, kernels removed
  • 800 grams water

Assembly

  • Cavatelli dough
  • Corn pudding
  • Corn stock

Steps

  1. Place the corn husks in a blender and process until they are powdered. Reserve.
  2. Place flour on a work surface; mix in corn husk powder, pepper, and salt. Create well in the center of the flour mixture; add cheese, milk, water, and egg to well; mix slowly into the flour mixture.
