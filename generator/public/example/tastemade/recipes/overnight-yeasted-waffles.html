Overnight Yeasted Waffles

These overnight waffles are the easiest make-ahead brunch or breakfast recipe. Just make the batter the night before and enjoy sleeping in — while still whipping up a delicious breakfast for everyone!

By julie nolke

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 8 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 2 cups warm milk
  • 1/2 cup salted butter, melted
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • Non stick spray or butter for waffle iron
  • Maple syrup
  • Whipping cream
  • Berries & banana

Steps

  1. Prepare the batter the evening before you would like to use it.
  2. In the largest bowl you have, pour in the warm warm water and add in the sugar. Stir to dissolve. Sprinkle in the yeast and let bloom for 5–7 minutes.
