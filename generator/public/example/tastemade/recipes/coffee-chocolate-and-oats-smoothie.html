Coffee, Chocolate and Oats Smoothie

Whether you're just starting your day or prepping for a long night, this protein-packed, caffeinated smoothie will fuel you up with everything you need.

By Arron Crascall

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Gamer Snacks

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 120 milliliters cold brewed coffee
  • 100 milliliters whole milk, or vegan alternative
  • 25 grams cacao powder
  • 50 grams oats
  • 1 banana
  • 38 grams peanut butter
  • 1 handful ice
  • Cold water, to taste
  • 50 grams salted caramel sauce
  • Espresso powder, to decorate

Steps

  1. Blend everything together until smooth, adding water if needed to loosen.
  2. Decorate the glass with the salted caramel sauce then pour the smoothie in and garnish with the coffee powder.
