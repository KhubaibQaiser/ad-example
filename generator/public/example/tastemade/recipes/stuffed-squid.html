Stuffed Squid

Fresh squid shines when stuffed with an herbaceous homemade pesto. Fry until tender and serve with a slice of lemon for a great summer meal!

By Guy Turland

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Sourced

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 5
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 5 squid tubes
  • 4 tablespoons pesto
  • 1 onion, diced fine
  • 1/2 cup pistachios
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 2 cups spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup ricotta
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 lemon rind
  • 2 garlics, minced
  • 1 chilli, minced
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. In a hot to medium pan add oil then garlic, fennel seeds, onion and chilli and cook till tender.
  2. Then add spinach and continue to cook till wilted, add pesto, pistachios, lemon rind and juice.
