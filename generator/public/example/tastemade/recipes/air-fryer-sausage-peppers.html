Air Fryer Sausage & Peppers

Perfect after a long day, this simple, but delicious meal with Hillshire Farm® Smoked Sausage and bright bell peppers can be made with a touch of a button.

(2)Recipe Notes (1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 Hillshire Farm® Smoked Sausage, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 orange bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons dried cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried coriander
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper
  • ½ cup cilantro leaves
  • 2 lemons, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 400˚F.
  2. Toss the sausage, peppers, and onion with the olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried cumin, dried coriander, smoked paprika, red pepper flakes and salt and pepper.
  3. Place in the air fryer and cook for 20 Minutes.
  4. Remove and serve garnished with cilantro and lemon wedges.
