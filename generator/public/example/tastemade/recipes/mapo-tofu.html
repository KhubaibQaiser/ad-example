Mapo Tofu

This mapo tofu makes a delicious plant-based meal.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups white rice
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 small dried chile peppers
  • 2 tablespoons minced and peeled ginger
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fermented bean sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 packets soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons crushed sichuan peppercorns
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 24 ounces firm tofu
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Salt
  • 3 scallions, sliced
  • 1/4 cup cilantro leaves

Steps

  1. Add the rice and 3 cups of water to a medium saucepan and bring to a boil. Turn down to simmer, cover and cook for 20 minutes.
  2. Add the vegetable oil to a large sauté pan over medium heat. Stir in the dried chile peppers and toast for 1–2 minutes until fragrant. Add the ginger, garlic, fermented bean sauce, sesame oil, soy sauce and Sichuan peppercorns.
