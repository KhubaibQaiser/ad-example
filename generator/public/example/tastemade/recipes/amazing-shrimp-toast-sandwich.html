Shrimp Toast Sandwich

Make your favorite Chinese dim sum dish, shrimp toast, with a twist: This version is deep-fried for extra crunch!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 24 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 6 slices slices of bread
  • 5 1/4 ounces raw shrimp
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1 knob of ginger, grated
  • 3 spring onions, finely chopped
  • 1 red chilli, deseeded and chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice flour, just enough to bind the mixture so it's not too sloppy
  • Black and white sesame seed
  • Oil, for frying
  • 2 spring onions, finely sliced lengthways
  • 1/2 cucumber, finely sliced into strips, lengthways
  • 1 handful coriander
  • Siracha
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Chopped chive, to serve

Steps

  1. Place the shrimp, garlic, ginger, onions, chilli, egg, soy and sesame oil into a food processor and blitz until you have a paste, adding a little bit of rice flour if your mixture is too sloppy.
  2. Spread the mixture generously over the slices of bread.
