Plantain Flan

When life gives you plantains, turn them into flan.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Plantain Flan

  • 1 cup sugar (for caramel)
  • 5 1/2 ounces sweet plantain
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/4 ounces sugar
  • 4 1/2 ounces milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 1/2 heavy creams
  • 2 tablespoons icing sugar

Caramelized Sweet Plantain

  • 1 cup sweet plantain
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup water

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, melt sugar until golden brown. Pour hot syrup into 4 individual baking molds, turning each dish to evenly coat the bottom and sides. Set aside.
  2. Place sliced sweet plantain, eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract into a blender. Blend until all ingredients are well combined and the mixture is smooth.
