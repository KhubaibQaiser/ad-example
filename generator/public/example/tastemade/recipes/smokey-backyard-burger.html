Smoky Backyard Burger

Perfect for summer, Chef Noah Galuten's smoky burger is topped with cheese, bacon, and a special sauce. Plus, you can whip up a batch of these burgers outside on the grill, or sear 'em inside on your stovetop!

By Noah Galuten

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Don't Panic Pantry

Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Yellow mustard
  • Dill relish
  • 1 pinch cayenne
  • Hot sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • 6 slices bacon
  • 20 ounces 80/20 ground beef
  • Smoked cheddar
  • Potato roll
  • Thinly sliced onion
  • Iceberg lettuce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F.
  2. Combine the mayo, ketchup, mustard, relish, cayenne, hot sauce and salt in a bowl and mix them well. Season to taste with salt and pepper and then adjust to taste. If it's too intense, the most common solution is more mayo. But it should be sweet, punchy, acidic, a little hot and a tiny bit spicy.
