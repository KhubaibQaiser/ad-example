Beer Bacon And Maple Syrup Poutine

The 3 main Canadian food groups: beer, bacon and maple syrup. Combine these all into a single balanced meal.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika

  • 2 cups of stout beer

  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • Salt
  • Cheese curd

  1. Start by slicing each potato lengthwise into four potato slabs slabs.
