Pork Gyoza

These Japanese dumplings will be your new favourite dish!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 24
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 24 round gyoza wrappers
  • 20 grams cabbage
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 spring onion
  • Small piece of fresh ginger
  • 150 grams pork mince
  • 5 milliliters sesame oil
  • 5 milliliters soy sauce
  • 100 milliliters water
  • Ponzu sauce to serve

Steps

  1. Boil the cabbage leaves until soft and then finely chop.
  2. Chop the garlic, spring onion, ginger and mix together with the pork and cabbage.
