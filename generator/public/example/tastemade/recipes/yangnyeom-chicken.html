Yangnyeom Chicken

If you've never made Yangnyeom chicken, you're missing out. This sweet, sour, and spicy Korean chicken recipe is perfect for dinner, or as an appetizer — watch as your party guests devour it!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

Yangnyeom Chicken

  • 6 chicken wings
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons sake
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Potato starch
  • Cooking oil

Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sake
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Roasted white sesame seed
  • 2 teaspoons grated garlic
  • 1 ounce gochujang chili paste
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar, optional

Toppings

  • Roasted white sesame seed
  • Scallion

Steps

  1. Place chicken wings in a mixing bowl. Add sake, salt, pepper and ginger. Use your hands to combine. Add the potato starch and mix to coat chicken.
  2. Heat oil to around 350°F. Add wings and cook for about 5 minutes until golden brown, flipping them occasionally. Remove chicken from oil and drain.
