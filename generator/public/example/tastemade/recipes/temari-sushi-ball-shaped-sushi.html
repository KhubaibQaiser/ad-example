Temari Sushi (Ball-Shaped Sushi)

Usually served at parties, this ball-shaped sushi is a classic celebratory Japanese dish. Create it at home to commemorate a special occasion of your own.

(1)

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Servings: 1
Average
 :

Ingredients

Sushi Rice

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 2/3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Temari Sushi (Ball-Shaped Sushi)

  • 1 slice tuna
  • 1 slice squid
  • 1 salmon
  • 1 slice fresh sardine
  • Tororo konbu (flaked konbu seaweed)
  • Sliced japanese egg omelette
  • 4 thin strips nori
  • 5 thin radishes
  • 3 thin slices cucumber
  • Wasabi

Assembly

  • Pickled ginger
  • Chopped chive
  • Perilla leaf
  • Cream cheese
  • Lemon wedge
  • Dill
  • Salmon egg
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Combine rice vinegar, sugar and salt together in a bowl. Mix this in with hot cooked rice and set aside.
  2. Divide the sushi rices into 8 balls.
