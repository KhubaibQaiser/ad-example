Coconut Chicken Curry

A nourishing bowl of curry is the perfect weeknight dinner, and easy to make thanks to this hands-off slow cooker recipe.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 6 hr 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs, quartered
  • 3 tablespoons mild curry powder
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 8 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 2 inches ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 3 carrots, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 14 1/2 ounces coconut milk
  • 2 1/2 cups green beans, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • Cooked brown rice, for serving
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro, for serving
  • 1/2 cup chopped salted cashews

Steps

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the chicken, curry powder and salt and toss all together. Add the onion, garlic, ginger, carrots and bell pepper. Cover and cook on low until the chicken is tender, about 6 to 8 hours.
  2. Stir in the coconut milk and the green beans. Cover, and continue to cook for 30 more minutes, until the beans are bright green but tender-crisp.
