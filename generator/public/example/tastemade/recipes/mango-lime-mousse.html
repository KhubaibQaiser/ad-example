Mango-Lime Mousse

This vegan mousse recipe is a light, refreshing dessert that gets plenty of flavor thanks to ripe mango and tart lime juice!

By laura miller

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

With Laura Miller

Prep Time: 45 min
Servings: 1
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 4–5 mangos
  • 1 cup coconut meat
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/2 cup agave nectar
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • Lime zest

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients, set in little glass and put in fridge for 30 minutes to set up.
