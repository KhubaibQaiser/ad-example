Eggplant Lasagna Roll Ups

This cheesy, meatless lasagna made with eggplant, spinach and more may be the heartiest dish you make all week.

By jerrelle guy

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 3
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Eggplant

  • 1 large eggplant, sliced lengthwise into 8 pieces

Filling

  • Olive oil for sautéing
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 can cannellini beans, drained
  • 1 cup whole milk ricotta
  • 2 cups frozen spinach (thawed and drained = 1/2 cup)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

To Assemble

  • 7 fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 7 lasagna sheets, cooked
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 (24 ounces) jar of your favorite marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Roast the eggplant.
  2. Slice the eggplant lengthwise into 8 pieces.
