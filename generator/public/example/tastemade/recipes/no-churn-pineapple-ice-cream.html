No-Churn Pineapple Ice Cream

3 ingredients and no ice cream maker required and served in a grilled coconut

By Danni Rose

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 6 hr
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 6
Easy
 :

Ingredients

No-Churn Pineapple Ice Cream

  • 14.5 14 1/2 ounces sweetened condensed coconut milk
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 20 ounces canned crushed pineapple, drained and pressed dry

Grilled Coconut Shells

  • 3 coconuts

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to beat the cream to medium peaks, about 3–5 minutes.
  2. Add the cold, sweetened condensed milk and beat until thickened, another 2 minutes.
