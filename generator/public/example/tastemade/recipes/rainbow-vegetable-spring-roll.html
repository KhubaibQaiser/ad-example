Rainbow Vegetable Spring Roll

Who knew rainbows could taste so fresh and nutritious?

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 1 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1/4 red cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 large carrot, cut into matchsticks
  • 1 large bunch fresh mint leaves
  • 1 large bunch fresh cilantro
  • 8–10 rice spring roll papers

Steps

  1. Prep the vegetables and set aside.
  2. Add hot water to a large skillet and submerge one rice paper at a time to soften for about 8–10 seconds.
