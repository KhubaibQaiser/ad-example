Persian Love Cake

Share the love by baking food writer Yasmin Khan's sumptuous Persian cake drizzled in rose water and decorated with pistachios and rose petals.

By Numra Siddiqui

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

If These Meals Could Talk

Prep Time: 35 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Cake

  • 7 ounces unsalted butter
  • 5 1/4 ounces caster (superfine) sugar
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 12 cardamom pods
  • 3 1/2 ounces plain (all purpose) flour, sifted
  • 9 3/4 ounces ground almonds
  • Zest and juice of 1 unwaxed lemon
  • 1 tablespoon rose water
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt

Drizzle Topping

  • 2 tablespoons caster (superfine) sugar
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Rose water

Icing

  • 7 ounces icing (powdered) sugar
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1–2 teaspoons cold water

Decoration

  • 2 teaspoons sliced pistachios
  • 2 teaspoons dried rose petals (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 320°F/Gas 3. Grease a 7 3/4-inches cake tin (one with a removable base) and line it with baking parchment.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugar together. When the mixture is thoroughly combined, beat in the eggs.
