Chocolate Chocolate Chocolate Protein Pancakes

These pancakes feature chocolate protein powder, cocoa powder, and chocolate chips for a protein-packed breakfast reminiscent of dessert.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 1
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 3/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 scoops chocolate protein powder
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons dark cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips
  • Coconut oil, for frying

Steps

  1. Combine everything in the blender except for oil and chocolate chips. Blend until smooth.
  2. Heat skillet over medium high heat and add the oil, allowing it to melt.
