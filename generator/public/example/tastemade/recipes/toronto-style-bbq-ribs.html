Toronto Style BBQ Ribs

Julie proves you don't need a pit to make fall-off-the-bone ribs.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 2 hr 20 min
Servings: 3
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds pork side ribs, st louis style
  • Salt and pepper
  • Bbq sauce
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup miso paste
  • 1/4 cup worcestershire
  • 1/4 cup rum
  • 1/2 cup chili sauce
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • Black pepper
  • 1 pinch cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F.
  2. Cut ribs into serving size (3–4) portions and season generously with salt and pepper. Wrap tightly in double thickness foil and place on a baking sheet.
