Alligator Po' Boy

Learn how to make a classic New Orleans sandwich, the po' boy, right at home, using plenty of seasoning and fried alligator!

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 pound alligator, cubed
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne powder
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • Sliced tomato
  • Shredded iceberg lettuce
  • French bread
  • Mayo
  • Hot sauce
  • Canola oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, cayenne powder, 1/2 portion of smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, thyme, and salt and pepper.
  2. In another bowl, combine buttermilk, egg, and the other half of the seasonings.
