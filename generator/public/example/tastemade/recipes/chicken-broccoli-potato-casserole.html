Chicken Broccoli Potato Casserole

This chicken broccoli potato casserole is a perfect make-ahead or mid-week dinner.

By lani nishiyama

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 2
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, shredded
  • 2 russet potatoes, shredded
  • 1 medium broccoli head, cut up into florets
  • 2 slices bacon
  • 1 green onion, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped or minced
  • 1 can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • Paprika
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded gruyere cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. While preheating oven, place bacon on a baking sheet with a cooling rack on it and place in the oven. Bacon should be cooked by the time the oven is heated. Remove bacon and set aside.
