Ricotta and Spinach Calzone

Making a calzone at home is easier than you think. Turn to this easy ricotta and spinach calzone recipe next time the craving strikes.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Calzone Dough

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/4 cup butter, cubed
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Large egg + 1 tablespoon water mixed to make an egg wash

Calzone Filling

  • 2 bundles spinach
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 3/4 cup ricotta
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon nutmeg

Steps

  1. Make the dough in a bowl with the flour, butter, water and olive oil.
  2. Form into a ball and let rest.
