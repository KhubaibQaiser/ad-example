Spiced Lentil Lettuce Wraps

Guy takes a trip to Maroubra Beach and makes a great vegetarian dish: spicy lentil lettuce wraps.

By guy turland

Updated: 9/24/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 ounces dryer porcini mushroom
  • 67 3/4 fluid ounces veg stock
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seed
  • 1 tablespoon fennel seed
  • 1 teaspoon dried chilli
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 onion
  • 1 red capsicum
  • 1 garlic
  • 2 zucchinis
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 1 cup lentils
  • 1 fresh chilli

Steps

  1. In a pot, add all dry ingredients together and infuse into veg stock.
  2. Dice all veg and fry till tender in a large pot.
