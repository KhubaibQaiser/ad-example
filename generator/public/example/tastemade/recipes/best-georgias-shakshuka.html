Georgia's Shakshuka

Looking for an easy brunch recipe? This flavorful shakshuka has everything you want: peppers, tomato, garlic and eggs all in one pan!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2–4 garlic cloves
  • 2 bell peppers, red and orange (your choice)
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley
  • 4 eggs
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Chop onion and garlic and cook on a medium heat in a deep frying pan with some oil.
  2. Add Peppers and fry over.
