Sleepytime DIY Neck Pillow

Fall comfortably asleep literally anywhere with this DIY neck pillow filled with aromatic essential oils.

By jerrelle guy

Updated: 10/2/2024

Servings: 1
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 2 piece of fabrics, cut about 5-inches by 20-inches
  • Needle and thread for sewing
  • Ruler
  • Scissors
  • White fabric marker
  • Sewing pin
  • 4 cups whole flax seeds
  • 5 cups dried lavender flowers
  • 1 cup dried lemon balm
  • 1 drop bergamot essential oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Stack the two pieces of fabric, then flip them over so their insides are facing out. Place a few sewing pins into the fabric to help hold it in place. Using the needle and thread, start at the long end of the fabric and stitch along the edges of the fabric, keeping 1 open small end open.
  2. Carefully flip the fabric inside out to hide the stitch lines and any excess fabric.
