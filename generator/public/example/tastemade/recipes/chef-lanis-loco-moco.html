Chef Lani's Loco Moco

Chef Lani Nishiyama makes the ultimate Hawaiian comfort food: Loco Moco, or rice piled high and topped with a juicy burger, gravy and a fried egg!

By lani nishiyama

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 35 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked white rice
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cups beef broth, must be made ahead if homemade
  • 1/2 pound ground beef, makes 2 patties
  • 1/4 cup grated onions
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 egg (only plan to plate one loco moco dish)
  • 1 green onion, for garnish and taste

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a pan (medium heat) and add the flour to thicken for about 5 min.
  2. Add Worcestershire sauce.
