Loaded Baked Cauliflower

Who needs potatoes when you can dig into a loaded baked cauliflower? This hearty side dish is full of nutrition and can be completely vegetarian depending on your choice of toppings.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :

Ingredients

Loaded Baked Cauliflower

  • 4 cups riced cauliflower
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 sprig fresh thyme
  • 1 small garlic, smashed
  • 1 fresh bay leaf
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese (optional)

Toppings

  • 1 1/3 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 3/4 cup sour cream
  • 8 strips applewood-smoked bacon, cooked and chopped
  • 4 tablespoons chopped chives
  • Kosher salt
  • Fresh ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Set an oven rack in upper third, and preheat to 400°F. Grease 4 small baking dishes.
  2. In a large pot over medium heat, add the cauliflower, milk, butter, thyme, garlic and bay leaf. Bring to a simmer, or until you see gentle bubbles forming around the perimeter – do not boil. Cook for 10 minutes to soften the cauliflower.
