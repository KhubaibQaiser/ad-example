Zucchini Corn Pancakes

These refined flour-free zucchini corn pancakes are the best way to enjoy spring and summer veggies!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 3 mediums grated zucchini
  • 1 cup of fresh corn kernels, cooked
  • 1/4 cup of finely diced red onion
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup of almond milk
  • 1/2 cup of chickpea flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Olive oil, for pan

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except for the oil in a large bowl and stir until mixed.
  2. Heat oil in a griddle or skillet over medium-high heat.
