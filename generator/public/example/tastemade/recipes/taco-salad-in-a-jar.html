Taco Salad in a Jar

A prettier, healthier way to get your taco fix.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 yellow onion, sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice (approx. 1 lime)
  • 1 cup grape tomatoes, whole
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1–2 cups baby spring mix

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper and cook the chicken breast until golden brown, and cooked through. Set aside and allow to cool. Once cool, dice into bite-size pieces.
  2. Add the red pepper, onion, garlic, and cumin, and cook until tender.
