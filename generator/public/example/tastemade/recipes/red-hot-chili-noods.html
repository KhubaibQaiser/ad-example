Spicy Shrimp Ramen

Spice up your instant ramen with just a few simple ingredients: flavorful broth, spices, and steamed shrimp turn this pantry staple into a weeknight dinner for champions.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked instant ramen noodles
  • 1 cup mushroom stock
  • 1/2 cup neutral oil
  • 2 tablespoons chilli flakes
  • 2 cups sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1 tablespoon old bay
  • 4 steamed shrimps
  • Green onion, as needed
  • Nori, as needed

Steps

  1. In a small sauce pan heat the oil for 5 minutes
  2. Combine 2 tablespoons chilli flakes, 2 cups sesame seeds, 1/2 tablespoon cumin, 1 tablespoon smashed coriander seeds, 1 tablespoon old bay in a heat proof bowl
