Zucchini Lasagna Boats

Looking for a low-carb alternative to lasagna? Allow us to introduce you to these zucchini boats!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchinis (2 1/2 pounds)
  • 8 3/5 ounces part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, plus more for garnish
  • 5 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 ounces finely shredded parmesan cheese
  • 8 ounces 93 percent lean ground beef or lean ground turkey
  • 4 teaspoons olive oil, divided
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 3/4 cups roasted garlic marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil, plus more for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Using a spoon, scoop centers from zucchini while leaving a 1/4-inch rim to create boats. Set aside.
