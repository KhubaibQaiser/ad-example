Baked Bloomin' Onion

Get all the crispy, crunchy bloomin' onion flavor you love, without the greasiness of a fryer.

By jerrelle guy

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 45 min
Cook Time: 45 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

Baked Bloomin' Onion

  • 1 very large sweet yellow onion
  • 4 cups low-fat buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons mild hot sauce (optional)
  • 2 cups brown rice flour
  • Seasoned salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • 4 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika

Harissa Dip

  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons harissa spread
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped
  • Juice of 1/2 lime

Steps

  1. Peel the onion, slice off the top (not the root) and slice the onion, cut-side down, into quarters. Then slice the quarters in thirds, keeping the root intact. This should create petals.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the buttermilk and hot sauce. Submerge the onion cut-side down in the bowl and let soak for 30 minutes until the petals open up.
