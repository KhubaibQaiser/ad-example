Chipotle-Style Chicken Burrito Bowl

Chef Frankie Celenza recreates a restaurant favorite: A burrito bowl with tender chicken, fresh guac, sweet corn salsa and black beans served over warm rice.

(1)Recipe Notes (1)

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Chicken Bowl

  • 2 cups white rice
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro leaves
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flake
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 6 ounces cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 head romaine lettuce, shredded
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups corn salsa
  • 1 cup guacamole

Corn Salsa

  • 1 jalapeno, seeded and minced
  • 1/2 red onion, minced
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 12 ounces frozen corn, thawed
  • 2 limes, zested and juiced
  • Salt and pepper

Fresh Guacamole

  • 2 avocados
  • 1/2 red onion, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Add the rice to a medium saucepan with 4 cups of water. Bring to a boil, turn down to simmer, cover and cook for 20 minutes. Remove from heat, fluff with a fork and fold in cilantro and lime juice. Season with salt to taste.
  2. Cut the chicken into bite size pieces. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add the vegetable oil. Add the chicken and sear until golden brown on all sides. Stir in the cumin, paprika, red pepper flakes, chili powder and cook for one minute.
