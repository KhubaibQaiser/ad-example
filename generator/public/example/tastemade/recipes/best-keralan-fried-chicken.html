Keralan Fried Chicken

Fried chicken with a Keralan twist!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 32 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken pieces, skinless
  • 6 3/4 fluid ounces coconut milk
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 inches fresh ginger root
  • 2 birds eye green chillies
  • 4 stems fresh coriander, washed
  • 2 stems curry leaves, de-stemmed, washed
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 3 1/2 ounces plain flour
  • 3 1/2 ounces coconut flour (if you don’t have this, just use 7 ounces plain flour)
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg powder (optional)
  • 6 stems curry leaves, washed, dried and de-stemmed, for garnishing

Steps

  1. In a blender, blitz coconut milk, garlic, ginger, chillies, coriander and curry leaves and empty into a bowl.
  2. Add the chicken along with salt, garam masala, coriander powder, paprika and turmeric. Cover & refrigerate for as long as possible (ideally 24 hrs) but at least a couple of hours.
