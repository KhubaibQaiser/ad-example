Pretzel Crust Chocolate Swirl Cheesecake

When it comes to rich chocolate marble cheesecake, once you go pretzel crust, you'll never go back.

By lazarus lynch

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 1/2 cups pretzel pieces
  • 6 pieces chocolate graham crackers
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 pinch salt

Filling

  • 6 ounces semi-sweet chocolate morsels
  • 16 ounces plain cream cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F.
  2. In a food processor, combine the pretzels, graham crackers and sugar and pulse until coarse, about 30 seconds. Add the melted butter and pulse until fine, about 5 to 6 times. Lightly coat the bottom and sides of a 9x13-inches springform pan with cooking spray. Press the crust into the bottom and sides of the pan.
