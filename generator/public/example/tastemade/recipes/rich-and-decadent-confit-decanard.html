Confit De Canard

A whole leg of duck, marinated in salt and spices, slow cooked in duck fat for 2 hours, shallow fried, then served with sautéed potatoes and fine green beans.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 3 hr
Servings: 4
Average
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Duck

  • 4 duck legs
  • ¼ cup coarse sea salt
  • 2 cups duck or goose fat
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3 sprigs thyme
  • ¾ tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns

Potatoes

  • 2 lbs potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch chunks
  • Thyme, oregano or rosemary sprigs

Salsa

  • 5 green tomatoes
  • A pinch of cumin seeds
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, roughly chopped
  • ⅓ oz coriander, inc. leaves and stalks
  • ⅓ oz parsley, inc. leaves and stalks
  • ⅓ oz oregano, inc. leaves or use 1 tsp dried
  • ⅔ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2-3 tsp brown sugar
  • 1-2 green or jalapeno chillies, roughly chopped
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 1 tbsp red wine or sherry vinegar

Steps

Duck

  1. Mix the duck and salt in a bowl, cover with a dish towel and leave to chill overnight (ideally 12 hours).
  2. Heat the oven to 300F. Take the duck legs out of the salt and dust off the excess and discard any juices. Put the duck in a casserole along with the rest of the duck ingredients heat the duck fat and pour over to submerge the duck legs and cook for 2-2 ½ hours until the duck is very tender.
  3. Once the duck has been in for 2 hours, add 2-3tbsp of the duck fat to a roasting tin and add the potatoes, herbs and salt and pepper, mix well. Turn the heat up to 350F once the duck comes out, for 20-30 mins, until golden, turning halfway.

Potatoes

  1. Bring the potatoes to the boil in a pan of salted water. Simmer for about 8 mins or until just tender, drain and allow to steam dry then give them a little shake to fluff up.

Salsa

  1. Fry the green tomatoes in some duck fat in a pan over a medium heat for 2-3 mins, add the garlic and cumin and fry for 2 more mins, allow to cool. Add to a blender along with the oil, sugar, chillies, lime juice and vinegar, herb stalks and half the leaves and blend until smooth, then finely chop and stir in the rest of the herbs, add sugar, salt, pepper, lime/vinegar to taste.
