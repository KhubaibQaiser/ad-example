Braised Beef Calzone

Ingredients

Braised Beef Calzone

  • Olive oil
  • 17 3/4 ounces beef, cubed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 3/4 cups beef broth
  • 1/4 bunch parsley, chopped

Dough

  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 3/4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten, for brushing

Steps

  1. Coat the bottom of a Dutch oven over medium-high heat with olive oil. Add beef chunks and stir to coat. Once they begin to brown, add onion, garlic, salt and pepper. Stir to combine. Add red wine and cook until the alcohol has evaporated. Stir in tomato paste, beef broth and parsley. Cover and continue to cook over medium heat for 1 hour.
