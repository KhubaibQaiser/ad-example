Coconut Curry Fried Rice

Combine the richness of coconut curry with fried rice for one flavor-packed Thai-inspired meal.

By jerrelle guy

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon thai green curry paste
  • Juice of 1/2 lime + lime wedges for serving
  • 1 teaspoon coconut palm sugar/coconut sugar or cane sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup unrefined coconut oil
  • 1 shallot, diced
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 2 cups day-old brown rice, cold
  • 5 thai basil (regular basil is fine too!)s, julienned
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine the curry paste, lime juice and palm sugar and set aside.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs.
