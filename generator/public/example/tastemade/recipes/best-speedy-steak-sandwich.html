Speedy Steak Sandwich

Make no mistake, this steak sandwich is the one!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 sirloin steak, trimmed of fat
  • 1 bag baguette, sliced in half lengthways
  • 1 big handful of watercress
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil
  • 1 knob butter
  • 2 tablespoons creamed horseradish
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Get your frying pan nice and hot, oil up your steak, then cook for 2/3 minutes each side, leave to rest and slice thinly, tumble onto your baguette that's filled with rocket, drizzle over salt and pepper and horseradish mayonnaise.
