Eggplant Rolatini

Think of this as a savory lasagna roll-up with eggplant instead of pasta.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cups crushed san marzano canned tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 large italian eggplants
  • 2 cups ricotta
  • 2 tablespoons parmesan
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil to a medium sauce pot over medium heat. Add garlic, onions, salt, chili flakes, and oregano. Sauté for 5 - 10 minutes. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes. Finish with basil and remove from heat.
