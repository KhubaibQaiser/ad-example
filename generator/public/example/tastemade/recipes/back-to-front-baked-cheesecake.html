Back-to-Front Baked Cheesecake

Chef Nadiya Hussain's cheesecake recipe takes a bit of patience, but the end result is worth it: a creamy dessert topped with a salted caramel and cookie crumble.

By Nadiya Hussein

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Nadiya's Family Favourites

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 9
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Cheesecake

  • 900g/2lb full-fat cream cheese
  • 200g/7oz caster sugar
  • 150ml/5fl oz sour cream
  • 3 tbsp plain flour
  • 3 free-range eggs, beaten
  • 2 tsp vanilla bean paste

Honey Salted Caramel

  • 50g/1¾ oz butter
  • 170g/6 oz set honey
  • 300ml/10 fl oz double cream
  • ½ tsp salt

Tiffin Crumble

  • 150g/5½ oz digestives, roughly crushed
  • 75g/2½ oz unsalted butter, melted
  • 30g/1 oz demerara sugar
  • 50g/1¾ oz dark chocolate chips or chunks
  • 50g/1¾ oz toasted hazelnuts, roughly chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 160C/140C Fan/Gas 3.
  2. Grease the base of a 20cm/8in round cake tin (not loose-bottomed), and line it with baking paper.
