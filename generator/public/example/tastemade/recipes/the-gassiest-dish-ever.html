The Gassiest Dish Ever

Cabbage, stinky cheese, beans, and more are all combined in a stew to make a dish with a surprising result.

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

cabbage

  • 1 cabbage head
  • 6 tablespoons melted butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of a stinky a stinky cheese of your choice, of your choice
  • Fresh thyme, to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Stew

  • 1/4 cup bacon, chopped
  • 1/2 cup onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup carrot, diced
  • 1/4 cup celery, diced
  • 1 cup farro
  • 1 cup canned red beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup kale, chopped
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup canned crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Cut cabbage into round wedges. Combine melted butter, garlic, thyme, salt and pepper. Drizzle the mixture over the cabbage, top with some cheese, and roast in the oven for 30–35 minutes or until browned.
