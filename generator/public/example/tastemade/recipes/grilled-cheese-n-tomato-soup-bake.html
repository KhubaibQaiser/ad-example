Grilled Cheese 'n' Tomato Soup Bake

Think of this as the grown-up version of the childhood classic, perfect for a chilly evening at home.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/24/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2–3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, peeled and diced
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 28 ounces san marzano tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • Salt and pepper
  • Softened garlic butter, for spreading
  • 8 slices slices of bread
  • 6 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 12 slices cheddar cheese
  • Parmesan cheese, for the top

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil and sauté onions and garlic over medium heat until tender. Add in tomatoes and cook until caramelized and cooked down. Add chicken stock, bring to a boil, reduce heat to a simmer and cook for about 10–15 minutes, until flavors have melded. Season with salt and pepper and blend.
