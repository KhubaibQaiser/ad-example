Cheesy Chicken Enchiladas

A spiced-to-perfection cheesy enchilada platter to level up your leftover chicken.

(3)Recipe Notes (3)

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 14 min
Servings: 4
Hard
 :

Ingredients

Queso

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 3 cups grated pepper jack cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 8 ounces green chiles
  • 2 teaspoons hot sauce
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Enchilada Filling

  • 1 poblano pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1–2 cups leftover chicken, shredded
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 6 (10-inches) flour tortillas
  • 8 ounces salsa verde
  • 2 tablespoons cilantro leaves

Steps

  1. Grate the pepper jack cheese into a bowl.
  2. In a medium saucepan, melt butter on medium high heat and whisk in the flour. Continue whisking for about 1 minute then slowly add the heavy cream.
