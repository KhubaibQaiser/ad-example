Lamb Chops with Greek Salad and Buttered Potatoes

This summery meal features hearty lamb chops with fresh Greek salad and decadent potatoes.

By Numra Siddiqui

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

If These Meals Could Talk

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

For the Lamb Chops

  • 4 lamb chops
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, chopped
  • Olive oil

For the Greek Salad

  • 3–4 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 green pepper, deseeded and sliced
  • 5 grams dried oregano
  • 1 block feta, crumbled
  • 20 kalamata olives
  • 50 milliliters red wine vinegar
  • 100 milliliters extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 grams sugar
  • 1 pinch salt

For the Buttered Potatoes

  • 300 grams baby potatoes
  • 50 grams butter
  • 1 small handful chives, chopped

Steps

  1. To make the lamb chops, marinate them with the rosemary and olive oil.
  2. Preheat a barbecue or griddle and cook the cutlets over a high heat for 2–3 minutes on each side, depending on their thickness, then rest in a warm place.
