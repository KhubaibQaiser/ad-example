Jerk Chicken Ramen

Laz is combining Japanese Ramen with bold jerk chicken flavor from the Caribbean.

By lazarus lynch

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 4 hr
Cook Time: 40 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Jerk Chicken Ramen

  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper, seeds removed
  • 2 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 2 scallions, roughly chopped
  • 1 piece ginger, skin removed
  • 2 tablespoons dark brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons thyme
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice; 2 teaspoons zest
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 piece chicken
  • 1 tablespoon whole allspice berries
  • 5 cloves cloves
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 5 ounces fresh ramen noodles
  • Shredded carrot
  • Corn
  • Steamed cabbage
  • Bean sprout
  • Small red chili pepper, sliced into thin rounds
  • Chopped scallion
  • Lime wedge, for garnish

Tare

  • 4 1/2 cups low-sodium chicken stock, divided
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup mirin
  • 3 tablespoons sake
  • 2 cloves whole garlic cloves
  • 1 piece ginger, sliced
  • 1/2 ounce dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 6 whole black peppercorns
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon whole allspice berries
  • 1 tablespoon light brown sugar
  • 2 scallions, finely chopped (white and green parts)
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves

Steps

  1. In a food processor, combine the scotch bonnet pepper, garlic, scallion, ginger, brown sugar, thyme, soy sauce, lime juice and zest, olive oil, water, salt and pepper. Pulse until smooth.
  2. Reserve a 1/4 cup of marinade in a small bowl. Place chicken in a storage bag and pour the remaining marinade over the chicken.
