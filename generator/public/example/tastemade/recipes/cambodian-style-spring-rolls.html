CAMBODIAN-STYLE SPRING ROLLS

Veggie-filled spring rolls taste so fresh and yummy.

By katie quinn

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 2
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Cambodian-Style Spring Rolls

  • Rice paper wrapper
  • Japanese-style thin rice noodle
  • Lettuce
  • Carrots, cut into thin slices approx. 4 inches
  • Cucumbers, cut into thin slices approx. 4 inches
  • Cilantro
  • Basil

Sauce

  • 3 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons crushed peanuts

Steps

  1. Cook the rice noodles and strain.
  2. Prepare the vegetables by cleaning and cutting.
