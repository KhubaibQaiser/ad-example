Papdi Chaat

It's layer upon layer of extreme flavour in this twist on your favourite street food!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 50 min
Cook Time: 50 min
Servings: 1
Average
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Green Mint and Coriander Chutney

  • 1 cup chopped mint
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander
  • 1 green chilli
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • Salt, to taste

Papdi Chaat

  • 24 papdis
  • 8 1/2 fluid ounces greek yoghurt (full fat)
  • 1 large potato, boiled, peeled and chopped
  • 2 cups of chickpeas (tinned)
  • 1 small red onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1–2 teaspoons chaat masala (if you want it)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice

Tamarind Chutney

  • 1/2 cup of jaggery, finely chopped (or brown sugar)
  • 1/2 cup of tamarind paste
  • 1/2 cup of seedless dates
  • Boiled down with 8 3/4 fluid ounces of water
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger powder
  • 2 cups of water
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Blend all the ingredients together to a smooth paste using very little water.
