Crispy Peking Duck in Pancakes

Ducks. Plums. Pancakes. This Chinese-inspired recipe is a winner!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 5
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 duck legs
  • Salt
  • Five-spice
  • Fresh ginger
  • 6 de-stoned plums
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 pinch five-spice
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 pinch chilli powder
  • Grated orange zest
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1 bunch spring onions
  • 2 packs pre-made pancakes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 340°F.Rub the duck legs with lots of salt, inside and out and then all over with five-spice. Place the duck legs in a roasting tray and put it in the oven.
  2. To make the plum sauce. Place de-stoned plums into a pan with sugar, a five-spice 2 tablespoons of soy sauce and chilli powder. Bring to the boil, then simmer until its thick.
