MAGIC Bars

Topped with nuts, chocolate and coconut, think of these sweet treats as a guilt-free alternatives to candy bars.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 4
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup raw almonds
  • 2 tablespoons coconut sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 4 1/2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted

Coconut Milk Caramel

  • 1 can full-fat coconut milk
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of sea salt

Filling

  • 1/2 cup roasted almonds, chopped
  • 1/2 cup pecans, chopped
  • 3/4 cups dark chocolate
  • 1/2 cup coconut flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a food processor or high-speed blender, add the oats, almonds, coconut sugar and pulse into a fine meal, making sure no large chunks remain.Add the melted coconut oil and salt and pulse until it becomes a crumbly dough.
