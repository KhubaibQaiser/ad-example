Summer Herb Cheese Ball with Christmas Caponata

A colorful, festive spread of tangy, herby cheese topped with a savory eggplant caponata.

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Caponata

  • 1 eggplant
  • 1 large whole tomato
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, sliced
  • 1/2 cup basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/3 cup white vinegar
  • 1/3 cup sugar

Cheeseball

  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 16 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons dijon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped dill
  • 2 tablespoons chopped chives
  • 2 tablespoons chopped basil

Crostini

  • 1 baguette
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Dice the eggplant and tomato into 1/2–3/4 inch cubes. Heat a large sauté pan over medium heat and add the olive oil. Stir in the garlic and saute until fragrant but not brown, about 3 minutes.
  2. Add the cubed eggplant and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the tomato and cook for another 5 minutes.
