Ribeye Tagliata with Gremolata

This Italian steak recipe features juicy ribeye topped with herby gremolata.

By daniele uditi

Updated: 9/27/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Ribeye Tagliata

  • 2 (about 12 ounces ea) rib-eye steaks, cut 1-inch thick
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 5 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 parsley stems
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Gremolata

  • 1/3 cup parsley leaves, roughly chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh oregano, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon calabrian chili, minced
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon

Steps

  1. Season the steak liberally with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a cast iron pan pan on medium-high heat and add 4 tablespoons olive oil.
