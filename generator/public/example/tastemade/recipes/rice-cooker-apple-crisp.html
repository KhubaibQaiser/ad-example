Rice Cooker Apple Crisp

No oven, no problem! Use a rice cooker to whip up a satisfying, impressive apple crisp dessert.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/27/2024

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 1
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 apples, chopped into small cubes
  • 2 tablespoons butter, or 2 butter patties
  • 1 tablespoon or 1 packet sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 packet instant oatmeal, maple sugar flavor preferred
  • Ginger snap (crushed), vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Melt butter in the bottom of a rice cooker.
  2. Add apples and sugar. Let cook for a few minutes, until apples start to soften. Stir in cinnamon.
