Brandy Brioche French Toast with Berry Sauce

Laz gets his brioche French toast drunk.

By lazarus lynch

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

French Toast

  • 6 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 8 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 cup brandy, divided
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground allspice
  • Unsalted butter, for the griddle
  • 10 1 1/2-inches thick slices of brioches
  • Powdered sugar, for garnish
  • 8 ounces mascarpone cheese, for garnish

Berry sauce

  • 2 cups raspberries
  • 2 cups strawberries
  • 1/4 cup brandy
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs with the heavy cream, sugar, sugar, ground ginger, cinnamon, and allspice. Transfer to a deep pan.
  2. Cut a 2-inches pocket into the top of the bread.
