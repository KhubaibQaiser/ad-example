Black Forest Mousse Cake

May the (black) forest be with you with this chocolate mousse cake!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Prep Time: 40 min
Cook Time: 8 hr
Servings: 8
Average
 :

Ingredients

Black Forest Cake

  • 1 box brownie mix
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/2 cup oil

Cherry Filling

  • 15 ounces sweet cherries in syrup
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons kirsch (can substitute cognac, brandy, other cherry liquor or omit entirely)

Mousse

  • 12 ounces semisweet chocolate chips, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3 cups heavy whipping cream, divided
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar

Garnish

  • 1/4 cup melted chocolate for drizzle
  • Fresh mixed berry

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Line the bottom of a 9-inches springform baking pan with parchment paper and grease the sides.
