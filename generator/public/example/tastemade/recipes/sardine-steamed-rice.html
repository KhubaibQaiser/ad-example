Sardine Steamed Rice

Use up your stash of canned sardines to make this easy, garlicky mushroom and tomato rice dish.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 1 hr 5 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Servings: 1
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups uncooked rice
  • 1 can sardines in oil
  • 1 3/4 ounces mushroom, cut in half
  • 6 pearl onions, peeled
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bunch watercress, cut into 3/4-inch pieces
  • Black pepper
  • 10 1/2 fluid ounces water
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon bouillon granules
  • 1 chili pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Take out the sardines from the can and set aside.
  2. Combine uncooked rice, the oil from the can, minced garlic and the seasonings and give it a quick mix.
