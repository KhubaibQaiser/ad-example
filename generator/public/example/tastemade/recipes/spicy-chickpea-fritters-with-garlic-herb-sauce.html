Spicy Chickpea Fritters with Garlic Herb Sauce

Put a cheesy twist on falafel and serve it with a creamy, flavor-packed sauce.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/26/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Spicy Chickpea Fritters

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil plus more for
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup almond meal
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice (approx. 1/2 lemon)
  • 15 ounces no salt added chickpeas, drained, rinsed and dried

Garlic Herb Sauce

  • 1/3 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons hummus
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Add egg, garlic powder, almond meal, parsley, parmesan cheese, cumin, cayenne, 1/2 teaspoon sea salt, ground black pepper, 1 tablespoon olive oil, and lemon juice to a food processor. Blend until evenly mixed.
