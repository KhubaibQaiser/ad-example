Taro Ice Cream

Creamy taro makes your favorite frozen dessert even yummier.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 4 hr 10 min
Cook Time: 8 min
Servings: 2
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 250 grams taro, peeled
  • 100 milliliters milk
  • 100 grams heavy cream
  • 60 grams granulated sugar
  • Pinch salt
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Wash taro well and microwave for 8 minutes at 600 watts. Peel off the skin.
  2. In a blender, combine taro, milk, cream, sugar, salt and vanilla extract.
