Rice Paper Bacon

A simple, flavorful marinade transforms basic rice paper into a vegetarian-friendly "bacon"!

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Tastemade's Recipe Rundown

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 10 min
Servings: 3
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Rice paper bacon

  • 4 rice paper sheets
  • Bacon marinade

Basic marinade

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon liquid smoke
  • 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Lay out rice paper sheets. Working with 2 sheets at a time, brush a small amount of water onto one side of each sheet.
