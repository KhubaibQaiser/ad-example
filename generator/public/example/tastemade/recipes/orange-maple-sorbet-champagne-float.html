Orange Maple Sorbet Champagne Float

Your brunch mimosa with a twist.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Pinterest's Deliciously Entertaining

Prep Time: 4 hr
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 8
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 4 cups orange juice
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup pure cane sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup orange liqueur
  • Champagne
  • Orange slice, to garnish

Steps

  1. Freeze the canister of an electric ice cream maker overnight prior to making.
  2. In a small sauce pot, heat the orange juice, maple syrup, pure cane sugar, water and orange liqueur just until the sugar is dissolved, about 3–5 minutes.
