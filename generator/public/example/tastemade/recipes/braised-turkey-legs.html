Braised Turkey Legs

Thanksgiving dinner will be a breeze this year thanks to Chef Frankie Celenza's easy, tender braised turkey legs served with a flavorful gravy.

(1)

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 1 hr 30 min
Servings: 6
Easy
Â :

Ingredients

  • 6 turkey drumsticks
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 6 cups chicken stock
  • 2 sprigs rosemary
  • 2 sprigs sage
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons minced parsley leaves

Steps

  1. Pat the turkey legs dry with a paper towel and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large dutch oven over medium high heat. Working in batches, add the turkey legs to the dutch oven and cook until crisp and golden brown on all sides. Remove and set aside.
