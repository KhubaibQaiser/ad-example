Italian Chopped Salad

This Italian chopped salad features mortadella, salami, and plenty of bitter greens.

By daniele uditi

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Italian Chopped Salad

  • 6 slices salami
  • 4 slices mortadella
  • 1/3 cup provolone cheese, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup cooked garbanzo beans
  • 1/3 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup baby gem lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup radicchio, chopped
  • 1/3 cup fennel, shaved
  • 1/3 cup fresh oregano

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon calabrian chili, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups olive oil
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the salami and mortadella into 3-inches strips.
  2. Toss all the ingredients in a bowl.
