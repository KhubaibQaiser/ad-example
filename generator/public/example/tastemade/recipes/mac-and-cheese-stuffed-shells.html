Mac and Cheese Stuffed Shells

Dive into layers of dairy-free cheesy goodness with Chef Gabrielle Reyes' Vegan Mac and Cheese Stuffed Shells Recipe. Check out this feature on SpringHill's Recipe for Change, at youtube.com/MakeSpringhill

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min
Servings: 12
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 2 cups macaroni noodles
  • 3 cups large shell pasta noodles
  • 1/4 cup vegan butter
  • 5 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 4 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 3 tablespoons flour of choice
  • 2 cups coconut cream
  • 2 cups red marinara sauce
  • 2 cups follow your heart cheddar shreds
  • 1 1/2 cups follow your heart parmesan
  • 1 cup follow your heart veganaise
  • 1 1/2 cups crushed potato chips
  • 1/4 cup chopped chives

Steps

  1. Heat your oven to 385°F in preparation to bake the pasta dish. Boil two pots of hot water in preparation to cook the pasta
  2. In a large pot on medium heat, melt down vegan butter and sauté minced garlic until slightly golden brown
