Ube Macapuno Ensaymada

Ube and coconut make these Filipino sweet buns a delicious dessert that everyone will love. If you haven't tried ensaymada, this recipe is a great place to start!

By jen phanomrat

Updated: 9/29/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

Ube Macapuno Ensaymada

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 4 eggs, plus 1 for egg wash

Filling

  • 3/4 cup ube jam
  • 1/2 cup macapuno string

Topping

  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup grated mild white cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk milk, butter, and eggs. In another bowl, combine flour, sugar, and yeast.
  2. Slowly stir the wet mixture into the dry bowl and mix until a sticky dough forms.
