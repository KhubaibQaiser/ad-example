Mexican Huevos Rancheros

A struggle take on the traditional Mexican breakfast dish, with crispy tortillas, creamy refried beans, salsa and fried eggs

By Frankie Celenza

Updated: 9/28/2024

Recipe from:

Struggle Meals

Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 15 min
Servings: 4
Average
 :

Ingredients

  • 8 (6-inches) corn tortillas
  • 6 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 white onion
  • 4 ounces diced jalapeno peppers, drained
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 28 ounces diced fire roasted tomatoes
  • 30 ounces refried beans
  • 8 eggs
  • 8 ounces queso fresco, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup cilantro leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Brush the tortillas with 1 tablespoon of oil and season with salt. Place in the oven on the racks. Bake until crisp, about 15 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add 2 tablespoons of oil. Peel and mince the onion. Add it to the pan with the jalapenos. Cook for 5–7 minutes, until soft.
