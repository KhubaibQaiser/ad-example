Aunt Vie's Codfish Cakes with Garlic Aioli and Chives

Elevate your weeknight with the bright, bold, island flavors of Chef Rashida Holmes' codfish cakes and garlic aioli.

By Rashida Holmes

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 5 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :
Nutrition information unavailable

Ingredients

Codfish Cakes

  • 1 pound salt cod
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 white onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 4 eggs
  • 2–3 medium yukon gold potatoes
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 teaspoons red pepper flakes
  • 4 cups canola oil
  • Rice flour
  • Chive (for garnish)

Garlic Aioli

  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 ea lemon, juiced
  • 2 eas cloves of garlic, microplaned
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 cups blended canola and olive oil

Steps

  1. Start salt cod in cold water and bring to a boil, discard the water and repeat 3 times until all the salt has cooked out of the fish and it is soft
  2. Using forks and your fingers, pick apart the cod fish into tiny flakes so it resembles little hairs
