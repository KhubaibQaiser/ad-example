Honey-Roasted Carrots with Torn Burrata and Salsa Rustica

Use an entire bunch of fresh carrots in Chef Clodagh McKenna's simple side dish recipe, which roasts the carrots in honey for extra sweetness and finishes with a drizzle of carrot-top pesto.

By Numra Siddiqui

Updated: 9/29/2024

Recipe from:

If These Meals Could Talk

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 2
Easy
 :

Ingredients

For the carrots

  • 16 small carrots
  • 100 milliliters olive oil
  • 30 milliliters runny honey
  • 14 grams flat-leaf parsley, chopped
  • 14 grams mint, chopped
  • 100 grams whole almonds, chopped
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 x medium burrata balls
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

For the pesto

  • 473 grams carrot tops and stems, chopped
  • 40 grams pine nuts, toasted
  • 30 grams pumpkin seeds, toasted
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and grated
  • 120 milliliters quality extra virgin olive oil
  • 50 grams parmesan cheese, grated
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. To make the honey-roasted carrots, preheat the oven to 200°C/Gas mark 6.
  2. Put the carrots in a roasting tray, toss with 30 milliliters olive oil, 15 milliliters honey and season with salt and pepper.
