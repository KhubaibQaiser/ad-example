Gamjatang: Pork Bone Korean Hot Pot

Gamja-tang, a pork bone stew, is the perfect comfort meal. Learn how to make this spicy Korean hot pot right at home with our easy recipe.

Cook Time: 25 min
Servings: 8

 :

Ingredients

Gamjatang: Pork Bone Korean Hot Pot

  • 17 3/4 ounces pork spare ribs
  • 1 green
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3 potatoes
  • 3 1/2 ounces kimchi
  • 1 leek (whites)
  • 1/2 bag enoki mushroom
  • 3 cabbage leaves
  • 1/2 chinese chive
  • Salt, as needed
  • Pepper, as needed
  • A
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1 ginger, grated
  • 5 japanese chili peppers
  • 2 tablespoons miso
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sake

Soup

  • 10 dried sardines
  • 28 1/4 fluid ounces water

Steps

  1. Bring a pot with plenty of water to a boil. Add pork spare ribs and leek (greens) and boil for about 10 minutes.
  2. Heat sesame oil in another pan, add the ingredients from step 1 and cook until brown.
