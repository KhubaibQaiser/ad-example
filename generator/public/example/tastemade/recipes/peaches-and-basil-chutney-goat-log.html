Fresh Goat Log with Peaches and Basil Chutney

Peaches and basil chutney served with the creamy and tangy Fresh Goat Log compliments the sweetness of peaches and the bright herbaceous pop with the vinegar and basil.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 9/25/2024

Prep Time: 10 min
Servings: 6
Easy
Unit conversion unavailable

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fresh goat cheese
  • 2 each large ripe peaches
  • 6 leaves of fresh basil
  • 2 Tablespoon Olive oil
  • 1 Tablespoon White Balsamic vinegar
  • 1 Tablespoon Honey
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grilled or toasted bread or crackers

Steps

  1. Temper goat cheese for 30 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, cut peaches in half and remove pit, dice peaches into 1⁄4" cubes and place in a mixing bowl.
  3. Peaches are chopped and mixed with fresh basil, honey and balsamic vinegar. The creamy and tangy Fresh Goat Log compliments the sweetness of peaches and the bright herbaceous pop with the vinegar and basil.
  4. Stack 3-4 leaves of fresh basil and roll into a cylinder, slice ribbons of basil. Add to peaches.
  5. Drizzle on 1 tbs olive oil, 1 tbs white balsamic vinegar, 1 tbs honey salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Mix throughly. Allow to briefly macerate.
  7. Spread tempered goat cheese in serving bowl or dish.
  8. Spoon peach mixture over goat cheese.
  9. Serve with grilled or toasted bread.
