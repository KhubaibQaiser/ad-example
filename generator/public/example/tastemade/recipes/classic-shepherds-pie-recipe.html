Classic Shepherd's Pie

We can't say for sure whether this is indeed a shepherd's favourite pie, but it definitely is ours. This British classic is hearty and warming, perfect for winter. It's the perfect dinner that everyone in the family will enjoy.

By Tastemade Culinary Team

Updated: 10/2/2024

Prep Time: 1 hr
Cook Time: 3 hr
Servings: 2
Average
 :

Ingredients

Classic Shepherd's Pie

  • 30 milliliters beef dripping, divided
  • 1 kilogram minced lamb, preferably from the shoulder
  • 2 large onions, finely diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and finely diced
  • 2 celery sticks, diced
  • 2 sprigs each rosemary and thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 9 grams flour
  • 100 milliliters red wine
  • 400 milliliters good lamb stock (or beef stock)
  • 30 milliliters worcestershire sauce
  • 15 milliliters tomato purée

Topping

  • 1 1/2 kilograms floury potatoes
  • 100 grams butter, softened
  • 15 milliliters double cream
  • 75 grams parmesan, grated

Steps

  1. Heat 15 milliliters of the beef dripping in a large cast iron pot set over a medium heat.
  2. Brown the mince (you may need to do this in batches) and when golden, remove from the pot with a slotted spoon and set aside.
