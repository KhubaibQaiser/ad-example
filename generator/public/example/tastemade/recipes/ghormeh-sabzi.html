Ghormeh Sabzi

This Persian stew features lamb, chickpeas, spinach, and plenty of herbs.

By Einat Admony

Updated: 10/2/2024

Recipe from:

Make this Tonight

Prep Time: 30 min
Cook Time: 2 hr
Servings: 4
Easy
 :

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds lamb shoulder, trimmed and cut into 1 1/2 thicks thick chunks
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 5 teaspoons kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons dried fenugreek leaves
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 14 ounces chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 5 ounces baby spinach
  • 1 1/4 cups coarsely chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 cup coarsely chopped fresh dill
  • 1/4 cup coarsely chopped fresh mint
  • 2 dried persian limes, cracked
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 4 cups homemade or low sodium store-bought chicken stock

Steps

  1. Remove lamb from fridge about 30 mins before cooking.
  2. Preheat oven to 325°F.
